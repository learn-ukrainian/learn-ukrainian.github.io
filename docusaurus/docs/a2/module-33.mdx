## Introduction

- **When** I woke up, I drank coffee.

## Deep Dive: Before vs After

## Deep Dive: Complex Time Connectors

Sometimes simple ยซwhenยป is not enough. You need precision to express nuanced time relationships. Ukrainian offers several sophisticated time connectors that allow you to be very specific about how events relate to each other temporally.
