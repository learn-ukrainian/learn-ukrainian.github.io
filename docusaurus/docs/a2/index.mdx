# A2 - Elementary Level

**Complete - 57 Modules** | **~1,050 new vocabulary words**

A2 completes your foundation in Ukrainian grammar. You'll master all 7 cases, understand verbal aspect, and handle everyday conversations with confidence.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Phase A2.1: Completing the Cases (Modules 01-10)

Master the final two cases:

- **Dative case (Давальний)** — indirect objects, recipients (кому?)
- **Instrumental case (Орудний)** — means, accompaniment (ким? чим?)
- **Full pronoun declension** — all cases for all persons
- **Reflexive possessive свій** — "one's own" vs. його/її

### Phase A2.2: Aspect & Comparison (Modules 12-24)

Unlock the heart of Ukrainian verbs:

- **Verbal aspect (Вид)** — perfective vs. imperfective
- **Aspect pairs** — читати/прочитати, робити/зробити
- **Comparison** — більший, найкращий, comparative/superlative
- **Aspect in all tenses** — past, present, future

### Phase A2.3: Complex Sentences (Modules 25-34)

Building sophisticated expression:

- **Conjunctions** — тому що, щоб, коли, якщо
- **Relative clauses** — який, котрий, що
- **Conditional sentences** — real and hypothetical
- **Reported speech** — що він сказав, що...

### Phase A2.4: Word Formation (Modules 35-43)

Understanding how Ukrainian words work:

- **Prefixes** — meaningful additions to verbs and nouns
- **Suffixes** — diminutives, augmentatives, agent nouns
- **Root patterns** — recognizing word families
- **Compound words** — building complex vocabulary

### Phase A2.5: Vocabulary Expansion (Modules 44-55)

Thematic vocabulary for real life:

- **Health & body** — symptoms, doctor visits
- **Travel & transport** — booking, directions, schedules
- **Work & education** — professions, studies, workplace
- **Emotions & opinions** — expressing feelings and views

### Phase A2.6: Review & Consolidation (Modules 56-57)

- **Integration checkpoint** — comprehensive review
- **A2 capstone** — demonstrating all skills
