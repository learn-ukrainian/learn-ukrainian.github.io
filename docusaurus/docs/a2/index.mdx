# A2 - Elementary

**Level:** A2 (Elementary)
**Modules:** 50 modules
**Prerequisite:** A1 Complete
**Focus:** All 7 cases, verb aspect, comparison, complex sentences

---

## Level Overview

A2 is the "Bridge" level where you complete the Ukrainian case system and begin understanding verb aspect. By the end, you'll handle routine situations at shops, banks, and medical appointments with confidence.

### Learning Outcomes

- Master all 7 grammatical cases (Dative & Instrumental added)
- Understand perfective/imperfective aspect distinction
- Form comparative and superlative adjectives
- Use conditional mood (if I were...)
- Build complex sentences with subordinate clauses
- Recognize word formation patterns (prefixes, suffixes, roots)
