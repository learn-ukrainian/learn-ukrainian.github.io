# Literature Modules

Immersive reading experiences with authentic Ukrainian literary texts. These modules:

- Feature works from classic Ukrainian authors (Kotliarevsky, Kvitka-Osnovianenko, Shevchenko)
- Include vocabulary support and cultural context
- Provide comprehension activities and discussion prompts
- Cover various genres: poetry, prose, drama

**Prerequisites:** B1+ level recommended

**Available Modules:** 13 (Kotliarevsky's Eneida & Natalka Poltavka, Kvitka-Osnovianenko's prose, early Shevchenko)
