>
> English speakers often ask: "Why can't Ukrainian just use one verb form like English?" The answer lies in how Slavic languages evolved to encode *speaker perspective* directly in the verb.
>
> In English, you say "I read" and context clarifies meaning. In Ukrainian, the verb itself tells listeners:

>
