# B1 - Intermediate

**Level:** B1 (Intermediate)
**Modules:** 80 planned (10 available)
**Prerequisite:** A2 Complete
**Focus:** Aspect mastery, motion verbs, complex sentences, participles

---

## Level Overview

B1 marks the transition from elementary to independent user. At this level, you'll achieve **aspect mastery**, learn the complete **motion verb system**, and develop fluency with **complex sentence structures**. The curriculum uses a **TTT (Test-Teach-Test)** approach with narrative-driven content.

### Learning Outcomes

- Master verbal aspect selection in all contexts
- Use all 14 motion verb pairs with the full prefix system
- Build complex sentences with all subordinate clause types
- Understand and use adverbial participles
- Navigate real and unreal conditional constructions
- Handle synthetic future tense forms
