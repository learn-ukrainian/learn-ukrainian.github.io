# C2 - Mastery

**Level:** C2 (Mastery)
**Modules:** 80 planned
**Prerequisite:** C1 Complete
**Immersion:** 98% Ukrainian
**Focus:** Stylistic perfection, literary creation, professional specialization

---

## Level Overview

C2 represents **native-level mastery** of Ukrainian. At this level, you don't just use the language fluently—you create with it. The focus shifts to stylistic perfection, original literary production, and domain-agnostic professional excellence.

### Learning Outcomes

- Understand virtually everything heard or read effortlessly
- Summarize information from different sources with coherence
- Express yourself spontaneously with complete precision
- Differentiate finer shades of meaning in complex situations
- Write in multiple literary and professional registers
- Produce original creative and professional texts
