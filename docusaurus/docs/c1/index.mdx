# C1 - Advanced

**Level:** C1 (Advanced)
**Modules:** 115 planned
**Prerequisite:** B2 Complete
**Immersion:** 95% Ukrainian
**Focus:** Academic writing, literary analysis, sociolinguistics, register mastery

---

## Level Overview

C1 represents the transition from "learning Ukrainian" to "using Ukrainian" at the highest academic, professional, and analytical levels. You will engage with university-level texts, analyze literature, and master the full range of registers and styles.

### Learning Outcomes

- Understand extended speech with implicit meanings
- Express ideas fluently and spontaneously
- Write clear, well-structured text on complex subjects
- Recognize dialectal features and Surzhyk
- Produce academic papers, critiques, and research texts
- Analyze literary works across periods and styles
