# B2 - Upper-Intermediate

**Level:** B2 (Upper-Intermediate)
**Modules:** 125 planned (15 available)
**Prerequisite:** B1 Complete
**Immersion:** 80% Ukrainian
**Focus:** Passive voice, participles, register system, phraseology, Ukrainian history

---

## Level Overview

B2 marks the transition from "independent user" to "proficient user." At this level, the focus shifts from "learning to read" to **"reading to learn"** through content-based instruction with substantial Ukrainian texts. You'll master the passive voice system, understand all 5 functional registers, and engage with Ukrainian history and culture.

### Learning Outcomes

- Master all 4 passive voice constructions
- Understand and use active and passive participles
- Navigate all 5 functional styles (register system)
- Use phraseology, proverbs, and set expressions
- Build multi-clause sentences with discourse markers
- Engage with Ukrainian history and biographical texts
