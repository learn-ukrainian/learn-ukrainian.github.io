### The Ukrainian Home

**Historical Context:** Ukrainian homes reflect a blend of Soviet-era apartment design and modern European styles. **Modern Reality:** Most Ukrainians live in apartments (ĞºĞ²Ğ°Ñ€Ñ‚Ğ¸Ñ€Ğ¸), not houses. In cities like Kyiv, Lviv, Kharkiv, and Odesa, apartments dominate the housing market.
