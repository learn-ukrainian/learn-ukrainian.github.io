## Warm-up

Ukraine is a large country, and traveling by **–ø–æ—ó–∑–¥** (train) is the most popular way to get around. The Ukrainian Railways (**–£–∫—Ä–∑–∞–ª—ñ–∑–Ω–∏—Ü—è**) connect major cities like Kyiv, Lviv, Odesa, and Kharkiv with comfortable overnight and intercity trains.

In this module, you will learn how to buy a **–∫–≤–∏—Ç–æ–∫** (ticket), choose your seat, and check the schedule. Whether you are planning a weekend trip or a long journey, these phrases will get you there!

:::danger[üõ°Ô∏è Myth Buster]
**Myth:** Ukrainian trains are old, slow, and uncomfortable.

**Fact:** While there are still some older trains, Ukraine has a modern fleet of **Intercity+** (Hyundai) high-speed trains that are fast, clean, and comfortable. They connect Kyiv with other major cities in just 5-6 hours. You can even buy tickets online with an app!
:::
