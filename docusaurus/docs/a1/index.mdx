# A1 - Beginner Level

**Complete - 34 Modules** | **~750 vocabulary words**

A1 level introduces you to Ukrainian from scratch. By the end, you'll understand basic conversations and express yourself in simple, everyday situations.

---

## Curriculum Overview

### Phase A1.1: First Contact (Modules 1-10)

Your introduction to Cyrillic and basic Ukrainian:

- **The Cyrillic alphabet** — reading and pronunciation
- **Basic greetings** — Привіт, Добрий день, До побачення
- **Self-introduction** — Я, ти, він/вона, name and origin
- **Numbers 0-100** — counting, phone numbers, ages
- **Gender system** — masculine, feminine, neuter nouns

### Phase A1.2: Navigation (Modules 11-20)

Building sentences and navigating the world:

- **Accusative case** — direct objects, direction (куди?)
- **Locative case** — location (де?), months
- **Genitive case** — absence (немає), possession
- **Plurals** — regular and common irregular forms
- **Daily routine** — time, activities, scheduling

### Phase A1.3: Time & Self (Modules 21-34)

Expressing yourself across time:

- **Past tense** — describing yesterday and before
- **Future tense** — planning tomorrow
- **Adjectives** — describing people, places, things
- **Family & relationships** — vocabulary and possessives
- **Shopping & services** — practical transactions

---

## What You'll Master

| Skill | A1 Achievement |
|-------|----------------|
| **Reading** | Cyrillic alphabet, simple texts |
| **Speaking** | Basic introductions, daily transactions |
| **Listening** | Slow, clear speech on familiar topics |
| **Writing** | Simple sentences, forms, short messages |
| **Grammar** | 5 cases (Nom, Acc, Gen, Loc, Voc), 3 tenses |

---

## Prerequisites

**None** — complete beginners welcome! No prior knowledge of Ukrainian or Cyrillic required.
