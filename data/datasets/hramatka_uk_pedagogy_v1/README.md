# Hramatka Ukrainian Pedagogical Alignment Dataset (`hramatka-uk-pedagogy-v1`)

> **Target Audience**: UNLP (Ukrainian Natural Language Processing) researchers, open-model fine-tuners, and AI alignment engineers.  
> **Purpose**: Fine-tuning open-weights models (Gemma 4, Llama 4, Mistral) to achieve Gemini 3.6 Flash level activity density, pedagogical rigor, and 100% Ukrainian immersion.

---

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains structured B1 Ukrainian lesson authoring pairs generated from authentic Ukrainian anchor texts (*«Як ми шукали квартиру у Львові»*).

- **Gold Reference Lessons**: Exemplary lessons generated by Gemini 3.6 Flash featuring 8 distinct activity types (`quiz`, `true-false`, `cloze`, `match-up`, `mark-the-words`, `fill-in`, `error-correction`, `text-questions`), high item density (>= 5 items per activity), and 100% VESUM morphosyntactic verification.
- **SFT / DPO Preference Pairs**: Direct alignment pairs contrasting raw Gemma 4 31B outputs (which suffer from field casing issues, shallow item density, and English label leaks) against elevated gold targets.

## How to Use for Fine-Tuning Gemma 4 31B

1. **Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT)**: Train Gemma 4 31B using `anchor_text` as prompt and `elevated_target_lesson` as target completion.
2. **Direct Preference Optimization (DPO)**: Use `raw_input_lesson` as rejected response and `elevated_target_lesson` / `gemini_gold` as chosen response.
