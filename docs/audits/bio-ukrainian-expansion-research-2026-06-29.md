## Required Build Framing

The following cautions must be carried into future per-module writer briefs or
plan metadata, not left only in this audit memo:

- Максим Залізняк / Іван Гонта: high-content-warning treatment of Uman,
  Polish/Jewish/Uniate memory, civilian harm, and anti-hagiography.
- Нестор Махно: explicitly teach language/statehood/anarchism complexity;
  do not retrofit him into a simple Ukrainian-state patriot.
- Улас Самчук: include WWII occupation-era newspaper/editorship context and
  avoid hagiography.
- Soviet-era cultural figures: frame Ukrainian-language cultural memory under
  constraint, including compromise/co-optation where relevant.
- Living cultural figures kept in BIO: use completed contributions only; no
  predictive "future national leader" framing.
- Current wartime watchlist figures: do not add to BIO until the trajectory is
  stable enough for non-predictive biography.

## Sequencing Decision

BIO roster preparation can proceed in parallel with FOLK and HIST work. BIO
writing does not need to wait for the entire HIST track, but state-building,
UNR, dissident, independence, and wartime biographies need a HIST-alignment
checkpoint so the biography modules do not invent their own historical frame.
