ULP 1-22 | Are You Going to the Party? – First Verb Conjugation in Ukrainian

  • Anna Ohoiko
  • December 21, 2016
Copied!

In this lesson, you will learn more about the first verb conjugation in Ukrainian.

As always, we present it in context. Listen to two friends discussing a party and learn more Ukrainian with our Podcast!

In this Ukrainian Lesson, you will:

  • listen to the conversation between two friends at the bus stop in Ukraine;
  • learn the difference between іти, йти and їхати in Ukrainian;
  • learn the details about the first verb conjugation in Ukrainian;
  • practice using the 1st conjugation verbs in the present tense;
  • discover if Ukrainians are punctual people.
