### Specific Examples (A1)
- **Grammatical/Lexical (Calque):**
  - `what-will-happen.yaml`: *"Будеш знаходити нових друзів (m)."*
  - **Issue:** Calque of the English phrasing "you will find". In Ukrainian, the perfective "знайдеш" (знайдеш нових друзів) is the natural idiom here, not the imperfective process of "finding".
- **Naturalness:**
  - `what-will-happen.yaml`: *"Fortune teller at a fun fair — predicting the future"*
  - **Issue:** Western trope. Ukrainian "ворожка" is typically visited in a private home/village setting, not at a public "fun fair" (луна-парк).
- **Level-appropriateness:**
  - `checkpoint-actions.yaml`: *"Job interview — describing your typical day, skills, and schedule"*
  - **Issue:** A job interview is generally a B1+ situational context. It is too formal and complex for an A1 checkpoint.
- **Duplication Audit:**
  - The "planning a weekend/trip with friends" scenario is overly repetitive across the track (`my-plans.yaml`, `linking-ideas.yaml`, `checkpoint-time-nature.yaml`, `weather.yaml`).

## A2 Track Summary
- **Total Modules Reviewed:** 69
- **Duplicates Flagged:** Massive over-reliance on classroom/student/teacher meta-settings for grammar modules.
- **Naturalness Issues Flagged:** 1 logical absurdity.
- **Grammatical/Lexical Flags:** 4 (🚨 CRITICAL)
- **Coverage Audit:** Lacks modern bureaucratic settings (e.g., ЦНАП) or personal care (Перукарня), relying too heavily on school and cafes.

### Specific Examples (A2)
- **Grammatical/Lexical Flags (🚨 CRITICAL):**
  - `instrumental-means.yaml`: *"Різаю ножицями (pl, scissors)."* 🚨 **CRITICAL**
    - **Issue:** "Різаю" is a non-existent verb form (morphological error). The correct 1st person singular is "Ріжу" (від *різати*).
  - `synthetic-future.yaml`: *"Вона здасть іспит (m, exam)!"* 🚨 **CRITICAL**
    - **Issue:** Direct Russianism ("сдавать экзамен"). The correct Ukrainian collocation is "складе іспит".
  - `sviy-and-sebe.yaml`: *"Ні, це свій — я його купив собі!"* 🚨 **CRITICAL**
    - **Issue:** "Це свій" used as a standalone predicate nominative is grammatically incorrect in this context. It should be "він мій" or "це мій власний".
  - `checkpoint-genitive.yaml`: *"До Хрещатику (m)"* 🚨 **CRITICAL**
    - **Issue:** Incorrect ending. The Genitive form for this specific proper noun is "Хрещатика".
- **Naturalness (Logical Absurdity):**
  - `real-conditionals.yaml`: *"Якщо йтиме дощ (m, rain), польємо пізніше."*
    - **Issue:** Logically absurd. If it rains, you wouldn't water the garden later.
- **Duplication & Scenario Crutch:**
  - `metalanguage-morphology.yaml`, `checkpoint-syntax.yaml`, `metalanguage-words-and-cases.yaml`, `metalanguage-sentences-and-classroom.yaml`, `metalanguage-verbs-and-time.yaml`, `metalanguage-phonetics.yaml`, `metalanguage-syntax-cases.yaml`
  - **Issue:** Nearly all grammar and metalanguage modules use "Teacher explaining to student" or "Students quizzing each other in a library". This violates the mandate for real-life communicative settings.
