## Track Overview

The RUTH track covers the **Ruthenian (Руська мова / Middle Ukrainian)** period — the "bridge" from medieval to modern Ukrainian. It traces five centuries of linguistic evolution through document literacy, stylistic analysis, and the polyphonic mix of registers (Chancery, Church Slavonic, Prosta Mova).

### Key Concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Руська мова** | The administrative and literary language of Ukrainian lands in the GDL and Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth |
| **Проста мова** | "Simple/vernacular language" — the written form closer to actual spoken Ukrainian |
| **Диглосія** | Coexistence of Church Slavonic (high) and Ruthenian (middle/low) registers |
| **Канцелярський стиль** | Chancery style — formal legal/administrative language |
