# A1 Curriculum V3 — Built from State Standard + Textbooks + Anna Ohoiko

**Date:** 2026-03-20
**Status:** DRAFT — needs Gemini adversarial review
**Method:** State Standard 2024 defines WHAT. Ukrainian textbooks define HOW. Anna Ohoiko adapts for L2 adults.

## Sources

### State Standard 2024 (docs/l2-uk-en/state-standard-2024-mapping.yaml)
- **§4.1 Phonetics:** alphabet, apostrophe, soft sign, vowels/consonants, stress, euphony, intonation
- **§4.2 Morphology:** noun declension (4 types), adjective declension, numerals, pronouns
- **§4.2.3 Cases:** nominative, accusative, locative, vocative (dative/instrumental = A2)
- **§4.2.4 Verbs:** indicative (present/past/future), imperative (2nd person), reflexive (-ся/-сь)
- **§4.3 Syntax:** simple sentences (SVO, questions, negation), basic complex (і, а, але, бо, що, коли, де)
- **§3 Themes:** людина, дім, місто, побут, діяльність, дозвілля, подорожі, купівля, ресторан, здоров'я, природа, традиції

### Ukrainian Textbooks (RAG verified)
- **Большакова Grade 1 буквар:** звуки→літери→склади→читання. Golden rule: "Ми чуємо і вимовляємо звуки, а бачимо і пишемо літери." 33 letters, 38 sounds (6 vowels, 32 consonants). Голосні/приголосні taught through songs and games.
- **Захарійчук Grade 1 (NUS 2025):** Апостроф taught with б, п, в, м, ф, р before я, ю, є, ї. М'який знак as тверді/м'якшені sounds.
- **Grade 4:** Full case table with helper words — Н. (є), Р. (немає), Д. (даю), Зн. (бачу), Ор. (милуюся), М. (на, у), Кл. (!)

### Anna Ohoiko ULP Season 1 (40 episodes, free)
Progression: greetings → introduce yourself → where from → numbers → family + У мене є → possessives → professions → market → food → days/months → weather → city → verb conjugation I → verb conjugation II → colors → past tense → future tense → shopping + accusative → question words → interviews

Key methodology: conversation-first, grammar emerges from need, pronunciation trainers scattered throughout, cultural facts in every episode, review every 10 episodes.

## Design Principles

1. **State Standard coverage is mandatory** — every §4 requirement mapped to at least one module
2. **Textbook pedagogy, not English pedagogy** — teach звуки/літери the way Большакова does, not "letters that look like English"
3. **Conversation from day one** — following Anna: learners greet people before they finish the alphabet
4. **Grammar through situations** — every grammar point introduced through a REAL communicative need
5. **Cases through helper words** — following Grade 4 textbooks: Хто? Що? = Називний, Кого? Що? = Знахідний
6. **No dative/instrumental at A1** — State Standard explicitly puts these at A2
7. **Imperative only 2nd person** — State Standard §4.2.4.2 specifies this for A1
8. **12 themes covered** — every §3 theme appears in at least one module
9. **Anna's video resources** — pronunciation playlist linked where relevant
10. **Checkpoints for consolidation** — following Anna's review-every-10 pattern

## Sub-levels

Based on the textbook progression and Anna's episode structure:

### A1.1: Sounds, Letters, and First Words
**State Standard:** §4.1 (phonetics), §3 theme: людина (people)
**Textbook source:** Большакова Grade 1 буквар chapters 1-3
**Anna equivalent:** Before Episode 1 (she skips alphabet, we can't for written medium)

What the learner achieves: reads Cyrillic, knows sounds vs letters, can say hello and introduce themselves.

### A1.2: My World
**State Standard:** §4.2.1.1 (nouns/gender), §4.2.1.2 (adjectives), §4.2.1.3 (numerals), §4.2.2 (pronouns), §3 themes: дім (home), купівля (shopping)
**Textbook source:** Grade 2 noun gender, Grade 3 adjective agreement
**Anna equivalent:** Episodes 5-9 (numbers, family, professions, market)

What the learner achieves: describes objects with gender/adjectives, counts, talks about family.

### A1.3: Actions
**State Standard:** §4.2.4.1 (verb conjugation I/II, reflexive), §3 themes: побут (daily life), діяльність (activities)
**Textbook source:** Grade 3-4 verb conjugation tables
**Anna equivalent:** Episodes 22, 24 (verb groups)

What the learner achieves: talks about what they do, want, can, must. Asks questions.

### A1.4: Time and Nature
**State Standard:** §4.1.5 (stress), §3 themes: природа (nature), дозвілля (leisure)
**Textbook source:** Grade 2 time/calendar, Grade 3 weather
**Anna equivalent:** Episodes 15-16 (days/months, weather)

What the learner achieves: tells time, plans a week, describes weather, talks about hobbies.

### A1.5: Places
**State Standard:** §4.2.3.3 (locative), §4.2.3.2 (accusative for direction), §4.1.7 (euphony), §3 themes: місто (city), подорожі (travel)
**Textbook source:** Grade 4 case tables — Місцевий (на, у), Знахідний (бачу)
**Anna equivalent:** Episodes 17-18 (city, directions)

What the learner achieves: navigates a city, says where things are, gives directions.

### A1.6: Food and Shopping
**State Standard:** §4.2.3.2 (accusative objects), §3 themes: купівля (shopping), ресторан (restaurant)
**Textbook source:** Grade 4 Знахідний case with helper word "бачу"
**Anna equivalent:** Episodes 11-13, 31-32 (food, shopping, accusative)

What the learner achieves: orders food, buys things, uses accusative for objects.

### A1.7: Communication
**State Standard:** §4.2.3.4 (vocative), §4.2.4.2 (imperative), §4.3.2 (complex sentences), §3 themes: традиції (traditions)
**Textbook source:** Grade 4 Кличний case, Grade 5 Наказовий спосіб
**Anna equivalent:** Episodes 23, 29 (holidays)

What the learner achieves: addresses people, makes requests, links ideas, talks about holidays.

### A1.8: Past, Future, and Graduation
**State Standard:** §4.2.4.1 (past/future tense), §3 themes: здоров'я (health)
**Textbook source:** Grade 3-4 past/future tense formation
**Anna equivalent:** Episodes 26-28 (past tense, future tense)

What the learner achieves: tells stories about yesterday, makes plans, handles emergencies, combines all skills.

| Aspect | V2 | V3 |

| Alphabet approach | "Familiar/False friends/New" (English-centric) | Звуки/літери (Ukrainian pedagogy from Большакова) |
| Case introduction | Mixed into themes | Follows Grade 4 case table with helper words |
| Dative | Included (M46) | Removed (State Standard says A2) |

| Each module has | Arbitrary structure | State Standard reference + textbook basis + Anna reference |
| Checkpoints | 8 | 8 (same, one per sub-level) |

## Removed from V2 (State Standard compliance)
- **Dative case** (M46 give-me-i-like) — §4.2.2.3 is A2, not A1. "Мені подобається" taught as lexical chunk, not grammar.
- **Instrumental case** (M37 кава з молоком) — §4.2.2.5 is A2. Food vocabulary doesn't need instrumental.
- **Adjective case agreement** (V2 M42) — too advanced for A1, merged into locative/accusative modules.
