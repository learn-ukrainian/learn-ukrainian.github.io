# C2 Curriculum Restructure Proposal

**Issue:** #834 — C2 State Standard 2024 Compliance
**Date:** 2026-03-13
**Status:** APPROVED — Gemini scored 9.5/10 (two minor sub-topics added per review)

## Problem Statement

Gemini adversarial review scored our C2 compliance claim **2/10**. The analysis found that 10-12 of the 21 State Standard thematic areas (Каталог Б) have **zero dedicated coverage** in the current 91-module C2 curriculum.

The current curriculum excels at:
- Functional style mastery (7 styles) — State Standard §4.3.1.1 ✅
- Literary analysis and creative writing ✅
- Professional language and document types ✅
- Linguistic meta-skills (grammar review, error analysis, sociolinguistics) ✅

But completely misses:
- Sport, Health, Environment, Economy, Consumer culture, Urban/rural life, Travel, Traditions, Social relations, Politics, Foreign affairs — all explicitly mandated by the Standard with specific C2-level sub-topics.

## Root Cause

The original C2 design assumed that at C2 level, learners have "meta-skills" to self-study any domain. While true pedagogically, the **State Standard exam tests specific vocabulary and discourse competence** across all 21 thematic areas. A student who cannot discuss "парниковий ефект" (greenhouse effect), "медичне страхування" (health insurance), or "шкода від допінгу" (harm of doping) will fail the C2 certification — regardless of how well they write literary essays.

## Solution: New Phase C2.4 — Thematic Discourse (Тематичний дискурс)

### Architecture

Insert a new 14-module phase between the current C2.3 (Professional Language) and C2.4 (Meta-Skills & Capstone):

| Phase | Name | Modules | Status |
|-------|------|---------|--------|
| C2.1 | Stylistics & Rhetoric | 26 | KEEP (unchanged) |
| C2.2 | Literature & Creative Writing | 17 | KEEP (unchanged) |
| C2.3 | Professional Language | 21 | KEEP (unchanged) |
| **C2.4** | **Thematic Discourse** | **14** | **NEW** |
| C2.5 | Meta-Skills & Capstone | 27 | KEEP (renumbered from C2.4) |
| **Total** | | **105** | |

### Pedagogical Rationale

At C2, thematic modules are NOT vocabulary lists or A1-style topic introductions. They are **analytical discourse modules** that:

1. Present C2-level specialized vocabulary in context (15-20 terms per module)
2. Develop argumentation and critical analysis skills on real-world topics
3. Practice register switching: discussing the same topic in scientific, journalistic, and conversational registers
4. Connect to Ukrainian cultural/political reality (decolonization lens)
5. Apply the stylistic tools mastered in C2.1 to real-world content

### Module Design

Each module covers 2-3 related State Standard themes, focusing on the **C2-specific sub-topics** the Standard explicitly names.

## New C2.4 Modules — Detailed Design

### Module 65: suspilstvo-i-hromadyanstvo (Суспільство і громадянство — Society & Citizenship)
**State Standard themes:** 3.7 Суспільні відносини + 3.8 Внутрішня політика

**Content areas:**
- Social institutions and societal problems (корупція, злочинність, патріотизм)
- Gender equality, minority rights, youth subcultures
- State structure (законодавча, виконавча, судова гілки влади)
- Electoral system, political movements, reform (децентралізація, пенсійна, земельна)
- War and peace, demographic processes
- Discourse practice: analyzing political speeches, debating reform proposals

**C2-level vocabulary (sample):** децентралізація, електоральний, правоохоронний, ґендерна рівність, субкультура, демографічний, законодавчий, реформування, безчинство, правозахисний

**Content areas:**

## Risk Assessment

- **Over-engineering risk:** LOW — these modules address explicit State Standard requirements, not hypothetical needs
- **Redundancy risk:** MEDIUM — some overlap with C2.3 professional modules on work/economy topics. Mitigated by keeping C2.3 focused on professional register while C2.4 covers thematic breadth
- **Module count risk:** 105 modules is large but C2 is the final level with the broadest requirements. The State Standard mandates all 21 themes.
