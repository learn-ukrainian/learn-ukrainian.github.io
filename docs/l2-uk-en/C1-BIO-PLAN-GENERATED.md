## Track Overview

Comprehensive biographies of significant Ukrainians across all fields and eras

### Vocabulary Focus

- Biographical terminology (народження, смерть, освіта, кар'єра)
- Historical period vocabulary
- Domain-specific terms by field (politics, art, science, military)
- Honorifics and titles

### Pedagogical Approach

**Approach:** seminar

**Structure:**
- Historical context of the era
- Early life and formation
- Key achievements and contributions
- Legacy and contemporary relevance
- Discussion questions (decolonization focus where applicable)

**Decolonization:**
- Present Ukrainian figures in their own context, not as 'Russian' or 'Soviet'
- Highlight resistance to imperial oppression
- Correct Soviet-era distortions of biography
- Emphasize Ukrainian agency and contribution
