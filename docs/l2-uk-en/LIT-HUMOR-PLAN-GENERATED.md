## Track Overview

Ukrainian humor tradition from Kotliarevsky's burlesque to internet memes — how laughter preserved identity under empires and how it fights today. Ukrainian humor is not 'simple folk comedy' — it is sophisticated, subversive, and has survived every imperial attempt to silence it.

### Vocabulary Focus

- Humor terminology (сатира, іронія, бурлеск, гротеск, пародія)
- Colloquial/informal register
- Dialectal humor vocabulary
- Internet slang and meme vocabulary

### Grammar Focus

- Satirical stylistic devices
- Colloquial register in literature
- Dialectal features in humorous texts
- Contemporary informal Ukrainian

### Pedagogical Approach

**Approach:** seminar

**Structure:**
- Humorous text excerpt with glossary
- Analysis: what makes this funny? (cultural context required)
- Discussion: humor as survival mechanism under empire
- Comparative: how does Ukrainian humor differ from neighboring traditions?

**Decolonization:**
- Ukrainian humor targets POWER, not weakness — it punches up
- Ostap Vyshnia was imprisoned for being funny — humor was dangerous to the empire
- Meme culture during 2022 invasion = direct continuation of Eneida tradition
- Russian humor about Ukraine (малоросійські анекдоти) is imperial — ours is resistance
