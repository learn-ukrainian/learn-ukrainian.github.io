---

## Architecture

The Ukrainian State Standard 2024 defines minimum expectations but not teaching methodology. We combine three sources:

| Source | Role | How we use it |
|--------|------|---------------|
| State Standard 2024 | WHAT to teach | Every module maps to a § reference |
| Ukrainian textbooks (Большакова, Захарійчук, Заболотний, Авраменко) | HOW native teachers teach | Pedagogy, exercises, progression |
| Anna Ohoiko ULP | HOW to adapt for L2 adults | Situation-first, pronunciation, cultural context |

### Key constraints from State Standard
- **A1 cases:** Nominative, Accusative, Locative, Vocative ONLY
- **A1 verbs:** Present, past, future; imperative 2nd person only; reflexive -ся/-сь
- **A2 adds:** Genitive, Dative, Instrumental as full grammar; aspect pairs; complete imperative; comparative/superlative; complex subordination
- **Genitive, Dative, Instrumental are A2** — any A1 usage (Я з України, мені подобається, кава з молоком) is taught as memorized chunks, not grammar
- **Possessive/demonstrative pronouns** — taught at A1 as vocabulary in nominative only; full paradigms at A2

# A1 — Початковий рівень першого ступеня

## State Standard A1 Requirements (§ references)

**§4.1 Phonetics:** alphabet (§4.1.1), apostrophe (§4.1.2), soft sign (§4.1.3), vowels/consonants (§4.1.4), stress (§4.1.5), euphony (§4.1.7), intonation (§4.1.8)

**§4.2 Morphology:** noun gender + plural nominative (§4.2.1.1), adjective nominative + accusative + locative agreement (§4.2.1.2), cardinal/ordinal numerals (§4.2.1.3), personal pronoun declension (§4.2.1.4), possessive/demonstrative pronoun gender forms (§4.2.2)

**§4.2.3 Cases:** nominative (§4.2.3.1), accusative without/with prepositions (§4.2.3.2), locative with у/в/на (§4.2.3.3), vocative (§4.2.3.4)

**§4.2.4 Verbs:** conjugation I & II present tense (§4.2.4.1), past tense (gender-marked), analytic future (буду + infinitive), imperative 2nd person (§4.2.4.2), reflexive -ся/-сь

**§4.3 Syntax:** simple sentences — SVO, questions (хто? що? де? куди? коли?), negation не/ні (§4.3.1); basic complex with і, а, але, бо, що, коли, де (§4.3.2)

**§3 Themes (12):** людина, дім, місто, побут, діяльність, дозвілля, подорожі, купівля, ресторан, здоров'я, природа, традиції

---

## A1 Design Principles

1. **Interleave phonetics with communication** — learner says Привіт in Module 1, not Module 5 (Gemini review finding)
2. **Cases through helper words** — following Grade 4 textbooks: Н. (є), Зн. (бачу), М. (на, у), Кл. (!)
3. **Genitive/Dative/Instrumental = chunks only** — "Я з України", "мені подобається" are memorized, not analyzed
4. **Grammar through situations** — every grammar point has a REAL communicative context
5. **Anna's video resources** — pronunciation playlist linked per module
6. **Textbook-grounded** — every teaching point traceable to a Ukrainian school textbook

# A2 — Початковий рівень другого ступеня

## What A2 Adds (State Standard)

| Category | A1 had | A2 adds |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **Cases** | Nom, Acc, Loc, Voc | + **Genitive** (без, від, для, до, з, після, біля), **Dative** (indirect object, мені холодно), **Instrumental** (з/із, за, між, над, перед, під) |
| **Verbs** | Present, past, future; imperative 2nd; reflexive | + **Aspect pairs** (доконаний/недоконаний), **motion verbs** (їхати/ходити), **complete imperative** (1st pl: читаймо, 3rd: хай/нехай) |
| **Adjectives** | Nominative + Acc/Loc agreement | + **Full paradigm** all cases, **comparative** (більший), **superlative** (найбільший) |
| **Pronouns** | Personal declension, possessive/demonstrative (Nom only) | + **Full paradigm** possessive/demonstrative/interrogative |
| **Numerals** | Cardinals/ordinals basic | + **Declension** of cardinals and ordinals |
| **Syntax** | Simple + basic complex (і, а, але, бо) | + **тому що, для того щоб, якщо, хоча, який/яка/яке** |
| **Stylistics** | — | + **Synonyms, antonyms, diminutives**, word order variation |
| **Phonetics** | Basics | + **Consonant assimilation**, expanded stress patterns, expanded euphony |
| **Themes** | 12 | 14 (+ навчання, робота, послуги, їжа as distinct from ресторан) |
