## Pedagogical Differences

### 1. Aspect System

- **Dobra Forma:** 4 chapters (27.1-27.4), rule-based approach
- **Our Curriculum:** 11 modules (A2 M28-30, B1 M06-15), narrative-driven
- **Rationale:** Aspect requires extensive practice; we use stories to build intuition

### 2. Terminology

- **Dobra Forma:** "First/Second Conjugation"
- **Our Curriculum:** "Class I/II" or "Declension Groups"
- **Rationale:** Aligns with modern Ukrainian linguistics

### 3. Case Introduction Order

- **Dobra Forma:** Locative ‚Üí Accusative ‚Üí Genitive ‚Üí Instrumental ‚Üí Dative
- **Our Curriculum:** Accusative ‚Üí Locative ‚Üí Genitive ‚Üí Instrumental ‚Üí Dative
- **Rationale:** Accusative is more frequent in beginner contexts

### 4. Verbs of Motion

- **Dobra Forma:** 3 chapters (28.1-28.3)
- **Our Curriculum:** 9 modules (B1 M16-24)
- **Rationale:** Motion verbs are complex; we scaffold extensively
