## Track Overview

The C1-PRO track develops advanced professional communication skills for executives, academics, and specialists requiring mastery-level Ukrainian. It covers executive communication, academic publishing, deep industry specialization, and cross-cultural competence.

**Learning Goals:**
- Master executive-level communication and leadership language
- Develop academic writing and publishing skills in Ukrainian
- Build deep expertise in chosen professional domains
- Understand cross-cultural business communication
- Develop basic translation and interpretation skills

**Pedagogical Approach:** ESP (English for Specific Purposes) + CLIL (Content and Language Integrated Learning)
