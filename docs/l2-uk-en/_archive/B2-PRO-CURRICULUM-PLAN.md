## Track Overview

The B2-PRO track develops professional communication skills in Ukrainian for learners seeking career applications. It covers business communication, technical domains, and media/public discourse.

**Learning Goals:**
- Master business correspondence and meeting communication
- Develop domain-specific vocabulary (IT, finance, legal, medical)
- Analyze Ukrainian media and public discourse critically
- Build presentation and public speaking skills

**Pedagogical Approach:** ESP (English for Specific Purposes adapted for Ukrainian)
