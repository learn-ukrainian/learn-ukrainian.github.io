## Track Overview

Ukrainian speculative fiction from Gothic folklore to cyberpunk — proving Ukrainian imagination has always transcended realism and imperial constraints. Destroys the myth that Ukrainian literature is 'just village prose' — showcases cosmic philosophy, urban fantasy, magic realism, and cyberpunk.

### Vocabulary Focus

- Sci-fi terminology (космос, кіборг, штучний інтелект, паралельний всесвіт)
- Fantasy/Gothic vocabulary (чаклунство, прокляття, примари, лісовик)
- Philosophical vocabulary (свідомість, буття, трансцендентність)
- Ukrainian mythological vocabulary (мавка, русалка, упир)

### Grammar Focus

- Gothic narrative register
- Philosophical argumentation in fiction
- Mythological vocabulary and symbolism
- Contemporary genre fiction style

### Pedagogical Approach

**Approach:** seminar

**Structure:**
- Genre excerpt with glossary
- Genre analysis: what makes this Ukrainian (not just translated Western tropes)?
- Cultural context: how does this work engage with Ukrainian mythology/identity?
- Comparative analysis with world genre traditions

**Decolonization:**
- Ukrainian speculative fiction is NOT derivative of Russian sci-fi
- Berdnyk was imprisoned for his ideas — his sci-fi was resistance
- Ukrainian Gothic predates and differs from Russian Gothic
- Contemporary genre fiction proves Ukrainian culture is modern, urban, global
