**Vocabulary Target:** Content-driven (~24/module)

> **Note:** This track was relocated from B2 M71-131 as part of RFC #409 Curriculum Reorganization. History content is now optional, allowing learners to focus on grammar/skills in core path.

---

## Track Overview

The B2-HIST track covers Ukrainian history from prehistoric times through the full-scale Russian invasion of 2022. It follows a **chronological narrative** approach with synthesis modules at key historical turning points.

**Learning Goals:**
- Understand Ukrainian history as continuous national development
- Recognize patterns of imperial oppression and resistance
- Analyze primary sources and historical narratives
- Build historical vocabulary and narrative structures

**Pedagogical Approach:** CBI (Content-Based Instruction) with narrative arcs

## Decolonization Perspective

This track explicitly addresses Russian imperial myths and presents Ukrainian history from a national perspective:

- M26: "Російські міфи про Україну" - debunks common myths
- M45: "Сургунлік: Депортація 1944" - Crimean Tatar genocide
- M48: "Діаспора: Ковчег держави" - diaspora as state preservation
