# B1 Curriculum V4 — Revised (State Standard 2024 Compliant)

**Date:** 2026-03-22
**Status:** REVISED DRAFT
**Method:** State Standard 2024 (WHAT) + Ukrainian textbooks (HOW) + 100% Ukrainian Immersion (B1 Paradigm)

## Architecture & Revisions (Based on Claude's Adversarial Review)

This V4 revision directly addresses all findings from the adversarial review:
1. **Communicative Rebalancing:** Added 10 new communication modules to cover all missing §3 themes (Health, Housing, Daily Life, Education, Shopping, Leisure, Nature, Traditions).
2. **Grammar Gaps Filled:** Added adverb formation (§4.3.7), short-form adjectives (§4.2.1.2), and vocative expansion for formal contexts (§4.2.2.7).
3. **Pacing and Scope:** Compressed the metalanguage bridge from 5 to 3 modules, merged participle introductions, and split the overloaded numerals module.
4. **Sequencing:** Moved spatial prepositions to immediately precede the motion verb phase. Phase 9 reframed to "Text and Register".

**Net Result:** 92 modules (up from 85), perfectly balancing intensive grammar with practical B1-level communication.
