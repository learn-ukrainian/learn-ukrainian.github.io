## Overview

This document analyzes compliance gaps between our curriculum plans and the official Ukrainian State Standard 2024 (Каталог В - Grammar). These gaps should be addressed in the improvement plans after C1 and C2 assessments are complete.

**Status:** ✅ Good coverage with minor gaps

- ✅ Future tense (буду + infinitive)
- ✅ Imperative mood basics
- ✅ Personal pronouns (all cases)
- ✅ Possessive pronouns (мій, твій, наш, ваш, його, її, їхній)
- ✅ Demonstrative pronouns (цей, той, такий)
- ✅ Cardinal numerals 1-100
- ✅ Ordinal numerals (перший, другий...)
- ✅ Basic prepositions (в/у, на, з/із/зі, до, від)
- ✅ Word order (SVO)
- ✅ Negation (не)
- ✅ Question formation

### Gaps to Address

| Gap | State Standard Requirement | Action |

### Priority

**Low** - A1 is well-aligned. Minor additions only.

## B2 Compliance

**Status:** 🔴 Critical - 0% implemented

### State Standard Requirements (Каталог В for B2)

The State Standard requires mastery of:

1. **Passive voice forms** (all types):
   - Passive participles (-ний/-тий)
   - Impersonal passive (-но/-то)
   - Reflexive passive (-ся)
   - 3rd person plural passive

2. **Aspect nuances**:
   - Aspectual pairs in all contexts
   - Secondary imperfectivization (переписати → переписувати)
   - Aspect in imperative

3. **Participles**:
   - Active participles (present/past)
   - Passive participles (present/past)
   - Adverbial participles (дієприслівник)
   - Participle phrase constructions

4. **Complex syntax**:
   - All subordinate clause types
   - Correlative constructions (той...хто, такий...який)
   - Parenthetical constructions

5. **Register awareness**:
   - Formal vs informal
   - Written vs spoken
   - Academic style basics

### Current Plan Coverage

The B2-CURRICULUM-PLAN.md covers:
- ✅ Passive voice (M01-05)
- ✅ Participles (M11-15)
- ✅ Syntax patterns (M16-20)
- ✅ Register (M26-30)
- ✅ Word formation (M31-35)
- ⚠️ Need to verify plan aligns with State Standard
