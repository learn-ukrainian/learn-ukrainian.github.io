> **Stage 2 correction.** The prior plan taught Latysheva as **«Піонерка авіації»** — "one of Ukraine's first female pilots" with a dual mathematician/aviator life and a birthplace of **Кременчук**. The aviation career is **fabricated** (she never flew) and the birthplace is **wrong**. The verified person is **Клавдія Яківна Латишева** (Київ 1897 – Київ 1956), a **mathematician** — the first woman in Ukraine to defend a physico-mathematical candidate dissertation, professor and dean at Kyiv University, whose name lives in the **Frobenius–Latysheva method**.

- **Pseudonyms / aliases:** none. Russian-language render «Клавдия Яковлевна Латышева» (forbidden as a body-text form).
- **Born:** 14 березня 1897 | Київ | Russian Empire. In the family of a military serviceman. [T1: ЕСУ — «14 берез. 1897, Київ»; T3: uk.wikipedia — «14 березня 1897, Київ, … в родині військовослужбовця»]
- **Died:** 11 травня 1956 | Київ | Ukrainian SSR. Buried at the Лук'янівський cemetery, Kyiv (ділянка № 5, ряд 9, місце 20). [T1: ЕСУ — «11 трав. 1956, там само»; T3: uk.wikipedia — burial plot]
- **Family / education key facts:** secondary education 1916 (Друга жіноча гімназія, Kyiv); 1921 graduated the physics-mathematics division of the Kyiv higher pedagogical women's courses (Київські вищі педагогічні жіночі курси) — the route women used because the university itself was closed to them. Worked at Kyiv University 1920–56 (with a wartime break, 1941–45 at the Saratov Auto-Road Institute during evacuation). [T1: ЕСУ; T3: uk.wikipedia]

No source disagreements on the core dates. **Birthplace correction logged:** the prior plan's «Кременчук» is contradicted by both ЕСУ and uk.wikipedia, which give **Київ**.

## 2. Oppression mechanism — gendered structural barriers + survival inside a terror-decimated school (NOT personal repression)

> **Anti-fabrication discipline.** There is **no evidence Latysheva was personally arrested, exiled, or repressed.** She received Soviet state honours (Order of Lenin, 1954). To invent a persecution would repeat exactly the error this rebuild exists to correct. Her honest "oppression mechanism" is twofold and indirect:

**(a) Gendered structural barrier.** Women were barred from the university proper, so Latysheva had to take the *вищі жіночі курси* route (1921). Despite finishing in 1921 and teaching from 1920, she became the **first woman in Ukraine to defend a physico-mathematical candidate dissertation only in 1936**, and reached the **doctorate and professorship only in 1952 — at age 55.** The lag between her ability and her formal recognition is the gendered mechanism. [T3: uk.wikipedia — «першою в Україні жінкою, яка захистилась на ступінь кандидата фізико-математичних наук»; T1: ЕСУ — доктор + професор 1952]

**(b) Survival inside a milieu the Soviet terror destroyed (decolonial dimension).** Latysheva's closest scientific collaborator was **Михайло Пилипович Кравчук** — they delivered a joint report to the Institute of Mathematics (1936), and she compiled the bibliography for his monograph «Застосування способу моментів…» (1932–35). Kravchuk was **arrested by the NKVD in 1938**, sentenced, and **died on 9 March 1942 in a Kolyma camp**; he was branded an "enemy of the people" and erased. [T1: ЕСУ/IEU — Кравчук Михайло Пилипович, d. 9 March 1942 (per `bio-research-source-tiers.md` worked example)]. Latysheva continued working in a Kyiv mathematical school whose Ukrainian leadership had been liquidated, under a system that would later absorb her achievements into a Russocentric "Soviet science" narrative. Naming this — rather than inflating her own biography into a martyrdom — is the decolonial point.

- **What survived / what was destroyed:** her 40+ works and the named method survive; the Ukrainian institutional identity of the Kyiv math school (and Kravchuk himself) was destroyed and only recovered post-1991.

## 3. Major works

- `1932` — *Математичний задачник для хімічних інститутів* (textbook). [T3: uk.wikipedia]
- `1932–35` — bibliography compiled for М. Кравчук's monograph *Застосування способу моментів до розв'язування лінійних диференціальних та інтегральних рівнянь* (author's written thanks). [T3: uk.wikipedia]
- `1936` — candidate dissertation *«Наближене розв'язування за допомогою способу моментів лінійних диференціальних рівнянь, що мають особливості в коефіцієнтах»* — **first woman in Ukraine to defend a phys-math candidate degree**. [T3: uk.wikipedia]
- `1936` — co-organiser of the **First All-Ukrainian Mathematical Olympiad** (Kyiv University). [T3: uk.wikipedia]
- `1942` — *Елементи наближених обчислень* (textbook). [T3: uk.wikipedia]
- `1952` — doctoral dissertation *«Нормальні розв'язки лінійних диференціальних рівнянь з поліноміальними коефіцієнтами»* → doctor of phys-math sciences + professor. [T1: ЕСУ; T3: uk.wikipedia]
- career-spanning — the **метод Фробеніуса–Латишевої** for constructing *normal (generalised power-series) solutions* of linear differential equations with polynomial coefficients near a singular point. [T3: uk.wikipedia; see §6 on the popular mis-description]
- works on аналітична теорія диференціальних рівнянь, теорія крайових задач, electrodynamics, theory of oscillations, probability. [T1: ЕСУ; T3: uk.wikipedia]

## 6. Contested points

- **How the Frobenius–Latysheva method is described.** Popular/encyclopedic summaries (incl. uk.wikipedia) call it a method "для розв'язування систем диференціальних рівнянь з частинними похідними" (systems of PDEs). Her own dissertation titles, however, are explicitly about **linear ODEs with singularities / polynomial coefficients and their "normal solutions"** — i.e. a Frobenius-type construction near a singular point. Present the accurate ODE characterisation and **flag the popular PDE phrasing as imprecise**, do not silently copy it.
- **"First woman professor of mathematics in Ukraine."** Widely repeated (e.g. STEM is FEM). The solidly-sourced claim is narrower and stronger: **first woman in Ukraine to defend a phys-math candidate dissertation (1936)**; doctorate + professorship 1952. Use the precise version.
- **What popular memory simplifies:** the Order of Lenin is foregrounded while the destruction of the Kyiv math school (Kravchuk) is omitted; the rebuild restores both.
- **Where Russocentric framing absorbs her:** filing her under undifferentiated "Soviet mathematics," erasing the Ukrainian institutional context and the terror that shaped it.
