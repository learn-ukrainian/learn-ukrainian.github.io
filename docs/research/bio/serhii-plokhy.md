- **Pseudonyms / aliases:** Serhii Plokhy, Serhii Plokhii
- **Born:** 23 May 1957 | Горький (Gorky, now Nizhny Novgorod, Russia) | Російська РФСР (family returned to Zaporizhzhia shortly after his birth)
- **Died:** N/A (Living)
- **Family / education key facts:** Born to Ukrainian parents in Russia (father was an engineer sent there by state assignment); grew up in Zaporizhzhia. Graduated from Dnipro University (1980) as a historian. Received his Candidate of Sciences degree in Moscow (1982) and Doctor of Historical Sciences in Kyiv (1990). Emigrated to Canada (Alberta) in 1991, moved to CIUS in 1996, and later joined Harvard University.
