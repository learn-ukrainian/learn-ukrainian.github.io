## 2. Oppression mechanism

- **Primary (physical) — torture-killing by the Gestapo.** As **Голова Проводу українських націоналістів (ПУН) ОУН** from January 1944, Olzhych was arrested by the **Gestapo in Lviv on 25 травня 1944** (вул. Личаківська, 32). He was held in the **Целленбау** block at **Заксенгаузен** — the isolation block for high-value prisoners — and **tortured to death during a night interrogation on 10 червня 1944** by a Gestapo trio named in the record as **Вольф, Вірзінг, Шульц**. His body was probably cremated in the camp; he has no grave (a symbolic grave is now beside his parents' on the Лук'янівське cemetery, Kyiv). [T3: uk.wikipedia — §"Діяльність у лавах ОУН" / death narrative]
- **By whom (specific agency):** German **гестапо** (Reichssicherheitshauptamt apparatus). His son and some researchers raised the possibility of NKVD involvement in betraying him, but this is a **hypothesis**, not established fact, and is presented as such. [T3: uk.wikipedia — "Є думка, що до цього був причетний НКВС… розгадку… треба шукати… в архівах Москви" — flagged as opinion]
- **Secondary (the "Russia-oppressed" mechanism):** the Soviet state forced his father into emigration, erased Olzhych from the Soviet canon, and made the OUN its long-term repression target; his name returned to Ukraine openly only in the late 1980s–1990s.
- **What survived / what was destroyed:** his poetry (three collections) and archaeological papers survived in emigration; the documentary record of his ПУН leadership is fragmentary. The 2002 Шевченко-Prize documentary trilogy «Я камінь з Божої пращі…» reconstructed his life.

## 3. Major works

**Poetry:**
- `1935` — **«Рінь»** (Львів) — debut; historiosophical, archaeologically-inflected lyric of "deep time," hardness and warriorhood. [T3; T4: ukrlib-olzhych — Ю. Ковалів]
- `1940` — **«Вежі»** — sharply ideological-political; two long poems, «Городок. 1932» and the cycle **«Незнаному Воякові»** — "an aestheticised political programme of the Ukrainian underground." [T4: ukrlib-olzhych]
- `1946` (posthumous; prepared 1940) — **«Підзамчя»** — return to philosophical lyric and "watercolour" calm. [T1: ЕУ corpus]
- collected: «Поезії» (Нью-Йорк, 1956), «Величність» (1969).

**Scholarship / publicism:**
- `1930` — doctoral dissertation **«Неолітична мальована кераміка Галичини»** (Trypillian painted-ceramics typology). [T3]
- `1937` — **«Schipenitz: Kunst und Geräte eines neolithischen Dorfes»** (archaeology, in German). [T3]
- `1941` — **«Євген Коновалець»** (booklet, Прага), under the pseudonym **Д. Кардаш**. [T3]
- the essay **«Культурна політика українського націоналізму»** (a doctrinal statement of "heroic culture"). [T4: Попович М. — wave7 corpus]

**Quote 2 — «Незнаному Воякові», 9-й вірш, corpus-verbatim (corrected word):**

> «Захочеш — і будеш. В людині, затям, / Лежить невідгадана сила.»

A second-person imperative: almost everything in life depends on the self. [Corpus-attested: ukrlib-olzhych, chunk `dc2ceb58_c0001` — note the verbatim «невідгадана», not the popular misquotation «невгасима»]

**Quote 3 — «Нащо слова?», corpus-verbatim (his ars poetica of action):**

> «Нащо слова? Ми діло несемо. / Ніщо мистецтво і мана теорій. / Бо ж нам дано знайти життя само / В красі неповторимій і суворій.»

[Corpus-attested: ukrlib-olzhych, chunk `dc2ceb58_c0001`]

## 5. Language register

- **Register:** **austere modernist historiosophy** — neoclassically disciplined form (V. Державин noted his dense ономатопея and accumulation of liquid /р/, /л/), but pushed toward "barbaric," monumental, will-driven imagery. Aphoristic and command-like in «Вежі». [T4: Русанівський — wave9 corpus; Попович — wave7]
- **CEFR readiness for full reading:** **C1–C2.** The archaising lexicon, compressed syntax and historiosophical references (Каяла, neolithic/Trypillian motifs, ancient ruin) make him advanced reading; isolated aphoristic quatrains («Держава не твориться…») are usable earlier as set-pieces.
- **Lexicon notes:** archaisms / historisms (рінь = coarse gravel; брили, криця, спис, панцир), compounded epithets, and an archaeological–temporal vocabulary unusual in lyric.
- **Stylistic features:** epigrammatic closure, imperative address, sound-painting; the deliberate subordination of "poetry-as-temptation" to "чин" (deed) — a programmatic anti-aestheticism stated within the verse itself («Нащо слова?»).

## 6. Contested points

- **Anti-inflation (load-bearing):** popular accounts overstate his American career. The record: he repeatedly took part in the **American School of Prehistoric Research** field seasons (a paid summer job, to ~1938); the **Harvard lectureship is «непідтверджена гіпотеза»**; the **Український науковий інститут в Америці (УНІА) was never created** — its founders «не вдалося узгодити необхідні документи». The 1938 US trip's purpose was that (failed) institute, not a professorship. Some researchers (Л. Винар) doubt the trip happened at all. These must not be stated as accomplished facts. [T3: uk.wikipedia — verbatim "Читання лекцій у Гарвардському університеті є непідтвердженою гіпотезою"; "УНІА ніколи створений не був"]
- **Anti-omission (load-bearing NPOV):** on reaching Kyiv in 1941 with the Буковинський курінь, Olzhych **«взяв участь у формуванні місцевої адміністрації та поліції»** before the German repression of the nationalists began. This is part of the record and is not suppressed; it sits in the same complex 1941 moment as Teliha's — OUN(m) hopes for Ukrainian self-administration under occupation, ended by Nazi arrests from December 1941. [T3: uk.wikipedia — §"Діяльність у лавах ОУН", verbatim]
- **Ideology, stated honestly:** his essay «Культурна політика українського націоналізму» defines a deliberately **"heroic," anti-liberal culture** "що опановує цілу свідомість… одиниці"; Попович places this doctrine closer to the integral-nationalist / authoritarian aesthetics of its time. Reporting his real ideas is part of anti-hagiography. [T4: Попович М. — wave7 corpus]
- **No Gumilyov frame:** the Russian-imperial «пасіонарність» (Лев Гумільов) lens is **not** used here to explain him — per the brief and per decolonial method.
- **Russian/Soviet disinformation:** Soviet historiography branded the OUN «буржуазні націоналісти» and erased him; modern Russian propaganda extends the "Nazi" smear to the whole movement. Honesty about his real politics (above) is the answer, not denial.
