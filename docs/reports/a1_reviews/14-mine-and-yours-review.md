# Logic / Theory Review

**1. Clarity of Explanation:** 10/10
The explanation of Variable (мій/твій) vs Invariant (його/її) possessives is brilliant. The "Chameleon vs Frozen" analogy is a great mnemonic. The coverage of `свій` is particularly impressive—this is a nuanced topic often skipped in A1, but introduced here with perfect clarity ("subject owns it").

**2. Pacing of Concept Introduction:** 10/10
The module builds logically from Nominative gender rules (M01-M03) to possessive agreement. It separates the "easy" invariant ones from the "hard" agreeing ones.

**3. Coverage of Edge Cases:** 10/10
It covers `наш/ваш` clearly. It also touches on the formal vs informal `твій/ваш` distinction which is crucial for cultural competence.

**4. Pedagogical Flow (The "Click" Moment):** 10/10
The side-by-side comparison table of `мій` vs `його` creates an instant understanding of why one changes and the other doesn't.

**5. Tone and Engagement:** 10/10
The reference to "The Lord of the Rings" (Gollum vs Frodo grammar) is a nerd-culture masterpiece that makes abstract grammar concrete.
