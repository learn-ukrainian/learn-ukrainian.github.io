## Цікаво!

Only a small group of masculine nouns ending in **-к**, **-г** and **-х** take the endings **-ці**, **-зі**, and **-сі** respectively in the locative case. As you will learn in [module 4.4](https://dobraforma.ku.edu/nouns/locative-case-endings-in-%d1%97/), the rest of the masculine nouns ending in **-к** will add the ending **-у** and not undergo any stem changes. Compare:

**урок** – **на уро́ці**
**банк** –** в ба́нку**
