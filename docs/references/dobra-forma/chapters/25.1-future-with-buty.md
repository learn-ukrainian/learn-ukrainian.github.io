# 25.1 Future Tense of Verbs (With бути)

## Overview
This module teaches how to form the future tense of Ukrainian verbs using the auxiliary verb **бу́ти** (to be).

## Key Concepts

### The Verb бути in Future Tense
The conjugated forms of **бути** carry future meaning, unlike the present tense which uses only **є**. The future stem is **буд-**, combined with hard-vowel first conjugation endings:

| Person | Form |
|--------|------|
| я | бу́ду |
| ти | бу́деш |
| він/вона/воно | бу́де |
| ми | бу́демо |
| ви | бу́дете |
| вони | бу́дуть |

Example: "Я ско́ро бу́ду вдо́ма" (I will be home soon)

### Analytic Future Tense
Many Ukrainian verbs form future tense analytically—combining a conjugated future form of **бути** with an infinitive:

**[conjugated бути] + [infinitive]**

Examples:
- "Я бу́ду жи́ти в Євро́пі" (I will live in Europe)
- "Ви бу́дете подорожува́ти влі́тку?" (Will you travel in summer?)

## Learning Activities

The chapter includes six main exercises:
1. Identifying бути forms in conversation
2. Audio-based selection tasks
3. Filling blanks with appropriate verb forms
4. Dialogue sequencing
5. Future plans comprehension
6. Reading and writing practice with mountain travel narrative

## Summary Points

The future tense using **бути** allows speakers to express upcoming actions across all verb types. Question formation maintains the standard word order, with the conjugated **бути** preceding the infinitive rather than moving to sentence-initial position.
