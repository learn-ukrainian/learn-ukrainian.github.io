In this module, you will become familiar with two slightly different, less common endings of singular nouns in the instrumental case.  You will also revisit three common stem changes that occur in some nouns.

## Цікаво!

As the model above shows, some short masculine nouns with an –**i**– in their stem replace this –**i**– with an –**o**– in instrumental — as they do in all other cases other than the nominative singular. A few short masculine nouns, however, add a soft sign before this –**o**-.The easiest way to remember that words like **лід** replace the vowel in the stem is to remember a complete phrase **вода з льодом**.
