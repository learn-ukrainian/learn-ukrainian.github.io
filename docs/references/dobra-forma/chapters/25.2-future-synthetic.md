# 25.2 Future Tense of Verbs (Synthetic: Читатиму)

## Overview

This module teaches the synthetic future tense in Ukrainian, an alternative to the analytic future tense using бути. Both forms are semantically equivalent: "Я буду лежати" = "Я лежатиму."

## Formation

The synthetic future adds endings beginning with **-м-** to the infinitive form. These endings mirror бути conjugation patterns:

| Person | бути | жити |
|--------|------|------|
| я | буду | житиму |
| ти | будеш | житимеш |
| він/вона/воно | буде | житиме |
| ми | будемо | житимемо |
| ви | будете | житимете |
| вони | будуть | житимуть |

## Key Characteristics

- **Formation**: Only from imperfective verbs
- **Register**: More literary; commonly used in formal contexts like news broadcasts
- **Meaning**: Identical to analytic future forms

## -ся Verbs

For reflexive verbs, synthetic future endings precede the -ся suffix:

| Person | дивитися |
|--------|----------|
| я | дивитимуся |
| ти | дивитимешся |
| він/вона/воно | дивитиметься |
| ми | дивитимемося |
| ви | дивитиметеся |
| вони | дивитимуться |

**Note**: The third-person singular form differs (-меть-), and synthetic futures are less common in longer reflexive verbs, particularly plural forms.

## Practical Applications

The module includes multiple exercises covering:
- Identifying synthetic future forms in conversations
- Subject-verb agreement
- Contextual usage in news articles
- Distinguishing between past, present, and future tenses
- Dialogue completion with appropriate verb forms
