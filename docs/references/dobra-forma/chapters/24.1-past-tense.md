# 24.1 Past Tense of Verbs – Добра форма

## Overview
This module teaches formation of past tense for most Ukrainian verbs, created by Tetiana Kidruk.

## Key Formation Rules

**Basic Pattern:** Drop the infinitive ending **-ти** and add appropriate past-tense endings based on subject gender and number.

### Singular Past-Tense Endings
- **Masculine:** -**в** (читав, писав)
- **Feminine:** -**ла** (читала, писала)
- **Neuter:** -**ло** (було, робило)

### Plural Ending
- **All persons:** -**ли** (читали, писали, робили)

## Important Exception

Verbs ending in **-увати** or **-ювати** retain the **-ва-** suffix in past tense:

танцювати → танцював/танцювала/танцювали (NOT танцю́ю in present)

подорожувати → подорожував/подорожувала/подорожували

## Special Cases

**Neuter subjects** use -**ло** ending. The form **було** appears in impersonal constructions describing past conditions, equivalent to English "It was…"

Example: "Це було важко" (It was difficult)

**Irregular verb ї́сти (to eat):**
| Gender | Form |
|--------|------|
| Masculine | їв |
| Feminine | ї́ла |
| Neuter | ї́ло |
| Plural | ї́ли |

## Learning Activities

The module includes:
- Dialogue analysis identifying past-tense verbs
- Fill-in-the-blank exercises with audio prompts
- Image captioning with past-tense descriptions
- Conversation completion tasks
- Comprehension questions
- Grammar concept matching
