### Overall: **GOOD with phonetics gap**

The A2 curriculum is strong on morphology, cases, verbs, and syntax. The primary gap is phonetics (intonation, assimilation, stress patterns), which the SS explicitly expands at A2 but the curriculum largely defers from A1. The `reading_situations` and `writing_tasks` field absence across all plans is a schema-level issue worth addressing.

### Priority Actions:
1. **HIGH**: Decide on `reading_situations` / `writing_tasks` — add to plan schema or confirm covered elsewhere
2. **MEDIUM**: Add intonation guidance to syntax modules (`because-and-although`, `real-conditionals`)
3. **MEDIUM**: Add consonant assimilation / stress expansion content to early A2 modules
4. **LOW**: Add `dialogue_situations` to the 6 missing plans (3 metalanguage, 2 checkpoints, 1 review)
5. **LOW**: Verify Accusative vs Instrumental contrast for під/над/між in motion contexts
