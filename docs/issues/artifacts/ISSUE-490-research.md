## 2. Grammar Focus: The Past Tenses
The core linguistic goal of Phase 2 is mastering the narrative past tenses, which are distinct from the modern analytic past.

### A. Aorist (The Narrative Past)
*   **Function**: Single, completed actions in the past. The "backbone" of the story.
*   **Markers**: -хъ, -ch (1sg), -e (2/3sg), -hom (1pl), -ste (2pl), -sha (3pl).
*   **Key Examples**:
    *   *ide* (he went)
    *   *reche* (he said)
    *   *vzyasha* (they took)
*   **Target Module for Deep Dive**: **M29 (Death of Igor)** - strict sequence of events.

### B. Imperfect (The Descriptive Past)
*   **Function**: Ongoing, repeated, or background actions. Setting the scene.
*   **Markers**: -ah, -ashe, -ahu.
*   **Key Examples**:
    *   *byashe* (he was)
    *   *zhiviyahu* (they were living)
    *   *molyashe* (he was praying)
*   **Target Module for Deep Dive**: **M26 (Intro/Geography)** - describing how tribes *lived*.
