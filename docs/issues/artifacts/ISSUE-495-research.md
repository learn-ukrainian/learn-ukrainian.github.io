---

## 1. Source Material
*   **The Codifier**: *Grammar* (1619) by Meletiy Smotrytsky.
    *   **Significance**: First comprehensive Church Slavonic grammar; defined the "High Style".
    *   **Structure**: Orthography, Etymology, Syntax, Prosody.
*   **The Ascetic Polemicist**: *Epistles* by Ivan Vyshensky.
    *   **Significance**: Fierce defense of Orthodoxy; critique of Western influence; unique "muscular" style.
    *   **Key Works**: *Epistle to Bishops*, *Epistle to Ostrozky*.
*   **The Lament**: *Threnos* (1610) by Meletiy Smotrytsky.
    *   **Significance**: Personification of the Orthodox Church lamenting her children's apostasy. High Baroque style.

---

## 2. Linguistic Focus: The Baroque Synthesis
Phase 3 explores the "high tension" between preserving the purity of Church Slavonic and mastering the rhetorical weapons of the Latin West.

### Rhetorical Strategies
*   **Amplification**: Heaping up synonyms and clauses (Vyshensky's trademark).
*   **Personification**: The Church as a weeping mother (*Threnos*).
*   **Codification**: Smotrytsky's attempt to make CS as rigorous as Latin.

### Vocabulary
*   **Theological**: *Ipostas*, *Sushchestvo*, *Yeres*.
*   **Rhetorical**: *Argument*, *Syllogism*, *Figur*.
*   **Emotional**: *Plach*, *Rydanie*, *Horkost*.
