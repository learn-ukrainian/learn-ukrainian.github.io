## 2. Linguistic Focus: The Fall of the Yers
Phase 4 covers the most drastic change in Slavic history: the loss of the ultra-short vowels *ъ* (yer) and *ь* (yer').

### Mechanism (Havlik's Law)
*   **Strong Yers**: Become full vowels (*o, e*).
    *   *sъnъ* -> *son* (sleep)
    *   *dьnь* -> *den'* (day)
*   **Weak Yers**: Disappear.
    *   *sъna* (genitive) -> *sna*
    *   *dьnьsь* -> *dens'* (today)
*   **Curriculum Integration**:
    *   Students trace words from "Early OES" (Phase 1-3) to "Late OES/Early Ruthenian" (Phase 4).
