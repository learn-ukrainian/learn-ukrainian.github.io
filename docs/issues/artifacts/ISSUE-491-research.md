## 2. Grammar Focus: Precision & Logic

### A. The Dual Number (Dvoyina)
*   **Significance**: Highly preserved in legal texts (body parts, witnesses, pairs).
*   **Forms**:
    *   Nouns: *-a* (o-stems), *-i* (a-stems/consonant stems).
    *   Verbs: *-ta* (2nd/3rd person).
    *   Pronouns: *va* (you two), *na* (us two), *yul* (them two).
*   **Key Examples**:
    *   *dva vidoka* (two witnesses)
    *   *ochi* (eyes) / *ushi* (ears) - often in injury lists.
    *   *rukama* (with two hands).
*   **Target Module**: **M55 (Injury & Compensation)**.

### B. Conditional Syntax (Legal Logic)
*   **Structure**: "If [Condition]... Then [Consequence]".
*   **Markers**: *Ashche* (If), *Ozhe* (If/When), *To* (Then).
*   **Target Module**: **M52 (The Conditional Structure)**.
