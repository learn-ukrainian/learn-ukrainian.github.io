## 2. Linguistic Focus: Chancery Slavonic
Phase 1 introduces the official administrative language of the Grand Duchy of Lithuania.

### Characteristics
*   **Base**: Old Ruthenian (Proto-Ukrainian/Belarusian).
*   **Formulaic Style**: Rigid syntactic structures for legal precision.
*   **Lexical Layers**:
    *   *Native*: *Pravda*, *Sud*, *Uryad*.
    *   *Polish Loans*: *Vykonavets*, *Pozvany*, *Obyvatel*.
    *   *Latin Loans* (via Polish): *Appeliatsia*, *Dekret*, *Mandat*.
