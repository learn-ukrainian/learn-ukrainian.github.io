## Deep Dive: Is there a Subjunctive Mood?

You might ask: "Why do we use the Past Tense for a wish?"
*Ğ¯ Ñ…Ğ¾Ñ‡Ñƒ, Ñ‰Ğ¾Ğ± Ñ‚Ğ¸ Ğ¿Ñ€Ğ¸Ğ¹ÑˆĞ¾Ğ².* (I want you to come - literally "that you came").

Instead, we use the **Past Tense particle `Ğ±Ğ¸`** (would).
- *Ğ¯ Ñ…Ğ¾Ñ‚Ñ–Ğ² Ğ±Ğ¸...* (I would like...).

In `Ñ‰Ğ¾Ğ±` (Ñ‰Ğ¾ + Ğ±Ğ¸), the particle `Ğ±Ğ¸` is already built-in!
