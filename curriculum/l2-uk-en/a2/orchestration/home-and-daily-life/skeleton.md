## Сценарій 1: Моє помешкання (Scenario 1: My Home) (~600 words total)
- P1 (~60 words): [Introduction to the new apartment context — describing the excitement of moving into a "помешкання" (n, dwelling). Briefly introduce the speaker, Олена, who is about to show her home to an online friend via video call.]
- P2 (~160 words): [Dialogue: Video tour between Олена and her friend Марк. Олена moves through the "коридор" (m) into the "вітальня" (f). She points out the "диван" (m), "крісла" (pl), and "килим" (m) on the floor. Mark asks about the "балкон" (m). Cases focus: Nominative for naming objects and Locative for their position (на стіні висить картина, у кутку стоїть торшер).]
- P3 (~100 words): [Vocabulary deep dive: Kitchen and bathroom items. Focusing on feminine nouns ending in -я (кухня, спальня, ванна) and how they change in the Locative (у кухні, у спальні, у ванні) vs. the masculine "балкон" (на балконі).]
- P4 (~120 words): [Grammar Focus: The Genitive of absence and quantity. Explaining how to say there is "немає телевізора" (Gen.) or "багато кімнат" (Gen.Pl.). Contrasting this with the Nominative existence (Тут є стіл). Use examples like "біля вікна" (Gen.) for spatial relation.]
- P5 (~110 words): [Furniture and Materials: Using the Instrumental case to describe characteristics. Explaining "стіл з дерева" (Instr.) or "кімната з великими вікнами" (Instr.Pl.). Description of the "спальня" (f) with a "зручне ліжко" (n) and a "велика шафа" (f).]
- Exercise: [fill-in, focus: Complete a description of a home with the correct case forms for room and furniture nouns, 8 items]

## Сценарій 2: Мій звичайний день (Scenario 2: My Typical Day) (~600 words total)
- P1 (~100 words): [Narrative: Morning routine. Ihor describes his morning using reflexive verbs like "вмиватися", "одягатися", and "готуватися". Focusing on time expressions using the Locative: "о сьомій годині", "о пів на восьму".]
- P2 (~120 words): [Grammar Focus: Adverbs of time vs. Nouns in cases. Comparing "вранці" (morning) with "після сніданку" (Gen.) and "перед роботою" (Instr.). Explain the sequence of actions: "спочатку", "потім", "після того".]
- P3 (~110 words): [Commuting and Work: Using the Instrumental for means of transport ("їду автобусом", "користуюся метро"). Describing the workplace: "працюю в офісі" (Loc.) "з колегами" (Instr.). Introduction of the verb "обідати" and "перекушувати".]
- P4 (~120 words): [Evening and Rest: Returning home and household chores. Narrative includes actions like "прибирати пилососом" (Instr.), "мити посуд" (Acc.), and "прасувати одяг" (Acc.). Contrast with weekend activities: "у вихідні я не працюю", "ходжу в кіно" (Acc. direction).]
- Exercise: [quiz, focus: Choose the correct case form in daily routine sentences (time expressions, prepositions, verbs), 8 items]
- Exercise: [match-up, focus: Match daily activities with the correct time of day and appropriate case construction, 8 items]

## Сценарій 3: В гостях (Scenario 3: Visiting Someone) (~500 words total)
- P1 (~120 words): [Dialogue: Arrival at a friend's house for dinner. Social etiquette and hospitality. Use of "Заходьте, будь ласка!", "Роззувайтеся", and "Ось ваші капці". Introducing the Dative case for social interaction: "дякую господарям" (Dat.), "допомагаю Оксані" (Dat.).]
- P2 (~100 words): [The Dining Scene: Sitting down to eat. Explaining the difference between direction "сідайте за стіл" (Acc.) and position "ми сидимо за столом" (Instr.). Vocab: "гостинці" (pl, gifts), "пригощатися" (to help oneself).]
- P3 (~110 words): [Grammar Focus: Partitive Genitive for food and drink. Asking "Хочете чаю чи кави?" (Gen.) and responding "Я буду воду з лимоном" (Acc. + Instr.). Explain the usage of "трохи" and "багато" with food items.]
- P4 (~120 words): [Casual Conversation: Comparing daily routines while eating. "А о котрій ви зазвичай снідаєте?", "Хто у вашій родині готує вечерю?". Synthesis of Locative for time and Nominative/Accusative for roles and objects.]
- Exercise: [error-correction, focus: Find and correct grammar errors in sentences involving case usage in home/routine contexts, 6 items]

## Мовленнєве завдання: Опишіть свій дім (Speaking Task: Describe Your Home) (~300 words total)
- P1 (~80 words): [Task Instructions: Learner is prompted to write a 10-sentence paragraph describing their current living situation and one typical day. Provide a prompt: "Привіт! Я живу у... Моє помешкання має... Щодня я..."]
- P2 (~70 words): [Grammar Checklist: Reminders to use specific cases: Nom. (for objects), Gen. (for absence/quantity), Acc. (for direction/actions), Instr. (for transport/with whom), Loc. (for location/time).]
- P3 (~150 words): [Model Answer: A comprehensive description of a small apartment and a workday. Example: "Я живу в однокімнатній квартирі (Loc.). У вітальні (Loc.) є великий диван (Nom.). Біля дивана (Gen.) стоїть торшер. Вранці я встаю о шостій годині (Loc.). Снідаю з дружиною (Instr.). Їду на роботу (Acc.) машиною (Instr.). Увечері я повертаюся додому і відпочиваю на балконі (Loc.)."]
