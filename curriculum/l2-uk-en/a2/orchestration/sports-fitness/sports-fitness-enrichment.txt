## Cultural Hooks

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Case Mismatch (Play)**: *грати футбол* (Direct Object) → *грати у футбол* (Preposition + Accusative). *Reason*: English "play football" has no preposition.
2. **Case Mismatch (Practice)**: *займатися спорт* (Nominative) → *займатися спортом* (Instrumental). *Reason*: Learners forget the government of the reflexive verb.
3. **Verb Choice**: Confusing *грати* (play a game) vs *гратися* (play with toys/child's play). Adults *грають у теніс*, they do not *граються*.
