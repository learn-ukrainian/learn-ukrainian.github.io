## Cultural Hooks
1. **Diia (Дія)**: Ukraine is the first country in the world to equate digital passports to physical ones via the "Diia" app ("State in a smartphone"). Over 20 million Ukrainians use it for everything from taxes to marriage certificates.
2. **IT Nation**: The IT sector is a major part of Ukraine's economy (Kyiv, Lviv, Kharkiv hubs). It is very common for young Ukrainians to work in IT or have friends who do.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Prepositions**: *на інтернеті* (calque from English "on the internet") → **в інтернеті** (Locative case).
2. **Lexical Choice**: *скачати* (colloquial/Russian influence) → **завантажити** (standard Ukrainian term for "download").
3. **Case Governance**: *шукати інформацію в гугл* (indeclinable proper noun usage error) → *шукати в ґуґлі* (Locative) or *шукати в Google*.

## Cross-References
- Builds on: a2-49 (Work and Professions) — office equipment vocabulary.
- Prepares for: a2-67 (Social Media Ukrainian) — deeper dive into slang/chats; a2-51 (Hobbies).

## Notes for Content Writing
- Focus on the distinction between *завантажити* (download) and *викласти/опублікувати* (upload/post).
- Use "Diia" as a culturally relevant example for "додаток" (app).
- Ensure "смартфон" is used alongside "телефон".
