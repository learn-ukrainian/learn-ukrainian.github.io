## Давальний чи знахідний? Порівняння (Dative vs. Accusative) (~480 words total)
- P1 (~90 words): Semantic distinction between the two cases. Accusative is for "Doing something TO a person" (Direct Object), while Dative is for "Doing something FOR/TO a person as a recipient" (Indirect Object).
- P2 (~100 words): Minimal Pair comparison with verbs. Show the difference between "Я бачу маму" (Accusative - physical sight) and "Я допомагаю мамі" (Dative - helping her). List other pairs like `любити/чекати` (Acc) vs `радити/дякувати` (Dat).
- P3 (~100 words): Verbs that take both: `давати` (to give), `казати` (to say), and `показувати` (to show). Explain the "Give SOMETHING (Acc) to SOMEONE (Dat)" structure. Example: "Я даю яблуко (Acc) братові (Dat)."
- P4 (~100 words): Pronoun contrast. Highlight the difference between `мене/тебе/його/її` (Accusative) and `мені/тобі/йому/їй` (Dative). Provide a "Decision Strategy" tip: ask if the person is a target (Acc) or a listener/recipient (Dat).
- Exercise: Quiz (8 items), focus on choosing between dative and accusative for the underlined noun or pronoun in sentences like "Він бачить (подругу/подрузі)" or "Він дзвонить (подрузі/подругу)."
