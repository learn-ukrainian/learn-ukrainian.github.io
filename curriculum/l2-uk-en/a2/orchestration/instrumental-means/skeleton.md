- P1 (~130 words): [Introduction to Instrumental plural endings. Explain that the plural is simpler because all genders share the same endings. Introduce -ами for hard stems (руками, столами, книгами) and -ями for soft/jotated stems (олівцями, паличками, дітьми - irregular).]
