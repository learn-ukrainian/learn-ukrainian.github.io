## Grammar Rules
- Adjective Dative Endings: Pravopys §77, §78 — Masculine/Neuter adjectives in Dative end in **-ому** (hard) / **-ьому** (soft). Feminine adjectives end in **-ій**.
- Pronoun Dative Endings: Pravopys §86, §87 — Possessive and demonstrative pronouns follow adjective-like declension in the Dative (моєму, нашій, цьому, тому).
