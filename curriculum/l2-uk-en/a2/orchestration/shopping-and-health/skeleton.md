- P1 (~100 words): [Introduction to the vibrant culture of Ukrainian food markets (ринок/базар). Explain why buying from local vendors involves more dialogue than a supermarket, using terms like свіжий (fresh), домашній (homemade), and скуштувати (to taste).]
- P2 (~110 words): [Teaching weight units with Genitive plural and singular. Focus on кілограм (kilogram) and півкіло (half a kilo). Provide examples with masculine and feminine nouns: кілограм помідорів, півкіло картоплі, два кілограми яблук.]
- P3 (~100 words): [Measuring liquids and items in containers. Explain usage of пляшка (bottle), літр (liter), and банка (jar). Examples: пляшка води, літр молока, банка меду, склянка соку.]
- P4 (~90 words): [Quantities for specific items: десяток (ten/dozen) and пачка (pack/packet). Contrast десяток яєць (ten eggs) with пачка масла (pack of butter) or пачка солі (pack of salt).]
- P5 (~110 words): [Partitive Genitive: expressing "a piece of" or "a slice of". Use шматок (piece) and скибка (slice). Examples: шматок сиру (piece of cheese), скибка хліба (slice of bread), шматочок сала (small piece of lard).]

- P6 (~80 words): [Asking about price and quality. Phrases: По скільки помідори? Скільки з мене? (How much do I owe?). Discussing expensive/cheap: дорого vs дешево.]

## У лікаря: що вас турбує? (~700 words total)
- P1 (~90 words): [Introduction to the clinic (поліклініка) or doctor's office (кабінет лікаря). Basic opening etiquette and the standard question: Що вас турбує? (What is bothering you?).]
- P2 (~120 words): [Expressing the absence of symptoms or conditions using "немає" + Genitive. Contrast with English "I don't have". Examples: немає температури (no fever), немає апетиту (no appetite), немає сил (no strength/energy).]
- P3 (~110 words): [Using the preposition "від" to express causes or triggers of health issues. Explain "від" meaning "from/due to". Examples: болить від холоду (hurts from cold), кашель від алергії (cough from allergy), чхаю від пилу (sneezing from dust).]
- P4 (~110 words): [Anatomy in the Genitive case context. Focus on parts that commonly hurt: горла (throat), спини (back), голови (head). Explain constructions like "біль у ділянці серця" (pain in the heart area) vs "немає болю" (no pain).]
- Dialogue 2 (~120 words): [The Consultation. Patient describes symptoms (cough, runny nose) and doctor checks for fever. Keywords: кашель, нежить, легені, дихайте.]
- P5 (~100 words): [Doctor's recommendations using quantities and Genitive. Focus on "багато" (much) and "мало" (little). Advice: пийте багато води, менше стресу, більше відпочинку.]
- Exercise 2: [quiz, focus on choosing correct Genitive phrase for complaints and symptoms, 8 items]

## В аптеці та повсякденне здоров'я (~550 words total)
- P1 (~100 words): [Navigating the Pharmacy (аптека). Asking for help: Що ви порадите від...? (What do you advise for...?). Vocabulary: рецепт (prescription), фармацевт, вітаміни.]
- P2 (~110 words): [Specific remedies using "від" + Genitive. This mirrors the market "bottle of..." structure. Examples: ліки від кашлю (cough medicine), краплі від нежиті (drops for runny nose), таблетки від головного болю (pills for headache).]
- P3 (~110 words): [Instructions for taking medicine using time-based prepositions with Genitive. Focus on після (after), до (before), and під час (during). Examples: після їжі (after a meal), до обіду (before lunch).]
- P4 (~80 words): [Everyday health and lifestyle. Using Genitive for purpose: для здоров'я (for health), для імунітету (for immunity). Phrases: без цукру (without sugar), без кофеїну (caffeine-free).]
- Exercise 3: [match-up, focus on matching health problems to their remedies using "від", 8 items]
- Exercise 4: [true-false, focus on correct usage of Genitive case in shopping and health instructions, 8 items]
- P5 (~100 words): [Consolidation of the Genitive patterns learned. Compare "кілограм яблук" (quantity) with "немає яблук" (absence) and "ліки від кашлю" (purpose/remedy).]
