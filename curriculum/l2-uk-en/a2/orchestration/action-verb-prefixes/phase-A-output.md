## Cultural Hooks
1. "Bread Respect" (доїсти): Ukrainian culture has a deep reverence for food, shaped by historical trauma and agricultural roots. Finishing all food on one's plate (доїсти) is culturally significant.
2. The "Hinge Logic": Ukrainian strictly differentiates physical opening of spaces (відчинити - removing a barrier with hinges like doors/windows) and abstract opening (відкрити - uncovering, discovering, or starting a process like a bank account or business).

## Common Learner Errors
1. "Rewrite vs. Finish" Trap → confusing *переписати* (to rewrite/do over) with *дописати* (to finish writing), as English speakers might say "write more".
2. The "Open" Trap → using Surzhyk *відкрити двері* (interference from Russian "открыть дверь") instead of the correct *відчинити двері*.
3. The "Za-" Trap → overgeneralizing *за-* as just "closing" (зачинити), ignoring its other meanings like starting an action (заспівати) or excess.
