## Cultural Hooks
1. Lviv Coffee Culture (Львівська кава): Choosing coffee in Lviv is a deeply rooted ritual. Locals often use the Galician word "філіжанка" (cup) and differentiate between traditional brewing methods like "кава на піску" (coffee brewed in hot sand).
2. Proverbial Wisdom: "На колір і смак товариш не всяк" (To each their own taste and color) highlights the Ukrainian cultural acceptance of differing personal preferences and politely handling disagreement.

## Common Learner Errors
1. "Я подобається" → "Мені подобається" — Nominative overload: direct mapping of the English nominative subject "I" to Ukrainian, failing to recognize the Dative experiencer logic.
2. "Мені подобається цю книжку" → "Мені подобається ця книжка" — Object case error: incorrectly treating the liked object as an Accusative direct object, instead of the Nominative subject that is doing the pleasing.
