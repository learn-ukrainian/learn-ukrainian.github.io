## Cultural Hooks
1. "Чорний кіт перейшов дорогу" (A black cat crossed the path) — A common Ukrainian superstition about bad luck involving crossing spatial paths.
2. The "Mirror Ritual" for returning: In Ukrainian culture, returning home shortly after leaving (because you forgot something) is considered bad luck. To neutralize this, one must look in the mirror (подивитися у дзеркало) before leaving again.

## Common Learner Errors
1. Preposition Mismatch (Approaching vs Entering) → `Підійти в магазин` (Incorrect) vs `Підійти до магазину` (Correct) — Learners often use `в/у` (into) when the prefix `під-` specifically denotes approach (to/towards), requiring `до` + Genitive.
2. Coming vs Going (Arrival vs Departure) → `Пішов` (left) vs `Прийшов` (arrived) — Learners mix up the absolute direction relative to the speaker.
3. Perfective Future with `буду` → `Буду прийти` (Incorrect) vs `Прийду` (Correct) — Applying imperfective analytical future rules to perfective prefixed verbs.
