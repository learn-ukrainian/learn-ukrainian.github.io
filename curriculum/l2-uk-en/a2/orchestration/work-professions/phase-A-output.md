# Дослідження: Work and Professions

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.2.5.1: "характеристика особи за професією чи статусом: Олег буде програмувальником. Микола хоче стати викладачем. Він працював менеджером."
Alignment: This module directly addresses the A2 morphology requirement to use the Instrumental case without a preposition to characterize a person by their profession or status, specifically triggered by verbs of being and working.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **IT Sector Prominence**: Ukraine has a massive and globally integrated IT industry. The "програміст" is a defining modern cultural figure, strongly associated with "працювати віддалено" (working remotely) and "гнучкий графік" (flexible schedule).
2. **Teacher's Day (День вчителя)**: Celebrated on the first Sunday of October. It highlights the deep traditional respect for educators ("вчитель", "викладач") in Ukrainian society, where teachers act as mentors.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Omission of Instrumental in Past/Future**: Using Nominative instead of Instrumental with "бути". Learners correctly say "Я — лікар" in present tense, but incorrectly say "Я був лікар" instead of the required "Я був лікарем".
2. **Gender Ending Mismatches**: Confusing masculine (-ом/-ем) and feminine (-ою/-ею) Instrumental endings (e.g., saying "вона працює секретаром" instead of "секретаркою").

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing**: Present Ukrainian formal address ("Пан/Пані" + Name/Surname) as an authentic European polite tradition. Emphasize feminine profession names (фемінітиви like лікарка, вчителька, програмістка) as historically natural to the Ukrainian language, distinct from neighboring languages where they might carry pejorative weight.
- **Immersion Band 2 (60-75%)**: Use English *only* for the abstract grammatical explanation of the Instrumental case logic. All section intros, dialogues, practice prompts, and cultural notes must be in Ukrainian.
- **IPA Requirements**: Provide IPA on the *first* occurrence of new words only (e.g., [ɔ], [ɛ], [t͡ʃ]). Ensure proper Ukrainian angular quotes «...» are used for all Ukrainian text.
