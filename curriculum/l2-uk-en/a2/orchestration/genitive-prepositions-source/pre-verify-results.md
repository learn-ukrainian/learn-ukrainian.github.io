## Grammar Rules
- з/із/зі/зо: Правопис §25 — Use 'з' before vowels and most consonants; 'із' primarily before sibilants (з, с, ц, ч, ш, шч) and between clusters; 'зі' before clusters starting with sibilants (e.g., зі Львова, зі школи); 'зо' with 'два', 'три', 'мною'.
