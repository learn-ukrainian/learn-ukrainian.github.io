## Cultural Hooks
1. Proverb: "Якби та аби, та виросли в роті гриби" (If and maybe, then mushrooms would grow in the mouth) — used to gently mock empty "what ifs" instead of taking action.
2. The Lviv coffeehouse culture of philosophical dreaming ("філософування за кавою"), where discussing hypothetical futures ("Я б хотів...") is a common social pastime.

## Common Learner Errors
1. Tense mismatch: Using Present/Future after "якби" (e.g., "Якби я маю..."). → Correct: "Якби я мав...". — Caused by English "If I have..." mapping.
2. Particle misplacement: Using "би" instead of "б" after vowels (e.g., "Я би..."). → Correct: "Я б...". — Caused by ignoring Ukrainian euphony rules.
3. Splitting conjunctions: "Як я був би...". → Correct: "Якби я був...".
