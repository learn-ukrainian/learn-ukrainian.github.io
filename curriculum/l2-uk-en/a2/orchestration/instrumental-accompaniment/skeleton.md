## Орудний відмінок: Знайомство (~500 words total)
- P1 (~100 words): Introduce the Instrumental case (Орудний відмінок), answering the questions "Ким?" (By whom?) and "Чим?" (With what?). Frame it as the seventh case in the Ukrainian language journey, completing the core noun paradigm.
- P2 (~120 words): Explain the core meaning of the Instrumental case: acting as the "tool" of an action (e.g., "пишу ручкою" - I write with a pen) or the "companion" of an action. Emphasize that this module focuses on companionship — doing something WITH someone or something.
- P3 (~130 words): Provide a quick preview of all Instrumental case functions to give the learner the big picture. Mention accompaniment (з другом), tool (олівцем), profession (бути лікарем), and spatial relationships (під столом, над головою), noting that these will be covered in detail across upcoming modules.
- P4 (~150 words): Connect the Instrumental case to the previously learned cases (Nominative, Accusative, Genitive, Dative). Briefly explain how adding the Instrumental case completes the picture of noun relationships, allowing learners to construct much more complex and descriptive sentences.

## Закінчення орудного відмінка однини (~650 words total)
- P1 (~150 words): Detail the endings for masculine nouns (ІІ відміна). Explain the "-ом" ending for hard stems (другом, столом, братом), the "-ем" ending for soft stems and sibilants (вчителем, ножем, товаришем), and the "-єм" ending for stems ending in [й] (чаєм, героєм).
- P2 (~150 words): Detail the endings for feminine nouns (І відміна). Explain the "-ою" ending for hard stems (сестрою, мамою, книгою), the "-ею" ending for soft stems and sibilants (землею, душею, задачею), and the "-єю" ending for stems ending in [й] (мрією, надією). Point out the distinct full vowel ending in Ukrainian compared to other Slavic languages.
- P3 (~150 words): Detail the endings for neuter nouns (ІІ відміна). Explain the "-ом" ending for words ending in -о (вікном, молоком, маслом) and the "-ем" or "-ям" endings for words ending in -е or -я (морем, життям, ім'ям).

## З/із/зі + орудний відмінок (~550 words total)
- P1 (~150 words): Introduce the preposition "з" (with) combined with the Instrumental case. Explain its primary meaning of accompaniment for people and living things, providing clear examples: "Я гуляю з другом", "Вона живе з батьками". Contrast this explicitly with using the Instrumental case without a preposition for tools.
- P2 (~150 words): Explain the second highly frequent meaning: attribute or composition (ingredient), especially common with food. Use examples like "чай з лимоном", "хліб з маслом", and "борщ зі сметаною" to show how to describe dishes.
- P3 (~150 words): Explain the phonetic variants (милозвучність) of the preposition "з". Break down the rules: use "з" before vowels and most single consonants (з другом, з молоком), "із" before consonant clusters (із сестрою), and "зі" before words starting with з-, с-, ш- (зі мною, зі сметаною, зі школи).
