## Місцевий з абстрактними іменниками (Locative with Abstract Nouns) (~550 words total)
- P1 (~110 words): Introduction to the conceptual expansion of the Locative case. Contrast A1 physical location (я у кімнаті) with A2 abstract "location" or domains (я у політиці). Explain that the Locative case acts as a container for contexts and fields of activity.
- P2 (~130 words): Formation rules for feminine abstract nouns ending in -а and -я. Explain that they follow the standard Locative pattern of changing to -і, but emphasize their role as domains. Provide 4 examples with usage: освіта → в освіті (in education), культура → у культурі (in culture), політика → у політиці (in politics), релігія → у релігії (in religion).
- P3 (~110 words): Masculine and Neuter abstract nouns in the Locative. Explain the -і and -і/-я endings for fields like бізнес → у бізнесі (in business), мистецтво → у мистецтві (in art), and право → у праві (in law). Highlight that these express immersion in a subject rather than a physical spot.
- P4 (~120 words): The "на" vs "у/в" distinction for abstract nouns. Contrast "на роботі" (at work as an activity/status) with "в офісі" (physical) or "у бізнесі" (the industry). Explain that "на роботі" is a fixed idiomatic expression that learners must memorize as the primary way to say "at work" in a general sense.

## Часовий місцевий відмінок (Temporal Locative) (~600 words total)
- P1 (~120 words): Calendar months as temporal "containers." Explain the use of the preposition "у/в" with months. Specifically detail the stem change for masculine months ending in -ень where the 'e' drops: січень → у січні, березень → у березні, травень → у травні. Provide a list of 3-4 common months to illustrate the consistency.
- P2 (~100 words): The "на" exception for weeks. Explain that while months take "у", the noun "тиждень" (week) requires "на". Provide the essential temporal triad for weeks: на цьому тижні (this week), на минулому тижні (last week), and на наступному тижні (next week).
- P3 (~120 words): Life periods as broad temporal contexts. Teach "у дитинстві" (in childhood), "у молодості" (in youth), and "у старості" (in old age). Explain that these function as "backgrounds" for events, using the Locative to define the era of one's life. Include "у минулому" (in the past) and "у майбутньому" (in the future).
- P4 (~130 words): Dialogue 1: Two friends (Марія and Ігор) catching up. Марія explains she changed jobs "у минулому місяці" and now works "в освіті." Ігор mentions he talked to his mom "по телефону." This dialogue synthesizes abstract domains with temporal markers and introduces communication means.
- P5 (~80 words): Logical contrast between the Locative and Accusative for time. Explain the difference between "Коли?" (When? - Locative for months/weeks) and "Як довго?" (How long? - Accusative for duration). Example: "Я був там у червні" (Loc) vs. "Я був там весь червень" (Acc).
- Exercise: [fill-in, Complete sentences with the correct locative form of the noun (у минулому ___, на цьому ___, по ___), 8 items] (~50 words)

## По телефону, по радіо: місцевий із прийменником «по» (Locative with "po") (~500 words total)
- P1 (~120 words): Using "по" + Locative to express the means or channel of communication. Provide the core set: по телефону (by phone), по радіо (on the radio), по пошті (by mail), по телевізору (on TV). Note that the masculine nouns here often take the -у ending in the Locative when used with "по".
- P2 (~110 words): The semantic distinction between "про" (Topic) and "по" (Tool). Explain that we talk *about* something (про + Acc/Loc) *via* something (по + Loc). Example: "Я розмовляв про роботу (topic) по телефону (tool)." This prevents the common error of using "про" for the communication medium.
- P3 (~120 words): Spatial-contextual expressions with "по". Introduce "по дорозі" (on the way) as a context of movement. Contrast "по дорозі додому" (on the way home) with "на дорозі" (standing physically on the asphalt). Explain that "по" implies being "within the process" of the journey.
- P4 (~100 words): Dialogue 2: Student and Tutor. The student discusses their schedule "на цьому тижні" and mentions reading about history "у підручнику" (in a textbook). The tutor asks if they can talk "по телефону" later. This reinforces the "means" and "abstract source" functions.
- Exercise: [match-up, Match locative expressions with their English equivalents across all four function types, 8 items] (~50 words)

## Місцевий відмінок: від місця до сенсу (From Place to Meaning) (~350 words total)
- P1 (~120 words): Synthesis of the Locative's evolution. Explain that for an A2 learner, the Locative is no longer just the "Where" case, but the "Context" case. It answers three vital questions: Де? (Location/Domain), Коли? (Time periods/Months), and Як? (By what means of communication).

- Підсумок (~150 words):
    - Q: Який прийменник ми вживаємо для місяців?
    - A: Прийменник "у" або "в" (у січні).
    - Q: Як сказати "this week" українською?
    - A: На цьому тижні (використовуємо "на").
    - Q: Як ми позначаємо засіб зв'язку (телефон, радіо)?
    - A: За допомогою прийменника "по" та місцевого відмінка (по телефону).
    - Q: Яке закінчення мають абстрактні іменники жіночого роду (освіта, політика)?
    - A: Закінчення -і (в освіті, у політиці).
