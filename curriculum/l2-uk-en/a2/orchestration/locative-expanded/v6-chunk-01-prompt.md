## Місцевий з абстрактними іменниками (Locative with Abstract Nouns) (~550 words total)
- P1 (~110 words): Introduction to the conceptual expansion of the Locative case. Contrast A1 physical location (я у кімнаті) with A2 abstract "location" or domains (я у політиці). Explain that the Locative case acts as a container for contexts and fields of activity.
- P2 (~130 words): Formation rules for feminine abstract nouns ending in -а and -я. Explain that they follow the standard Locative pattern of changing to -і, but emphasize their role as domains. Provide 4 examples with usage: освіта → в освіті (in education), культура → у культурі (in culture), політика → у політиці (in politics), релігія → у релігії (in religion).
- P3 (~110 words): Masculine and Neuter abstract nouns in the Locative. Explain the -і and -і/-я endings for fields like бізнес → у бізнесі (in business), мистецтво → у мистецтві (in art), and право → у праві (in law). Highlight that these express immersion in a subject rather than a physical spot.
- P4 (~120 words): The "на" vs "у/в" distinction for abstract nouns. Contrast "на роботі" (at work as an activity/status) with "в офісі" (physical) or "у бізнесі" (the industry). Explain that "на роботі" is a fixed idiomatic expression that learners must memorize as the primary way to say "at work" in a general sense.
