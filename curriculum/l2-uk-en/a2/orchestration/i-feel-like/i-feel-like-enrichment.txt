## Cultural Hooks
1. **Meteosensitivity (Метеозалежність)**: Ukrainians frequently attribute changes in mood or physical well-being (headaches, fatigue) to weather changes. It is culturally acceptable and common to complain about "magnetic storms" (магнітні бурі) or pressure changes.
2. **Folk Wisdom**: The proverb "Ломить руки, або коліна – буде погоди переміна" (Hands or knees ache – the weather will change) reflects this deep cultural connection between body states and nature.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Literal Translation (Nominative vs Dative)**: "Я холодний" (I am a cold person/object) instead of "Мені холодно" (I feel cold).
   *   *Why*: Direct mapping from English "I am...".
2. **Wrong Case for Pronouns**: Using "Вона сумно" instead of "Їй сумно".
   *   *Why*: Failure to switch subject to Dative experiencer.
3. **Adjective vs Adverb**: "Мені холодний" instead of "Мені холодно".
   *   *Why*: Confusion between the quality of a person and the state they feel.

## Cross-References
- **Builds on**: A1 vocabulary for basic feelings (happy/sad adjectives); Introduction to Dative (if covered previously for "подобатися").
- **Prepares for**: B1 Health & Medicine modules (describing symptoms); B1/B2 expressing complex emotional states and opinions.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Distinction**: Clearly distinguish "радий" (Short adjective, agrees with gender: я радий/рада) vs "щасливий" (Long adjective) vs "мені весело" (Adverbial state).
