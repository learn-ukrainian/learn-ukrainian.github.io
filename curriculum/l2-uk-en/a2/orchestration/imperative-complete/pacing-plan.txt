Section 1 "Хай і нехай: Наказ для третіх осіб (3rd Person Imperatives)": ~550 words — Focuses on forming indirect commands, permissions, and wishes for "he/she/they" using the particles хай and нехай.
Section 2 "Читаймо! Ходімо! Перша особа множини (1st Person Plural Imperatives)": ~450 words — Focuses on how to make group suggestions and invitations using the authentic -мо suffix, contrasting it with the softer давайте alternative.
Section 3 "Кличний + наказовий + орудний: Побажання (Vocative + Imperative + Instrumental: Wishes)": ~550 words — Focuses on the cultural and grammatical structure of wishing someone well using the vocative case, the imperative form of "to be", and the instrumental case for adjectives.
Section 4 "Вид дієслова в наказовому способі (Aspect in Imperatives)": ~450 words — Focuses on choosing between perfective and imperfective verbs when giving commands, especially the nuances of polite invitations and negative warnings.
Summary "Підсумок": ~150 words — Recaps the three new imperative forms, the aspect rules, and the cultural contexts learned in the module.
