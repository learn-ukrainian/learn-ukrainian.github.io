## Grammar Rules
- Genitive Case Function: Правопис §82-96 — Answering 'Кого? Чого?'. Primary function in this module: expressing absence with "немає" and quantity with "багато/мало/кілька".
- Masculine Singular Endings: Правопис §82-83 — Use **-а/-я** for animate beings (брата), specific items (стола), towns (Києва), and units of measure/time (метра, понеділка). Use **-у/-ю** for substances (цукру), abstract concepts (часу), and collective nouns (народу).
- Feminine Singular Endings: Правопис §87 — Use **-и** for hard stems (книги) and **-і** for soft stems or stems ending in sibilants [ж, ч, ш] (землі, межі, каші).
- Genitive Plural Endings: Правопис §96 — Masculine nouns typically take **-ів/-їв** (братів, столів), while feminine and neuter nouns usually have a **zero ending** (книжок, вікон). Note: "гроші" takes **-ей** (грошей).
- Vowel Alternation: [о], [е] alternate with [і] in closed syllables (стіл — стола) and appear as "fleeting vowels" in zero-ending plurals (вікно — вікон, книжка — книжок).

## Calque Warnings
- у мене немає: OK — This is the standard construction for absence/possession in Ukrainian.
- завдяки / дякуючи: Calque — Use "завдяки" (thanks to) as a preposition, not the participle "дякуючи".
- багато людей: OK — Standard usage (though "багато народу" is also common for "a lot of people" as a collective).
