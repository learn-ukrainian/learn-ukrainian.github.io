You might also see it written as two separate words: «не має». What is the exact difference? As noted in Ukrainian school textbooks, «немає» (written as one word) is an impersonal predicate meaning "there is no." It does not change, and it requires the Genitive case. However, «не має» (written as two words) is the normal verb «мати» *(to have)* with the negative particle «не».
