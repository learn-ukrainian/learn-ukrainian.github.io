## Коли є багато або мало (When There Is a Lot or a Little) (~780 words total)
- P1 (~130 words): Introduction to Quantity words. Explain that words like `багато` (a lot), `мало` (little/few), `кілька/декілька` (a few), and `скільки` (how many) act like "Genitive magnets." When you have a quantity of something, that "something" must be in the Genitive Plural.
- P2 (~160 words): Genitive Plural for Masculine nouns. The dominant ending is `-ів` or `-їв` for vowel stems (e.g., `братів`, `столів`, `музеїв`, `доларів`). Mention the small group taking `-ей` like `гостей` or `друзів` (special case).
- P3 (~140 words): Genitive Plural for Feminine and Neuter nouns. Introduce the "Zero Ending" (нульове закінчення). This is where the vowel disappears: `книги` -> `багато книг`, `школи` -> `мало шкіл`, `вікна` -> `кілька вікон`.
- P4 (~140 words): Phonetic adjustments: The "Fleeting Vowel." Explain that when a zero ending creates a difficult cluster of consonants, Ukrainian inserts a helper vowel `о` or `е`. Use examples like `книжка` -> `книжок`, `сестра` -> `сестер`, `вікно` -> `вікон`.
- P5 (~120 words): Usage examples combining quantity and absence. "У мене багато друзів, але мало часу." "У кімнаті немає вікон." Explain how this allows for more complex descriptions of a scene.
- Exercise: Match-up, focus: Genitive Plural Formation with Quantity Words, 8 items. Match quantity words with correctly declined plural nouns.
- Exercise: Match-up, focus: Translate sentences with 'a lot of...' / 'I don't have...', 8 items. Focus on English-to-Ukrainian translation of basic Genitive phrases.
- Exercise: Unjumble, focus: Reorder words to form correct genitive phrases with немає and quantity expressions, 6 items. Focus on correct word order (e.g., "У / мене / немає / багато / грошей").
