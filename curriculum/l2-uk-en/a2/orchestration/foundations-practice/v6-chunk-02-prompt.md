What do we have, and what is missing? Compare the imperfective plan, the perfective intention, and the perfective result: * Imperfective Plan: **Я буду готувати вечерю.** *(I will be cooking dinner - process.)* * Perfective Intention: **Я приготую вечерю.** *(I will cook dinner - focusing on getting it done.)* * Perfective Result: **Я приготувала вечерю.** *(I cooked dinner - it is finished and ready.)* * Imperfective Plan: **Ми будемо купувати торт.** *(We will be shopping for a cake.)* * Perfective Result: **Ми купили торт.** *(We bought a cake - here it is.)* You use perfective verbs when the action is completely done, closed, and the result is what matters right now for the current situation. <!-- INJECT_ACTIVITY: quiz, Role-play: Planning a Party -->
