## Common Learner Errors
1. Translating "take medicine" literally as *брати ліки* → Correct form: *приймати ліки* (formal) or *пити ліки* (casual) — Direct English interference.

3. Confusing the conjunction of purpose *щоб* with the conditional pronoun + particle *що б* → Correct form: *щоб* (in order to) — Phonetic similarity masking structural difference.
