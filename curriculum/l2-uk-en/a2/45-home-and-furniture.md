> [!cultural] Ukrainian Apartments
> Many Ukrainians live in apartments rather than houses.
> A typical apartment has a living room, bedroom, kitchen, and bathroom.
> The balcony is an important space for storage and relaxation.
> Apartment buildings from the Soviet era are still common in Ukrainian cities. These buildings usually have five to nine floors. Newer buildings may have elevators, but older ones often do not.
