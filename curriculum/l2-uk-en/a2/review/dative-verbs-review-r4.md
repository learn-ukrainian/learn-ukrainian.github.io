## Findings

[Linguistic accuracy] [Critical]
Location: `In Ukrainian, you do not use the preposition **до** *(to)* with these verbs for people.`
Issue: Factually incorrect grammar rule. The construction «дзвонити до когось» (e.g., "дзвонити до мами", "дзвонити до лікаря") is standard, correct, and widely used in Ukrainian alongside the bare Dative «дзвонити комусь». Teaching learners that it is never used is false.
Fix: Change to acknowledge that while "до" is used, the bare Dative is what's being taught and encouraged here.

[Linguistic accuracy] [Major]
Location: `працювати над проектом.`
Issue: Outdated spelling. According to the 2019 Pravopys, words with the Latin root -ject- must use 'є' (проєкт, об'єкт, суб'єкт).
Fix: Change "проектом" to "проєктом".

[Linguistic accuracy] [Major]
Location: `Давайте детально розглянемо дієслово **допомагати**`
Issue: Stylistic error (Calque). Using "давайте" + finite verb is a direct calque from Russian ("давайте рассмотрим"). The natural Ukrainian imperative form for the first person plural is synthetic ("розгляньмо").
Fix: Change "Давайте детально розглянемо" to "Розгляньмо детально".

[Linguistic accuracy] [Minor]
Location: `радити, заважати, та відповідати.`
Issue: Punctuation error. In Ukrainian orthography, placing a comma before a single "та" joining homogeneous sentence members is incorrect (no Oxford comma).
Fix: Remove the comma before "та".

[Linguistic accuracy] [Minor]
Location: `співчувати, та заздрити.`
Issue: Punctuation error. No comma before a single "та" joining a list.
Fix: Remove the comma before "та".

[Linguistic accuracy] [Minor]
Location: `закінчуються на 2, 3, або 4,`
Issue: Punctuation error. No comma before a single "або" joining a list.
Fix: Remove the comma before "або".

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is very strong structurally and pedagogically, but the false rule about the preposition "до" with "дзвонити", along with minor orthographic errors (outdated spelling, calques, and Oxford commas), requires revision.
