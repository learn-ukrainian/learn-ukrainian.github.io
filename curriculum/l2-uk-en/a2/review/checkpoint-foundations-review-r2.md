## Linguistic Scan
One critical linguistic error found:
- **Wrong grammar rule**: The text states that masculine nouns ending in a soft consonant take the Genitive Plural ending **-їв**. This is factually incorrect. Soft consonant nouns (e.g., *вчитель*, *хлопець*, *день*) take **-ів** (вчителів, хлопців) or **-ей** (днів). The **-їв** ending is reserved for nouns ending in **-й** (e.g., *музей* → *музеїв*).
