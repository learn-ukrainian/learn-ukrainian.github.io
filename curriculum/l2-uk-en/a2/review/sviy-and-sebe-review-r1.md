## Linguistic Scan
Errors found:
1. **Calques:** The text repeatedly uses "давайте" + a 1st-person plural verb (e.g., "давайте подумаємо", "давайте подивимося"). This is a morphological calque of the Russian "давайте подумаем". In Ukrainian, the imperative should be synthetic (e.g., "подумаймо", "подивімося").
2. **Grammar:** In the dialogue, the phrase "Ні, це свій!" is ungrammatical. The pronoun "свій" must refer back to the subject of the sentence. In "це (є) свій", "це" is the subject, resulting in the nonsensical meaning "It is its own". It needs an animate subject performing an action, such as "я взяла свій" (I took my own).
