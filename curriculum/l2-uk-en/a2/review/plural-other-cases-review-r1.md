```
Fix: Replace with clear, direct English.
```

```
[DIALOGUE] [MAJOR]
Location: Practice dialogue — "— **Вчителька:** Ми їдемо **вагонами** *(by train cars)*."
Issue: Unnatural collocation. GRAC concordance shows «вагонами» exclusively in freight/industrial contexts. No native speaker says "ми їдемо вагонами" for personal travel. Natural: «поїздами» (already introduced by the student in the previous line). A2 learners will memorize this as normal speech.
Fix: Replace «вагонами» with «поїздами» and adjust the teacher's response to add a different Instrumental form elsewhere.
```

```
[ENGAGEMENT] [MINOR]
Location: Section "Три відмінки разом" — "The verb or preposition acts as a 'commander' that dictates the required case ending."
Issue: Slightly heavy-handed metaphor, though more forgivable than "Golden Trio" since it has pedagogical value. Combined with other gimmicky phrasings, contributes to uneven tone.
Fix: Simplify to direct language.
```

## Verdict: REVISE

Three major findings (two engagement, one dialogue naturalness) and one minor. Linguistic accuracy is strong — all forms verified. The «вагонами» issue is the most important fix: it teaches an unnatural collocation to A2 learners. Engagement language needs toning down. All fixes are surgical — no structural issues.
