## Findings
[Linguistic accuracy] [Critical]
Location: `- **—В–Є—И–∞** *(silence)* вЖТ –±–∞–≥–∞—В–Њ **—В–Є—И** *(of silences)*` and `–Ґ—Г—В –±—Г–ї–Њ –±–∞–≥–∞—В–Њ —В–Є—И. *(There were many silences here.)*`
Issue: `—В–Є—И–∞` is predominantly an uncountable noun, and its plural `—В–Є—И` does not exist in standard dictionaries (confirmed absent in VESUM). It is unidiomatic and wrong to teach this as a standard mixed-group zero-ending example.
Fix: Replace `—В–Є—И–∞ -> —В–Є—И` with `–≥—А—Г—И–∞ -> –≥—А—Г—И` and adjust the example sentence.

[Linguistic accuracy] [Major]
Location: `–£ –Љ–µ–љ–µ —Б—М–Њ–≥–Њ–і–љ—Ц –і—Г–ґ–µ –±–∞–≥–∞—В–Њ –≤–∞–ґ–ї–Є–≤–Є—Е –і—Ц–ї. *(I have a lot of important things to do today.)*`
Issue: Using `–і—Ц–ї` to mean "things to do" is a direct Russianism calque from "–Љ–љ–Њ–≥–Њ –і–µ–ї". The standard Ukrainian expression is "–±–∞–≥–∞—В–Њ —Б–њ—А–∞–≤". Because this is in the neuter `-–Њ` ending section, the example should be replaced with a natural neuter noun like `–Њ–Ј–µ—А–Њ`.
Fix: Replace the sentence with a new neuter example, e.g., `–£ –ї—Ц—Б—Ц —Ф –Ї—Ц–ї—М–Ї–∞ –≥–ї–Є–±–Њ–Ї–Є—Е –Њ–Ј–µ—А. *(There are several deep lakes in the forest.)*`

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is exceptional in its pedagogical clarity and structural flow, hitting all the requirements. However, it contains an invalid plural form (`—В–Є—И`) and a Russianism calque (`–і—Ц–ї`) that must be deterministically fixed to preserve linguistic accuracy before shipping to learners.
