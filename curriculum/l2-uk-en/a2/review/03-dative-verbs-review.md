2. **Подобатися pattern** — The "flipped" structure is explained with literal translations showing how Ukrainian puts the experiencer in Dative while the thing liked is the subject.

5. **Mini-dialogue quality** — Dialogues demonstrate verbs in natural conversational contexts (asking for help, giving advice, making recommendations).
