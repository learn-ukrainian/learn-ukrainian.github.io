## Findings

**[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [SEVERITY: critical]**
Location: Section 1, plural subsection: "In the plural, adjectives of all genders take the Dative ending **-им**."
Issue: The ending is stated as only -им, but soft-stem adjectives take -ім (VESUM: синім = `adj:p:v_dav:compb`). The module's own example "сині моря → **синім** морям" contradicts the stated rule. A learner would read "-им" then immediately see "синім" and be confused about whether it's -им or -ім.
Fix: Change the rule statement to acknowledge both endings: -им (hard stems) / -ім (soft stems), matching how the singular section distinguishes -ому (hard) / -ьому (soft).

**[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [SEVERITY: critical]**
Location: Section 1, hard-stem reading practice: "Ми йдемо до школи по **новому мосту**. *(We are going to school along the new bridge.)*"
Issue: The preposition "по" in spatial meaning ("along") governs the місцевий відмінок (locative), not the давальний. In a module specifically about dative case, every bolded example must genuinely be dative.
