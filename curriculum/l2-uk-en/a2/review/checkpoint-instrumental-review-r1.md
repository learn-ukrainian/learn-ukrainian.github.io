## Findings

[2. Linguistic accuracy] [Critical]
Location: Part 1: "If the word ends in two consonants or an `р` or a labial consonant (б, п, в, м, ф), we do not double it. Instead, we use an apostrophe."
Issue: Factual error. Words ending in two consonants do not take an apostrophe in the Instrumental case (e.g., радість -> радістю). The apostrophe is only used after labials and 'р'.
Fix: Replace with "If the word ends in two consonants, there is no doubling (**радістю**). If it ends in an `р` or a labial consonant (б, п, в, м, ф), we use an apostrophe."

[2. Linguistic accuracy] [Major]
Location: Part 1: "For words ending in a vowel sound, we use **-єю** and **-єм**."
Issue: Phonetic inaccuracy. Words like 'край' end in a consonant (й), not a vowel sound. The orthographic rule actually applies to words ending in -я, -є, -ї, or -й.
Fix: Replace with "For words ending in **-я**, **-є**, **-ї**, or **-й**, we use **-єю** and **-єм**."
