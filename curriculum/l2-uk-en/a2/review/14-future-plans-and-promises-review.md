## Summary

Excellent module on future tense with aspect distinction. Contrasts perfective future (single conjugated verb: прочитаю) with imperfective future (буду + infinitive: буду читати).

## Strengths

1. **Two future patterns** - Clear distinction between perfective and imperfective future
2. **Plans vs. habits** - Perfective for specific plans, imperfective for ongoing activities
3. **Practical contexts** - Making plans, promises, predictions
4. **Formation clarity** - Shows exactly how to form each type
