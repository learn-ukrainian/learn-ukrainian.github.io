## Findings

[Dimension 2. Linguistic accuracy] [Severity: critical]
Location: Section "Коли є багато або мало (When There Is a Lot or a Little)", paragraph 2 ("For most masculine nouns ending in a consonant...")
Issue: The text incorrectly states that the spelling `-їв` is used after a "soft sign". In Ukrainian orthography, after a soft sign (e.g., `нь`, `дь`), the ending is `-ів` and the soft sign drops (e.g., *учень* → *учнів*, *камінь* → *каменів*). The `-їв` ending is used after `й` (as in *трамвай* → *трамваїв*).
Fix: Change the parenthetical explanation to `(or **-їв** after **й**)` to correctly reflect the orthography rule.

[Dimension 4. Vocabulary coverage] [Severity: major]
