## Findings

**[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [CRITICAL]**
Location: Section 1, paragraph 1: «Він відповідає на спеціальні питання **Ким?** *(With whom?)* та **Чим?** *(With what?)*»
Issue: The instrumental case questions Ким?/Чим? are wrongly translated. "Ким?" does NOT mean "With whom?" — that is "З ким?" (preposition + instrumental). Standalone "Ким?" means "By whom?" / "As whom?" (cf. "Він працює **ким?** — лікарем"). The module itself later correctly uses "З ким?" for accompaniment, creating an internal contradiction. This teaches a wrong grammatical concept that learners will internalize.
Fix: Change translations to "Ким? *(Whom? / As whom? / By whom?)* та Чим? *(What? / By what?)*" — then explain that з + Ким? = "З ким?" (with whom).

**[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [MAJOR]**
Location: Section 2, paragraph 1: «Також слово **стіл** *(table)* отримує закінчення **-ом**: **за столом** *(behind a table)*»
Issue: "за столом" most commonly means "at the table" (сидіти за столом), not "behind a table." The translation "behind" is misleading for the most frequent usage.
Fix: Change "*(behind a table)*" to "*(at the table)*".

**[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [MAJOR]**
Location: Section 2, paragraph 5: «слово **вікно** *(window)* стає **за вікном** *(behind the window)*»
Issue: "за вікном" means "outside the window" (viewing from inside), not "behind the window." "Behind the window" is an unnatural literal translation.
Fix: Change "*(behind the window)*" to "*(outside the window)*".

**[ENGAGEMENT & TONE] [MINOR]**
Location: Section 3, paragraph 3: «Милозвучність є базовим і головним правилом української граматики та фонетики.»
Issue: Overstatement. Euphony (милозвучність) is an important feature of Ukrainian phonetics, but calling it "the basic and main rule of Ukrainian grammar and phonetics" is an exaggeration — case agreement, verb conjugation, word order are all more fundamental "rules."
Fix: Soften to "Милозвучність є важливою рисою української мови."

**[PEDAGOGICAL QUALITY] [MAJOR]**
Location: Section 1, paragraph 1 — the initial framing of Ким?/Чим? as the case questions with wrong English glosses cascades into a pedagogical problem. When learners later encounter "Він працює ким? — лікарем" (He works as what? — a doctor), they will expect "with whom?" and be confused.
Issue: Same root cause as Finding 1 — the wrong translation of Ким?/Чим? creates a pedagogical trap.
Fix: Same fix as Finding 1, plus add a brief clarification: after the corrected translations, add a sentence explaining that when combined with з, the question becomes "З ким? (With whom?) / З чим? (With what?)" — and this is the focus of today's module.

## Verdict: REVISE

The module has strong Ukrainian linguistic quality (all forms VESUM-verified, correct endings per textbook sources, no Russianisms/surzhyk/calques) and solid pedagogical structure (PPP flow, abundant examples, natural dialogue). However, it has one critical error (wrong translation of case questions Ким?/Чим?) and two major translation errors (за столом, за вікном) that would teach incorrect concepts to learners. All fixes are straightforward text replacements.
