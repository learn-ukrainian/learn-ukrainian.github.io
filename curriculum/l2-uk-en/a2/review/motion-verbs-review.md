## Findings

**[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [minor]**
Location: Section 4, "Рух через простір" subsection — "Ми гуляємо по місту"
Issue: The text labels "по" + Locative, but "місту" is the dative form of "місто" (locative = "місті"). The rule as stated ("по + Loc.") is correct for "по вулиці" (вулиці is indeed locative) but inconsistent with the "по місту" example.
Fix: Either change the example to "по вулиці" (which is already used), or note that "по" can take both dative and locative with slightly different nuances. For A2 level, the simplest fix is to replace the example.
