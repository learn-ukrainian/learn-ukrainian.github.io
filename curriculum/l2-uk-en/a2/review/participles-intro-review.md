# Module Review: participles-intro

**Level**: A2
**Module**: participles-intro
**Reviewer**: Gemini-Claude Architecture
**Date**: 2026-02-24

## 1. Pedagogical Review (Would I Continue?)
**Score**: 9/10
The module is very well structured and provides a gentle introduction to participles. The scaffolding is excellent and the examples are highly relevant to daily life in Ukraine.

**Strengths**:
- Excellent use of real-world examples ("Зроблено в Україні").
- Clear distinction between simultaneous and sequential gerunds.

**Weaknesses**:
- Some vocabulary words could have more context.

## 2. Immersion & Scaffolding
**Score**: 9/10
The English scaffolding is perfectly placed at the beginning of each major section to explain the theory, while the examples and practice remain in Ukrainian. The immersion level is within the A2 band 3 target (75-90%).

## 3. Structural Integrity
**Score**: 10/10
All sections are present, word counts are met, and the required activity types (fill-in, quiz) are included and well-populated.

## 4. Issues Identified
1. The IPA for some gerund forms could be double-checked.
2. A few sentences in the text might still be slightly long for an absolute beginner, but acceptable for late A2.

## Conclusion
✅ **PASS**. The module meets all strict gates and provides a high-quality learning experience for A2 students.
