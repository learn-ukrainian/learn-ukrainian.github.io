## Findings
[Linguistic accuracy] [Critical]
Location: `Наприклад, на вихідних ви можете **гуляти з другом** *(to walk with a friend)* у міському парку.`
Issue: "На вихідних" is a common calque from Russian "на выходных". The correct natural Ukrainian expression is "у вихідні" or "вихідними".
Fix: Change "на вихідних" to "у вихідні".

[Linguistic accuracy] [Critical]
Location: `Подивімося, де знаходиться орудний відмінок у граматичній системі. *(Let's see where the instrumental case is located in the grammatical system.)*`
Issue: The use of "знаходитися" for abstract or physical location is a calque from Russian "находиться". In Ukrainian, "знаходитися" means "to be found" (e.g., after being lost). It should be "яке місце займає" or "де міститься".
Fix: Change to "яке місце займає" and update the English translation accordingly to match.

[Engagement & tone] [Major]
Location: `Кожен студент рівня A2 повинен знати цю коротку фразу напам'ять. *(Every A2 level student must know this short phrase by heart.)*`
Issue: Breaking the 4th wall with meta-commentary ("студент рівня A2"). The module should address the learner directly and immersively, rather than referring to them as a theoretical category of student.
Fix: Change to "Варто знати цю коротку фразу напам'ять. *(You should know this short phrase by heart.)*"

[Engagement & tone] [Major]
Location: `Це базова модель, яку дуже легко запам'ятати студентам. *(This is an absolutely basic model that is very easy for students to remember.)*`
Issue: Similar to the previous finding, referring to the reader in the third person as "студентам" sounds like a teacher training manual rather than a direct instructional text.
Fix: Change to "Це базова модель, яку дуже легко запам'ятати. *(This is an absolutely basic model that is very easy to remember.)*"

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is beautifully written, highly accurate phonetically, and adheres to the plan's word counts and structures. However, the presence of two critical linguistic calques ("на вихідних" and "знаходиться") and meta-commentary that breaks the immersion wall require targeted revision before publishing.
