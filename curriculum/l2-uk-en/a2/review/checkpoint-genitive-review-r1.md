## Findings
[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [SEVERITY: critical]  
Location: Part 1 вАФ "`Remember the core logic: concrete, countable objects and people take **-–∞/-—П** (`–±—Ц–ї—П —Б—В–Њ–ї–∞`, `–±–µ–Ј –±—А–∞—В–∞`), while abstract concepts, materials, and institutions take **-—Г/-—О** (`–±–µ–Ј —Ж—Г–Ї—А—Г`, `–і–Њ —Г–љ—Ц–≤–µ—А—Б–Є—В–µ—В—Г`).`"  
Issue: This teaches a false deterministic rule for masculine genitive singular. VESUM confirms parallel forms like `—Б—В–Њ–ї–∞/—Б—В–Њ–ї—Г` and `–Љ–∞–≥–∞–Ј–Є–љ–∞/–Љ–∞–≥–∞–Ј–Є–љ—Г`, so the explanation must be presented as a tendency, not a hard rule.  
Fix: Rewrite this sentence to say it is a broad tendency and note that some masculine nouns allow more than one genitive form.

[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [SEVERITY: critical]  
Location: Part 2 вАФ "`For feminine words, the ending is **-–Њ—Ч** (or **-–µ—Ч** for soft sounds).`"  
Issue: This is factually wrong as a rule for adjectives and pronouns. Verified feminine genitive forms include `—Б–Є–љ—М–Њ—Ч`, `–Љ–Њ—Ф—Ч`, `—Ж—Ц—Ф—Ч`; "`soft sounds = -–µ—Ч`" is not an accurate rule.  
Fix: Replace with wording that distinguishes adjective and pronoun patterns, e.g. adjectives often `-–Њ—Ч`, pronouns `-–Њ—Ч / -—Ф—Ч` depending on the word.

[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [SEVERITY: critical]  
Location: Part 2 вАФ "`For plural words of all genders, the adjective and pronoun ending is uniformly **-–Є—Е**.`"  
Issue: This is false. Verified genitive plural forms include both `–љ–Њ–≤–Є—Е` and `—Б–Є–љ—Ц—Е`, as well as pronouns `–Љ–Њ—Ч—Е`, `—Ж–Є—Е`.  
Fix: Change the rule to `-–Є—Е / -—Ц—Е, depending on the word`.

[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [SEVERITY: critical]  
Location: Part 2 вАФ "`Second, never forget that negation always demands the genitive: \"I don't have a sister\" is `–£ –Љ–µ–љ–µ –љ–µ–Љ–∞—Ф —Б–µ—Б—В—А–Є`, never `–£ –Љ–µ–љ–µ –љ–µ–Љ–∞—Ф —Б–µ—Б—В—А–∞`.`"  
Issue: The example with `–љ–µ–Љ–∞—Ф` is correct, but the universal claim "`always demands the genitive`" is grammatically false and overteaches the rule.  
Fix: Limit the claim to negative constructions like `–љ–µ–Љ–∞—Ф` instead of presenting it as an absolute rule for all negation.
