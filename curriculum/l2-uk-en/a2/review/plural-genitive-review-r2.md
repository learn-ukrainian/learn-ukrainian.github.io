The і in шкіл is not "a new sound that appears" — it is the existing о that changes to і.
Fix: Replace школа→шкіл example with a genuine fleeting vowel example (казка→казок), which correctly demonstrates vowel insertion to break a consonant cluster.

**[LINGUISTIC ACCURACY] [MAJOR]**
Location: Section 1, paragraph 4 (English): "words often change their internal vowels to maintain a nice rhythm"
Issue: The mechanism behind fleeting vowels and vowel alternation is phonotactic (avoiding unpronounceable consonant clusters), not "maintaining a nice rhythm." This imprecise description could confuse learners about why these changes occur.
Fix: Replace "maintain a nice rhythm" with "avoid difficult consonant clusters."
