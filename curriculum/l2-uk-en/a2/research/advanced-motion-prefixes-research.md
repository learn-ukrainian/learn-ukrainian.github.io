Alignment: This module serves as a practical A2 bridge to the systematic B1 word formation (§4.3.8). It introduces high-frequency prefixed motion verbs (пере-, про-, під-, до-, за-, об-, при-) as vocabulary items and aspectual partners to basic motion verbs, enabling learners to describe complex spatial trajectories required by the "Travel" and "City" themes (§3).

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Returning" Superstition**: It is considered bad luck to return home (`повертатися` / `вернутися`) shortly after leaving because you forgot something. If you must return, you should look in the mirror (`подивитися у дзеркало`) and smile/stick out your tongue to neutralize the negative energy. This is a perfect narrative hook for `за-` (popping back in) vs `по-` (leaving).
2. **Crossing the Path (`Перейти дорогу`)**: A black cat crossing your path (`чорний кіт перейшов дорогу`) is a classic bad omen. To ward it off, superstitious people might wait for someone else to cross first or spit over their left shoulder. This anchors the verb `перейти`.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Preposition Mismatch**: Using `до` with all prefixes.
   *   *Error*: Підійти *в* парк.
   *   *Correction*: Підійти *до* парку (approach *to*).
   *   *Reason*: `Під-` implies proximity, not entry.
2. **"Coming" vs "Going"**: Confusing `прийти` (arrive/come) with `піти` (go/leave).
   *   *Error*: Я прийшов на роботу (when meaning "I left for work").
   *   *Correction*: Я пішов на роботу.
   *   *Reason*: English "go" covers both start and end points depending on context; Ukrainian strictly separates departure (`пі-`) and arrival (`при-`).
3. **Perfective Future Confusion**: Trying to use `буду` with perfective verbs.
   *   *Error*: Я буду прийти завтра.
   *   *Correction*: Я прийду завтра.
   *   *Reason*: Prefixed motion verbs are usually perfective and form the future tense simply by conjugating (like `read` -> `will read`), without the auxiliary `буду`.

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Visuals are key**: Use simple ASCII or text-based diagrams to show the geometry of `пере-` (X -> Y), `про-` (-> | | ->), and `об-` (O ->).
- **The "Bridge" Metaphor**: Frame prefixes as "GPS instructions" for the verb. The root (`-ход-`/`-й-`) is the engine, the prefix (`пере-`) is the steering wheel.
- **Idioms**: Introduce `мені підходить` (it suits me) early as a high-value practical phrase derived from spatial logic ("it approaches me well").
