## Cultural Hooks
1. "State in a Smartphone" (Держава в смартфоні): Ukraine was the first country in the world to grant digital passports in the "Diia" (Дія) app the same legal force as physical ones.
2. Ukraine's IT Sector: Before 2022, Ukraine was one of Europe's largest IT hubs (with centers in Kyiv, Lviv, Kharkiv), making digital literacy a core part of modern Ukrainian identity.

## Common Learner Errors
1. "На інтернеті" → "В інтернеті" — English speakers use the calque "on the internet", but Ukrainian requires the Locative case with "в".
2. "Скачати" → "Завантажити" — Learners often pick up "скачати" from colloquial speech or Russian, but "завантажити" is the correct standard Ukrainian verb for "to download".
3. Brand declension: "Шукати на ґуґл" → "Шукати в ґуґлі" (adapted/declined) or "в Google" (original/undeclined).

- Ensure strict avoidance of Russianisms in hints and examples (use 'завантажити', not 'скачати'; 'надіслати', not 'відправити' if referring to abstract forwarding, though 'відправити' is acceptable).
