# Research Notes: Технології та медіа (Technology & Media)

**Track**: Core A
**Module**: A2 M50
**Level**: A2 (Late Elementary)
**Context**: This module integrates the student's extensive knowledge of cases, verb aspects, and complex sentence structures into the modern context of digital life and information consumption.

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **Державний стандарт української мови як іноземної (2024)**, this topic is situated within the **Thematic Catalog (Каталог Б)** for the **A2 level**:

*   **§ 2. Соціально-побутова сфера (Social-everyday sphere)**: Topic "Сучасні пристрої та засоби зв’язку" (Modern devices and means of communication).
*   **§ 3. Соціокультурна сфера (Sociocultural sphere)**: Topic "Засоби масової інформації" (Mass media).

The standard requires the student to be able to describe their digital habits, use communication apps, and understand short, adapted news items or social media posts.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency & Collocations

### High-Frequency Vocabulary
*   **Devices**: комп’ютер (computer), ноутбук (laptop), телефон/смартфон (phone), планшет (tablet), навушники (headphones).
*   **Internet/Media**: інтернет, сайт, сторінка (page), новини (news), стаття (article), соцмережі (social networks), повідомлення (message), додаток (app).
*   **Verbs (Aspect Pairs)**: 
    *   користуватися (to use — *requires Instrumental case*)
    *   писати / написати (to write)
    *   читати / прочитати (to read)
    *   дивитися / подивитися (to watch)
    *   шукати / знайти (to search / to find)
    *   надсилати / надіслати (to send)
    *   завантажувати / завантажити (to download/upload)

### Common A2 Collocations
*   **сидіти в інтернеті** — to surf/hang out on the internet.
*   **зайти на сайт** — to visit/log onto a website.
*   **надіслати імейл / повідомлення** — to send an email / message.
*   **бути онлайн / офлайн** — to be online / offline.
*   **користуватися смартфоном** — to use a smartphone.
*   **читати новини в Телеграмі** — to read news on Telegram.

## 3. Cultural Hook: The Digital Leap

*   **Дія (Diia)**: Ukraine is a global leader in e-government. The "Diia" app allows Ukrainians to carry digital passports, driver's licenses, and even open businesses on their phones. This "state in a smartphone" concept is a source of national pride and a vital part of daily life.
*   **IT Hub**: Ukraine has one of the largest IT sectors in Europe. Conversations about "айтішники" (IT specialists) and tech startups are common in urban social circles.
*   **Telegram Culture**: In Ukraine, Telegram is not just a messenger but the primary source of real-time news (especially during the war), with channels having millions of subscribers.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

*   **Instrumental Case Mastery**: This module is the perfect place to reinforce the use of the Instrumental case with the verb **користуватися**. Students often forget this requirement because the English "use" takes a direct object.
*   **Prepositional Nuances**:
    *   **В** + Locative: *в інтернеті*, *в інстаграмі*.
    *   **На** + Locative: *на сайті*, *на сторінці*.
    *   **По** + Dative: *по телевізору*, *по радіо*.
*   **Loanwords**: Many terms are cognates (комп’ютер, смартфон), which builds confidence, but teachers should emphasize the **hard 'р'** at the end and the correct stress (e.g., комп’ю́тер, не комп'юте́р).
*   **Aspectual Logic**: Focus on the difference between "I was reading news for an hour" (Imperfective) and "I found the information I needed" (Perfective).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### In-Scope (Based on Prior Knowledge)
*   **Cases**: All cases (Nom, Acc, Loc, Gen, Dat, Inst). Specifically, using Instrumental for means of communication and Dative for recipients of messages.
*   **Aspects**: Using Imperfective for habits (checking news every morning) and Perfective for completed actions (downloaded the app).
*   **Comparisons**: Comparing devices (Цей ноутбук швидший і кращий, ніж той).
*   **Conditionals**: Expressing simple needs (Якби в мене був інтернет, я б працював).
*   **Relative Clauses**: Describing sites/apps (Додаток, який я завантажив...).

### Out-of-Scope
*   **Technical Jargon**: Specific hardware specs (RAM, CPU clock speeds, etc.).
*   **Passive Voice**: "The file was deleted" (Should be expressed actively: "Хтось видалив файл" or "Файл видалився").
*   **Complex Subjunctives**: Hypothetical technical architectures.
*   **Professional IT Terminology**: Programming languages, backend/frontend distinctions, unless very basic.

## 6. Decolonization Note
Avoid using the Russian-style "у мене є" for tech ownership too exclusively; encourage the use of **"я маю"** or simply naming the device as the subject when describing its functions, to highlight Ukrainian agency and modern linguistic trends. Ensure that when discussing media, Ukrainian-language sources and creators are highlighted as the default.
