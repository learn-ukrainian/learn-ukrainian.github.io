## Cultural Hooks

1. **Дія — Ukraine's digital state app** (Wikipedia, verified): State mobile app, web portal, and "digital state" brand developed by Ukraine's Ministry of Digital Transformation at President Zelensky's initiative. As of 2024, 21+ million Ukrainians use it. Дія made Ukraine the **first country in the world** to legally equate digital documents (held in the app) with physical ones — a landmark in e-governance. Key vocab anchor: мобільний додаток, встановити додаток, ввести пароль.

2. **Ukraine as IT hub** (Wikipedia — Інформаційно-комунікаційні технології в Україні, confirmed): Ukraine has one of Europe's largest IT sectors by headcount; major hubs in Kyiv, Lviv, Kharkiv, Dnipro. This "IT Nation" framing (used in Ukrainian public discourse) grounds the module opener authentically. Textbook corroboration: Grade 11 Hlibovska (2024) notes Ukraine as "one of the world leaders in digital transformation of public services" — appropriate framing for A2 context.

3. **Мережевий етикет** (Grade 7, Заболотний 2024, textbook RAG): The concept of internet etiquette (нетикет) appears in grade 7 Ukrainian language textbooks, confirming that digital vocabulary is curriculum-grounded. Relevant terms from textbook: флуд, спам, інтернет-простір — useful as cultural/contextual notes, not core A2 vocab.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **"на інтернеті" → "в інтернеті"** — Direct English calque ("on the internet"). Ukrainian uses the preposition **в/у** + Locative for internet, not **на**. Correct: *шукати в інтернеті*, *знайти в інтернеті*. The "на" error is extremely common among English speakers.

2. **"скачати" → "завантажити"** — VESUM confirms "скачати" is not in the standard dictionary. It is colloquial and heavily Russian-influenced (скачать). Standard Ukrainian is **завантажити** (to download) / **завантажувати** (impf). Content should explicitly flag this substitution.

3. **Brand-name Locative declension** — Learners often treat brand names as undeclinable. Ukrainian declines well-adapted loanwords: **в ґуґлі** (Locative of Ґуґл), **у Вайбері**, **у Телеграмі**. Undeclined forms (в Google, в Telegram) are also acceptable in informal contexts but should be presented as the "original-script" variant, not the default.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **"скачати" is not in VESUM** — confirm in content that the standard form is завантажити/завантажувати; treat скачати as an error to correct explicitly.
- **Підписка** has low GRAC frequency (0.49 IPM) relative to other tech terms — introduce it contextually (e.g., "оформити підписку на YouTube") rather than as a primary vocab item.
- **Дія** is a perfect A2 cultural anchor: concrete, real, widely known, and demonstrates several key patterns (встановити додаток, ввести пароль, отримати послугу). The "Digital Grandma" scenario in §4 of the plan is pedagogically sound — grounds abstract app-install language in a relatable narrative.
- **Immersion band**: This is module 55, placing it in the "Consolidation" band (M51–70) = 70–90% Ukrainian. Content should be predominantly Ukrainian with English only in vocabulary tables.
- **Locative case** is the central grammar point — ensure the в/у prepositional alternation is demonstrated in multiple contexts: в інтернеті, у мережі, в акаунті, у Вайбері.
- Avoid "кібер-" compounds beyond кіберпростір for A2 — they read as overly formal/technical. Prefer colloquial digital language (додаток, мережа, посилання).
