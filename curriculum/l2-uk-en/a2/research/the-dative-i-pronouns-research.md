# Дослідження: The Dative I — Pronouns

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.2.3: "Давальний відмінок: особа або предмет, для яких (на користь чи на шкоду) відбувається дія... суб’єкт кількісної вікової ознаки."
Alignment: This module directly addresses the requirement to teach the Dative case for indirect objects ("Give TO me") and age expressions ("To me is 20 years").

## Cultural Hooks
1. **"Мені приємно" (To me it is pleasant)**: The standard, polite response when meeting someone new. It shifts focus from "I am pleased" to the feeling itself impacting the speaker, reflecting a slightly more passive/receptive cultural linguistic pattern.
2. **Gift Giving Taboos**: When saying "Це тобі" (This is for you), one must never hand the gift across a threshold (через поріг). It is bad luck. Also, flowers must be given in odd numbers (3, 5, 7); even numbers are strictly for funerals.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **"I like" = "Я подобаю"**: Learners try to map English SVO directly. Correct: "Мені подобається" (To me pleases...).
2. **"I need" = "Я треба"**: Learner uses Nominative "I". Correct: "Мені треба" (To me is needed...).
3. **"Help me" = "Допоможи мене" (Accusative)**: English "help" takes a direct object, but Ukrainian "допомагати" requires Dative (help TO someone). Correct: "Допоможи мені".

## Notes for Content Writing
- Keep the distinction between "Action Recipient" (Give to me) and "Experiencer" (I am cold) clear but connected by the concept of "Dative Subject".
- Use the "Threshold" cultural hook to make the Dative usage "Це тобі" memorable.
