# Research: Word Formation Mastery (A2 M43)

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **Ukrainian State Standard 2024 (Catalog B / A2 Level)**, word formation at this level is primarily taught through aspectual opposition (prefixation and suffixation) and degree comparison.

> **§ 4.3. Словотвір**
>
> **4.3.1. Ступені порівняння якісних прикметників:**
> *   проста форма вищого ступеня: *солодший, важливіший*;
> *   проста форма найвищого ступеня: *найсолодший, найважливіший*;
> *   суплетивні форми: *великий – більший, найбільший*.
>
> **4.3.2. Видові пари дієслів:**
> *   *робити – зробити, ділити – поділити, писати – написати, виходити – вийти, забувати – забути*.

**Analysis:**
While the standard explicitly lists aspect pairs and adjectives, implicit requirements for A2 (based on communicative competencies in § 2.4.2) include understanding common derived nouns (professions, places) which are formalized in the B1 standard but introduced lexically at A2. This module consolidates the logic behind these forms using the "Root Family" approach.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

This module focuses on synthesizing high-frequency vocabulary from the 8 "Super-Roots" covered in M41-M42.

*   **Root: ХІД / ХОД (Motion)**
    *   *вхід* (entrance) - Ubiquitous in daily life.
    *   *вихід* (exit) - Ubiquitous.
    *   *перехід* (crosswalk/underpass) - Critical for navigation.
    *   *похід* (hike/campaign) - Common leisure topic.

## 3. Cultural Hook

### The Pysanka Connection (Root: ПИС)
The world-famous Ukrainian Easter egg, **«Писанка»**, comes directly from the verb **«писати»** (to write). Traditionally, Ukrainians do not "paint" eggs; they "write" them (*писати писанки*) using beeswax and a stylus (*писачок*). The symbols are a written language of wishes and prayers.
*   **Relevance:** Connects the root *пис-* not just to text, but to patterning and creating meaning, reinforcing the "Word Architect" theme.

### "Exit" vs. "East" (Root: ХІД)
A common confusion for learners in the Kyiv Metro:
*   **Вихід** (Exit) - prefix *ви-* (out).
*   **Схід** (East) - prefix *с-* (together/down/off? Etymology: rising sun).
*   **Сходи** (Stairs) - "walking things".
*   **Вхід** (Entrance) - prefix *в-* (in).
*   **Cultural Reality:** Navigating the metro requires decoding these roots instantly.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

### English vs. Ukrainian Logic
*   **English:** Uses **Phrasal Verbs** (go in, go out, write down, sign up). Meaning is distributed across Verb + Preparticle.
*   **Ukrainian:** Uses **Prefixes** (заходити, виходити, записати, підписати). Meaning is fused into the word.
*   **Teaching Tip:** Frame prefixes as "GPS coordinates" for the root. *В-* is always "in/into", *Ви-* is always "out".

### Common Learner Errors
1.  **Prefix Confusion:** Using *прийти* (arrive) instead of *піти* (go/leave).
2.  **Root Blindness:** Seeing *перехід* as a totally new word, failing to recognize *хід* (walk).
3.  **False Friends:** *Схід* (East) looking like *Вхід* (Entrance).

### Sequence
1.  **Review Roots:** Ensure the 8 Super-Roots (M41/M42) are solid.
2.  **Prefix Logic:** Reinforce spatial meaning of prefixes (в-, ви-, при-, під-).
3.  **Synthesis:** Show how [Prefix + Root + Suffix] = New Word.
4.  **Drill:** "Guess the meaning" exercises before production.

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope (Safe to use)
*   **Grammar:**
    *   Present, Past, Future tenses (Imperfective/Perfective).
    *   All cases (Nominative to Locative).
    *   Imperative mood.
    *   Conditional (*якби*).
    *   Motion verbs (basic and prefixed forms like *прийти, піти*).
    *   Prefixes: *в-, ви-, при-, по-, за-, пере-, під-, від-*.
    *   Suffixes: *-ач, -ник* (agent), *-ка* (female/object), basic noun endings.

### OUT of Scope (Do NOT use)
*   **Grammar:**
    *   **Participles** (Active/Passive forms like *писаний, читаючий*). *Wait for B1/B2.*
    *   **Gerunds** (*читаючи, пишучи*). *Wait for B1.*
    *   **Abstract Noun Suffixes:** *-ння* (reading), *-ство* (community) - introduce only as lexical items (vocabulary), NOT as a productive grammar rule yet (B1 Standard § 4.3.4).
    *   **Complex Prefixes:** *недо-, зне-*.
