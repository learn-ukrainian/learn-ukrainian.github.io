## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **Державний стандарт української мови (2024)**, this module aligns with the requirements for the **A2 (Початковий рівень другого ступеня)**.

*   **Section 2.4.2.3 (Каталог Б. Тематичний каталог):**
    *   **§3.7 Навчання (Learning):** "дії, пов’язані зі здобуттям знань (вчити, вивчати, вчитися); навчальні дисципліни."
*   **Section 2.4.2.4 (Каталог В. Зміст мовної компетентності):**
    *   **§4.3.2 Видові пари дієслів (Aspect pairs):** The standard explicitly requires knowledge of pairs such as *робити — зробити, писати — написати*. This is the basis for exploring root families like **-пис-** across aspects.
    *   **§4.4.2 Складне речення (Complex sentences):** Requirements include sentences with conjunctions *що* (I know that...), *щоб* (for purpose), and *тому що/бо* (because). These are essential for the "Logic" component of the module.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency (A2 Level)

Focusing on high-productivity roots for communication and cognitive processes:

### High Frequency (Crucial for A2)
- **-дум- (Thought/Logic):** *думати* (verb), *думка* (noun).
    - Collocations: *мати думку* (to have an opinion), *на мою думку* (in my opinion).
- **-мов- (Speech/Communication):** *мова* (language), *розмова* (conversation), *розмовляти* (to speak/chat).
    - Collocations: *рідна мова* (native language), *вести розмову* (to hold a conversation).
- **-зна- (Knowledge):** *знати* (to know), *знання* (knowledge), *знайомий* (familiar/acquaintance).
    - Collocations: *дати знати* (to let know), *мені знайоме це* (I'm familiar with this).
- **-розум- (Intellect):** *розуміти* (to understand), *розумний* (smart/logical), *розум* (intellect).

### Medium Frequency (Expansion)
- **-чит- (Reading):** *читати*, *читання*, *прочитати*.
- **-пис- (Writing):** *писати*, *написати*, *записка*, *письмовий*.
- **-каз- (Information/Pointing):** *казати*, *розказати*, *наказ* (order), *вказати* (point out).

## 3. Cultural Hook

1.  **"Слово — не горобець..."**: The most famous Ukrainian proverb about communication is *"Слово — не горобець, вилетить — не піймаєш"* (A word is not a sparrow; once it flies out, you can't catch it). It emphasizes the weight and permanence of spoken thought in Ukrainian culture.
2.  **The "Duma" (Дума)**: In Ukrainian history and culture, a *Duma* is not just a "thought" (думка) but a specific genre of folk epic song performed by itinerant Cossack bards (kobzars). It represents the collective historical memory and philosophical reflection of the nation, linking "thinking" with "narrative" and "heritage."

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

*   **Root-Based Efficiency:** Ukrainian (like other Slavic languages) is highly transparent once roots are identified. Teaching the root **-мов-** allows students to unlock *мова, розмова, розмовляти, вимова, мовлення* simultaneously.
*   **Cognitive Distinctions:** English "know" covers both *знати* (facts/people) and *вміти* (skills/how-to). At A2, students often confuse these. *Я знаю українську мову* vs. *Я вмію розмовляти*.
*   **Prefix Power:** Since students just learned action verb prefixes (M40-M41), this module should demonstrate how prefixes change cognitive verbs: *думати* (to think) vs. *придумати* (to invent/come up with), *знати* (to know) vs. *дізнатися* (to find out).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### In Scope
*   **Cases:** Full paradigm (Nom, Acc, Gen, Loc, Dat, Instr).
*   **Tenses:** Present, Past, Future (Simple/Complex).
*   **Aspect:** Using imperfective/perfective pairs for cognitive acts (*я думав* vs *я придумав*).
*   **Syntax:** Complex sentences using *що* (Reported thought: *Він каже, що...*) and *бо/тому що* (Logic).
*   **Modals:** *могти, хотіти, треба, можна* (Logic/Necessity).

### Out of Scope
*   **Participles/Gerunds:** No *думаючи* or *написаний*.
*   **Passive Voice:** No *мова вивчається*.
*   **Subjunctive (Advanced):** No complex "if I had thought" (stay within simple *якби* + past).
*   **Verbal Nouns (Advanced):** Limit to common ones like *знання, читання*; avoid abstract jargon.
*   **Scientific Register:** Keep logic/thought vocabulary to daily life and basic argumentation.
