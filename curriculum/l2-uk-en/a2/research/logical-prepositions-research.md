# Дослідження: Logical Prepositions

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.2.2: "Genitive — without preposition + with prepositions (без, від, для, до, з/із, після, біля, коло)"
§4.2.2.4: "Accusative — expanded prepositions (including через, про)"
§4.4.2: "Complex sentences — subordinate clauses including causal (тому що, бо) and purpose (щоб)"
Alignment: This module consolidates §4.2.2.2 Genitive prepositions (для, без, після, від, крім, замість, протягом), introduces abstract Accusative usage (через, про), and previews §4.4.2 causal/purpose connectors as bridging constructions.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **"Піти по хліб"** — Going to buy bread is expressed with *по хліб* (purpose, colloquial), not *за хлібом*. This is a well-known folk-speech shibboleth Ukrainians use to spot Russian interference in Ukrainian speech. Real A2 context: grocery and market scenarios.
2. **"На протязі" = "in a draft of air"** — A well-documented learner trap. The Russianism *на протязі* is physically absurd in Ukrainian — *протяг* means a draft/draught of air. The correct word for "during/over the course of" is *протягом* or *упродовж*. Teachers use this absurdist contrast to make the correct form stick.
3. **Pharmacy register** — The high-register pattern *від кашлю / після їжі / для здоров'я* occurs on every medication label and pharmacy receipt in Ukraine. This is one of the most authentic A2 use cases: reading a prescription or medicine package.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **"із-за" for cause** → *через* — English speakers calque from Russian *из-за* for "because of". Ukrainian *із-за* is spatial only ("from behind"); *через* covers all causal meanings.
2. **"в 10 годині"** → *о 10 годині* — Clock time requires *о/об* + Locative in Ukrainian. English speakers default to *в* (by analogy with "at the cafe" = *у кафе*).
3. **"завдяки" for negative cause** → *через* — *завдяки* is semantically positive; using it for problems/mistakes is unnatural. Contrastive drill: *через помилку* (mistake caused problem) vs *завдяки підтримці* (support led to success).
4. **"на протязі"** → *протягом / упродовж* — Russicism. Textbook exercises targeting duration typically contrast these forms directly (see Grade 5 grammar sections on prepositions).

## Teaching Strategy

- **Discovery exercise**: Present two mini-dialogues side by side without labels. Dialogue A: *— Чому ти спізнився? — Через затори.* Dialogue B: *— Чому ти виграв? — Завдяки підготовці.* Ask learners: "Both answer 'why' — what's different?" Let them notice positive vs negative framing before naming the prepositions. Then introduce the rule.
- **Best context**: Pharmacy and daily planning — "Ці таблетки від кашлю. Приймати після їжі, для дорослих." Every word on a real Ukrainian medicine box. Alternatively, planning a trip: "Не поїхали через погоду. Але завдяки запасному плану відпочили вдома."
- **Contrastive pairs**: через/завдяки (negative vs positive cause), від/з (source: person vs place), протягом/упродовж (both correct — register variant), для/заради (purpose vs sacrifice).

## Notes for Content Writing

- All grammar terms should appear in Ukrainian with English gloss in parentheses (e.g., *родовий відмінок* (Genitive case)) — A2 band 1 immersion is 45-65%.
- The *по хліб* vs *за хлібом* distinction is a tone-setter — open Section 3 with it, not bury it.
- Keep example sentences ≤15 words (A2 rule). Use concrete nouns: *хліб, кава, робота, погода, здоров'я*, *урок*.
- No abstract nouns: avoid *відчуття, стан, сутність*.
- *завдяки* governs Dative, not Genitive — mark this clearly; learners coming from Russian expect Genitive.
- Section 4 drills (transformation: conjunction → prepositional phrase) are the hardest part; use a worked example first, then independent practice.
