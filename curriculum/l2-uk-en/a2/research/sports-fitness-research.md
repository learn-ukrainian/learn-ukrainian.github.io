# Дослідження: Sports and Fitness

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.2.5: "Орудний відмінок... Без прийменника: об’єкт дії і стану: ...Вчимося розмовляти українською мовою."
Alignment: This module directly addresses the A2 requirement to master the Instrumental case without a preposition for denoting the object of an action or state, specifically applied to sports and hobbies (займатися спортом, займатися боксом). It also covers thematic vocabulary under §3.4 (Дозвілля, відпочинок: види спорту, спортивних споруд і спортивне спорядження).

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Football and National Identity:** Andriy Shevchenko is a defining figure in Ukrainian sports history. He won the Ballon d'Or in 2004, becoming the third Ukrainian to do so. The phrase «Вболівати за Динамо» (Київ) is a cultural staple representing historical resistance and national pride.
2. **Boxing Champions:** Ukraine has a dominant modern legacy in boxing, notably the Klitschko brothers and Oleksandr Usyk (undisputed champion). This provides perfect cultural context for the verb phrase «займатися боксом».

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Missing preposition with "грати"**: Learners often say "грати футбол" (direct English translation of "play football"). → Correct form: "грати у футбол" (requires в/у + Accusative).
2. **Incorrect case with "займатися"**: Learners use Nominative instead of Instrumental, saying "займатися спорт". → Correct form: "займатися спортом" (Instrumental required for the object of practice/study).

## Notes for Content Writing
- Immersion Band 3 (75-90% Ukrainian): English should only be used in vocabulary table translation columns and for very brief clarifications of abstract concepts if absolutely necessary. The prose, dialogues, and explanations must be overwhelmingly in Ukrainian.
- Decolonized framing: Present the use of "у/в" with sports and the Instrumental case with "займатися" as elegant, logical features of Ukrainian verb government. Do not compare them to Russian structures. Emphasize Ukrainian agency in sports (Usyk, Shevchenko).
- Keep sentences short (max 15 words). Provide IPA only on the first occurrence of new words.
