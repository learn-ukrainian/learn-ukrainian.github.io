## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **Державний стандарт української мови (2024)**, the study of verbal aspect and word formation via prefixation is a core requirement for the A2 level.

**Relevant Section:**
> **§4.3.2. Видові пари дієслів:** робити – зробити, ділити – поділити, писати – написати, виходити – вийти, забувати – забути. (стор. 34)

While basic aspect pairs are introduced earlier, Module 38 focuses on the **lexical nuances** added by prefixes to general action verbs (beyond simple perfectivization). This aligns with the transition from A2 to B1 where "Word Formation" (Словотвір) becomes more systematic.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

At the A2 level, students should focus on high-frequency verbs and the most productive prefixes.

### Key Verbs for Prefixation:
- **Писати / Читати** (Write / Read)
- **Робити** (Do/Make)
- **Співати** (Sing)
- **Думати** (Think)
- **Вчити** (Learn/Teach)
- **Бачити / Чути** (See / Hear)
- **Мити / Пити** (Wash / Drink)

### High-Frequency Prefixes & Meanings:
1. **По-** (Delimitative: doing for a short time) — *почитати, погуляти, подивитися*. (Very High Frequency)
2. **За-** (Inceptive: starting an action; or resultative) — *заспівати, заговорити, зателефонувати*. (High Frequency)
3. **Ви-** (Resultative: completion/achievement) — *вивчити, випити, вимити*. (High Frequency)
4. **До-** (Completion: finishing to the end) — *дочитати, дописати, доробити*. (Medium-High Frequency)
5. **Роз-** (Intensive/Distributive) — *розповісти, розпитати, розібрати*. (Medium Frequency)
6. **Пере-** (Repetitive or change) — *перечитати, переписати, переробити*. (Medium Frequency)

### Common A2 Collocations:
- **Подивитися** фільм (Watch a movie)
- **Заспівати** пісню (Sing a song)
- **Вивчити** вірш / нові слова (Learn a poem / new words)
- **Дочитати** книжку (Finish reading a book)
- **Розповісти** історію (Tell a story)

## 3. Cultural Hook

**The Precision of "Nuance"**:
In Ukrainian, using the "wrong" prefix doesn't just make you sound like a foreigner; it changes the entire "vibe" of the action. For example, a Ukrainian host might say "**По**сидьмо" (Let's sit for a bit) rather than just "**Сядьмо**" (Let's sit). The prefix **по-** adds a layer of coziness and limited duration that is essential to Ukrainian social etiquette.

**Folklore & Song**:
The prefix **за-** is ubiquitous in Ukrainian folk songs to signal the start of a narrative or emotional state (e.g., "*За*співав козак", "*За*плакала дівчина"). Understanding this "inceptive" force helps students appreciate the lyrical quality of the language.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

**Key Differences from English:**
- **Phrasal Verbs vs. Prefixes**: English uses particles (eat *up*, read *through*, write *down*). Ukrainian uses prefixes (*ви*їсти, *до*читати, *за*писати). Students should be taught to "match" English phrasal verbs to Ukrainian prefixes.
- **Aspect + Lexical Meaning**: Students often think prefixes *only* change the aspect (Imperfective -> Perfective). It is critical to show that they also add specific meanings (e.g., *до-* means "to the end").

**Common Learner Errors:**
- **The "З-" Overuse**: Beginners often try to use **з-** as a universal perfective prefix (e.g., *зчитати* instead of *прочитати*).
- **Misinterpreting 'Ви-'**: Learners often assume **ви-** always means "out" (directional), failing to recognize its resultative meaning in **вивчити** (learn fully).

**Teaching Sequence:**
1. Review known aspect pairs (писати/написати).
2. Introduce **По-** (time-limited) as the "easiest" semantic prefix.
3. Contrast **До-** (finishing) with **Про-** (duration).
4. Group verbs by prefix to show the "pattern of meaning."

## 5. Scope Boundaries

**IN SCOPE:**
- **Tenses**: Past (Perfective), Future (Perfective/Prefixed).
- **Cases**: Nominative, Accusative (Direct Object), Genitive (Negation/Quantity), Dative (Recipient), Instrumental (Means/Tools), Locative (Place).
- **Sentence Structure**: Simple and complex sentences with *бо, тому що, щоб, який, коли*.
- **Mood**: Imperative and Conditional (*якби*).

**OUT OF SCOPE:**
- **Participles & Gerunds**: Students have not learned active/passive participles (e.g., *прочитаний*) or gerunds (*прочитавши*). Avoid these in examples.
- **Passive Voice**: Focus on active constructions (e.g., "Я дочитав книгу" NOT "Книга була дочитана").
- **Obscure Prefixes**: Avoid archaic or highly specialized prefixes like *воз-, прі-, со-*.
- **Abstract Verbal Nouns**: While *читання* might be known, avoid complex prefixed nouns like *переосмислення*.
