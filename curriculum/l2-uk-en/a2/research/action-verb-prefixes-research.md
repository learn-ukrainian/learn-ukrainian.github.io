# Дослідження: Action Verb Prefixes

## State Standard Reference
§4.3.2: "Видові пари дієслів: робити – зробити, ділити – поділити, писати – написати, виходити – вийти, забувати – забути."
Alignment: This module explicitly teaches the semantic logic of prefixes (`пере-`, `до-`, `за-`, `від-`) used to form these aspectual pairs and modify verb meanings, directly addressing the A2 requirement for word formation and aspectual usage.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Hinges" Rule**: Ukrainian logic rigorously distinguishes between opening a physical barrier with hinges (`відчинити` - doors, windows, gates) and abstract "opening" (`відкрити` - season, soul, bank account). Using `відкрити` for a door is a marker of "Surzhyk" or Russian influence.
2. **The "Bread" Respect**: The prefix `до-` (finish to the end) is culturally heavy. You don't just "eat" (`їсти`), you `доїсти` (finish eating everything on the plate), reflecting the traditional reverence for bread and food scarcity trauma.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **The "Open" Trap**: `Відкрити двері` (Incorrect) → `Відчинити двері` (Correct). *Why*: Russian interference ("открыть дверь") or English generalization ("open" is one word).
2. **Rewrite vs. Finish**: Confusing `переписати` (do it again/copy) with `дописати` (finish what was started). *Why*: Both add to the root "write", meaning overlaps in English "write more/again".
3. **The "Za-" Trap**: Using `закрити` for physical doors. *Why*: See "Open Trap". Also `закрити` has a secondary meaning "to shut down" (a business), adding ambiguity.

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Visuals**: Use a clear "Hinge vs. Abstract" comparison table for Open/Close verbs.
- **Scaffolding**: Carefully distinguish `по-` as "a little bit" (delimitative) vs `по-` as perfective marker. Focus on "doing something for a while" (`почитати`) vs "starting" (`піти`).
