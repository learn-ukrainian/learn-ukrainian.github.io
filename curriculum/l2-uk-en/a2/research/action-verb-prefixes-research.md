# Дослідження: Action Verb Prefixes

## State Standard Reference

**§4.3.2** (A2 Word Formation): "Видові пари дієслів: робити – зробити, ділити – поділити, писати – написати, виходити – вийти, забувати – забути."
*(UKRAINIAN-STATE-STANDARD-2024.txt, lines 1380–1381)*

**§4.2.3.1** (A2 Indicative Mood): Includes prefixed perfective future forms as explicit examples: "майбутній час дієслів доконаного виду: скажу, закричите, поборють."
*(lines 1341–1359)*

**Alignment**: This module directly addresses §4.3.2 by teaching how prefixes (пере-, до-, від-, за-, по-) create aspectual pairs from base stems. It extends beyond the standard's minimal examples (писати→написати) to show the full semantic productivity of prefixation — completing the A2 picture of how Ukrainian builds meaning morphologically rather than through particles.

## Cultural Hooks

**1. Textbook-verified: відкривати ворота = НЕПРАВИЛЬНО**
Grade 5 Заболотний (2023), p. 20, contains an explicit "Культура мовлення" box:
- ПРАВИЛЬНО: `відчиняти ворота`
- НЕПРАВИЛЬНО: `відкривати ворота`
This is directly teachable — learners can see that the distinction is not pedantic but is the standard corrective form taught in Ukrainian schools. Source: `5-klas-ukrmova-zabolotnyi-2023_s0021`.

**2. Голодомор and the cultural imperative of доїсти**
The Голодомор (1932–1933) — Soviet-organized genocide by artificial famine — killed millions of Ukrainians. Ukrainian Wikipedia: "масовий штучний голод, що був організований на території Української СРР у 1932—1933 роках." The deeply ingrained cultural norm of finishing food on one's plate (доїсти все з тарілки) connects directly to this historical trauma. Teaching до- through доїсти anchors the prefix's "completion to the limit" logic in a genuinely Ukrainian cultural frame — not as a gimmick but as an honest connection between language and collective memory.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **відкрити двері instead of відчинити двері** → відчинити двері is the precise, standard form for physical hinged barriers. Error happens because відкрити is ~9× more frequent overall and feels universally applicable. Textbook explicitly marks відкривати ворота as НЕПРАВИЛЬНО.

2. **Confusing пере- (redo/copy) with до- (finish)** → "Треба переписати цей розділ" (rewrite it) vs. "Треба дописати цей розділ" (finish writing it). Both involve writing and both use perfective aspect; learners default to пере- because it's phonetically closer to English "re-." Clarify: пере- = again/differently; до- = reach the end.

3. **Triple ambiguity of за-** → Learners overgeneralize одне значення: closing (зачинити), starting (заспівати = start singing), or excess/dissolution (закрити бізнес = shut down a business). All three meanings are valid but contextually constrained; learners must learn which nouns license which meaning.

## Notes for Content Writing

- The Grade 5 Заболотний exercise (p. 58) uses бігти + all prefixes as a radial diagram — this pedagogical "prefix wheel" approach can be adapted for писати/робити in the practice section without copyright concern (it's a teaching pattern, not copied content).
- передати (46 IPM) should appear in dialogues naturally — it is the highest-frequency verb in this family and learners will encounter it constantly.
- доїсти is low-frequency (0.14 IPM) but pedagogically powerful as a cultural anchor; keep it to one focused example rather than drilling it.
- The за- triple-meaning section needs concrete minimal pairs: зачинити вікно (physical close) vs. заспівати (start) vs. закрити магазин (shut down). Keep each colocation distinct in register.
- A2 constraint: no participles. All prefixed verb examples must use them in finite forms (present/past/future indicative, imperative). Avoid nominalized forms like переписування.
- Immersion band for module 43 = 55-75% Ukrainian (Applied grammar band, M21-50). Grammar explanation paragraphs may be English; all examples, dialogues, collocations stay Ukrainian-only.
