# Дослідження: If I Were

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.3.3: "Умовний спосіб. Утворення форм умовного способу: говорив би, випила б, прочитали б."
Alignment: This module introduces the conditional mood (б/би) as formally specified in B1, but adapted early for A2 expressive needs (polite requests, basic hypotheses). It also covers real conditions (якщо) aligned with A2 §4.4.2 (Складне речення).

## Cultural Hooks
1. Proverb: "Якби та аби, та виросли в роті гриби" (If and maybe, then mushrooms would grow in the mouth) — used to gently mock empty "what ifs" instead of taking action.
2. The Lviv coffeehouse culture of philosophical dreaming ("філософування за кавою"), where discussing hypothetical futures ("Я б хотів...") is a common social pastime.

## Common Learner Errors
1. Tense mismatch: Using Present/Future after "якби" (e.g., "Якби я маю..."). → Correct: "Якби я мав...". — Caused by English "If I have..." mapping.
2. Particle misplacement: Using "би" instead of "б" after vowels (e.g., "Я би..."). → Correct: "Я б...". — Caused by ignoring Ukrainian euphony rules.
3. Splitting conjunctions: "Як я був би...". → Correct: "Якби я був...".

## Notes for Content Writing
- Decolonized Framing: Present the Ukrainian "б/би" conditional system as a clean, elegant structure relying strictly on the past tense form, framing it independently.
- Immersion Band 2 (60-75%): Use English ONLY for the abstract distinction between Real (якщо) and Unreal (якби) conditions and formation mechanics. All dialogues and examples must be strictly Ukrainian.
- IPA needed only for first occurrences of new vocabulary like "мрія" [ˈmrʲijɐ].
