# Дослідження: Complete Imperative

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.3.2: "Наказовий спосіб. Форми 2-ї і 3-ї особи однини і множини наказового способу деяких дієслів: мріяти (мрій, мрійте, хай / нехай мріє, хай / нехай мріють), казати (кажи, кажіть, хай / нехай каже, хай / нехай кажуть), пити (пий, пийте, хай / нехай п’є, хай / нехай п’ють)..."
Alignment: This module covers the full range of imperative forms (1st, 2nd, and 3rd person) as required by the A2 level of the Ukrainian State Standard 2024.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Ritual of Toasts**: "Будьмо!" is more than just a toast; it's a centuries-old call to collective presence and survival. In Ukrainian social etiquette, the third toast is traditionally dedicated "за любов" (to love) or "за жінок" (to women), often requiring men to stand as a sign of respect.
2. **Wishes as Social Glue**: Ukrainians frequently use third-person imperatives for greetings and partings (e.g., "Нехай щастить!" or "Хай щастить!"). These aren't just commands but shared hopes for the other person's success.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **The "Negative" Trap**: Learners often assume *нехай* is the negative version of *хай* because it starts with *не-*. In reality, they are interchangeable particles for the 3rd person imperative.
2. **Verb Form Confusion**: Forgetting that for *хай/нехай*, the verb remains in its regular 3rd person present/future form (e.g., *хай він читає*), unlike the 2nd person which uses a special stem (e.g., *читай*).
3. **Implicit "Let's"**: English speakers often try to use the infinitive with a "let's" equivalent, whereas Ukrainian uses the -мо/-імо suffix on the 1st person plural (e.g., *робімо*, *ідімо*).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Persona**: As the "Cultural Guide," frame the imperative not as a list of "orders" but as a toolkit for hospitality, toasting, and wishing well.
- **Scaffolding**: Since this is A2, use English to explain the "Let's" vs "Let them" distinction before diving into the Ukrainian paradigms.
- **Recipe Focus**: Use the Borscht instructions to contrast the 2nd person (*додайте*) with collective suggestions (*додаймо*).
- **IPA**: Ensure stress marks for all verbs: *неха́й*, *бу́дьмо*, *бажа́ю*, *ході́мо*.
