## State Standard Reference

**§4.2.3.2** (lines 1361–1365):
> "Наказовий спосіб. Форми 2-ї і 3-ї особи однини і множини наказового способу деяких дієслів: мріяти (мрій, мрійте, хай / нехай мріє, хай / нехай мріють), казати (кажи, кажіть, хай / нехай каже, хай / нехай кажуть), пити (пий, пийте, хай / нехай п'є, хай / нехай п'ють), боротися (борися, боріться, хай / нехай бореться, хай / нехай борються)."

Mapping file description (line 222): "Complete imperative — 1st person plural (читаймо), 3rd person (хай/нехай)." Required patterns: `хай`, `нехай`.

**Alignment**: Module covers exactly what the Standard requires at A2: 3rd person forms via хай/нехай (both singular and plural), plus introduces the 1st person plural (-мо/-імо suffix), which the textbook evidence confirms is taught alongside the Standard examples.

**Textbook corroboration** (Avramenko Grade 7, tier 1 — highest trust):
> "Зразок. Несу — несімо, неси, несіть, хай несе, хай несуть."

This exact paradigm (1pl -мо + 2sg/pl + хай 3sg/pl) is the canonical teaching model used in Ukrainian schools.

**Important textbook finding** (Avramenko Grade 11 §17): The "Спостереження" exercise explicitly flags «Давай виключим / Давай поговорим» as **morph norm violations** — these «давай + 1pl present» constructions are taught as errors to correct. This is a direct hook for the learner traps section.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Борщ as national symbol** (Wikipedia, Ukrainian): Борщ is described as "основна традиційна перша страва української кухні," a "символ української кулінарної культури і достатку." UNESCO inscribed Ukrainian борщ-making as Intangible Cultural Heritage in 2022. This anchors the practical section: giving cooking instructions mixes all three persons of imperative (додайте → 2pl; ходімо → 1pl; хай кипить → 3sg) naturally and authentically.

2. **«Будьмо!» in literary context** (Glazova Grade 11 textbook): The textbook includes the verse «Шануймося, єднаймося та будьмо! Тримаймося родинного стебла!» — confirming будьмо functions as a call to collective identity and presence, not merely a drinking toast. The Grade 11 poetry context gives cultural depth: it is used with шануймося (respect each other) and єднаймося (unite), reinforcing the "social glue" framing in the plan.

**Note**: Wikipedia has no separate «Будьмо» article. The cultural evidence comes from textbook literary sources, which is strong (tier 1–2 school corpora).

## Common Learner Errors

1. **«Давай + 1pl present» Russicism** → «Ходімо / Підімо / Читаймо»  
   Avramenko Grade 11 explicitly marks «Давай виключим / поговорим» as morph norm violations. English-L1 learners compound this by importing "let's" + infinitive logic. The correct Ukrainian 1pl imperative uses the -мо/-іймо/-аймо suffix directly.

2. **нехай ≠ негатив від хай** → both mean "let" and are interchangeable  
   Learners infer from the prefix *не-* that нехай negates хай. Drill: «Хай іде = Нехай іде» — same meaning, хай slightly more colloquial, нехай slightly more formal/literary.

3. **Using хай/нехай with 1pl forms** → «хай ходимо» ❌ → «ходімо» ✅  
   хай/нехай are 3rd person particles only. The 1st person plural imperative uses the -мо suffix on the verb itself. Confusing these is a structural error, not just a register slip.

4. **Negative 3rd person placement** → «хай не читає» ✅ vs «не хай читає» ❌  
   The particle хай/нехай cannot be split from the verb by не; не attaches directly before the verb form.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Paradigm table is essential**: State Standard gives 4 verbs × (2sg, 2pl, 3sg, 3pl) = 16 forms. Present them in a clean table. The borotysya example adds the reflexive (-ся) which confirms the suffix attaches before -ся.
- **No IPA at A2**: Plan mentions IPA in the plan file vocabulary_hints, but A2 quick-ref explicitly forbids IPA/phonetic brackets. Content writer should drop IPA notation from examples and use stress marks only.
- **будьмо cultural depth**: Frame it as a call to collective presence (шануймося та будьмо — textbook literary source), not only as a drinking phrase.
- **Genitive with бажати**: «бажаю здоров'я, щастя, успіхів» — Genitive is appropriate at A2 (fully in scope). Keep examples concrete.
- **"Let it be" — нехай буде**: This is a near-universal Ukrainian expression; tie it to the song/cultural register but keep language A2-concrete.
- **Suffix variants -мо vs -ймо vs -імо**: Glazova Grade 11 documents when each is used (after й → -ймо; after consonants b/p/v/m → -мо; after softened д/т/з/с → -ьмо). For A2, teach the most common patterns (ходімо, читаймо, будьмо) and note variants exist — don't overload.
