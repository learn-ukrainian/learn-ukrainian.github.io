# Research Notes: Покупки та послуги (Shopping & Services)

**Track**: Core A
**Module**: A2 M53 (shopping-services)
**Researched**: 2026-02-08
**Level**: A2 (Elementary / Pre-Intermediate)

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **Державний стандарт української мови (ДСТУ 2024)**, the topics of shopping and services are core components of the A2 level syllabus, spanning both thematic catalogs and communicative intentions:

*   **§ 3.9. Купівля (закупи)**: Includes "магазини і ринки; споживчі товари; одиниці ваги й об’єму; гроші." (Catalog B: Thematic Catalog).
*   **§ 3.11. Послуги**: Specifically lists "банк; пошта; бібліотека; спортзал, басейн; таксі."
*   **§ 1.4.1.2.1. Діалогічне мовлення**: Requirement to be able to "вступати в діалог й отримувати послуги в магазині, банку, лікарні, на пошті, у громадському транспорті" and "замовити послугу в ресторані, готелі, квитковій касі, таксі та скасувати замовлену послугу."
*   **§ 4.2.2.4. Знахідний відмінок**: Use for the direct object of shopping (*купити хліб*) and prices.
*   **§ 4.1.3. Засоби милозвучності**: Correct use of *у/в* and *і/й* in shopping lists and locations (e.g., *в аптеці*, *хліб і молоко*).

## 3. Cultural Hook: Digital Convenience & Logistics

1.  **Digital Payment Dominance**: Ukraine is one of the world leaders in contactless and digital payments. Even in small kiosks or markets, many vendors accept transfers via **Monobank** or **Privat24** by phone number or QR code if a terminal is missing. The phrase *"Можна на картку?"* (Can I pay to the card?) is a staple of modern informal shopping.
2.  **The "Nova Poshta" Phenomenon**: **Нова Пошта (Nova Poshta)** is a private postal service that has become a symbol of efficiency in Ukraine. It is used for everything from Vinted-style shopping to sending supplies to the front. Knowing how to "pick up a parcel" (*забрати посилку*) or "give the number" (*назвати номер*) is an essential survival skill for an expat in Ukraine.
3.  **Thematic Supermarkets**: Large chains like **Сільпо (Silpo)** often have unique, high-concept designs (e.g., Steampunk, GTA, Forest theme). Shopping in Ukraine is often treated as a visual experience in modern cities.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

*   **Aspectual Nuance**: Students at this level have learned verbal aspect. Shopping is the perfect sandbox for this: *Я купую яблука щодня* (Imperfective/Habit) vs. *Я хочу купити це яблуко* (Perfective/Single result).
*   **Instrumental vs. Accusative**: Remind students that payment *method* uses the Instrumental (*карткою*), while the *object* or *reason* for payment uses *за* + Accusative (*платити за каву*).
*   **Politeness in Imperatives**: While the student knows the imperative (*Дайте*), emphasize using the plural/formal form and adding *будь ласка* to avoid sounding rude in a service context.
*   **Price Agreement**: Numbers like 2, 3, 4 with *гривні* vs. 5-20 with *гривень* (Genitive plural) is a recurring difficulty that needs drilling in context.

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope
*   **Shopping/Service Nouns**: Standard stores, common services (post, hair, repair).
*   **Verbal Aspect**: Using *купувати/купити* and *замовляти/замовити* correctly.
*   **Instrumental of Means**: Payment methods (*карткою, готівкою, телефоном*).
*   **Direct/Reported Speech**: Asking for help or repeating instructions from a service provider.
*   **Numerals**: Handling prices up to thousands and basic agreement with currency.

### OUT Scope
*   **Consumer Law Terminology**: Words like *права споживачів* (consumer rights) or *гарантійне зобов'язання* (warranty obligation).
*   **Complex Financial Terms**: Interest rates, credit terms, or inflation adjustments.
*   **Passive Voice**: Avoiding "Services are provided" (*Послуги надаються*) in favor of "We provide services" (*Ми надаємо послуги*).
*   **Participles**: Adjectives like *куплений* (bought) or *вибраний* (chosen) should be avoided in favor of relative clauses (*який я купив*).
