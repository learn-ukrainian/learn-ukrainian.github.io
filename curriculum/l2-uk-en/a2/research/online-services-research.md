# Research Notes: Online Services

**Track**: Core A
**Module**: A2 M69 (Online Services)
**Level**: A2 (Pre-Intermediate)
**Focus**: Digital services, e-governance, delivery apps, and online banking.

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

The **Державний стандарт української мови як іноземної (2024)** for the **A2 level** emphasizes the ability to interact with the service sector, including digital platforms.

**Section Reference**: §3.2. "Комунікативні наміри та теми спілкування".
**Topic**: "Побутова сфера. Сфера обслуговування. Послуги (традиційні та електронні)".

**Quote**: 
> «Здобувач вміє використовувати електронні сервіси для задоволення повсякденних потреб: замовляти товари та послуги онлайн, здійснювати реєстрацію в мобільних додатках, розуміти алгоритм оплати та відстежувати статус замовлення».

## 3. Cultural Hook

### Diia (Дія): The State in a Smartphone
Ukraine is a global leader in e-governance. The **"Дія"** app allows citizens to have digital versions of their ID cards, driver's licenses, and passports that are legally identical to physical ones.
- **Fact**: Ukraine was the first country in the world to give digital passports the same legal status as paper or plastic ones. 
- **Lesson Insight**: Terms like "Шеринг документів" (Document sharing) and "Електронний підпис" (Electronic signature) are part of daily life.

### The "Nova Poshta" Culture
**Nova Poshta** (Private delivery service) is iconic for its speed. It is often said that "Nova Poshta works faster than the speed of light."
- **Fact**: Their network of **"Поштомати"** (automatic lockers) is ubiquitous, even in small villages.
- **Lesson Insight**: Learning how to "Відстежити посилку" (Track a parcel) and use a "Номер накладної" (Tracking number) is a survival skill in Ukraine.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

### "Застосунок" vs "Додаток"
In modern Ukrainian tech UI, **"Застосунок"** is the preferred term for "application/app," whereas **"Додаток"** can also mean an "addition" or "supplement." Students should be encouraged to use "застосунок."

### Prepositional Logic: On vs. In
- Use **"в / у"** for mobile applications: *у додатку*, *у застосунку*.
- Use **"на"** for websites: *на сайті*.
- Use **"в / у"** for personal accounts: *в особистому кабінеті*.

### Interface Imperatives
Apps use the **Imperative mood** for buttons. This is a great chance to review the forms learned in A2 M23.
- *Замовте зараз* (Order now)
- *Оплатіть замовлення* (Pay for the order)
- *Зареєструйтеся* (Register)

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope (Based on Prior Knowledge)
- **Aspect Pairs**: Crucial for digital actions. "Я замовляю" (process) vs "Я замовив" (result).
- **Imperatives**: For navigating app instructions and buttons.
- **Dative Case**: For paying *to* someone or something (*оплатити сервісу*).
- **Numbers + Nouns**: For prices (*п'ятсот гривень*) and delivery times (*два дні*).
- **Relative Clauses**: "The app **that** I downloaded" (*Додаток, який я завантажив*).

### OUT of Scope
- **Passive Voice**: "The parcel was delivered" (*Посилка була доставлена*). Instead, use "Посилку доставили" (Impersonal) or "Кур'єр доставив посилку" (Active).
- **Complex Tech Jargon**: Avoid backend terms like "API", "бек-енд", "хмарне сховище" unless they are direct loanwords used in daily life.
- **Advanced Word Formation**: Nouns derived from complex prefixed verbs that don't follow the high-frequency patterns.
