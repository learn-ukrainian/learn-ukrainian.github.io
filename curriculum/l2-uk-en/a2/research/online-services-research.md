## Cultural Hooks

1. **Дія (Держава у смартфоні):** Ukrainian government digital service launched by the Ministry of Digital Transformation under President Zelenskyy. As of 2024: 21+ million users. Дія was the first app in the world to grant digital documents (passport, driver's license, vehicle registration) full legal equivalence to physical documents. Source: Wikipedia — [Дія (сервіс)](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%94%D1%96%D1%8F_(%D1%81%D0%B5%D1%80%D0%B2%D1%96%D1%81))

2. **Monobank (кіт і банка):** Ukraine's first fully app-based neobank, founded 2017, operating as a subsidiary of Universal Bank. No physical branches — 100% mobile. Famous for its cat mascot and the "банка" (jar) collective fundraising feature that became a social phenomenon — especially for wartime charity drives and community campaigns. Source: Wikipedia — [Monobank](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monobank)

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Замовлення gender (feminine confusion):** Learners frequently assign feminine gender due to the -ня ending (cf. пісня, земля). Correct: замовлення is **neuter** → *моє замовлення*, *нове замовлення*. Why: The -ення/-ання suffix consistently signals neuter deverbal nouns in Ukrainian (навчання, завдання, замовлення).

2. **Кошик case alternation (Acc vs. Loc):** Learners collapse the case distinction — writing *у кошику* when meaning "add to cart" (motion → Accusative: *в кошик*). Correct: *додати товар в кошик* (motion/direction) vs. *товар у кошику* (static location). Why: The physical/digital metaphor is unfamiliar; learners default to one form.

3. **Скинути на картку — register/verb confusion:** Learners may reach for more formal equivalents (*перерахувати*, *переказати*) in casual peer-transfer contexts, producing unnatural register. The colloquial *скинути на картку* is the standard everyday phrase in Ukraine for mobile P2P transfers and should be drilled as a fixed collocation. Also note: *скинути* (pf) vs. *кидати* (impf) aspect awareness needed.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **доставка gender:** feminine noun — *безкоштовна доставка*, *служба доставки* (Genitive: -и)
- **додаток vs. застосунок:** Both are correct Ukrainian terms for "app." Дія's own materials use *застосунок*; Monobank uses *додаток*. For A2 simplicity, pick **додаток** as primary (higher GRAC frequency: 14.27 IPM) and note *застосунок* as a synonym once.
- **Decolonized framing:** Ukraine's digital infrastructure (Дія, Monobank, Uklon, Privat24) is world-leading and entirely Ukrainian-developed. Frame as Ukrainian innovation, not as adoption of external models. Never frame as "similar to [Russian app]."
- **Imperative mood (§4.2.3.2):** UI commands use 2nd person plural imperatives — *Натисніть, Оберіть, Підтвердіть, Введіть* — because app interfaces address the user formally. Contrast with informal spoken commands (*натисни, обери*) in casual dialogue.
- **Collocation density:** The plan's Практика section (475 words) should anchor on 5 minimal Acc/Loc pairs for кошик — use real app UI strings where possible (e.g., from Uklon, Glovo, Privat24).
- **банка cultural note:** In the Розповідь section, the Monobank "банка" (jar) is a genuine Ukrainian digital philanthropy phenomenon — wartime fundraising via the *банка* feature became widespread 2022–present. This is A2-appropriate cultural immersion with strong engagement potential.
