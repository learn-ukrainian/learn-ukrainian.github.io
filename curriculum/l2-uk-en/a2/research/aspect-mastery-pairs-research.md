# Дослідження: Aspect Mastery — Pairs

## State Standard Reference
§4.3.2: "Видові пари дієслів: робити – зробити, ділити – поділити, писати – написати, виходити – вийти, забувати – забути."
Alignment: This module directly addresses the A2 requirement for mastering the formation and usage of fundamental aspectual verb pairs, ensuring learners can automatically select the correct aspect in contextual speech.

## Cultural Hooks
1. UNESCO Intangible Heritage: Ukrainian Borscht making is a recognized cultural heritage, perfect for demonstrating sequential perfective actions (купив, помив, приготував).
2. Toloka (Толока): A traditional communal labor practice in Ukraine, often used to build a "khata" (house), which highlights the difference between the ongoing process of building and the completed result.

## Common Learner Errors
1. Prefix Overgeneralization → *змити instead of помити, *змалювати instead of намалювати. Why: Learners assume "з-" is the universal perfectivizing prefix.
2. Present-Perfective Trap → *Я напишу зараз instead of Я пишу. Why: Learners confuse the perfective future (напишу) with a present continuous action because they look similar to English present tenses.
3. Duration vs. Result → *Я купив молоко дві години instead of Я купував молоко дві години. Why: Misunderstanding that perfective cannot be used with duration markers.

## Notes for Content Writing
- Decolonized framing: Present Ukrainian aspect as a logical, rhythmic system of precision. Highlight how prefixes add specific semantic nuances rather than just being arbitrary markers.
- Ensure 50-60% immersion (Band 1): Use English for explaining the taxonomy of prefixes/suffixes, but use Ukrainian for all dialogues and examples.
- Include syntactic roles (підмет, присудок, додаток) if discussing sentence structure.
