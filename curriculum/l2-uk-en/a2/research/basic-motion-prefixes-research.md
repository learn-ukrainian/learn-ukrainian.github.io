# Дослідження: Basic Motion Prefixes

## State Standard Reference

**Primary: §4.2.2.4.2** (lines 1298–1302):
> "в, у, на (кінцевий пункт руху суб'єкта): Діти їдуть в Одесу. Ми зайшли в кімнату. Туристи піднялися на гору"

**Secondary: §4.2.3.1** (lines 1341–1359): Conjugation of base motion verbs — іти (іду, ідеш…), їхати (їду, їдеш…), ходити (ходжу, ходиш…), їздити (їжджу, їздиш…). These are the stems that prefixes attach to.

**Tertiary: §4.3.2** (lines 1380–1381): Aspect pairs — the Standard explicitly cites "виходити – вийти" as a canonical example: "виходити – вийти, забувати – забути."

**Alignment note**: The full prefixed motion verb system is formally introduced at B1 §4.3.8 ("ви-, при-, за-, від-"). At A2, this module teaches the в-/ви- pair as the first prefixed motion pattern, grounded in §4.2.2.4.2 (accusative destination prepositions) and §4.3.2 (aspect pairs). This is the correct pedagogical bridge: A2 introduces the concept via two high-frequency prefixes; B1 expands to the full system. The module should frame itself as "first step into prefixes" rather than claiming to cover the full prefix system.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Київський метрополітен** (Wikipedia verified): 52 stations, 3 lines, 69.6 km. Operates daily 05:30–23:00; also functions as air raid shelter during alerts. Вхід/Вихід signs appear at every station entrance — ubiquitous real-world exposure for learners. The metro is a practical, high-stakes context where learners encounter these exact lexical items.

2. **Поріг (threshold) folk tradition**: The threshold (поріг) is a culturally charged spatial boundary in Ukrainian folk tradition, documented in multiple ethnographic sources. The prohibition against greeting, shaking hands, or passing objects через поріг (across the threshold) reflects the in/out binary as a sacred divide between domestic and exterior space. Referenced in sources on традиційне українське весілля and поховальна обрядовість — the threshold marks the transition between family-inside and world-outside. This makes поріг an authentic cultural anchor for the в-/ви- semantic contrast.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Wrong preposition with ви-**: "*Я виходжу в офісу*" → correct: "Я виходжу **з** офісу" — Learners transfer the Accusative destination preposition (в) to the exit verb. The exit vector requires з + Genitive. GRAC collocations confirm: виходити collocates overwhelmingly with з (90,404 instances), not в.

2. **Foot vs. vehicle confusion**: "*Машина входить у гараж*" → correct: "Машина **в'їжджає** у гараж" — Ukrainian strictly separates foot-motion verbs (входити/виходити) from vehicle-motion verbs (в'їжджати/виїжджати). This distinction has no parallel in English ("enter" covers both), making it a consistent source of A2 errors.

3. **Aspect confusion**: Using imperfective where perfective is required — "*Я входив до кімнати*" (I was entering the room — correct for in-progress/habitual) vs. "*Я увійшов до кімнати*" (I entered the room — correct for completed single event). Learners default to imperfective because it feels "safer."

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Preposition frame is the core grammar**: В-/ви- prefix meaning only makes sense paired with the preposition that follows. Teach the combos as units: **входити в/до** (Acc/Gen) vs. **виходити з** (Gen). Don't teach prefixes in isolation.
- **Aspect pedagogy**: §4.3.2 gives виходити–вийти as the State Standard example. Lead with this pair; then add входити–увійти. The perfective form увійти (not ввійти) is the standard form — note this spelling: у+в+ійти = увійти (not double-в in standard usage, though ввійти exists as a variant).
- **Frequency asymmetry matters**: вихід (45/million) and виходити (26/million) are significantly more frequent than вхід (11/million) and входити (5/million). Lead with вихід/виходити in examples — learners will encounter these first in the wild.
- **Grade 5 Zabolotnyi (tier 1, 2023)** uses a prefix wheel exercise (#121) where learners add prefixes (including в- and ви-) to the verb бігти to derive new meanings — this is the school-textbook pedagogical pattern for prefix introduction. The module can mirror this "stem + prefix = new direction" logic.
- **Apostrophe rule**: в'їжджати, в'їхати — the apostrophe (not soft sign) is required before я, ю, є, ї after prefixes ending in a consonant. Content should show the correct spelling explicitly.
- **Don't fabricate a specific §4.2.3.1 sub-section for prefixed verbs at A2** — the Standard doesn't have one. The correct claim is that this module operationalizes §4.2.2.4.2 (Acc prepositions for motion) + §4.3.2 (aspect pairs) and prepares for B1 §4.3.8.
