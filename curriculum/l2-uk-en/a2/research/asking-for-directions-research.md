## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

The Ukrainian State Standard (Державний стандарт української мови, 2024) defines the requirements for level A2 regarding spatial orientation and movement.

**Relevant Sections:**
- **§1.1.2.1.1 (Monologue Speech):** "вказівки, як дістатися з одного місця в інше" (instructions on how to get from one place to another).
- **§3.5 (Environment):** "Середовище перебування (місто чи село): ... напрямок руху (прямо, ліворуч, праворуч)" (Environment (city or village): ... direction of movement (straight, left, right)).
- **§3.6 (Travel):** "Подорожі: ... основні дії на позначення руху: іти – ходити, їхати – їздити" (Travel: ... basic actions indicating movement: to go [on foot] – to go [by vehicle]).
- **§4.2.2.5.2 (Morphology/Instrumental):** "над, під, перед, за, між (простір, шлях руху об’єкта)" (above, under, in front of, behind, between - space, path of object's movement).

## 3. Cultural Hook

1. **The "Ви" Barrier & Addressing Strangers:** In Ukraine, addressing a stranger with "Ти" is considered very rude. Learners must use the formal "Ви" and polite openers like *"Вибачте, будь ласка..."* or *"Перепрошую..."*. When addressing someone specifically, *"Пане"* (Mr.) or *"Пані"* (Ms.) are the standard polite forms.
2. **Navigation by Landmarks (Орієнтири):** Unlike many Western cultures that rely heavily on street names and numbers, Ukrainians frequently give directions based on landmarks. You will often hear: *"Біля АТБ"* (Near the ATB supermarket), *"За церквою"* (Behind the church), or *"Біля пам'ятника"* (Near the monument). This reflects a communal urban memory where landmarks serve as primary nodes.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

- **Imperative Forms:** This module is the primary playground for the imperative mood (learned in M23). Students should practice both singular (*поверни*) and plural/formal (*поверніть*) forms, but the focus should be on the formal/plural as it is used with strangers.
- **Motion Verb Aspect:** A subtle but common error is using the imperfective for specific turn commands. Correct: *"Поверніть праворуч"* (Perfective - do it once now). Use imperfective for general movement: *"Ідіть прямо"* (Imperfective - keep going).
- **Case Mastery:**
    - **Genitive (Родовий):** Used after "до" (destination) and "біля/навпроти" (location).
    - **Accusative (Знахідний):** Used after "на/в" for direction (*на вулицю*, *в центр*).
    - **Locative (Місцевий):** Used for location (*на вулиці*, *в центрі*).
- **Listening Challenge:** In reality, the "asking" is easy; the "understanding" is hard. The module must include practice for recognizing sequence markers like *спочатку* (at first), *потім* (then), and *далі* (further).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

Based on the student's prior knowledge (Full A2 grammar, motion prefixes, complex sentences):

- **IN SCOPE:**
    - **Motion Prefixes:** *дійти до*, *зайти в*, *перейти через*. The student has already mastered these (M36-38).
    - **Imperatives:** Full paradigm (M23).
    - **All 7 Cases:** Singular and basic plural agreement.
    - **Complex Sentences:** Purpose clauses (*щоб*) and time clauses (*коли, після того як*).
    - **Conditionals:** *"Якби я мав карту..."* (M22).

- **OUT OF SCOPE:**
    - **Participles:** Avoid "the building standing there" (*будівля, що стоїть...* is fine, but not *стояча будівля*).
    - **Passive Voice:** *"Вулиця називається"* is in, but complex passive constructions are out.
    - **Gerunds:** Avoid *"Йдучи по вулиці..."* (While walking down the street).
    - **Advanced Toponymy:** Technical geographical terms.
