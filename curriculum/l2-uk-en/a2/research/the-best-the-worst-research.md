# Research Notes: A2 M19 "Найкраще, найгірше, найбільше: Світ рекордів"

**Track**: a2
**Module**: the-best-the-worst
**Researched**: 2026-02-08

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **Державний стандарт української мови (ДСТУ 2024)** for the **A2 (Waystage/Елементарний)** level, learners must master the morphology of adjectives, specifically the degrees of comparison.

> **§ Морфологія. Прикметник:**
> "Творення та вживання ступенів порівняння прикметників: вищий (проста і складена форми: *добріший, більш добрий*) та найвищий (проста і складена форми: *найдобріший, найбільш добрий*). Узгодження прикметників у вищому та найвищому ступенях із іменниками у роді, числі та відмінку."

For this module (M19), the focus is on the **Simple Superlative (Проста форма найвищого ступеня)**, formed by adding the prefix **най-** to the comparative form.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

At the A2 level, vocabulary should focus on common descriptors used in records, geography, and daily preferences.

### High-Frequency (Essential)
- **Найкращий** (best) — Extremely high frequency in daily conversation.
- **Найгірший** (worst) — Common for expressing opinions.
- **Найбільший** (biggest/largest) — Essential for describing cities, objects, or crowds.
- **Найменший** (smallest) — Core descriptor.
- **Найцікавіший** (most interesting) — High frequency for reviews and hobbies.

### Medium-Frequency (Topic Specific)
- **Найвищий** (highest/tallest) — Geography and architecture.
- **Найшвидший** (fastest) — Sports and transport.
- **Найстаріший** (oldest) — History and family.
- **Найдорожчий** (most expensive) — Shopping and travel.

### Common Collocations (A2 Level)
- **Найкращий друг** (best friend)
- **Найвище дерево** (tallest tree)
- **Найбільше місто** (biggest city)
- **Найцікавіша книга** (most interesting book)
- **У світі** (in the world) — Used frequently with superlatives.

## 3. Cultural Hook

To engage learners, use verified Ukrainian world records:

- **The Deepest Metro Station**: The "Arsenalna" station in Kyiv is the deepest in the world at **105.5 meters** (найглибша станція метро у світі). This is a point of pride and a common trivia fact.
- **The Longest Musical Instrument**: The **Trembita** (Трембіта), a wooden horn from the Hutsul region, is the world's longest musical instrument, reaching up to 4 meters (найдовший музичний інструмент). It is a symbol of the Ukrainian Carpathians.
- **Kharkiv's Freedom Square**: One of the largest city squares in Europe (**найбільша площа в Україні**).

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

### Key Differences from English
- **Prefix vs. Word**: English uses "the most" or "-est". Ukrainian uses a single prefix **най-** attached to the comparative.
- **Agreement**: Unlike English (where "best" never changes), Ukrainian superlatives must agree with the noun in **Gender, Number, and Case**. Since students know all cases, drills should include sentences like "Я живу в *найбільшому* місті" (Locative).
- **The "The" Factor**: There is no definite article "the" in Ukrainian. Learners often look for a word to translate "the" in "the best"; they must be taught that the context and the superlative form itself carry this weight.

### Common Learner Errors
- **Using "най-" with positive degree**: Students might say *найдобрий* instead of *найкращий* (incorrectly skipping the comparative step).
- **Double comparison**: Mixing analytic and synthetic forms (e.g., *найбільш кращий* instead of *найкращий*).
- **Case mismatches**: Forgetting to decline the adjective when it isn't in the Nominative case.

### Teaching Sequence
1. Review Comparative (M18).
2. Introduce the prefix **най-**.
3. Practice with irregulars (гарний -> кращий -> **найкращий**).
4. Apply to records/geography (World and Ukraine).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### In Scope
- **Simple Superlatives**: Prefix `най-` + comparative adjective.
- **Irregular Superlatives**: Focus on high-frequency ones (кращий, гірший, більший, менший).
- **Full Declension**: Using superlatives in all 6 cases (N, A, G, L, D, I).
- **Prepositional Phrases**: "Найкращий **у** групі", "Найбільший **у** світі".

### Out of Scope
- **Complex Superlatives**: Prefixes like **якнай-** or **щонай-** (*якнайкращий*) are B1+ material.
- **Analytic Superlatives**: While mentioned in the standard, the focus at A2.1 should be the synthetic form. *Найбільш* + adjective is often used with long/technical words but can be postponed to avoid cognitive overload.
- **Adverbial Superlatives**: "Він бігає **найшвидше**" (the patterns are identical, but the focus here is on modifying nouns/adjectives).
- **Comparative with "ніж/за"**: Focus solely on the "Best of All" (superlative) rather than "Better than X" (comparative) in this specific module.
