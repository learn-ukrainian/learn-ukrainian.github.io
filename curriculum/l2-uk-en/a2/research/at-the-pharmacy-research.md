# Дослідження: At the Pharmacy

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.2.2: "Родовий відмінок: ... Із прийменниками:"
Alignment: This module focuses on the usage of the Genitive case with the preposition "від" to express the purpose of medication against ailments (e.g., "ліки від кашлю"), which aligns with the State Standard's requirement for Genitive case usage with prepositions at the A2 level.

## Cultural Hooks
1. Since 2022, Ukraine has widely implemented the «е-рецепт» (electronic prescription) system via SMS for antibiotics and other prescription drugs to regulate dispensing and modernize healthcare (Source: moz.gov.ua).
2. Many pharmacies in Ukraine operate as a «чергова аптека» (duty pharmacy), remaining open 24/7 for nighttime emergencies, identifiable by a prominent green cross. Additionally, there is a strong cultural preference for «фітотерапія» (herbal remedies) as a first line of defense for minor ailments.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Ліки для кашлю** → **Ліки від кашлю** — English speakers directly translate "medicine for a cough" using "для", but Ukrainian requires "від" (against/from) + Genitive.
2. **Це ліки** → **Ці ліки** — Learners treat "ліки" as a singular noun, but it is pluralia tantum (always plural) and requires plural agreement for adjectives and demonstratives.
3. **Лікарство** → **Ліки** — Using the Russianism "лікарство" instead of the standard Ukrainian word "ліки".

## Notes for Content Writing
- Maintain the decolonized framing: explain the pluralia tantum nature of "ліки" as an inherent feature of Ukrainian grammar without drawing comparisons to Russian equivalents.
- Ensure strict adherence to A2 immersion band 3 (75-90% Ukrainian). Grammar rules can be scaffolded in English if complex, but explanations of vocabulary, examples, and all practice should be in Ukrainian.
- The module must reach a minimum of 3000 words. Expand on cultural context, provide robust example sets for each symptom/medication pair, and include thorough dialogues to naturally meet the target.
- Explicitly contrast formal language found on medicine boxes ("приймати", "застосування") with colloquial daily speech ("пити таблетки").
