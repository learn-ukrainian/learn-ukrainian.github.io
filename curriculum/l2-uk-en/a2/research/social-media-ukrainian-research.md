## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Telegram Phenomenon**: In Ukraine, Telegram is not just for messaging; it is a critical lifeline. It serves as the primary news source for the vast majority of Ukrainians and is heavily relied upon for instant air raid alerts and official government updates (e.g., channels of state officials or the Air Force).
2. **Instagram as the New Storefront**: Many Ukrainian micro-businesses (from independent coffee roasters to local ceramic artists) bypass traditional websites entirely. They operate exclusively through Instagram, making the phrase «Пишіть у дірект» (Write to DM) a fundamental part of the modern Ukrainian shopping experience.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Сайт vs. Сторінка** → Learners often call an Instagram profile a "сайт" (website) due to false-friend interference. The correct term is "сторінка" (page/profile). 
2. **Case usage with "підписатися"** → Using the Locative instead of Accusative. Correct: «підписатися на сторінку» (Accusative). Incorrect: «підписатися на сторінці» (Locative).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing**: Emphasize Ukraine's vibrant, independent digital ecosystem. Focus on platforms globally used in Ukraine (Instagram, Telegram, TikTok, X/Twitter) rather than Russian networks (which are banned).
- **Immersion Target**: This is A2 Band 3 (M67). The module must be almost entirely in Ukrainian (75-90% immersion). English should only be used in vocabulary translation tables or for brief, complex clarifications.
- **Tone**: Professional yet encouraging, focusing on practical survival and integration in the Ukrainian digital sphere. Use the angular quotes («...») for all UI terms.
