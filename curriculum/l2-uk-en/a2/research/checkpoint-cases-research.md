# Дослідження: Checkpoint — Cases

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.2: "Catalogue C — Linguistic competence (morphology)"
- **§4.2.2.1 Nominative**: Identification ("Його звати Петро...") and interest ("Мені подобається Ігор").
- **§4.2.2.2 Genitive**: Time/date ("десятого березня"), negation ("Не вивчив вірша"), and prepositions (з, до, для, біля).
- **§4.2.2.3 Dative**: Recipients ("Подарувати книжку студентові") and age ("Дідусеві вісімдесят років").
- **§4.2.2.4 Accusative**: Direct objects and motion (в/у, на).
- **§4.2.2.5 Instrumental**: Profession/status ("бути програмувальником"), instruments ("пише ручкою"), and prepositions (з, над, під, перед, за, між).
- **§4.2.2.6 Locative**: Location (в/у, на) and path (по).
- **§4.2.2.6 [sic] Vocative**: Address forms ("Пане Юрію", "Бабусю").

Alignment: This module provides a comprehensive review (Checkpoint) of all seven cases defined for the A2 level, specifically integrating them into "Services" (Post & Bank) as per Catalogue B.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Stamps as Currency (1918)**: In 1918, the Ukrainian People's Republic (UNR) issued postage stamps that were used as money due to a shortage of coins. They were printed on thick paper with a note on the back saying they circulate equally with coins.
2. **Digital Banking Leadership**: Ukraine is a global leader in digital banking. Monobank (mobile-only) and the government's "Diia" app allow Ukrainians to open bank accounts and share documents for services instantly via smartphone, a concept often ahead of many Western banking systems.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Nom. instead of Voc.** → "Сергій, привіт!" instead of "Сергію, привіт!" — English lacks a distinct vocative case, so learners default to the dictionary form (Nominative).
2. **Nom. after Negation** → "У мене немає гроші" instead of "У мене немає грошей" — Learners forget that negation requires the Genitive case.
3. **Nom. for Instruments** → "Я пишу ручка" instead of "Я пишу ручкою" — Learners omit the Instrumental ending when describing the tool used for an action.

## Notes for Content Writing
- Focus on the **Service Interaction** dialogues (Post & Bank) to provide a practical "Mixed Case" environment.
- Emphasize the **Dative case** for age and expressions of feeling (мені холодно/цікаво) as these are high-frequency A2 functions.
- Ensure the **Self-assessment checklist** includes a "Preposition + Case" matching section, as this is a major friction point identified in research.
