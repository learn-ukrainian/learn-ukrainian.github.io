# Дослідження: Checkpoint — Cases

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.2.1–§4.2.2.6 (A2 case usage — all 7 cases):
- Nominative §4.2.2.1: "expanded: subject in passive, comparative constructions"
- Genitive §4.2.2.2: "without preposition + with prepositions (без, від, для, до, з/із, після, біля, коло)"
- Dative §4.2.2.3: "indirect object, impersonal constructions (мені холодно)"
- Accusative §4.2.2.4: "expanded prepositions (під, над, між — with motion)"
- Instrumental §4.2.2.5: "means, manner, agent; with prepositions (з/із, за, між, над, перед, під)"
- Locative §4.2.2.6: "expanded location contexts" / Vocative: "expanded address forms"

Alignment: This checkpoint consolidates all 7 A2 case functions, with special focus on preposition–case matching (§4.2.2.2–§4.2.2.5), error-correction drills, and practical service contexts (§3 Catalogue B: послуги).

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Monobank & Diia app**: Ukraine is a global leader in digital banking and e-government. Monobank (2017) became the most downloaded banking app in Ukraine within two years. Diia allows citizens to show digital passports — accepted legally since 2020. Practice: *Я відкрив рахунок у Монобанку. Де відділення пошти?* — natural case usage in a context learners find impressive and modern.

2. **Пошта as civic institution**: Nova Poshta (Нова пошта) is omnipresent in Ukrainian daily life — used by millions for parcel delivery, online shopping pickups, and money transfers. Postal vocabulary (відправити, посилка, відділення) appears constantly in real interactions and is immediately useful for learners.

3. **UNR Stamps as Currency (1918)**: The Ukrainian People's Republic issued postage stamps that circulated as coins during the currency shortage of 1918. This historically grounded narrative (stamps = money, пошта + рахунок + гроші) lets the integration challenge use cases organically in a mini-story.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Nominative instead of Instrumental for tools/profession**: *Я пишу ручка* → *Я пишу ручкою*. Happens because English uses no special ending for instruments ("I write with a pen"). Drill: object-as-instrument transformations.

2. **Nominative instead of Genitive under negation**: *У мене немає гроші* → *У мене немає грошей*. English negation doesn't change noun form; Ukrainian mandatory negation shift to Genitive is invisible to L1 English speakers. Drill: У мене є/немає minimal pairs.

3. **Nominative instead of Vocative for direct address**: *Сергій, привіт!* → *Сергію, привіт!* English has no vocative; learners default to citation form. Particularly salient in Ukrainian where vocative is unmarked in speech rhythm.

4. **з (із) = Genitive vs. Instrumental confusion**: *розмовляти з другом* (Instrumental — accompaniment) vs. *приїхати з Києва* (Genitive — origin). Same preposition, two cases. Learners freeze; need explicit contrastive drilling.

## Teaching Strategy

- **Discovery exercise**: Present 6 sentences in Ukrainian — learners sort them into groups by which question each noun answers (Хто? Кого? Кому? Кого/Що? Ким? На чому?). No rule given first — learners discover the question-word → case connection themselves. Then reveal the case names. *TTT: Test → Teach → Test*.
- **Best context**: Service environment — post office queue (черга) and bank counter. "Я хочу відправити посилку Олені. Скільки це коштує?" covers Accusative (посилку) + Dative (Олені) + Nominative (це) in one natural utterance.
- **Contrastive pairs**: з + Instrumental vs. з + Genitive (same preposition, different case = different meaning); в/у + Accusative (motion: іти в магазин) vs. в/у + Locative (location: бути в магазині).

## Notes for Content Writing

- Module sequence is A2-013; this is a consolidation checkpoint — NO new grammar, only review and error correction.
- Immersion 50–60% (Core grammar band per A2 quick-ref). Grammar theory sections may use English for metalanguage.
- Keep sentence max 15 words; max 2 clauses.
- TTT pedagogy: start each case review with a test (discovery), then teach, then drill.
- The "Services and Digital Ukraine" section is the highest-engagement section — lead with Monobank/Diia to hook learners, then layer in case practice naturally.
- Error-correction activities (виправити помилку format) are especially effective for checkpoints — learners feel metacognitive momentum.
- Avoid all abstract nouns (стан, почуття, сутність) per A2 register rules.
