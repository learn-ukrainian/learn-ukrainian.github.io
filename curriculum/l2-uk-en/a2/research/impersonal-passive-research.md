# Дослідження: Impersonal Passive Forms (-но/-то Construction)

## State Standard Reference

**Direct A2 mapping: NOT FOUND for -но/-то.**

I read the full A2 verb section §4.2.3 (lines 1341–1365) and syntax §4.4 (lines 1383–1416). Neither mentions -но/-то impersonal passive forms. The A2 verb section covers indicative conjugation and imperative mood only.

The closest Standard reference is at **B2, §4.3.3** (lines 3409–3418): "Односкладні речення — безособові, інфінітивні, номінативні" ("One-member sentences — impersonal, infinitive, nominative"). This is the formal syntactic home of -но/-то.

**Pedagogical justification for A2 placement:** Ukrainian school textbooks (Grade 7, tier-1 sources: Літвінова 2024, Авраменко 2024) teach -но/-то as functional literacy — reading signs, labels, completed-action reports. A2 learners encounter these forms constantly in real-world Ukrainian. The module is framed as *receptive/functional* mastery at A2, with full productive mastery deferred to B1+. This is a defensible curriculum design choice, but content writing should acknowledge the B2 Standard anchor.

**Closest A2 Standard reference to cite:** §4.2.3.1 (indicative + aspect, lines 1341–1359) — the formation of -но/-то is aspect-gated (perfective only), which is introduced at this section. Cite this as the aspect prerequisite anchor, and note that the full syntactic treatment of -но/-то is formalized at B2 §4.3.3.

## Cultural Hooks

**1. "Зроблено в Україні" — National Brand (Wikipedia, verified)**
- The brand "Зроблено в Україні" was officially registered as a Ukrainian trade mark in **February 2024** by the Ministry of Economy (Мінекономіки).
- Under Soviet rule, Ukrainian goods were labeled "Сделано в СССР" / "Сделано в УССР" — the shift to "Зроблено в Україні" after 1991 was an act of economic and linguistic independence.
- Post-2022 (повномасштабне вторгнення), the brand gained explicit patriotic significance — buying Ukrainian-made goods became a form of wartime solidarity.
- The brand is used for both domestic market and export labeling.
- Linguistic note: "Зроблено" is the grammatically correct impersonal passive; alternatives seen on older labels ("Виготовлено в Україні", "Вироблено в Україні") also use -но/-то forms — excellent material for a sign-reading exercise.

**2. -но/-то as Ukrainian-language natural register (Авраменко Grade 11, 2019)**
- Textbook source (grade 11): "Речення правої колонки звучать природно, по-українськи. Пасивні дієприкметники здебільшого вживають у ролі означення… Безособові форми на -но, -то вживають замість пасивних дієприкметників, коли треба наголосити не на ознаці, а на дії."
- Key decolonization framing: -но/-то is the **natural Ukrainian construction** for reporting completed actions. Passive participial predicates (like "Ухвала прийнята") sound unnatural in Ukrainian and reflect calquing from other languages. This is a Ukrainian linguistic norm, not a borrowing.
- Grade 11 Глазова (2019): "Пасивні конструкції з дієслівними формами на -но, -то в українській мові використовують обмежено" — note that this refers to long passive constructions with expressed agents (з + instrumental), not to the impersonal -но/-то itself which is very common.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Nominative instead of Accusative with -но/-то** → **Correct: accusative object**
   - Error: *"Замовлення відправлений"* / *"Лист написаний"* (using nominative + adjective)
   - Correct: "Замовлення відправлено" / "Лист написано"
   - Why: Learners map -но/-то onto "is + adjective" structures from English ("the letter is written") and add a nominative noun + agreeing adjective. In Ukrainian, -но/-то takes an ACCUSATIVE object: "Написано лист" (the letter has been written — лист in accusative).

2. **Confusing -но/-то predicate with participial adjective agreement** → **-но/-то is invariable**
   - Error: *"Магазин закритий"* (participial adjective — describes the shop as a property) used when the intended meaning is "the shop is closed right now" (temporary state/completed action)
   - Correct: "Магазин закрито" for the impersonal passive state
   - Why: Both express a "closed" shop, but the participial adjective agrees in gender/number; -но/-то never changes form. Learners attempt gender agreement on -но/-то: *"замовлений відправлено"* or *"замовлення відправлена"*.

3. **Using imperfective verb base** → **Perfective only for standard -но/-то**
   - Error: *"читано книгу"* (from читати, imperfective) in a context requiring completed action
   - Correct: "прочитано книгу" (from прочитати, perfective)
   - Why: Imperfective -но/-то forms (читано, робленo) do exist in proverbs and folklore ("Що нашвидкуруч роблено, те скоро ламано" — Avramenko Grade 7 textbook), but they are archaic/literary. Standard modern Ukrainian uses perfective aspect for -но/-то. Learners coming from A2 aspect pairs (a2-073) need explicit reminders.

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **Accusative case in -но/-то sentences:** The object of -но/-то takes ACCUSATIVE, not nominative. "Написано лист" (лист = accusative of лист, same form as nominative for inanimate masculine — but "написано книгу", not *"написано книга"*). Content must model this clearly with at least 3 feminine-noun examples where the accusative -у ending is visible: "написано книгу", "закрито крамницю", "відправлено посилку".

2. **відкрито dual-entry warning:** When teaching "Магазин відкрито" (shop is open), explicitly contrast with "говорити відкрито" (adverb). The shop-sign context disambiguates this well.

3. **Decolonization frame for -но/-то:** Present the construction as the natural, idiomatic Ukrainian way to report completed actions — not as a "passive voice" borrowed from other traditions. Ukrainian uses -но/-то where English uses "it has been done" and where passive participle predicates sound unnatural in Ukrainian. The textbook (Авраменко 2019) provides the authoritative "sounds natural, sounds Ukrainian" framing.

4. **"Зроблено в Україні" post-2022 context:** The brand was officially registered in February 2024. The cultural-hook section should mention: (a) the Soviet labeling "Зроблено в УРСР" as a historical contrast; (b) the 2024 official registration; (c) post-2022 patriotic significance. Do NOT frame as "reclaiming from Russian influence" — frame positively as Ukrainian national branding.

5. **Grammar scope warning for A2:** Participles (§4.2.3 in B1 State Standard) are **forbidden at A2**. This module is explicitly about the *-но/-то form* — an invariable verbal form — NOT about passive participles as a category. The contrastive section (Section 3 of the plan) should present the participial adjective comparison at A2 level: show "закритий" (adjective describing noun) vs "закрито" (impersonal predicate reporting completed state), without teaching the full passive participle system.

6. **Sign vocabulary authenticity:** The plan's sign vocabulary (Магазин зачинено, Вхід заборонено, Оплату прийнято, etc.) is all verified in VESUM and attested in GRAC. These are real-world high-frequency forms — this is the module's strongest motivational hook.
