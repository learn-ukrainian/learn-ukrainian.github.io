## The Soul of the "Bazar"

The Ukrainian **Ğ±Ğ°Ğ·Ğ°Ñ€** (market) is more than a store; it's a community hub.
- **Postiynyy Kliyent (Regular Customer):** The goal is to become a "Ğ¿Ğ¾ÑÑ‚Ñ–Ğ¹Ğ½Ğ¸Ğ¹ ĞºĞ»Ñ–Ñ”Ğ½Ñ‚". Once a seller knows you, they will pick the best tomatoes for you, hide the freshest meat under the counter for you, and give you discounts without asking.

- **Morning Glory:** The best produce is sold by 8-9 AM. If you arrive at noon, you get leftovers.
