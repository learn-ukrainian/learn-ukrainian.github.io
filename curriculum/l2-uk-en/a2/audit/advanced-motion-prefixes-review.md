## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Russianisms
- **Location**: Line 48 / Section "Presentation 1" and Vocabulary
- **Original**: "означає перетнути його насквозь" / `насквозити`
- **Problem**: `насквозь` and `насквозити` are Russian. Ukrainian uses `наскрізь` and `протягнути` (or `наскрізний`).
- **Fix**: Replace `насквозь` with `наскрізь`. Remove `насквозити` from vocab or replace with `протягнути`.

### Issue 2: Spelling Error
- **Location**: Vocabulary
- **Original**: `обьїхати`
- **Problem**: Missing apostrophe. Ukrainian requires an apostrophe after prefixes ending in a consonant before 'я, ю, є, ї'.
- **Fix**: Change to `об'їхати`.

### Issue 3: Weak Activity Design
- **Location**: Activities / fill-in
- **Original**: `options: - варіант А`
- **Problem**: "варіант А" (Option A) is a placeholder distractor that provides no educational value.
- **Fix**: Replace with valid grammatical distractors (e.g., other prefixes).

### Issue 4: Vocabulary Definition Error
- **Location**: Vocabulary / `п'ята`
- **Original**: `translation: heel, five (female variant)`
- **Problem**: `п'ята` is "heel" or "the fifth" (ordinal). The number five is `п'ять`.
- **Fix**: Correct translation to "heel; the fifth".
