# Research Notes: Alim Aliev & Sevgil Musayeva: Mustafa Dzhemilev

**Track**: lit-crimea | **Module**: aliev-musayeva-dzhemilev | **Researched**: 2026-02-24

## The Living Legend
This biographical work documents the life of Mustafa Dzhemilev, framing the historical struggle of the Crimean Tatars (the Surgunlik) through the lens of continuous, non-violent resistance.

## Decolonization Focus
Centering the indigenous narrative of Crimea and rejecting the imperial 'Russian Crimea' myth.
