# Research Notes: Ahatanhel Krymskyi: Historical Crimean Poetry

**Track**: lit-crimea | **Module**: krymskyi-historical-poetry | **Researched**: 2026-02-24

## The Scholar-Poet
Krymskyi, a polyglot and foundational figure of the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences, wrote extensively on Eastern cultures and Crimea. His poetry blends Ukrainian modernism with oriental motifs.

## Decolonization Focus
Establishing Ukraine's direct, non-imperial intellectual connection to the Islamic world and the East, bypassing St. Petersburg.
