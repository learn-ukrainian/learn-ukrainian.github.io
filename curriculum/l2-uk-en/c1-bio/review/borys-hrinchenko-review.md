# Review: borys-hrinchenko (C1-BIO)

## 1. Would I Stay?
**YES.** The module provides an excellent, decolonized perspective on Borys Hrinchenko, correctly framing his lexicographic work as a political act.

## 2. 12 Dimensions Scoring
1. **Agency Pass (10/10):** Hrinchenko is always the active subject. «Грінченко категорично вимагав повної, абсолютної автономії».
2. **Conflict Mapping (10/10):** The conflict with older Ukrainophiles is well mapped. «Грінченко публічно і справедливо звинувачував їх у тому, що вони свідомо звели великий національний рух до нешкідливих етнографічних студій».
3. **Anti-Hagiography (9/10):** Shows his radical, uncompromising nature which sometimes alienated others.
4. **Global Synchronicity (9/10):** Contextualizes within broader European nation-building.
5. **Epistemic Humility (10/10):** Good use of modal verbs and careful assertions.
6. **Chronological Backbone (10/10):** The timeline from early years to the dictionary is clear.
7. **Material Anchoring (9/10):** Mentions «паперові картки з виписаними почутими словами» (150,000 items).
8. **Decolonization Lens (10/10):** Destroys the myth of the «малоросійський діалект».
9. **Primary Sources (10/10):** Good citations. «Якщо мова московська, то й думки московські будуть».
10. **Linguistic Complexity (10/10):** The vocabulary is highly advanced and appropriate for C1.
11. **Activity Alignment (10/10):** Activities perfectly match the text, including the comparative study.
12. **Formatting (10/10):** Proper H2/H3 usage and engagement callouts.

## 3. Issues & Recommendations
- **Issue 1:** The section on "Художня творчість" is slightly under the recommended word count (warning).
  - *Recommendation:* Consider expanding on specific plots of his prose works in the future.
- **Issue 2:** The sentence «Ця структура стала класикою, яка на довгі десятиліття...» could use even more specific examples of dictionary entries.

## 4. Final Verdict
**PASS.** The module is outstanding and ready for production.
