## Issues Found and Fixed

### Issue 1: Anachronistic Vocabulary
**Location:** Section "Вступ" / "Методологія"
**Original:** "торговий хаб", "кризові менеджери"
**Problem:** While effective metaphors, these are too modern/corporate for a historical text about the 11th century.
**Fix:** Replaced with "торговий вузол" and "кризові управителі".
**Status:** ✅ Fixed

### Issue 2: Vocabulary Terminology
**Location:** Vocabulary list
**Original:** "скриптолог"
**Problem:** "Скриптолог" is a rare/calqued term. The standard term for someone studying ancient scripts is "палеограф".
**Fix:** Replaced with "палеограф" + updated definition/IPA.
**Status:** ✅ Fixed
