## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Lexical Error (Meaning)
- **Location**: Section "Вступ", Paragraph 1
- **Original**: "безсилих, **жалісливих** скарг на несправедливу долю"
- **Problem**: "Жалісливий" means "compassionate/pitying" (feeling pity for others). "Жалібний" means "plaintive/sorrowful" (expressing sorrow). Complaints are sorrowful, not compassionate.
- **Fix**: "жалібних"

### Issue 2: Stylistic Collocation
- **Location**: Section "Вступ", Paragraph 4
- **Original**: "аристократом духу і **плоті**"
- **Problem**: "Аристократ плоті" sounds awkward, implying carnal/bodily aristocracy. The standard idiom for lineage is "blood".
- **Fix**: "крові"

### Issue 3: Russianism / Archaism
- **Location**: Section "Останні роки", Paragraph 2
- **Original**: "єдиний надійний **залог** збереження"
- **Problem**: In modern Ukrainian, "залог" is primarily grammatical voice or financial collateral. The meaning "guarantee/pledge" is best rendered as "запорука". "Залог" here sounds like a Russianism ("залог успеха").
- **Fix**: "запорука"

### Issue 4: Pedagogical Disconnect (Hallucinated Content in Activities)
- **Location**: Activities `select` and `translate`
- **Problem**: The activities test knowledge of terms "**Ratio Studiorum**", "**Infima**", "**Pauper**", "**Київські Атени**", and the role of "**панегірики**". NONE of these terms appear in the text. A student reading the text cannot answer these questions.
- **Fix**: Add a "Structure of the Academy" paragraph to the text to introduce these terms, enriching the module.
