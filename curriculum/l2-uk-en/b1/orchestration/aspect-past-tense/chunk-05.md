## Підсумок — Summary (~150 words)

Let us recap the core definitions governing the past tense. The **недоконаний вид** (imperfective aspect) focuses on a continuous **процес** (process) or recurrence. The **доконаний вид** (perfective aspect) emphasizes a final result, absolute completedness, and a single occurrence. Remember that aspect is a deliberate choice, so look for time markers but trust your communicative intent.

Перед тим, як сказати дієслово в минулому часі, поставте собі три запитання. По-перше, чи є конкретний результат? По-друге, чи була ця дія регулярною? По-третє, чи хочу я наголосити на процесі?

> *Before saying a verb in the past tense, ask yourself three questions. First, is there a specific result? Second, was this action regular? Third, do I want to emphasize the process?*

Your answers will guide you to the correct form. If an event happened **раптом** (suddenly) or **одного разу** (once), you know to use the perfective. If it happened **щодня** (every day), the imperfective is required. In the next module, we will apply this logic to the future tense, where aspect changes the very structure of the sentence.
