:::info
**Граматика** — Підсумок Фази 8
- **Складносурядні речення (M66):** *і, а, але, або* (connecting equal ideas).
- **З'ясувальні речення (M67):** *що, щоб, чи* (reporting thoughts or speech).
- **Означальні речення (M68):** *який* (describing nouns).
- **Обставинні речення (M69-73):** *коли, бо, якщо, щоб, хоча* (expressing time, reason, condition, purpose, and concession).
- **Пряма і непряма мова (M74):** Quoting and reporting what others said.
:::

You can use attributive clauses to explain why a certain **виставка** (exhibition) inspired you, or why a theatrical **вистава** (performance, show) was unforgettable. You also learned how to properly write the name of each Ukrainian **свято** (holiday, celebration). Capitalization rules for these events are essential for written communication. Now you can confidently send holiday greetings without making common spelling mistakes.

We explored how Ukrainians **святкувати** (to celebrate) winter holidays like **Різдво** (Christmas) and sing a traditional **колядка** (Christmas carol). You can use time and reason clauses to explain these historical events. You also learned about the rich history behind these winter celebrations. They connect modern Ukrainians to their ancient past.

You can also describe spring traditions, such as **Великдень** (Easter) and the creation of a colorful **писанка** (decorated Easter egg), showcasing the richness of our **культура** (culture). Describing these events requires the imperfective aspect for repeated actions. You now know exactly when to use it to describe ongoing customs. This makes your storytelling authentic and historically accurate.

Concessive and conditional clauses help you explain the deep meaning behind each ancient **традиція** (tradition). Furthermore, you can confidently walk into any **ресторан** (restaurant) and ask the waiter for a **меню** (menu). You can navigate the diverse world of regional cuisines. Whether you are in Lviv or Odesa, you will know exactly what to order.

Finally, you can make a polite **замовлення** (order), evaluate a traditional **страва** (dish), and use reported speech to tell the **шеф-кухар** (chef) that the food was absolutely delicious! Using indirect speech elevates your conversational skills. It allows you to share opinions and cite culinary experts. Your Ukrainian is now ready for deep cultural immersion!
