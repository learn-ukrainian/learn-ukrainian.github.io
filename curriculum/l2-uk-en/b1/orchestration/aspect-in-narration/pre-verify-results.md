- Not found: "тим-часом" (found as separate words "тим часом", which is a standard set expression; also "тим часом як" is a verified complex conjunction).

## Grammar Rules
- Aspect in Narrative: Недоконаний вид signals ongoing/uncompleted actions (що робити?), while Доконаний вид signals completed actions/results (що зробити?) — Zabolotnyi Gr 7 §25.
- Narrative Structure: "Сюжет (послідовність подій)" consists of exposition, plot-jump, development, climax, and resolution — Zabolotnyi Gr 7 "Композиція".
- Punctuation: Complex conjunctions like "тим часом як" and "після того як" require a comma separating the clauses — Karaman Gr 10 §78.

## Calque Warnings
- "в той же час": Use with caution. While found in textbooks, "водночас" or "у той самий час" are often preferred for higher naturalness.
- "у той час як": OK, but "тоді як" or "поки" can be more concise depending on context.
- "передній план": OK — frequently used in textbooks to denote "coming to the fore" or "foreground" (Avramenko Gr 11).
