## Grammar Rules
- **Переносне значення**: While not a spelling rule in Pravopys, it is a foundational concept in the Ukrainian State Standard (e.g., Avramenko Grade 5) where verbs of motion are used figuratively for time, weather, and processes.
- **Безособові форми на -ться**: The verb "йтися" (йдеться про...) is a standard impersonal construction used to denote the subject of discussion, verified in СУМ-11.
