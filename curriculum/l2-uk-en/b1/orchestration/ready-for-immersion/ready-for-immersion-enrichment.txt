# Дослідження: Готові до занурення

## State Standard Reference
§4.5.1: "Стилістичні засоби лексики... Стилістичні засоби синтаксису"
Alignment: This module explicitly prepares learners to distinguish the "instructional/formal" register of grammar explanations from the "conversational" register they have used so far. It also covers §4.1.5 (Abbreviations) essential for reading dictionary entries and grammar rules (див., пор., напр.).

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Frequency / Source | Key collocations |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| **правило** | High (General) | за правилом, виняток з правила, золоте правило |
| **виняток** | Medium (Academic) | без винятку, становити виняток, рідкісний виняток |
| **наголос** | Medium (Linguistic) | поставити наголос, змінний наголос, логічний наголос |
| **значення** | High (General) | лексичне значення, мати значення, переносне значення |
| **частина мови** | Low (Term) | самостійна частина мови, службова частина мови |

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Forbidden Letter 'Ґ'**: Abolished in the 1933 orthography reform (to harmonise Ukrainian with Russian), restored in 1990. It represents linguistic resilience and the fight for identity. Perfect metaphor for "precision" in language.
2. **Proverb**: «Слово — не горобець, вилетить — не спіймаєш» (A word is not a sparrow; if it flies out, you won't catch it). Emphasises the permanence and weight of spoken words, aligning with the "precision" theme of the module.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Letter vs Sound**: Confusing `буква` (written character) and `звук` (phoneme). *Example*: "In 'я', there are two sounds but one letter."
2. **Stress shifting meaning**: `пла́чу` (I am crying) vs `плачу́` (I am paying). Critical for self-correction.
3. **False Friends**: `Правило` (rule) vs `Право` (law/right). Learners often say "У мене є правило" when they mean "I have the right".

## Cross-References
- **Builds on**: b1-01 (Grammar terminology), b1-02 (Verb terminology), b1-03 (Reading rules), b1-04 (Syntax).
- **Prepares for**: b1-06 (Aspect complete system - First 100% immersion module), b1-15 (Aspect checkpoint).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Tone Shift**: This is the "Airlock". It must feel supportive but firm. The English safety net is ending.
- **Visuals**: Use the "Tree of Speech" analogy for Parts of Speech (Roots = Meaningful, Branches = Service).
- **Immersion**: This is B1.0. Intro can have English bridging (max 2 paragraphs). The rest must be Ukrainian, but the *subject* is how to read Ukrainian.
