## Огляд наказового способу (~650 words total)
- P1 (~200 words): Recap of Module 20 — The full Imperative paradigm. Systematize the endings for all persons: 2sg (-и or -∅: пиши, читай), 1pl (-імо or -мо: пишімо, читаймо), 2pl (-іть or -те: пишіть, читайте). Explain the "Let" construction for the 3rd person: `хай` / `нехай` + present tense. Use the verb "робити" as a model: роби, робімо, робіть, хай робить.
- P2 (~150 words): Orthographic hurdles in the Imperative. Explain the presence of the soft sign (ь) in stems ending in dentals (д, т, з, с, ц, л, н) such as "будь", "глянь", "станьмо". Contrast this with labials and hissing sounds where it is absent: "ріж", "їжмо", "поставте". This is a critical B1 accuracy check.
- P3 (~200 words): Aspectual choices in commands. Explain that Perfective (PF) is for specific, result-oriented actions ("Відчини вікно"), while Imperfective (IPF) is for general advice or repeated actions ("Відчиняй вікно щоранку"). Crucially, review the negation rule: `не` + Imperative almost always requires IPF: "Не забудь!" (PF) vs the more natural "Не забувай!" (IPF) for general reminders.
