## Cultural Hooks
1. **Миля Незламних (The Mile of the Unbroken):** A special distance within major Ukrainian marathons dedicated to wounded veterans and military personnel, symbolizing resilience and national solidarity.
2. **Найдовший марафон (The Longest Marathon):** A wartime initiative created by Nova Post, where people globally run for Ukrainian defenders who have been fighting non-stop, demonstrating running as a form of powerful cultural diplomacy.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Motion Verbs Confusion:** "Я люблю бігти вранці" → Correct: "Я люблю бігати вранці" — Learners often confuse the unidirectional verb (бігти) with the habitual/multidirectional verb (бігати).
2. **Case Governance:** "Брати участь марафону" → Correct: "Брати участь у марафоні" — Learners use the Genitive case instead of the required Locative case with the preposition "у/в".
3. **Time Expressions:** "Я пробіг годину" → Correct: "Я бігав годину" / "Я пробіг за годину" — Confusing the expression of process duration (Accusative) with the time taken to achieve a result (за + Accusative).
