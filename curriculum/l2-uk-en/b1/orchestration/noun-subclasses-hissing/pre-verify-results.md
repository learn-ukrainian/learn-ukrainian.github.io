## Grammar Rules
- **Мішана група (II declension)**: Pravopys 2019 does not use the specific term "мішана група" in section headings, but textbooks (Zabolotnyi Gr. 6 §29, Litvinova Gr. 6 §31) define it as nouns with stems ending in hissing consonants [ж, ч, ш, щ].
- **Instrumental Singular (-ем)**: According to Zabolotnyi Grade 6 p.102 and ULIF, nouns of the mixed group take the ending **-ем** (or **-ем/-ям** for soft/mixed): *товаришем*, *сторожем*, *читачем*, *ножем*, *борщем*, *плечем*. The ending *-ом* is incorrect and a common Russicism.
- **Vocative Singular**: Nouns of the mixed group typically take **-у** (especially with suffix *-ач* or inanimate: *читачу*, *товаришу*, *плащу*) or **-е** (e.g., *стороже*). Confirmed by ULIF paradigms.
- **Parallel Endings**: Dative and Locative cases for animate nouns allow parallel endings **-ові/-еві** and **-у/-ю**: *товаришеві* / *товаришу*.

## Calque Warnings
- **мішана група**: OK — standard pedagogical term in Ukrainian linguistics (found in all Grade 6 textbooks).
- **паралельні закінчення**: OK — standard term for synonymous endings like -ові/-у.
- **сторожем**: OK — correct Ukrainian form. Warning: avoid the Russian-influenced *сторожом.
