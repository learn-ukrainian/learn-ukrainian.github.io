## Cultural Hooks
1. **Кордоцентризм (Cordocentrism):** The Ukrainian "philosophy of the heart," prominently featured in the works of Hryhorii Skovoroda and Pamfil Yurkevych. In Ukrainian culture, the heart (серце) is traditionally viewed as the center of not just emotion, but also profound cognition and human essence, contrasting with purely rationalistic approaches.
2. **Folk Wisdom on Empathy:** The proverb "Поділена радість — подвійна, а поділене горе — вдвічі менше" (Shared joy is double, shared grief is half) perfectly encapsulates the cultural value placed on shared emotional experiences and community support.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Емпатія vs Співчуття** → "Емпатія" means feeling *with* someone (sharing their exact emotional state), while "співчуття" means feeling *for* them (compassion, pity).
2. **"Я вибачаюся"** → Incorrect reflexive form (literally "I forgive myself"). Correct forms: "Я перепрошую" or "Прошу вибачення".
3. **"Приносити вибачення"** → Russian lexical calque. Correct natural phrasing: "Просити вибачення" or "Складати вибачення" (though the latter is highly formal; "просити" is best).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **100% Immersion:** Do not use English to explain grammatical or cultural concepts. Use Ukrainian only. English is reserved solely for vocabulary table translations.
- **Decolonized Framing:** Do not explain incorrect calques (like "приносити вибачення") by comparing them to Russian. Simply refer to them as "мовні кальки" or "неприродні конструкції" and emphasize the authentic Ukrainian standard.
- **Persona:** Adopt the "Conflict Mediator" role — empathetic, balanced, using modal hedging to deliver delicate advice.
