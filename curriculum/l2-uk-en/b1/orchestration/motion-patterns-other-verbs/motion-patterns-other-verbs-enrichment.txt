Alignment: This module extends the standard's logic of motion prefixes (§4.3.8) to non-motion verbs, demonstrating how spatial meanings (`ви-` = out) transform into abstract meanings (`ви-` = result/completion) in word formation (§4.3.4-4.3.8).

## Cultural Hooks
1. **ПЕРЕ- as Resilience**: The proverb "Життя прожити — не поле перейти" (To live life is not just to cross a field) perfectly illustrates `пере-` as crossing a difficult space/time. It anchors the concept of "overcoming" and "duration/process".
2. **ЗА- as Initiation**: In Ukrainian culture, `за-` often marks the *moment of entry* into a state (inchoative), reflected in folk songs where action starts suddenly: "Засвіт встали козаченьки" (The Cossacks rose/started moving at dawn). It contrasts with the "behind/hidden" meaning, offering a dualism of "hiding" vs "starting".
3. **ВИ- as Achievement**: `Ви-` carries the cultural value of "working until the end" (`випрацювати`, `вивчити`). It implies an effort was made to extract the result "out" of the process.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Preposition Confusion**: Learners often confuse the prefix with the preposition.
   - *Error*: "Я написав в зошит" (Correct, but specific) vs "Я записав в зошит" (Focus on keeping the record).
   - *Fix*: Teach that `за-` implies "fixing/securing" information, while `на-` is just "surface contact".
2. **Aspect/Tense Mismatch**: Using `заговорити` (perfective/start) for "to speak" (process).
   - *Error*: "Він заговорив зі мною дві години" (He started speaking with me for two hours).
   - *Fix*: Clarify `за-` as the *point* of entry, not the duration.
3. **Polysemy of ВІД-**: Confusing "away" (movement) with "return/response" (abstract).
   - *Error*: Interpreting `відповісти` as "to say away".
   - *Fix*: Map `від-` to "re-" (reaction/return) in abstract contexts.
