Alignment: This module directly addresses the formation of the conditional mood (past + by) and its use in complex sentences with the conjunction `якби` for unreal conditions, as explicitly required by the B1 standard.

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Frequency / Source | Key collocations |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| якби | High (Conjunction) | якби тільки (if only), якби ж то (if only - emphatic), якби не (if not for) |

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Taras Shevchenko Quote**: "Якби ви вчились так, як треба, / То й мудрість би була своя." (If you learned as you ought to, / Then wisdom would be your own.) — *Epistle to my countrymen* (І мертвим, і живим...). This is a universally known quote in Ukraine, perfect for illustrating the `якби` + past tense structure.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Real vs. Unreal Confusion**: Using `якщо` (if [real]) instead of `якби` (if [unreal]).
   *   *Error*: Якщо я був тобою... (If I am you...)
   *   *Correct*: Якби я був тобою... (If I were you...) — Why: Learner applies English "if" logic without distinguishing mood.
2. **"Якщо б" Calque**: Using `якщо б` instead of `якби`.
   *   *Error*: Якщо б я знав...
   *   *Correct*: Якби я знав... — Why: Calque from Russian "если бы" or misapplication of particle rules. `Якби` is the standard synthetic form.
3. **Verb Tense mismatch**: Using Present or Future tense with `якби`.
   *   *Error*: Якби я маю час...
   *   *Correct*: Якби я мав час... — Why: Forgetting that Ukrainian conditional REQUIRES the Past tense form + `би`.

## Cross-References
- Builds on: `b1-31` (Conditionals Real якщо) — must contrast explicitly with this.
- Prepares for: `b1-33` (Conditionals Mixed Complex) — lays the foundation for mixing tenses.

- **Pedagogy**: Explicitly contrast `якщо` + Present/Future vs `якби` + Past. This is the single biggest hurdle. Use a "Reality Check" table.
- **Immersion**: Ensure explanations of the difference are 100% in Ukrainian. Use clear signaling words: "Реальність" vs "Фантазія/Мрія".
