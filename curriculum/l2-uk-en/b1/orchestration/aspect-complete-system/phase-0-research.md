## Cultural Hooks
1. **Сергій Параджанов "Тіні забутих предків" (1965)**: A masterpiece of Ukrainian poetic cinema that perfectly illustrates the "Director's Cut" metaphor for aspect. The film lingers on the *process* of Hutsul rituals and nature (Imperfective/Slow Motion) vs. sharp, tragic *events* (Perfective/Snapshot).
2. **Philosophical perception of time**: Ukrainian worldview distinguishes between "living through" (переживати - Impf) and "accomplishing" (здійснити - Perf). This aligns with the "philosophy of reality perception" point in the plan.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Perfective in Present**: Trying to say "I am doing (completed)" now.
   *   *Error*: "Я напишу зараз" (meaning "I am writing now").
   *   *Correction*: "Я пишу зараз" (Impf).
   *   *Reason*: Perfective cannot express the present moment; it always implies a future result or past completion.
2. **Future Tense Confusion**: Mixing up compound (Impf) and simple (Perf) future forms.
   *   *Error*: "Я буду написати" (I will be write).
   *   *Correction*: "Я писатиму" / "Я буду писати" (Impf) OR "Я напишу" (Perf).
   *   *Reason*: Applying English "will + verb" logic indiscriminately.
3. **Aspect Pair Blindness**: Failing to recognize that *читати* and *прочитати* are the same lexical meaning but different aspects.
   *   *Correction*: Treating them as a single semantic unit with two functional forms.
