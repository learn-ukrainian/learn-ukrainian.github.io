## Linguistic Scan
Found 2 critical linguistic/factual errors:
1. **Grammatical misstatement**: The text claims that state is expressed by an "adverb of neuter gender" («прислівником середнього роду»). Adverbs are invariable in Ukrainian and do not have gender. Impersonal verbs in the past tense take neuter gender, but adverbs do not.
2. **Invalid preposition**: The text uses the non-existent/dialectal preposition «навсупроти» (which is not found in VESUM). The standard literary preposition for "contrary to" with the dative case is «всупереч».

## Findings
[2] [CRITICAL]
Location: Section "Давальний суб'єкта стану", paragraph 1: «Сам стан виражається безособовим словом *(impersonal word)*, найчастіше — прислівником середнього роду.»
Issue: Adverbs (прислівники) are invariable in Ukrainian grammar; they do not have gender. It is factually wrong to teach learners that "холодно" or "сумно" is "neuter gender".
Fix: Change to «предикативним прислівником».
