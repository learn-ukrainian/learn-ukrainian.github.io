## Linguistic Scan
No major linguistic errors (Russianisms, Surzhyk, calques, paronyms) found. The Ukrainian is highly idiomatic and correctly handles complex morphology. However, there are two distinct factual errors regarding phonetic and grammatical rules:
1. **Phonetics:** The text refers to `[щ]` as a basic sound (`В українській фонетичній системі це чотири базові звуки: [ж], [ч], [ш], [щ]...`). In Ukrainian phonetics, `щ` is a letter that always denotes two sounds: `[ш]` and `[ч]`.
2. **Grammar:** The text claims that nouns with the suffix `-ар` belong to the mixed group (`До другої категорії належать слова із суфіксами «-ач» або «-ар»`). This is factually incorrect. Stems ending in `-ар` (e.g., *лікар*, *календар*) do not end in hissing consonants and belong to either the hard or soft group, not the mixed group.
