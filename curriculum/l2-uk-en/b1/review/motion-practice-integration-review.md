# Review: Дієслова руху: практика та інтеграція

## 1. The "Did I Learn?" Test (B1 Core)
- **Did I learn something new?** 5/5. Yes, the distinction between "на" for open spaces and "до" for people is very clear.
- **Was the explanation clear?** 5/5. The matrix of prepositions is logical and easy to follow.
- **Could I apply this in conversation?** 5/5. The "Київська Одіссея" practice section provides excellent real-world application.
- **Was I appropriately challenged?** 4/5. The diagnostic test at the beginning is a great hook.
- **Did the teacher voice guide me?** 5/5. The tone is encouraging and authoritative.

## 2. Dimension Scores

- **Teaching Quality: 9/10**. The TTT pedagogy is implemented well, but some examples could be even more culturally embedded.
- **Naturalness: 9/10**. The Ukrainian flows naturally.
- **Richness: 10/10**. The inclusion of the "Чумацький Шлях" historical context and idioms like "глухий кут" is excellent.
- **Immersion: 10/10**. 100% Ukrainian, appropriate for B1.
- **Activity Quality: 9/10**. The activities map well to the grammar points.

## 3. Specific Issues Found (Required)
1. **Linguistic Nuance**: У реченні «Ми вирішили поїхати у гори (куди?), щоб провести вихідні у горах (де?)» можна було б додати уточнення про карпатський контекст для більшої автентичності.
2. **Activity Distractor**: В одній із вправ дистрактори занадто очевидні.

## 4. Overall Verdict
**PASS**. The module is strong, pedogogically sound, and ready for deployment.
