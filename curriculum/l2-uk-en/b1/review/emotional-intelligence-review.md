## Issues/Critique

- **Minor Schema Error**: In `vocabulary/emotional-intelligence.yaml`, the entry `надалі` has `pos: adv` but also `gender: m`. Adverbs do not have gender in Ukrainian.
- **Proper Nouns**: The entry `ройз` (Svitlana Roiz) should be capitalized as `Ройз` in the vocabulary list.
- **Idiomatic Context**: The word `палка` is included in the vocabulary, but its primary meaning (stick) is less relevant here than its use in the idiom `перегнути палку` (to overdo it). A note in the vocabulary table would be beneficial.
- **Audio Placeholder**: No audio files are present for the key vocabulary and phrases (as noted in audit).
