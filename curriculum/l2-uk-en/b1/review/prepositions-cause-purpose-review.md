## Linguistic Scan
Two minor linguistic issues found:
1. **Pleonasm / Calque:** The phrase "Давайте глибше заглибимося" contains a pleonasm (tautology), as "заглибитися" inherently means going deeper. Furthermore, the construction "давайте" + verb used as an imperative is widely considered a colloquial calque from Russian in modern Ukrainian styling; the standard literary form is the synthetic 1st-person plural imperative (e.g., "заглибмося", "підсумуймо").
2. **Inconsistent Orthography:** The text contains two instances of the outdated spelling "проект". According to the 2019 Ukrainian orthography (§ 126), words with the Latin root -ject- must be spelled with an "є" (проєкт). The writer correctly used "проєкт" in later sections but missed these two early occurrences.
