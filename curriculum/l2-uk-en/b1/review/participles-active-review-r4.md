## Linguistic Scan
Several linguistic errors were found regarding the validity of certain present active participles:
- **«палаючий»** — flagged as NOT IN VESUM. It is a Russian calque (from "пылающий") but is incorrectly presented in the text as a "stylistically flawless" natural Ukrainian word.
- **«падаючий»** — flagged as NOT IN VESUM. Another calque (from "падающий") incorrectly presented as a "natural adjective-like participle" in the documentary dialogue.
- **«сяючий»** — flagged as NOT IN VESUM. Used as a positive example of a valid participle.
- **«зеленіючий»** — flagged as NOT IN VESUM. Used as an example to distinguish an adjective from a participle.
- **«квітнучий», «синіючий»** — flagged as NOT IN VESUM. These forms are theoretically impossible even as calques (the true adjective form is *квітучий* from *цвісти/квісти*, not formed directly from the *квітнути* present stem).

The text correctly identifies many calques (існуючий, миючий, діючий) but fails by treating other calques (палаючий, падаючий) as valid Ukrainian vocabulary.
