## 3. Detailed Analysis

### Strengths
1.  **Cultural Anchoring**: The module effectively frames grammar terminology not as "boring rules" but as "what Ukrainian kids learn." The inclusion of the genuine "На Різдво..." mnemonic is a brilliant touch that connects the learner to the culture.
2.  **Pedagogical Empathy**: The "Why This Matters" sections correctly identify the anxiety learners feel when facing a text in a new language and offer a concrete solution (learn the labels).
3.  **Activity Robustness**: The activities are not just placeholders. There are 14 quizzes, 20 match-ups, 28 fill-ins, etc. This is drill-heavy in a good way for terminology acquisition.
