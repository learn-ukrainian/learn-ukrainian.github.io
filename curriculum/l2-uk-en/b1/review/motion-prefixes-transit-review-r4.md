СУМ-11 documents 11+ distinct meanings of "перевести" beyond physical movement, including: transferring to another job/position, changing gears/controls, shifting one's gaze (перевести очі), catching one's breath (перевести дух), wasting/squandering (перевести гроші = spend wastefully), etc. Additionally, "перевести гроші на рахунок" (transfer money) is itself NOT physical movement — it contradicts the "лише фізичне переміщення" claim within the same sentence. The pedagogical intent (warning against "перевести" = translate) is correct and valuable, but the overgeneralization with "лише" teaches an incorrect rule.
