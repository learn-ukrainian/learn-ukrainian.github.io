## Linguistic Scan
- **Critical Error**: The word `зустрічений` was generated as the passive participle for `зустріти`, which is factually incorrect. The verb `зустріти` forms the passive participle `зустрітий` using the `-т-` suffix (the root is monosyllabic: -стрі-). The `[т] → [ч]` alternation rule does not apply to this verb.
- **Critical Error**: The word `пораджений` was provided as an example of `[д] → [дж]` alternation from the verb `радити`. However, `пораджений` is not attested in standard dictionaries (VESUM) and is practically unused in modern Ukrainian (the impersonal form `пораджено` exists, but the attributive form is non-standard).
- **Stylistic Polish (Russianism/Calque)**: The construction `Давайте + дієслово` (e.g., "Давайте згадаємо", "Давайте детально розглянемо") appears 5 times. In standard modern Ukrainian, the first-person plural imperative (наказовий спосіб) should be used directly: `Згадаймо`, `Розгляньмо`, `Подивімося`, `Потренуймося`.
