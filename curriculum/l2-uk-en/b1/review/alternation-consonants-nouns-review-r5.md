## Linguistic Scan
- **No linguistic errors found** regarding standard Russianisms (e.g., *кіт/кот*, *хорошо/добре*) or Surzhyk.
- **Critical Error**: The word for "knight" is consistently misspelled as **«вітязь»** (likely influenced by the name of the lake *Світязь* or Russian *витязь* spelled with Ukrainian *і*). In standard Ukrainian, it is **«витязь»** (with *и*). This error appears in the plan references and is repeated in the prose twice.
- **Minor Error**: The name **«Пенелопа»** (Penelope) is used as an example of a name that resists consonant alternation in the dative/locative. However, the consonant 'п' does not alternate to [з, ц, с] or [ж, ч, ш] under any circumstances in Ukrainian, making this a poor and misleading example for a module focused on [г, к, х] alternations.
- **Factually Obscure**: The claim that **«колесо — на колішні»** is an alternation of the noun *колесо*. While *колішня* shares a root with *колесо* and shows a historical [с]/[ш] shift, *колішня* is a separate noun (meaning the front part of a plow) and not a case form of *колесо*. However, this point was specifically requested by the plan.
