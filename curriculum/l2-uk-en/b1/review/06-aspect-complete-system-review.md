## Strengths

### 1. **Intent-Based Teaching**
Instead of just saying "Perfective is past tense," the module asks: *"What is more important - process or result?"* This trains the learner's brain to think like a Ukrainian speaker, not just translate.

### 2. **Cultural Depth**
The reference to *Тіні забутих предків* (Shadows of Forgotten Ancestors) adds immediate prestige and cultural context, showing that aspect isn't just a textbook rule but a living part of the language's soul.

### 3. **Nuanced Scenarios**
The "Complex Cases" section (e.g., "Хто читав цю книгу?" vs "Хто прочитав цю книгу?") addresses the exact confusion intermediate learners face when both aspects are technically "correct" but mean different things.
