## Linguistic Scan
- **Russianisms / Calques:**
  - `давайте обговоримо`, `Давайте згадаємо`, `Давайте подивимося` — The construction "давайте + infinitive/future tense" is a syntactic calque from Russian ("давайте обсудим"). Ukrainian requires the first-person plural synthetic imperative: `обговорімо`, `згадаймо`, `подивімося`.
  - `відправка` — While sometimes used colloquially, the standard literary Ukrainian noun for this process is `відправлення` (as it avoids the Russian-style `-ка` suffix for verbal nouns).
  - `мовна вільність` — A literal calque of "language fluency" or "linguistic liberty". The natural Ukrainian phrasing is `вільне володіння мовою`.

- **Other Notes:**
  - `доставка` — The suffix `-ка` is often flagged by purists in favor of `доставляння`, but `доставка` is heavily attested in standard dictionaries (СУМ-11) and modern usage ("доставка піци"), so it is acceptable here.
  - `примірочна` — A colloquialism (often a calque of "примерочная"), with `приміряльня` being the standard. However, since the plan specifically demanded `примірочна` in the vocabulary list, its inclusion is correct.
