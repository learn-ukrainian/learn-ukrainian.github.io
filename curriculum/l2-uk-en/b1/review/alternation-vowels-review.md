## Findings

[Linguistic accuracy] [Critical]
Location: Section "Чергування [о], [е] з нулем звука", paragraph 2 ("А великий старовинний «замок» має форму родового відмінка «замка».")
Issue: The text claims the genitive of an old castle ("великий старовинний замок") is "замка". According to Pravopys §82 2.1, buildings/castles take the -у ending ("за́мку"). The form "замка́" is exclusively used for padlocks/locks. In a module dedicated to morphophonemics and declension, this is a critical morphological error.
Fix: Change «замка» to «замку».

[Vocabulary coverage] [Major]
Location: Section "Чергування [о], [е] з нулем звука", paragraph 1 ("Такі голосні часто називають **випадними** *(fleeting)*...")
Issue: The required vocabulary term "біглий голосний" is completely missing from the text. The writer used "випадний", which is linguistically accurate and preferred by modern linguists, but violates the strict requirement to include the plan's exact vocabulary terms.
Fix: Add "(або традиційно — **біглими голосними**)" after "випадними" to satisfy the requirement while maintaining modern linguistic accuracy.

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is phenomenally written and beautifully explains a very difficult grammatical concept, but contains a critical morphological error regarding the genitive ending of "замок" (castle) and misses one required vocabulary term. Both issues must be fixed before publishing.
