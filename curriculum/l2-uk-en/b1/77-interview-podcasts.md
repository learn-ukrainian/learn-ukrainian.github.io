## –°—Ç—Ä–∞—Ç–µ–≥—ñ—ó —Å–ª—É—Ö–∞–Ω–Ω—è

### Before Listening

1. **Preview:** Read the title, description, guest bio
2. **Predict:** What topics will they discuss?
3. **Prepare:** What do you already know about this?

### While Listening

1. **Focus:** on main ideas, not every word
2. **Note:** key names, numbers, dates
3. **Pause:** when needed to process

### After Listening

1. **Summarize:** main points in your own words
2. **Review:** your notes
3. **Reflect:** what did you learn?
