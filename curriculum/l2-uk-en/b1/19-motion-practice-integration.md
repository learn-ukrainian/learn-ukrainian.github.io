**The Practice Approach**

In real Ukrainian conversation, motion verbs rarely appear in isolation. Native speakers chain them together to describe complete journeys. Each verb captures one "segment" of movement. Learning to think in chains ‚Äî exit ‚Üí board ‚Üí travel ‚Üí exit ‚Üí walk ‚Üí enter ‚Äî is the key to sounding natural.
