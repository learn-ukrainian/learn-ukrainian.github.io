## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

This module focuses on structural vocabulary (connectors) rather than semantic fields.

### High Frequency (Core Active)
These are essential for A2/B1 fluency and appear constantly in spoken and written Ukrainian.
- **Connectors:** *тому що* (because), *бо* (because - colloquial), *коли* (when), *якщо* (if), *щоб* (in order to/that), *хоча* (although).
- **Reporting Verbs:** *казати* (to say), *говорити* (to speak), *питати* (to ask), *відповідати* (to answer), *думати* (to think).
- **Particles:** *чи* (whether/if - for yes/no questions), *нехай* (let - in imperatives).

### Mid Frequency (B1 Specific)
Used to add nuance or formality.
- **Connectors:** *оскільки* (since/as), *через те що* (due to the fact that), *після того як* (after), *перед тим як* (before), *незважаючи на те що* (despite the fact that), *поки* (while/until).
- **Reporting Verbs:** *стверджувати* (to claim), *заперечувати* (to deny), *цікавитися* (to be interested/wonder), *наказувати* (to order), *просити* (to request).

### Common Collocations
- *Він сказав, що...* (He said that...)
- *Вона запитала, чи...* (She asked if/whether...)
- *Незважаючи на погоду* (Despite the weather)
- *Я спізнився, тому що...* (I was late because...)
- *Поки я чекав* (While I was waiting)

## 3. Cultural Hook

### The "Nuance" of Indirect Speech
In Ukrainian culture, reporting what someone said often carries a subtle emotional evaluation based on the verb choice. Unlike the neutral English "said", Ukrainians frequently use verbs that imply attitude:
- *Він **ляпнув**, що...* (He blurted out that... - implies stupidity/carelessness).
- *Вона **зауважила**, що...* (She noted/remarked that... - implies precision/correction).
- *Вони **вимагали**, щоб...* (They demanded that... - implies conflict/pressure).

### Proverbs & Wisdom
Complex sentence structures are preserved in many famous Ukrainian proverbs, often used in daily life:
- ***Хоча** в кишені пусто, аби в хаті густо.* (Although the pocket is empty, as long as the house is full [of friends/food].) - Concessive.
- *Не кажи «гоп», **поки** не перескочиш.* (Don't say "hop" until you jump.) - Temporal.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

### Key Learning Challenges (English vs. Ukrainian)

1.  **The "Sequence of Tenses" Trap (CRITICAL):**
    *   **English:** "He said he **lived** in Kyiv." (Backshift: lives -> lived).
    *   **Ukrainian:** "Він сказав, що він **живе** в Києві." (NO Backshift: original tense remains).
    *   *Teaching Tip:* Emphasize that Ukrainian is logical—you report exactly what was said, just changing the person (I -> he). If he said "I live", you report "that he lives".

2.  **The "Command" Subjunctive:**
    *   **Structure:** *Він попросив, **щоб** я прийшов.* (He asked that I come/to come).
    *   **Error:** Students try to use infinitive (*Він попросив мене прийти* - correct but less common for complex commands) or copy English structure incorrectly.
    *   *Fix:* Drill the *щоб + past tense form* pattern for generic "subjunctive" needs.

3.  **Punctuation (The Comma Rule):**
    *   **Rule:** Commas strictly separate clauses. Before *що, який, коли, щоб, бо*, a comma is almost always mandatory.
    *   **Error:** Students omit commas (English influence).

4.  **"Чи" vs. "Якщо" (If vs. If):**
    *   **Challenge:** Both map to English "if".
    *   **Difference:**
        *   *Якщо* = Conditional (If it rains...).
        *   *Чи* = Interrogative/Indirect Question (He asked **if** it is raining).
    *   *Drill:* Use "whether" as the mental check for *чи*.

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope (Target for M41)
- **Clause Types:** Concessive (*хоча*), Causal (*тому що, бо*), Temporal (*коли, поки*).
- **Reported Speech:** Statements (*що*), Questions (*чи, хто, де*), Imperatives (*щоб*).
- **Tense Usage:** Maintaining the original tense in indirect speech.
- **Mood:** Realis mood mostly; introductory Subjunctive with *щоб* (for commands/purpose).

### OUT of Scope (Do NOT Include)
- **Participles:** Active/Passive participles (*роблячий, зроблений*) - covered in M42-M46.
- **Passive Voice Constructions:** (*Будинок будується*) - covered in M47.
- **Deep Stylistics:** Archaic conjunctions (*бодай, зане*) - C1 level.
- **Complex Mixed Conditionals:** (If I had gone, I would be happy now) - covered in B2.
