Alignment: This module directly addresses these requirements by detailing the suffixal derivation of adjectives from nouns and the formation of adverbs from adjectives (including the `-о/-е` and `по-...-и` patterns).

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Незалежність:** The prefix `не-` added to `залежний` forms `незалежний` (independent). This single word formation is the cornerstone of modern Ukrainian civic identity.
2. **Жовто-блакитний:** Compound adjectives with equal components are hyphenated. A prime cultural example is `жовто-блакитний` (yellow-blue), representing the national flag.
3. **Козацький:** The suffix `-ськ-` is incredibly productive for creating relational adjectives denoting origin or belonging, deeply rooted in historical terms (козацький, чумацький, київський).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Alternation mistakes with `-ськ-`:** Learners often forget the consonant changes before `-ськ-`. Note: The plan contains typos! It lists `к→ч (козак→козацький)` and `г→ж (Прага→празький)`. These are wrong. The correct alternations before `-ськ-` are: `к, ч, ц + ськ = цьк` (козак → козацький) and `г, ж, з + ськ = зьк` (Прага → празький). Phase B must use the correct phonetic rules, not the flawed ones in the plan.
2. **Manner adverbs without hyphens:** Writing `*по українськи` (missing hyphen) or incorrectly declining `*по українському` when translating "in Ukrainian". While "по-українському" exists, "по-українськи" is preferred for language manner.
3. **Misidentifying participles as adjectives:** Confusing pure adjectives ending in `-н-` (незрівнянний) with passive participles (написаний), leading to stress and spelling errors (double `-нн-` vs single `-н-`).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing:** Do not compare Ukrainian suffixes to Russian. Present the rich system of diminutive suffixes (`-еньк-`, `-есеньк-`) as a unique, inherent feature of Ukrainian qualitative adjectives that adds emotional depth to the language.
- **100% Immersion:** This is module B1.57. Zero English scaffolding is allowed. All explanations, examples, and meta-language must be in Ukrainian.
- **Agency Pass:** Use active voice when explaining grammar rules ("Ми додаємо суфікс...", "Суфікс утворює...").
- **Correction Required:** Explicitly fix the consonant alternation rules that were misstated in the plan (к→цьк, г→зьк).
