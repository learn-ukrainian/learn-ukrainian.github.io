## 3. Cultural hook

1.  **The Chumak Way (Чумацький шлях)**: Historically, the South was the destination for salt traders (Chumaks). The Milky Way galaxy is called "Chumatskyi Shliakh" in Ukrainian because it pointed the way south to Crimea for salt. This connects astronomy, history, and the steppe landscape.
2.  **Odesa's Mythos**: Odesa is culturally distinct with its specific humor and "Odesa language" history. It was built as a "window to Europe" (specifically South) and remains a melting pot. *Fact*: The Potemkin Stairs create an optical illusion; looking down, you see only landings, looking up, only steps.
3.  **Crimean Tatars (Qırımtatarlar)**: The indigenous people of Crimea. Their cuisine (chebureky, yantykh) is a staple of Southern Ukrainian street food. The 1944 deportation is a central trauma, and 2014 occupation is the modern tragedy.

## 4. Pedagogical notes

*   **Preposition Precision**:
    *   *Correct*: **у** Криму (in Crimea), **в** Одесі.
    *   *Error*: Students may use "на" for Crimea (Russian influence or confusion with islands), but standard Ukrainian prefers "у Криму".
*   **Soft Sign Declension**:
    *   *Миколаїв* -> *Миколаєва* (Gen), *Миколаєві* (Loc). Note the alternation `ї` -> `є`.
    *   *Херсон* -> *Херсона*, *Херсоні*.
*   **Gender of Cities**:
    *   *Одеса* (F) -> *Одеса була...*
    *   *Миколаїв/Херсон* (M) -> *Миколаїв був...*
*   **Adjective Agreement**: "Одеська область" but "Одеський порт".

## 5. Scope boundaries

**IN SCOPE (B1 Standard)**:
*   **Past/Present/Future tenses**: Fully available.
*   **Motion Verbs**: *їздити, плавати, літати* (Multidirectional vs Unidirectional).
*   **Cases**: All 7 cases, specifically Locative and Genitive for geography.
*   **Complex Syntax**: "Місто, яке було засноване..." (City which was founded...).
*   **Passive Participles**: *заснований* (founded), *розташований* (located).

**OUT OF SCOPE (Too Advanced)**:
*   **Highly Archaic/Poetic Vocabulary**: Keep descriptions modern standard.
*   **Complex Gerund Phrases (Дієприслівникові звороти)**: Avoid overusing "Подорожуючи на південь, ми..." -> Use "Коли ми подорожували..." instead for clarity if the sentence gets too long.
*   **Obscure Nautical Terminology**: Stick to *корабель, порт, капітан, моряк*, avoid specific riggings or hull parts.
