# Дослідження: Числівники: збірні та дроби

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.1.3: "кількісні числівники один (одна, одне, одні), два (дві), три, чотири... неозначені числівники: багато"
Alignment: The raw text of the B1 standard focuses on basic cardinal/ordinal declension. However, **collective numerals** (збірні) are functionally required for B1 communicative competence when describing groups of people ("нас було троє") or objects with only plural forms ("двоє дверей"), which aligns with the curriculum mapping. This module exceeds the strict explicit list of the standard to ensure natural speech patterns.

## Cultural Hooks
1.  **Magical Number 3**: In Ukrainian folklore and superstition, things often happen in threes ("Бог трійцю любить" — God loves the trinity). Folktales typically feature three brothers, three tasks, or three roads.
2.  **Holy Number 12**: The concept of "12" is culturally anchored by the **12 dishes** (12 страв) prepared for Christmas Eve (Sviat Vecher), representing the 12 apostles and the 12 months of the year.

## Common Learner Errors
1.  **The "Grandmother" Trap**: Using collectives with feminine nouns.
    *   *Error*: `троє бабусь` (three grandmothers)
    *   *Correct*: `три бабусі`
    *   *Rule*: Collectives generally do NOT go with feminine nouns (except `обидві`).
2.  **The "Phone" Trap**: Using collectives with inanimate masculine nouns.
    *   *Error*: `двоє телефонів`
    *   *Correct*: `два телефони`
    *   *Rule*: Collectives are for living males, young animals, or pluralia tantum (objects with only plural form like `ножиці`).
3.  **The Case Mismatch**: Failing to use Genitive Plural after collectives.
    *   *Error*: `троє студенти`
    *   *Correct*: `троє студентів`
    *   *Rule*: Collective numeral + Noun (Genitive Plural).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Pedagogy**: Use the "Who/What counts?" logic flowchart for collectives (Is it a man? A baby animal? A pair of scissors? -> Use Collective).
- **Immersion**: Explain the "plurale tantum" concept (`штани`, `окуляри`, `гроші`) carefully in Ukrainian, as this is the primary trigger for collective numeral usage with objects.
- **Tone**: Keep it practical. Focus on "How to count people at a table" (`нас п'ятеро`) rather than abstract math.
