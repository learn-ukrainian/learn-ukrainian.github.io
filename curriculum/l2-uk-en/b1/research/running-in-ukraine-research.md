## Cultural Hooks

1. **Kyiv Marathon founding (verified — Wikipedia "Київський марафон"):** The first Kyiv City Marathon took place on **16 жовтня 2010 року** with 546 participants from 13 countries. The 2016 edition grew to **6,600+ учасників з 50 країн**. The marathon route passed Kyiv Pechersk Lavra (Києво-Печерська Лавра), St. Michael's Cathedral (Михайлівський собор), and the Museum of the History of Ukraine in the Second World War. The marathon is part of the **Run Ukraine Running League** — a 5-event national running series. The full name used officially: **"Київський марафон незламності"**.

2. **Wartime adaptations (Wikipedia "Київський марафон незламності"):** The event continued during the full-scale invasion with adapted formats. Medical protocols included 22 emergency response teams, 40 Red Cross volunteers, 6 MotoHelp units — illustrating the safety infrastructure of major Ukrainian running events. This authentic organizational vocabulary (волонтер, медичний супровід, пункт гідратації) is directly usable in the module's "Організація та підготовка" section.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **бігти vs бігати (directed vs habitual):**
   - Error: *Я люблю **бігти** вранці* — treats directional verb as habitual
   - Correct: *Я люблю **бігати** вранці* — бігати = non-directional/habitual motion
   - Why it happens: English "run" is a single verb; learners apply the first Ukrainian verb they learn (бігти) to all contexts. The distinction is unidirectional (concrete journey, right now) vs. multidirectional/habitual (general ability or repeated action).

2. **Locative after "брати участь у" (not Genitive):**
   - Error: *брати участь **марафону*** or *брати участь **марафону*** (bare genitive)
   - Correct: *брати участь **у марафоні*** (Locative with preposition у)
   - Why it happens: A2 learners associate participation verbs with genitive; the fixed prepositional phrase "брати участь у + Loc." is a collocation that must be memorized.

3. **Aspect with time expressions — process vs. result:**
   - Error: *Він **бігав** за дві години* (imperfective + result-time) — sounds like the running happened during those two hours, but without clear endpoint
   - Correct: *Він **пробіг** за дві години* (perfective = completed result in two hours) vs. *Він **бігав** дві години* (imperfective = process lasted two hours)
   - Why it happens: Learners confuse "за + time" (result frame, needs perfective) with plain duration (imperfective). The preposition за signals completion.

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **Use "півмарафон" everywhere — not "напівмарафон"** (VESUM-verified form). Update vocabulary table accordingly.

2. **Run Ukraine Running League context:** The Kyiv Marathon is one of five events in this national series. Useful for the "Community and Running Diplomacy" section — a Ukrainian-led national infrastructure, not a derivative of any Russian sports tradition.

3. **Organizational vocabulary is rich and authentic:** Wikipedia data confirms event infrastructure terms: пункт гідратації (hydration station), медичний супровід, волонтер на трасі, маршал, реєстрація обов'язкова. These are genuine B1-appropriate register terms directly usable in section 3.

4. **Decolonized framing:** Ukrainian running culture developed independently from 2010 onward as a civil society–driven mass sport movement, connected to Euromaidan-era civic activation. Frame it as a distinctly Ukrainian civic phenomenon — community-built, amateurism-embracing, wartime-resilient. Do not reference Soviet sports infrastructure as a baseline.

5. **Grammar section (Section 4):** The бігти/бігати contrast is a motion verb pair (однонаправлений vs. різноспрямований рух) — standard Ukrainian grammar metalanguage at B1. The textbook search did not return a direct textbook passage for this specific pair, but the aspect/motion verb distinction is thoroughly covered in B1 grammar scope (§4.2.3.1). Frame examples using running contexts: *Він щодня бігає* vs. *Зараз він біжить до фінішу*.

6. **"Миля Незламних":** No Wikipedia article exists. Content writer should describe it as a charitable run format (not a marathon) organized in support of veterans and military families — a wartime solidarity event. Avoid unverified specifics (founding date, organizer names) unless sourced.

7. **Textbook connection (Grade 8, Zaболотний, tier 1):** Fitness text from Grade 8 confirms vocabulary in scope: *тренування мають бути регулярними*, *перед тренуванням необхідно розминатися*, *фізичні навантаження*. These register naturally at B1.
