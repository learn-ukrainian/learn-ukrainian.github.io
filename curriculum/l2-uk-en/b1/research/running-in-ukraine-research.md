This module focuses on applying motion verbs in the context of sports and hobbies, specifically shifting from general "movement" to specific "sporting activity".

## 3. Cultural Hook

1.  **The "Nova Poshta" Marathons**: The "New Post" (Нова Пошта) runs are ubiquitous in Ukraine. A famous campaign was "The Marathon for No One" (Марафон для нікого) in New York (village in Donetsk region) just kilometers from the front line, highlighting resilience.
2.  **Kyiv City Marathon**: The major annual event (Wizz Air Kyiv City Marathon) that takes over the city center, crossing bridges over the Dnipro. It symbolizes the modernization and European integration of Kyiv's lifestyle.
3.  **"Steel Border" / Veteran Runs**: Since 2014, and especially after 2022, running events often have a strong charitable component ("Run for Ukraine", "Veterans Do Not Break"), raising money for the Armed Forces or prosthetics.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

**Key Differences from English:**
- **Go vs. Run**: English speakers often overuse "go" (*йти*). In Ukrainian, if you do it for sport, you clearly distinguish *ходити* (walk) vs *бігати* (run).
- **Motion Verbs**: The classic *бігти* (I am running right now to the bus) vs *бігати* (I run every morning for health) distinction is critical here.
- **Prepositions of Time**: "Running for an hour" vs "Ran it in an hour".
    - *Я бігав годину* (Accusative, duration).
    - *Я пробіг за годину* (Accusative + *за*, result).

**Common Learner Errors:**
- Using *бігти* (unidirectional) for hobbies: *"Я люблю бігти"* (Wrong) -> *"Я люблю бігати"* (Correct).
- Confusing *змагання* (competition) and *забіг* (race/run).

**Teaching Sequence:**
1.  Review *бігти/бігати* forms.
2.  Introduce vocabulary (gear, distances).
3.  Read about a specific event (Kyiv Marathon).
4.  Discuss personal habits (using *бігати*).
5.  Roleplay registration for a race.

## 5. Scope Boundaries

**IN Scope (B1):**
- **Verbs of Motion**: *бігти, бігати, побігти, пробігти*.
- **Aspect**: Imperfective for process (*тренуватися*), Perfective for result (*пробігти 10 км*).
- **Cases**:
    - **Accusative** for distance (*пробіг кілометр*) and duration (*бігав годину*).
    - **Locative** for place (*бігати у парку*).
    - **Genitive** after numbers (*5 кілометрів*).
- **Time expressions**: *вранці, ввечері, щодня, раз на тиждень*.

**OUT of Scope (Reserved for B2+):**
- **Complex prefixed motion verbs** with metaphorical meanings (*уникати* - avoid/run away from, *забігати наперед* - get ahead of oneself).
- **Deeply technical anatomy terms** (hamstrings, lactate threshold) - keep it to *м'язи* (muscles) or *ноги* (legs).
- **Participle clauses**: Use "який..." structures instead of active participles (*людина, яка біжить* NOT *біжуча людина*).
