# Дослідження: Біг в Україні

## State Standard Reference
§3.4: "Дозвілля, відпочинок: способи проведення вільного часу; хобі та зацікавлення; види спорту і спортивних споруд (стадіон, басейн, корт, ковзанка)"
Alignment: This module directly addresses the "types of sport" and "leisure" requirements of the B1 standard, focusing on running as a popular hobby and mass participation event. It also touches on §3.7 (Health) regarding well-being ("самопочуття") and §3.12 (Services) regarding sports facilities ("спортзал").

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Kyiv City Marathon**: Established in 2010, this is the biggest running event in Ukraine. The route historically passes iconic landmarks like Kyiv Pechersk Lavra and the Motherland Monument. Even during the war, "Online" or "Safety First" formats (e.g., "The Longest Marathon" project) have kept the tradition alive to raise funds for defense or rehabilitation.
2. **"The Mile of the Unbroken" (Миля Незламних)**: A symbolic distance often included in modern Ukrainian running events (like the Kyiv Half Marathon), dedicated to veterans, military personnel with injuries, and showing resilience. It highlights the shift of running culture from pure sport to social solidarity.
3. **Parkrun in Ukraine**: Before 2022, Ukraine had a rapidly growing Parkrun community (Kyiv, Lviv, Odesa, Dnipro, Kharkiv). While official events were paused due to safety, local running clubs (*бігові клуби*) like *Run21* or *Tribe 10* continue to organize community runs, emphasizing "running diplomacy" and mental health support.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Motion Verbs**: Confusing *бігти* (actual motion now/one way) vs *бігати* (habitual/ability).
   *   *Error*: Я люблю бігти вранці. (Incorrect if meaning general habit)
   *   *Correction*: Я люблю **бігати** вранці.
2. **Case Governance**: Incorrect case after *брати участь* (to participate).
   *   *Error*: Я беру участь марафону / в марафону.
   *   *Correction*: Я беру участь **у марафоні** (Locative).
3. **Time/Duration**: Confusing *за* + Accusative (time to complete) vs *на* + Accusative (duration intended) vs Accusative only (duration of process).
   *   *Error*: Я бігав за годину. (Meaning "I ran for an hour")
   *   *Correction*: Я бігав **годину** (Accusative duration) OR Я пробіг дистанцію **за годину** (Result achieved in time).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Tone**: The "Kyiv Marathon Organizer" persona should be encouraging but organized. Use the "Active Voice" mandate to empower the learner ("Ви реєструєтесь", "Ви біжите").
- **Context**: Acknowledge the context of running during air raids/war (safety rules, shelters) — this is a reality for runners in Ukraine today ("Безпека понад усе").
- **Pedagogy**: Ensure the distinction between *марафон* (42.195 km) and general *забіг* (race) is clear, as beginners often call any race a "marathon".
- **Immersion**: Use *забіг* more often than *рейс* (race) or *івент* (event) to maintain linguistic purity.
