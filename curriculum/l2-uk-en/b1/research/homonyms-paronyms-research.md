## Cultural Hooks
1. **Folklore Wordplay:** Ukrainian folk riddles and humor frequently rely on homonyms (e.g., "мати" as a mother vs "мати" to have), showcasing the language's historically playful nature.
2. **Bureaucratic Traps:** The confusion between "особистий" (personal) and "особовий" (personnel) is a classic trap in Ukrainian officialdom and military terminology, highly relevant for navigating modern Ukrainian society.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Пам'ятка vs Пам'ятник:** Learners (often due to Russian interference, where "памятник" blurs these lines) use "пам'ятник" for historical landmarks. In Ukrainian, "пам'ятник" strictly means a statue/monument, while "пам'ятка" is a historical or cultural site/relic.
2. **Адрес vs Адреса:** Using "адрес" (a written formal congratulation/address) when asking for someone's location ("адреса").
