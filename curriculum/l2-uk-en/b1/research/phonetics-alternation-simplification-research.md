## Cultural Hooks
1. "Милозвучність" (Euphony) is deeply ingrained in the Ukrainian linguistic identity. The systematic avoidance of consonant clusters makes the language flow and is historically tied to the open-syllable structure of its roots.
2. Скоромовки (tongue twisters) like "Бабин біб розцвів у дощ" are traditional tools for mastering rhythm and avoiding phonetic traps, celebrated in folk and modern vocal training alike.

## Common Learner Errors
1. "Cluster Literalism": Pronouncing the silent 'т' in 'чесний' (saying [чесТний]) due to visual interference from its spelling or root word 'честь'. → Fix: Audio contrast drills and "spell explicit, speak smooth" exercises.
2. Forgetting vowel alternations: Failing to alternate 'о' to 'і' in closed syllables (saying 'кота' but also 'кот' instead of 'кіт'). → Fix: Drilling the open/closed syllable test.
3. Ignoring euphony boundaries: Saying "в Львові" instead of "у Львові", breaking the vocal flow. → Fix: Visual maps of surrounding consonants vs. vowels.
