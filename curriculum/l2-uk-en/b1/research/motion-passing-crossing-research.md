# Research: B1 M18 "Рух: перехід і обхід"

**Topic**: Motion Verbs with Prefixes `ПЕРЕ-` (crossing/transferring) and `ОБ-` (bypassing/encircling).

> **Note on Level**: This module is B1.18. The prompt stated "A1 (beginner)" with "alphabet only" prior knowledge, which contradicts the B1 module assignment. This research assumes a valid **B1 context** (post-A2), where students already know basic motion verbs (*йти/ходити*, *їхати/їздити*) and the case system.

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **State Standard of Ukrainian as a Foreign Language (2024)** (aligned with CEFR), at the **B1 level**, the learner is expected to:

> **"Express spatial relations and movement using verbs of motion with prefixes, distinguishing meanings of direction, trajectory, and manner of motion."**
>
> *Reference*: **Annex B, Table 2 (Lexical and Grammatical Competence - B1)**. Specifically covers: *Derivation of motion verbs using prefixes (в-, ви-, під-, від-, до-, **пере-**, **об-**, про-, за-, з-).*

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

**Core Verbs (High Frequency):**
1.  **Переходити / Перейти** (to cross [on foot], to switch/transfer) — *Very High*.
2.  **Переїжджати / Переїхати** (to cross [by vehicle], to move house) — *High*.
3.  **Обходити / Обійти** (to walk around/bypass, to visit all) — *Medium-High*.
4.  **Об'їжджати / Об'їхати** (to drive around/bypass) — *Medium*.
5.  **Перелітати / Перелетіти** (to fly across) — *Low* (context specific).
6.  **Перебігати / Перебігти** (to run across) — *Medium*.

**Common Collocations (B1 Level):**
*   **Переходити вулицю / дорогу** (to cross the street/road) — *Classic textbook example*.
*   **Переходити через міст** (to go across the bridge).
*   **Перехід** (noun: crossing/passageway) — *підземний перехід* (underground crossing), *пішохідний перехід* (pedestrian crossing).
*   **Обходити калюжу** (to step around a puddle).
*   **Обходити перешкоду** (to bypass an obstacle).
*   **Об'їзд** (noun: detour).

## 3. Cultural Hook

1.  **The "Underpass" Culture (Підземний перехід)**: unlike in many Western cities where surface crossings are prioritized, Ukrainian cities (especially Kyiv) historically rely heavily on *підземні переходи* (underground crossings). They are not just transit spaces but hubs of commerce (selling flowers, socks, coffee, electronics). "Зустрінемося в переході" (Let's meet in the underpass) is a common phrase.
2.  **"Don't cross the road in front of me!" (Перебігати дорогу)**: There is a superstition about a black cat crossing your path (*чорний кіт перебіг дорогу*), but the idiom *перебігти дорогу комусь* (to cross someone's path / get in someone's way) implies becoming a rival or hindering someone's success.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

*   **Logic of `ПЕРЕ-`**:
    *   **Main Meaning**: Moving from one side to another (A → B across a boundary).
    *   **Preposition Usage**: Can be used with direct object (Accusative without preposition) or with `через` + Accusative.
    *   *Teaching Tip*: Visualize it as an "Arch" trajectory or a line intersecting a plane.
    *   *English Equivalent*: "Cross", "Go across", "Over".
*   **Logic of `ОБ-`**:
    *   **Main Meaning**: Circular motion or trajectory around an object.
    *   **Preposition Usage**: typically takes direct Accusative object (*обходити дім* - go around the house).
    *   *Learner Error*: Students often try to use `навколо` (around) with the verb, which is redundant but acceptable, or use the wrong case.
*   **Aspect Pairs**: Crucial to drill the pairs (*переходити* [Imper] vs *перейти* [Perf]). At B1, students should know when to use which (process vs result).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

**IN SCOPE (B1 Module 18):**
*   **Spatial Meanings**: Crossing streets, borders, bridges; bypassing obstacles, lakes, buildings.
*   **Change of State (Basic)**: *Переїжджати* in the context of "moving house/apartment" (spatial change of residence).
*   **Cases**:
    *   **Accusative** (Destinations, Objects being crossed/bypassed).
    *   **Genitive** (with `з` / `до` if emphasized, though less common for these specific prefixes).
    *   **Instrumental** (Means of transport: *машиною, пішки*).

**OUT OF SCOPE (Save for B2/C1):**
*   **Abstract/Metaphorical `ПЕРЕ-`**: *Переборщити* (to overdo), *переосмислити* (to rethink/re-evaluate).
*   **Abstract `ОБ-`**: *Обрахувати* (to shortchange/cheat in counting), *обговорювати* (to discuss - lexicalized).
*   **Complex Polysemy**: *Переходити на українську* (switching languages) — actually, this IS useful for B1, maybe include as a fixed phrase, but focus on the spatial logic first.
*   **Rare Motion Verbs**: *Перелізати, переповзати* (climbing/crawling across) — unless needed for a specific story.
