## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

This checkpoint focuses on high-frequency aspect pairs essential for daily communication.

**High-Frequency Pairs (Core B1):**
*   **робити — зробити** (to do)
*   **читати — прочитати** (to read)
*   **писати — написати** (to write)
*   **їсти — з'їсти** (to eat)
*   **пити — випити** (to drink)
*   **готувати — приготувати** (to cook)
*   **купувати — купити** (to buy)
*   **брати — взяти** (to take)
*   **говорити — сказати** (to speak/say)
*   **бачити — побачити** (to see)

**Common Collocations & Triggers:**
*   **Imperfective (Process/Repetition):** *щодня* (every day), *часто* (often), *довго* (for a long time), *завжди* (always), *зазвичай* (usually).
*   **Perfective (Result/One-time):** *вже* (already), *нарешті* (finally), *раптом* (suddenly), *вчора* (yesterday - specific event), *за годину* (in an hour - result).

## 3. Cultural Hook

**The Philosophy of Result vs. Process:**
In Ukrainian culture, the distinction between "doing" (*робити*) and "getting it done" (*зробити*) is deeply ingrained.
*   **Completeness**: Using the perfective aspect implies a commitment or a guaranteed result. Saying "Я буду це робити" (I will be doing this) sounds weaker or evasive compared to "Я зроблю це" (I will get this done).
*   **Hospitality**: In offering food/drink, the Imperfective is often more polite/inviting (*Беріть!* - Take! [generic invitation]), while the Perfective might sound specific or urgent (*Візьміть!* - Take [this specific thing]!). However, in negations (prohibitions), the Imperfective is strictly used (*Не беріть!* - Don't take!).

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

**Key Learner Difficulties:**
*   **"I did it" Syndrome**: English speakers tend to overuse the Perfective (treating it like Past Simple) for general facts.
    *   *Error*: "Вчора я прочитав книгу." (Implying I finished it entirely).
    *   *Intention*: "I read a book yesterday." (Activity).
    *   *Correction*: "Вчора я читав книгу."
*   **Future Tense Confusion**: Mixing up *буду + infinitive* (Imperfective) and *simple perfective future*.
    *   *Error*: "Я буду прочитати." (Impossible).
*   **The "Double Imperfective" Trap**: In English "I was reading when he came", but in Ukrainian, students might miss the background action aspect.

**Teaching Recommendations:**
1.  **Binary Choice Drills**: Force students to choose based on keywords (*вже* vs *довго*).
2.  **Contextual Contrast**: Show two pictures—one person reading (process), one holding a closed book smiling (result).
3.  **Narrative Chains**: Practice sequencing events (*Встав, умився, поснідав* - sequence = perfective) vs simultaneous description (*Сонце світило, пташки співали* - background = imperfective).
