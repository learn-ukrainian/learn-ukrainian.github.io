> "об'єкт дії і стану: Іван Петрович **керує кафедрою**. Ми **захоплюємося сучасною музикою**. Вчимося розмовляти українською мовою."

**Alignment**: The module directly addresses §3.4 (sport types + sport facilities listed by name: стадіон, басейн, корт, ковзанка) and §3.7 (самопочуття as core vocabulary). The grammar focus on *займатися* + Instrumental is grounded in §4.2.2.5.1 — the "object of action/state" function of the Instrumental, exemplified by *захоплюємося музикою* (exact structural parallel to *займатися спортом*).

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Качалка у Гідропарку (Kyiv)** — The Hydropark (парк на Венеційському та Долобецькому островах між Дніпром та Русанівською протокою) contains a legendary outdoor gym called "Качалка," built informally in the Soviet era from scrap metal and repurposed industrial parts, including tank chains. It became a symbol of Ukrainian grassroots ingenuity and has operated continuously since the 1970s. Wikipedia has a general Hydropark article but no dedicated "Качалка" entry — treat this as culturally documented but cite cautiously.

2. **Чорногора — Карпатський хайкінг** — Wikipedia confirms: *"Чорногора — найвищий гірський масив Українських Карпат, до нього належить гора Говерла, найвища точка України заввишки 2061 м."* The massif runs ~40 km between the Chorna Tysa and Chorny Cheremosh river valleys. Hiking the Chornohora ridge is a well-established Ukrainian recreational tradition — culturally resonant and pedagogically grounding for the module's Carpathian tourism angle.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **\*займатися спорт** (Accusative) → **займатися спортом** (Instrumental) — English speakers default to a direct object pattern ("do sport") which maps to Accusative. The Instrumental "object of action/state" function (§4.2.2.5.1) must be taught explicitly. Same error pattern: \*займатися йога → займатися йогою; \*займатися біг → займатися бігом.

2. **Preposition confusion — *в* vs *на*** — Learners overgeneralise one preposition. The Standard itself (§3.4) names the facilities: стадіон, корт, ковзанка take *на* (на стадіон, на корт, на ковзанку); закриті споруди (спортзал, басейн) take *в* (в спортзал, в басейн). Rule of thumb: open/surface venues → *на*; enclosed buildings → *в*.

3. **Motion verb confusion — *йти* vs *ходити*** — English has no aspectual distinction for motion. Learners mix these: *Я зараз йду в спортзал* (unidirectional, right now) vs *Я ходжу в спортзал тричі на тиждень* (habitual, multidirectional). The fitness context (habits and routines) makes this a high-frequency error environment.

## Notes for Content Writing

- The standard explicitly names **стадіон, басейн, корт, ковзанка** (§3.4) — these four facility types must appear in the module with correct prepositions (*на стадіон, в басейн, на корт, на ковзанку*).
- **Wellness / ЗОЖ framing**: §3.7 anchor is *самопочуття* — this is the Standard's own vocabulary item, high frequency (5.15 IPM), use it prominently in the intro and summary.
- **Decolonized framing note**: The Soviet physical culture (*фізкультура*) system framed Ukrainian bodies and recreation through a centralised Russian-language lens. Frame the shift to *активний відпочинок* and *wellness* as a Ukrainian-centric reclamation of leisure agency — not "departure from Soviet norms" but the natural development of Ukrainian cultural practices including Carpathian hiking traditions predating Soviet codification.
- **Воркаут-майданчик**: verified in VESUM, takes preposition *на* — must be consistent with *на майданчик*.
- **1.5× overshoot target**: aim for ~5500–5800 words in Phase B to safely clear the 4000-word gate after trimming.
