## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Quality" vs. "Selection" Nuance**: In modern standard Ukrainian, *який* (quality/feature) has largely displaced *котрий* (selection from a set) in relative clauses. A verified stylistic nuance is that *який* emphasizes the descriptive nature of the clause (describing *what kind* of object it is), whereas *котрий* is strictly for "which one of the group".
2. **Folklore Pattern**: The "Hero Definition". In Ukrainian ballads and dumas, heroes are often introduced with a noun followed immediately by a defining relative clause describing their weapon or horse (e.g., "Козак, *який* мав шаблю гостру..."). This structure is fundamental to traditional storytelling.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Case Logic Failure (The "Role" Trap)**: Learners often make *який* agree with the antecedent in *case*, instead of taking the case required by its role in the *subordinate* clause.
    *   *Error*: Я бачу дівчину (Acc), *яку* (Acc) стоїть там.
    *   *Correct*: Я бачу дівчину (Acc), *яка* (Nom) стоїть там. (She is the subject of "standing").
2. **Gender/Number Discord**: Simple failure to match the antecedent's gender/number.
    *   *Error*: Це місто, *який* я люблю.
    *   *Correct*: Це місто, *яке* я люблю.
3. **Ambiguous Antecedent**: Placing the relative clause too far from the noun it modifies, especially in genitive chains.
    *   *Error*: Син директора, *який* працює тут. (Who works? The son or the director? usually attaches to the nearest noun, but learners get confused).
4. **Overuse of "Що"**: Using invariant *що* (that) instead of declined *який*, which is grammatically possible but stylistically poorer and less precise in formal/written Ukrainian.

## Notes for Content Writing
- **The Agreement Algorithm**: Explicitly teach the 2-step decision process: 1) Look BACK for Gender/Number (Antecedent), 2) Look FORWARD for Case (Function in sub-clause). Use a visual or step-by-step text guide for this.
- **Immersion**: Explain "Antecedent" simply in Ukrainian (слово, яке ми описуємо).
- **Register**: Keep examples natural. Use *який* for descriptive richness.
- **Persona**: The "Portrait Photographer" persona from the plan is perfect for *describing* people and things using *який* ("This is the lens *which* captures light best"; "The model *who* arrived late...").
