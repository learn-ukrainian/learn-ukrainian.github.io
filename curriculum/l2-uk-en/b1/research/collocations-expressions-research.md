## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

This module focuses on high-frequency "support verbs" (light verbs) that form the core of Ukrainian collocations.

### High Frequency (Essential for B1)
*   **брати/взяти участь** (to participate) - *Extremely common in formal and semi-formal contexts.*
*   **приймати/прийняти рішення** (to make a decision) - *Standard literary norm.*
*   **мати рацію** (to be right) - *Preferred over "бути правим" in standard Ukrainian.*
*   **мати значення** (to matter/have significance) - *Key for argumentation.*
*   **звертати/звернути увагу** (to pay attention) - *Essential for instructions and dialogue.*
*   **надавати/надати допомогу** (to provide help) - *Formal equivalent of "допомагати".*
*   **досягати/досягти успіху** (to achieve success) - *Common in professional/personal growth topics.*

### Medium Frequency (B1 Expansion)
*   **виконувати обов'язки** (to fulfill duties) - *Work context.*
*   **складати іспит** (to take an exam) - *Note: NOT "здавати" (surzhik/calque) in standard normative use, though common in speech. Teach "складати".*
*   **викликати інтерес** (to spark interest) - *Literary/Journalistic.*
*   **справляти враження** (to make an impression) - *Key for describing experiences.*
*   **порушувати питання** (to raise a question) - *Meeting/Discussion context.*

### Common Calques (Learner Errors to Avoid)
*   *Робити рішення* (Make a decision) -> **Wrong**. Correct: **Приймати рішення**.
*   *Брати рішення* (Take a decision) -> **Wrong**. Correct: **Приймати рішення**.
*   *Приймати участь* (Take part) -> **Wrong** (Surzhik). Correct: **Брати участь**.
*   *Здавати екзамен* (Give an exam) -> **Colloquial/Surzhik**. Standard: **Складати екзамен**.

## 3. Cultural Hook

### "Мати рацію" vs. "Бути правим"
While English speakers say "I am right" (adjective), standard Ukrainian prefers the noun-based phrase **"Мати рацію"** (literally: to have reason/rationale, from Latin *ratio*).
*   **Cultural Insight**: Using *мати рацію* signals a higher level of education and command of the standard language. *Бути правим* is often criticized by linguists as a Russian calque (*быть правым*), although it is widely understood. Teaching *мати рацію* aligns with the trend of "Linguistic Purification" (очищення мови) popular in modern Ukraine.

### The Nuance of "Участь"
The collocation **"брати участь"** (to take part) is ubiquitous in Ukrainian civic life (activism, volunteering, community gatherings). It implies active engagement, not just passive presence.
*   **Fact**: In Ukrainian media, you will often hear about citizens who *"взяли активну участь"* (took active part) in the Revolution of Dignity or volunteer movements. It is a phrase of agency.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

### Key Teaching Challenges
1.  **L1 Interference (English)**: Students will instinctively translate "make", "take", "do", and "have" directly.
    *   *Make* a photo -> **Робити** фото (Correct).
    *   *Make* a decision -> **Приймати** рішення (Not "робити").
    *   *Make* a mistake -> **Робити** помилку (Correct) OR **Допускатися** помилки (Formal).
2.  **Verb Aspect**: Collocations usually exist in pairs.
    *   Students must learn **приймати рішення** (process) AND **прийняти рішення** (result).
    *   Drill idiomatic pairs in both imperfective and perfective contexts.

### Teaching Sequence
1.  **Introduce via Contrast**: Show the English phrase and the Ukrainian equivalent. Highlight the verb change (Make -> Приймати).
2.  **Matching Exercises**: Match the "Light Verb" (мати, брати, складати) to the Noun.
3.  **Contextual Gap-Fill**: Use sentences where only the specific collocation fits.
4.  **Error Correction**: Show "Surzhik/Calque" sentences and ask students to "polish" them to standard Ukrainian.

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope (B1 Level)
*   **Grammar**:
    *   Full case system usage (Genitive/Accusative/Instrumental are critical for these collocations).
    *   Verb aspect (Imperfective/Perfective distinctions).
    *   Government of verbs (e.g., *дякувати* + Dative, but *вибачати* + Accusative/Dative nuances).
*   **Lexis**:
    *   Abstract nouns (*рішення, участь, враження, успіх*).
    *   Professional and social context vocabulary.

### OUT of Scope (Reserved for B2+)
*   **Stylistic Register**: Archaic or highly poetic collocations (*здійняти галас*, *плекати надію* - save for B2/C1).
*   **Complex Administrative Legalese**: *Вчиняти позов*, *порушувати клопотання* (Legal B2).
*   **Rare Idioms**: Fixed phraseology that is opaque in meaning (e.g., *пекти раків*) is better for a specific "Idioms" module, not general collocations.
