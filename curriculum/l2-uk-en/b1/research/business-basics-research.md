## Cultural Hooks

1. **Василь Симиренко (1835–1915)** — Ukrainian sugar industrialist and culture patron. Wikipedia confirms: *"Коштом Василя Симиренка, але без розголосу, вийшло кілька видань «Кобзаря» Тараса Шевченка."* He donated 100,000 gold karbovantsi to the Shevchenko Scientific Society in Lviv, funded Mykhailo Drahomanov in exile, and ran schools on his estate with exclusive Ukrainian-language instruction — during the Ems Decree era. His factory had a theatre with Zankovetska and Starytsky. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Симиренко_Василь_Федорович

   ⚠️ **Content accuracy flag for Phase B:** The plan states the Simirenko family funded "the 1860 edition of Kobzar." Wikipedia confirms Vasyl funded *multiple editions* but Vasyl only returned to Ukraine after 1863 (his brother Platon died). The 1860 Kobzar (St. Petersburg, ed. Kulish) predates Vasyl's active patronage period. Phase B should say "кілька видань «Кобзаря»" without specifying 1860, or attribute it to the broader Simirenko family without the specific year.

2. **Терещенко Микола Артемійович (1819–1903)** — Ukrainian entrepreneur and philanthropist, Kyiv honorary citizen. Wikipedia confirms extensive philanthropy: schools for the blind (1881, first in the empire), children's shelters, hospitals, civic buildings. The family operated 10+ sugar refineries by 1870. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Терещенко_Микола_Артемійович

   ⚠️ **Content accuracy flag for Phase B:** The plan's specific claim of "80% of profits to hospitals and universities (KPI)" and motto "Прагнення до суспільної користі" are **not confirmed** in Wikipedia. Wikipedia describes the philanthropy in detail but gives no percentage figure and no KPI connection. Phase B should describe the Tereshchenko family's documented philanthropy (hospitals, schools for the blind, civic buildings) without the unverified 80% figure.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **«на протязі» → «протягом»** — "на протязі" literally means "in a draft/breeze" (протяг = draught of air). The correct professional time marker is "протягом" (during, over the course of). Extremely high-frequency word (197 IPM) — essential correction.

2. **«кампанія» vs «компанія»** — confirmed as a classic error in Zabolotnyi Grade 7 textbook (tier 1, 2024 edition), which explicitly presents this paronyme pair. "Страхова компанія" is given as an example of correct компанія usage. Learners confuse the spelling due to similar pronunciation.

3. **Nominative instead of Vocative in formal address** — Using "Іван" or "Директор" instead of "Іване", "Пане директоре". This is a systematic error: learners apply English-style zero-marking for direct address and treat Ukrainian the same way.

4. **«відмінити» vs «скасувати»** — Both are standard Ukrainian. "Скасувати" (23 IPM) is more common in formal/business register for cancelling meetings, flights, decisions. "Відмінити" is also standard but less frequent in formal written communication.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **компанія/фірма/підприємство distinction** is the lexical core. Frequency data supports: компанія (117 IPM) > бізнес ≈ підприємство (49–48 IPM). Фірма was not GRAC-tested but is colloquial/dated — Phase B should present it as slightly informal/older usage vs. компанія.
- **ФОП (Фізична особа-підприємець)** is not in the vocabulary_hints but is a key cultural item — the dominant legal structure for Ukrainian freelancers and IT sector. Abbreviations §4.1.5 (B1 lines 2030–2034) provides a textbook hook for introducing ФОП, ТОВ, etc.
- **Instrumental case with керувати** — "керувати бізнесом", "керувати відділом" — worth a brief grammatical reminder since Instrumental governance is already taught but easily forgotten in business collocations.
- **Register §4.5.1 tie-in:** The Vocative address example in the State Standard ("Олено Петрівно, ви успішно виступили...") is a ready-made model for formal business correspondence. Use it as a canonical example.
- **Avoid:** советизми/russianisms — "угода" not "соглашення", "зарплатня" not "зарплата" (both exist but зарплатня is the recommended Ukrainian form), "протягом" not "на протязі".
- The plan's §3.9 and §3.12 reference is accurate and well-targeted to the B1 themes catalogue.
