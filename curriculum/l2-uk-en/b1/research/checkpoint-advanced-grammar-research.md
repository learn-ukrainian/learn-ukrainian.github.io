## State Standard Reference

**Primary references:**

**§4.4.2** (lines 2367–2378): *"Просте ускладнене речення: просте речення з дієприкметниковим зворотом: Три години тому ми повернулися з екскурсії, організованої для учасників конференції; просте речення з дієприслівниковим зворотом: Повернувшись з екскурсії, ми смачно пообідали в ресторані; просте речення зі вставними словами мабуть, можливо, здається."*

**§4.2.3.1** (lines 2269–2291): Full verb conjugation paradigms — aspect in all tenses, both verb classes. Note: the Standard at B1 addresses verb conjugation comprehensively including aspect. Participles (дієприкметник) and gerunds (дієприслівник) appear in the mapping description for this section.

**§4.2.1.3** (lines 2085–2093): Numeral forms — ordinal declension (третій, сотий), cardinals (один–чотири declension), indefinite numerals (багато, кілька, декілька). **Note:** The mapping description mentions collective numerals at these lines, but the actual Standard text covers ordinals and basic cardinals. Collective numerals (двоє, троє) as a distinct category are treated in textbooks as Grade 6 content (Litvinova) and form part of B1 consolidation rather than having a separate §reference at these exact lines.

**Alignment:** This module directly addresses §4.4.2 (participial/gerundial phrases in complex simple sentences), synthesising the full B1 morphology scope. The checkpoint function maps to the State Standard's expectation that B1 learners can build "просте ускладнене речення" with participial phrases.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Кордоцентризм** (Wikipedia confirmed): Ukrainian philosophical concept — *"розуміння дійсності не стільки мисленням («головою»), скільки «серцем» — емоціями, почуттями, внутрішнім, «душею»."* Associated with Hryhoriy Skovoroda and Pamfil Yurkevych. Directly motivates the diminutives section: the prevalence of пестливі форми in Ukrainian reflects this "philosophy of the heart" — intimacy, warmth, emotional directness encoded at the lexical level.

2. **"Зроблено в Україні"** — The -но/-то construction appears on product labels, government stamps, official announcements. GRAC confirms зроблено at 31.51/M (extremely high for a grammatical form). This is a live, high-visibility construction — not a textbook abstraction. Leverage this as the anchor example: learners have seen it on packaging and government sites.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Dangling gerundial phrase** → *«Йдучи додому, почався дощ»* ❌ → *«Йдучи додому, я почув дощ»* ✅ — The subject of the gerundial phrase must be the subject of the main clause. Avramenko Grade 7 treats this explicitly. High-frequency error in B1 writing.

2. **Russian calques as active participles** → *«домінуючий», «діючий», «бувший»* ❌ → *«панівний / головний», «чинний», «колишній»* ✅ — Confirmed by Avramenko Grade 11, §18 (tier 2): *"У якому реченні вжито неприродні для української мови слова?"* The whole lesson opens with this contrast. Learners carry over Russian-model active present participles.

3. **Collective numeral with feminine nouns** → *«двоє студенток»* ❌ — Prohibited. Litvinova Grade 6 (tier 1) explicitly: collective numerals combine only with (a) masculine nouns denoting persons, (b) neuter nouns, (c) pluralia tantum. Never with feminine nouns. Карти-пастки: «двоє подруг» (wrong), «дві подруги» (correct).

4. **Instrumental agent with -но/-то** → *«Мною зроблено роботу»* ❌ → either *«Я зробив роботу»* or *«Роботу зроблено»* ✅ — Glazova Grade 11 (tier 2) and Avramenko Grade 11 both flag this explicitly: the -но/-то form is agent-neutral (result-focus). Adding an instrumental agent is a calque from Russian passive constructions.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Active participle calque section** is the most culturally charged part: frame as "Ukrainian has its own native alternatives" — *чинний, колишній, панівний* are established literary vocabulary, not workarounds. This is a feature, not a constraint.
- **Passive voice philosophy**: The preference for active constructions is documented in textbooks as a stylistic norm of Ukrainian — use Avramenko's own side-by-side contrast (*«Усі інгредієнти придбано. Учні уважно читають рецепт.»*) as the anchor.
- **Diminutives section**: Кордоцентризм is a verified philosophical frame, not a romanticisation. Ground it in Skovoroda. Then move quickly to productive suffixes. Include diminutive verbs (їстоньки, спатоньки) — these are unique to Ukrainian and will surprise learners.
- **Numerals section**: The GRAC frequency of двоє (82.96/M) is remarkably high — learners will encounter it constantly. Frame as "you already know this word; now understand when NOT to use it."
- **Word count allocation**: The integration/summary section (450 words) should include the editing exercise with канцеляризм text. This is the highest-value practice for B1 → B2 transition. Do not cut it short.
- **Decolonized framing**: Never frame calque correction as "Ukrainian avoids Russian forms." Frame as "Ukrainian has its own rich system: чинний (from чин — rank/order), колишній (from коли — when, i.e. 'of a former time')." Etymology anchors the native form.
