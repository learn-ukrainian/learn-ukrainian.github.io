## Cultural Hooks

2. **Digital Passports:** In 2021, via the "Дія" (Diia) app, Ukraine became the first country in the world to give digital passports the exact same legal validity as paper ones.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Застосунок vs. Додаток:** Learners (and some native speakers) incorrectly use the false friend "додаток" for mobile apps. "Додаток" grammatically means an attachment or addition (e.g., in a document), while "застосунок" is the correct terminology for software applications.
2. **Russianisms in Tech Verbs:** Using "закачати" (a Russianism) instead of the correct Ukrainian "завантажити" (to download/install).
3. **Aspectual Confusion:** Failing to distinguish between the process of coding (писав код - imperfective) and the completion of a project (написав застосунок - perfective).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing:** Highlight Ukraine as an independent hub of innovation, not a former Soviet outsourcing appendage. Emphasize active agency ("Українські розробники створили", not "Було створено").
- **100% Immersion:** Do not use English in the prose. All grammar and vocabulary explanations must be strictly in Ukrainian.
- **IPA Rule:** Ensure no Latin IPA transcriptions appear in the main text; use them only in the YAML vocabulary file.
- **Word Formation Table:** Create a structured table for suffixes (-ник, -іст, -ння) to clearly illustrate the State Standard rules in the corresponding section.
