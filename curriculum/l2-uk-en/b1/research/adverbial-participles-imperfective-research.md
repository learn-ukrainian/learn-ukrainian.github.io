## Cultural Hooks

1. **«За двома зайцями» — п'єса і фільм**: Михайло Старицький wrote the comedy in **1883**. The iconic 1961 Ukrainian film (*Ukrainian Soviet comedy*) based on the play is widely known across Ukrainian-speaking audiences. The proverb «За двома зайцями поженешся — жодного не впіймаєш» directly encodes the module's core concept: trying to do multiple things simultaneously leads to chaos. Source: uk.wikipedia.org — *«За двома зайцями» (п'єса)* and *«За двома зайцями» (фільм)*.

2. **«Юлій Цезар по-українськи»**: The cultural concept of multitasking (described in the plan as a motivational hook) can be grounded in the Ukrainian phrase «робити кілька справ одночасно» — a natural context for demonstrating imperfective adverbial participles. No single Wikipedia article needed; this is a well-grounded pedagogical frame.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Wrong stem for suffix attachment** → `писачи` (incorrect) vs `пишучи` (correct) — Happens because learners attach the suffix to the infinitive stem (`писа-`) rather than the 3rd-person plural stem (`пишуть → пиш-`). Confirmed in Avramenko Grade 7 test: "Неправильно утворено форму дієприслівника... прочитаючи" — learners try to form perfective verbs with the imperfective suffix.

2. **Dangling participle / subject mismatch** → `Йдучи додому, почався дощ` (incorrect) vs `Йдучи додому, я зустрів друга` (correct) — The subject of the main verb must also be the performer of the participle action. Textbook-confirmed in Litvinova Grade 7 §20 and Zabolotnyi Grade 8 (дієприслівниковий зворот section).

3. **Impersonal construction error** → `Читаючи цей текст, мені стало сумно` (incorrect) — Ukrainian forbids pairing a participle phrase (which needs a subject) with an impersonal predicate (which has no subject). The fix requires converting to a personal sentence: `Читаючи цей текст, я засмутився`. Confirmed through Grade 8 Zabolotnyi's treatment of безособові речення.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Formation rule to emphasize**: The link is to 3rd-person plural present: `вони пишуть → пишуть → пиш- + учи = пишучи`. This is the reliable derivation algorithm. Do NOT derive from infinitive.
- **Conjugation class mapping**:
  - Conjugation I (пишуть, несуть, беруть): `-учи/-ючи`
  - Conjugation II (бачать, кричать, стоять): `-ачи/-ячи`
- **«не дивлячись на»**: This needs careful disambiguation — it is a prepositional phrase meaning "незважаючи на / despite", NOT a literal participle. Zabolotnyi-level (Grade 8) material — appropriate for B1.
- **Reflexive forms**: Зберігають -ся after suffix: `насолоджуючись`, `борючись` (confirmed Grade 7 Litvinova)
- **Frequency data for activity design**: `кажучи` discourse markers (50 IPM) → high-value production target; `читаючи/йдучи/слухаючи` (3–4 IPM) → recognition + controlled production
- **Decolonized framing note**: Ukrainian дієприслівник has its own well-documented grammatical tradition; the construction is present in Polish (imiesłów przysłówkowy), Portuguese (gerúndio), and other Slavic languages — Ukrainian's system is independently described in Ukrainian grammars since the 19th century. Frame positively as a Ukrainian grammatical category, not by comparison to any external standard.
