## Cultural Hooks

**1. Козацька пісня «Засвистали козаченьки» / «Засвіт встали козаченьки»**
Source: Wikipedia — [Засвистали козаченьки](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%97%D0%B0%D1%81%D0%B2%D0%B8%D1%81%D1%82%D0%B0%D0%BB%D0%B8_%D0%BA%D0%BE%D0%B7%D0%B0%D1%87%D0%B5%D0%BD%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B8)
Attributed to Маруся Чурай (1625–1653), Poltava — the song describes Cossacks rising before dawn to depart on campaign. The title verb «засвіт встали» (засвіт = before dawn; встали = rose) perfectly demonstrates ЗА- as an inhoative marker of sudden/decisive entry into action, not spatial movement. The Cossacks *заспівали* as they departed — «за-» captures that first moment of action beginning. Маруся Чурай is a historically grounded cultural figure (Хмельниччина era), making this a strong decolonized anchor.

**2. Прислів'я «Життя прожити — не поле перейти»**
Source: widely attested Ukrainian folk wisdom (cf. Zabolotnyi Grade 5 textbook context).
ПЕРЕ- in «перейти» means traversal/overcoming of a space. The proverb applies the same prefix logic abstractly: to live a life is not to simply cross a field — life requires sustained effort, repeated overcoming (ПЕРЕ- = endurance + traversal). This maps directly onto the «атомарний аналіз» teaching approach: learners can decode the proverb's logic through prefix meaning.

## Common Learner Errors

**1. ЗА- inhoative vs. ЗА- directional**
Error: *«Він зайшов говорити»* (treating ЗА- as "entering a location") instead of recognizing *заговорити* = the moment of beginning to speak (inhoative ЗА-, no spatial movement involved).
Correct form: *«Він раптом заговорив українською»*
Why it happens: Learners encounter ЗА- first as spatial (зайти = to go inside), and over-apply the spatial reading to all ЗА- verbs. The inhoative function is semantically distinct and needs explicit contrast.

**2. ВІД- "away" vs. ВІД- "in response/back"**
Error: *«відповісти»* parsed as "to answer away from" (spatial analogy) — learners feel the abstract ВІД- is arbitrary or puzzling.
Correct understanding: ВІД- on non-motion verbs marks *reaction/rebound/return action*: відповісти = to respond (send an action back), відмовитися = to separate oneself (from agreement), відкрити = to reverse the closed state.
Why it happens: The spatial "away from" reading (відійти) doesn't map cleanly to "in response" — learners need explicit bridging between physical «від-» direction and abstract reactivity.

**3. ПЕРЕ- "again" vs. ПЕРЕ- "too much / across completely"**
Error: Learners assume all ПЕРЕ- means "re-" (переробити = redo). But ПЕРЕ- can also mean exhaustive completion (переглянути всі файли = to go through all files) or excess (переїсти = to overeat).
Correct approach: ПЕРЕ- = *traversal/overcoming* — the root determines which nuance dominates. Atomic analysis (prefix + root) predicts meaning; the root anchors the semantic direction.
Why it happens: English "re-" is a near-but-imperfect equivalent; learners latch onto it and miss the traversal/excess readings.

## Textbook Evidence (for Content Writing)

**Grade 5, Zabolotnyi (tier 1):** Prefix wheel exercise — «бігти» + prefix ring (при-, за-, до-, по-, на-, в-, з-, від-, ви-, пере-, об-, під-). Asks learners what «додаткові значення» each prefix contributes. This is the foundational B1 entry point — students have seen prefix-adds-meaning at Grade 5; B1-025 upgrades this into systematic abstract analysis.

**Grade 5, Avramenko (tier 1):** Mother–son dialogue — *«Учив і вивчив — різні поняття!»* Perfect anchor for the ВИ- lesson: the difference between imperfective (process) and perfective+prefix (completed result extracted). This real-world dialogue format suits the B1 dialogue section.

**Grade 6, Golub (tier 1):** Exercise 63 explicitly asks learners to illustrate the meanings of prefix ПЕРЕ- with examples. This confirms ПЕРЕ- polysemy is taught at Grade 6 in Ukrainian schools — B1 learners revisit this with new depth.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Атомарний аналіз** framing: the plan's «prefix + root = decoded meaning» metaphor is well-supported by Grade 5 textbook methodology (prefix wheel, вчив vs вивчив contrast). Phase B should make this explicit and visual.
- **ВИ- completion logic** is the highest-frequency teaching hook: виконати (31.48 IPM), вивчити (11.48 IPM), виробляти are all extremely common. Lead with these in the практика section.
- **відповісти** (25.92 IPM) and **відмовитися** (23.69 IPM) are two of the highest-frequency words in the whole set — the ВІД- section deserves strong emphasis despite seeming abstract.
- **заговорити** (2.24 IPM) is lower frequency — treat as a conceptual anchor rather than high-priority production item.
- Immersion note: module is 100% Ukrainian (B1.2 phase). All grammar meta-language (назви префіксів, терміни «інхоатив», «вичерпність») must be explained in Ukrainian, not glossed in English. Exception: vocabulary table translations only.
- The plan's dialogue 3 (emotional drama, РОЗ- vs З-) introduces two additional prefixes not in the six core ones. Content writer should note: РОЗ- (розходження/руйнування) and З- (зближення) are valid B1 extensions but should be clearly framed as supplementary — don't overload the core ПЕРЕ-/ЗА-/ВИ-/ВІД-/ПІД-/ПРИ- set.
