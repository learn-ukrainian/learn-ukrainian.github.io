# Research: Motion Prefixes in Other Verbs (B1 M23)

**Module**: B1 M23 "Префікси руху в інших дієсловах"
**Level**: B1 (Intermediate)
**Topic**: Applying motion prefix logic (*при-, ви-, пере-*) to non-motion verbs.

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **State Standard for Ukrainian as a Foreign Language (2024)**, Level B1 requires mastery of word formation mechanisms, specifically verbal prefixation.

> **Section: Морфологія. Дієслово.**
> "Студент повинен розуміти та вживати дієслова доконаного та недоконаного виду, утворені префіксальним способом, для позначення результату дії, початку дії, її завершеності або міри вияву (наприклад: *читати — прочитати, писати — записати, робити — зробити*)."
>
> **Section: Словотвір.**
> "Розуміння семантичних відтінків, які вносять префікси *в-, ви-, під-, від-, до-, пере-, за-, на-, при-* у значення базового дієслова."

This module bridges the gap between literal spatial meanings (learned in M16-M22 Motion Verbs) and abstract semantic meanings in general vocabulary.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

The focus is on high-frequency verbs where the prefix logic is transparent and parallels the spatial meaning.

### High Frequency (Core A2/B1)
These are essential daily verbs formed with "motion" prefixes:

*   **ПРИ- (Arrival / Joining)**
    *   *приготувати* (to prepare/cook [bring to readiness]) - Top 100 verbs
    *   *приєднатися* (to join)
    *   *привітати* (to greet/welcome)

*   **ВИ- (Exit / Result / Exhaustion)**
    *   *вивчити* (to learn [completely])
    *   *випити* (to drink up)
    *   *виробити* (to produce/manufacture)

*   **ПЕРЕ- (Crossing / Change / Repetition)**
    *   *перекладати* (to translate [carry across languages])
    *   *переробити* (to redo)
    *   *переписати* (to rewrite)

*   **ЗА- (Entry / Start / Deepening)**
    *   *записати* (to write down / record)
    *   *заплатити* (to pay [for])
    *   *закрити* (to close)

*   **ВІД- (Departure / Reaction / Cancellation)**
    *   *відповісти* (to answer [give back])
    *   *відкрити* (to open)
    *   *відмовити* (to refuse)

### Lower Frequency (B1/B2 Context)
*   *переосмислити* (to rethink)
*   *виговоритися* (to talk oneself out/vent)
*   *засидітися* (to stay too long)

## 3. Cultural Hook

**1. The "Kitchen Talk" Phenomenon (*Наговоритися*)**
In Ukrainian culture, the prefix *на-* or *ви-* often implies emotional satisfaction or exhaustion.
*   *Ми вчора так гарно **по**сиділи!* (We sat/hung out so well!)
*   *Мені треба **ви**говоритися.* (I need to speak out/vent my problems.)
This reflects a culture that values deep interpersonal connection and shared time. The prefix adds the emotional "result" to the neutral action of sitting or speaking.

**2. "Za" as the Universal "Too Much" or "Into a State"**
The prefix *за-* captures the Ukrainian tendency to describe entering a state deeply.
*   *Запрацюватися* (to work too much/lose track of time working).
*   *Зачитатися* (to get lost in reading).
This construction is unique and very "Slavic" — implying an involuntary immersion in an activity.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

### Key Differences from English
*   **English**: Uses **Phrasal Verbs** (particles).
    *   *Write* -> *Write **down***
    *   *Think* -> *Re**think*** (Latin prefix) or *Think **over***
*   **Ukrainian**: Uses **Prefixes**.
    *   *Писати* -> ***За**писати*
    *   *Думати* -> ***Пере**думати*

**Teaching Strategy**:
Use the "Spatial Metaphor" anchor.
1.  Remind them of the **Motion** meaning (*Вийти* = Go out).
2.  Show the **Abstract** meaning (*Вивчити* = Learn out/completely).
3.  Show the connection: "Out" implies "to the end/result".

### Common Learner Errors
*   **Mixing Prefixes**: Using *пере-* (re-) when they mean *від-* (back). E.g., trying to say "reply" (*відповісти*) but using a "re-" logic like *переповісти* (which means retell).
*   **Aspect Confusion**: Students often don't realize that adding a prefix usually makes the verb **Perfective**.
    *   *Я **про**читав книгу* (I read the book - Result).
    *   They might try to use it for present tense: *Я **про**читаю зараз* (I will read), whereas English speakers want to say "I am reading through".

## 5. Scope Boundaries

**IN SCOPE (What to teach):**
*   **The Big 6 Prefixes**: *при-, ви-, пере-, за-, від-, під-* applied to common stems (*писати, читати, робити, говорити, думати*).
*   **Semantic Logic**:
    *   *При-* = Result / Attachment
    *   *Ви-* = "Out" / Result
    *   *Пере-* = "Re-" / Change / Excess
    *   *За-* = Start / "Into"
    *   *Від-* = Back / "De-"
*   **Aspect Shift**: Briefly mention that Prefix = Perfective (usually).

**OUT OF SCOPE (Save for later):**
*   **Complex Double Prefixes**: *понапридумувати*.
*   **Polysemy of *по-***: *По-* has too many meanings (start, little bit, result). Stick to *по-* as a simple perfectivizer in A2/B1, don't overanalyze it here as a "motion" metaphor unless clear.
*   **Motion Verbs themselves**: Students already covered *йти/їхати* in M16-M22. Do not re-teach conjugation of *йти*. Focus on *other* verbs.
*   **Reflexive nuances**: *Наїстися, догратися* (unless as a fixed phrase). Keep to transitive verbs first (*переписати текст*).
