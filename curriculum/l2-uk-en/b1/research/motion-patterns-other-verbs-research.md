## State Standard Reference

§4.3.8: "Утворення префіксальних дієслів руху: іти – прийти, перейти, дійти, відійти, зайти, вийти; їхати – приїхати, переїхати, доїхати, від'їхати, заїхати, поїхати, виїхати, об'їхати."

**Alignment:** The Standard names motion-verb prefix formation as the core word-formation item for B1. This module extends that logic to non-motion verb roots, applying the *same spatial-prefix meanings* abstractly — the natural pedagogical next step after b1-22 (Motion full prefix integration). §4.3.8 establishes the prefix system on motion verbs; this module activates it across the full verbal lexicon.

**Note on scope:** The Standard lists the motion-verb paradigm as the exemplar. The transfer to non-motion verbs is the generalization step implied by B1-level word-formation mastery (§4.3 overall). No separate §number exists for this abstraction — it is the application layer above §4.3.8.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **«Засвистали козаченьки» / «Засвіт встали козаченьки»** — Ukrainian folk song attributed to the semi-legendary poet-singer Маруся Чурай (1625–1653) of Poltava, from the era of the Khmelnytsky Uprising. The song depicts a Cossack who, loyal to duty, **rises and departs** at dawn — the prefix **ЗА-** in «засвистали» encodes the precise moment of action starting (inceptive). Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Засвистали_козаченьки
   - *Pedagogical anchor:* Use «засвистали» / «засвіт встали» as the model inceptive — the Cossacks *заспівали*, *заговорили*, *зарушили* at a specific moment. The song is iconic and emotionally resonant, providing a memorable ЗА- anchor.

2. **Маруся Чурай** (1625–1653) — the poet herself exemplifies Ukrainian verbal creativity: her songs have been described as «записані в народній пам'яті» (written into folk memory), a collocation that demonstrates ЗА- as fixation/inscription. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Маруся_Чурай
   - *Pedagogical anchor:* «записати в пам'ять» ↔ «записати в зошит» — both use ЗА- as fixation, not spatial entry.

3. **Ukrainian proverb: «Життя прожити — не поле перейти»** — directly cited in the plan. Verified as authentic Ukrainian folk wisdom. ПЕРЕ- here signals traversal/completion of a long process. This pairs naturally with the plan's «Atomic Analysis» metaphor for prefix decomposition.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **ЗА- as duration vs. inception:** Learners confuse «заговорити» (to start speaking — inceptive, one moment) with «говорити» (ongoing). Error: *«Він заговорив цілий день»* → Correct: *«Він говорив цілий день»* / *«Він заговорив і не зупинявся»*. Why: English has no dedicated inceptive prefix, so learners over-extend ЗА- or under-use it.

2. **ВІД- spatial vs. abstract (reaction):** Learners map ВІД- only to spatial "away" and miss the reaction/response meaning. Error: *«відповісти» translated as "to answer away"* rather than "to respond/reply." Confusion deepens because English "reply" = ВІД- semantically but learners don't see it. Correct framing: ВІД- = «у відповідь на дію» (in response to an action). Parallels: відповісти, відреагувати, відмовитися (refuse = respond with refusal).

3. **«написати в зошит» vs. «записати в зошит»:** Learners use both interchangeably. Error: *«Напиши мені свій номер»* (write me your number — simple inscription) vs. the intended *«Запиши мій номер»* (write it down / fix it in memory). ЗА- signals fixation of information, НА- signals contact with surface. The distinction is real and productive but invisible to English speakers.

4. *(Bonus — ПЕРЕ- polysemy):* «переписати» has two distinct meanings — (a) rewrite a text (process: ПЕРЕ- = redo) and (b) transfer property ownership (administrative: register/re-register). Learners hit this false friend in legal/documentary contexts.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Prefix coverage:** Plan covers ПЕРЕ-, ЗА-, ВИ-, ВІД-, ПІД-, ПРИ-, РОЗ-, З-. Eight prefixes across five sections at 4000 words = ~500 words per prefix on average. Keep explanations dense with examples, not discursive.
- **«Atomic Analysis» metaphor:** Central organizing frame of the plan. Must be introduced early in Пояснення and used consistently. Name it explicitly: «Атомарний аналіз дієслова».
- **Frequency-informed priority:** Teach ВИ- (виконати 31.48 IPM), ВІД- (відповісти 25.92 IPM, відмовитися 23.69 IPM) as the primary production targets. ПЕРЕ- and ЗА- are secondary but culturally rich.
- **Immersion = 100% Ukrainian.** No English in explanations — use Ukrainian metalanguage. Disambiguation footnotes only for outright false friends (e.g., переписати admin vs. textual).
- **Dialogue register:** Job interview (Діалог 1) → formal register, no colloquialisms. Apartment renovation (Діалог 2) → neutral/informal. Emotional drama (Діалог 3) → informal, РОЗ- emotional verbs.
- **РОЗ- and З-:** Not listed in State Standard §4.3.8 examples but are genuine Ukrainian prefix pairs with high pedagogical yield (розійтися/зійтися, розізлитися/заспокоїтися). Include as "expansion" after core prefixes.
- **Decolonized framing:** Ukrainian prefix system has its own coherent spatial logic documented in Ukrainian academic tradition (Словотвір as linguistic science). Do NOT frame any prefix meaning as "borrowed from" or "similar to Russian" — describe each prefix's Ukrainian semantic field independently. The inceptive ЗА-, exhaustive ВИ-, reactive ВІД- are features of Ukrainian verbal morphology, documented in Ukrainian grammars (Вихованець, Городенська).
