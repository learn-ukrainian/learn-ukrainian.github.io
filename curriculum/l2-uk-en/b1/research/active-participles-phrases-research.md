# Research: Active Participles & Stylistic Alternatives (B1 M44)

**Module:** B1 M44 "Активні дієприкметники та їхній стиль"
**Topic:** Avoiding active present participles (calques) in favor of proper Ukrainian syntax.
**Actual Level:** B1 (Intermediate) - *Note: Prompt indicated A1, but subject matter "Active Participles" requires B1 prerequisite knowledge (cases, aspects, complex sentences).*

## 1. Grammar: State Standard & Orthography Reference

The core grammatical guideline for this module focuses on the **normative avoidance** of active present participles ending in **-ючий/-ачий**.

**Ukrainian Orthography 2019 (Чинний правопис)** references:
> **§ 120. Активні дієприкметники теперішнього часу**
> "В сучасній українській літературній мові активні дієприкметники теперішнього часу із суфіксами -уч- (-юч-), -ач- (-яч-) мають обмежене вживання. Вони не властиві українській народнорозмовній мові..."
> *(In modern literary Ukrainian, active present participles... have limited usage. They are not characteristic of spoken Ukrainian...)*

**Key Concept:**
Ukrainian prefers finite verbs (relative clauses) or verbal adjectives/nouns over the particulate structure common in Russian or English.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency & Collocations

Focus on replacing high-frequency "calques" (incorrect forms) with natural equivalents.

**Common "Bad" Forms (To Avoid) vs. Natural Ukrainian:**

| Incorrect / Clumsy (Calque) | Natural Equivalent (High Frequency) | English |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **бажаючий** (wishing person) | **охочий** (adj) / **той, хто бажає** | willing / whoever wishes |
| **починаючий** (beginning) | **початківець** (noun) | beginner |
| **оточуючий** (surrounding) | **навколишній** (adj) | surrounding/ambient |
| **хвилюючий** (worrying/exciting) | **зворушливий** (touching) / **бентежний** | exciting/moving |
| **слідуючий** (following/next) | **наступний** (adj) | next |
| **існуючий** (existing) | **навний** (adj) / **сущий** (context) | existing |
| **пануючий** (ruling) | **панівний** (adj) | ruling/dominant |

**Key Collocations:**
- *Навколишнє середовище* (Environment) — NOT *оточуюче*.
- *Наступна зупинка* (Next stop) — NOT *слідуюча*.
- *Охочі взяти участь* (Those willing to participate) — NOT *бажаючі*.

## 3. Cultural Hook

**"The Dynamic Language"**
Ukrainian grammar reflects a "dynamic" worldview. While English often uses static descriptions ("I am waiting"), Ukrainian prefers action ("I wait"). Using active participles (like "waiting person") freezes the action and sounds artificial or bureaucratic (simulating the " канцелярит" style).

**The "Surzhyk" Fighter:**
Using forms like "віддихаючий" (resting one) or "читаючий" (reading one) is a hallmark of "Surzhyk" (mixed Russian-Ukrainian). Mastering the replacement of these forms with *підрядні речення* (clauses like "той, що читає") is a rite of passage for moving from "translating in your head" to "thinking in Ukrainian."

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

**Common Learner Errors (L1 Interference):**
- **The "-ing" Trap:** English speakers try to translate "-ing" directly to "-ючий".
  - *Error:* "Я бачу танцюючого чоловіка." (I see a dancing man).
  - *Correction:* "Я бачу чоловіка, **який танцює**." (I see a man **who dances**).

**Teaching Sequence:**
1.  **Identify:** Show a "heavy" sentence with participial clutter.
2.  **Transform:** Use the "Magic Words" **який / що / хто** (who/which/that) to expand the participle into a clause.
3.  **Substitute:** Introduce specific adjectives (*наступний, охочий*) that replace common participles entirely.

**Stylistic Nuance:**
Explain that while *some* active participles exist in scientific terminology (e.g., *крокуючий екскаватор* - walking excavator), in daily life and literature, they are poor style.

## 5. Scope Boundaries (B1 Context)

**IN Scope (B1):**
- **Relative Pronouns:** *який, яка, яке, які* (in all cases).
- **Demonstrative Pronouns:** *той, та, те, ті*.
- **Syntax:** *Clause creation* ("Той, хто..."; "Все, що...").
- **Adjectival Vocabulary:** High-frequency replacements (*наступний, минулий, майбутній*).

**OUT of Scope (for this module):**
- **Passive Participles:** (e.g., *зроблений, написаний*) — These are standard and correct, but belong in the Passive Voice module.
- **Deep Gerunds (Дієприслівники):** (e.g., *читаючи, зробивши*) — "Doing/Having done". This is a separate B1/B2 topic. Focus here is strictly on adjectival participles (*which kind?*).
- **Archaic Forms:** Church Slavonic participles used in poetry.
