## Cultural Hooks
1. Geometric Center of Europe: Located near the village of Dilove in Zakarpattia Oblast, marked by a historical monument from 1887.
2. 7 Natural Wonders of Ukraine: Includes diverse sites like Lake Synevyr (often called the "Sea Eye" of the Carpathians) and the pristine biosphere reserve Askania-Nova.

## Common Learner Errors
1. *на горах* (Wrong) → *у горах* (Correct) — Confusion over spatial prepositions; 'гори' takes 'у/в' for location.
2. *в курорті* (Wrong) → *на курорті* (Correct) — 'курорт' is treated as an open surface/area conceptually, requiring 'на'.
3. Confusing *відпустка* (work leave for adults) with *канікули* (school/university break for students).
