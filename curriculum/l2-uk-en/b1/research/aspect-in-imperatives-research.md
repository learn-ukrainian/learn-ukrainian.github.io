## 1. Grammar: State Standard Reference
According to standard curriculum progressions for Ukrainian as a Foreign Language (B1):

*   **Imperfective (Недоконаний вид) in Imperative**:
    1.  **Polite Invitations & Requests**: Focus on the action/process itself.
        *   *Заходьте!* (Come in!)
        *   *Сідайте!* (Sit down!)
    2.  **Process/Duration**: Commands to do something for a period or repeatedly.
        *   *Читай це щодня.* (Read this every day.)
    3.  **Prohibitions (Negation)**: The general command "Do not do X".
        *   *Не відкривай вікно.* (Don't open the window.)

*   **Perfective (Доконаний вид) in Imperative**:
    1.  **Concrete Result**: A specific, one-time action with a result.
        *   *Відкрий вікно.* (Open the window [now/specifically].)
        *   *Напиши своє ім'я.* (Write your name.)
    2.  **Warnings (with Negation)**: "Watch out, don't let X happen."
        *   *Не впади!* (Don't fall! / Mind you don't fall!)
        *   *Не запізнись!* (Don't be late!)

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency
Focus on high-frequency pairs where the aspect distinction drastically changes the nuance.

| Imperfective (Invitation/Process) | Perfective (Specific Result) | Context |

## 3. Cultural Hook
*   **The "Guest Protocol"**: In Ukrainian culture, using the **Imperfective** for offers (*Беріть, їжте, пийте* — Take, eat, drink) is the standard for politeness. It implies "Go ahead, engage in the process, make yourself at home." Using the Perfective (*Візьміть, з'їжте*) can sound official, medical (take this pill), or overly directive, lacking the warmth of hospitality.
*   **"Дивись!" (Look/Watch out)**: The imperative *Дивись* is often used as a particle to signal a warning before a negated perfective verb. *Дивись, не загуби!* (Watch out, don't lose it!).

## 4. Pedagogical Notes
*   **The "Negation Trap"**: A common learner error is keeping the Aspect consistent when switching from Positive to Negative.
    *   *Correct*: Відкрий (Perf) вікно. -> Не відкривай (Impf) вікно.
    *   *Error*: Не відкрий (Perf) вікно. (This sounds like "Don't you dare open it" or a warning "Don't accidentally open it").
*   **English Interference**: English uses "Don't" for both prohibitions and warnings. Ukrainian strictly separates them via Aspect.
    *   "Don't touch it" (Rule) -> *Не чіпай* (Impf).
    *   "Don't break it" (Warning) -> *Не розбий* (Perf).
