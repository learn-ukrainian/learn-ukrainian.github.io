## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 reference

According to the **State Standard for Ukrainian as a Foreign Language (Level B1)**, the learner is expected to demonstrate the following competencies regarding motion:

> **§ Lexical and Grammatical Competence (B1)**
> "The user can understand and use a sufficient range of vocabulary and grammatical structures to describe movement, travel, and spatial relations. This includes the correct use of:
> *   Unidirectional and multidirectional verbs of motion (йти/ходити, їхати/їздити, etc.) without prefixes.
> *   Prefixed verbs of motion expressing direction (в-, ви-, при-, під-, від-, до-, пере-, про-, за-).
> *   Figurative meanings of motion verbs in common idioms.
> *   The aspectual usage of motion verbs in past, present, and future tenses."

*Note: This checkpoint validates the comprehensive mastery of the motion verb system introduced in Modules 16-24.*

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency & Collocations

Since this is a checkpoint, the vocabulary focus is on **consolidation** of high-frequency motion verbs and their derivatives.

### High-Frequency Pairs (The "Big 14")
1.  **йти / ходити** (to walk/go) — *Vital*
2.  **їхати / їздити** (to go by vehicle) — *Vital*
3.  **бігти / бігати** (to run) — *High*
4.  **летіти / літати** (to fly) — *High*
5.  **нести / носити** (to carry) — *High*
6.  **везти / возити** (to transport) — *High*
7.  **вести / водити** (to lead) — *High*
8.  **пливти / плавати** (to swim/sail) — *Medium*

### Key Prefixed Forms (B1 Level)
*   **при-** (arrival): прийти вчасно, приїхати додому
*   **ви-** (exit): вийти з кімнати, виїхати з міста
*   **пі-** (departure): піти на роботу, поїхати у відпустку
*   **пере-** (crossing): перейти вулицю, переїхати міст
*   **за-** (stopping by/entering deep): зайти в магазин, заїхати за другом
*   **про-** (passing through): пройти мимо, проїхати зупинку

### Common Collocations
*   **Час летить** (Time flies) — Figurative
*   **Справа йде** (Things are going [well]) — Figurative
*   **Ходити в гості** (To visit/go as a guest) — Cultural staple
*   **Їхати зайцем** (To ride without a ticket) — Idiom

## 3. Cultural Hook

**The Culture of "Going Visiting" (Ходити в гості)**
In Ukrainian culture, the concept of "ходити в гості" is a ritual, not just a casual visit. It implies bringing a " гостинець" (small gift, usually sweets or cake) and expecting a full table of food. It is distinct from merely "dropping by" (зайти).
*   *Fact*: You typically do not go empty-handed. "З пустими руками не ходять."

**Public Transport Etiquette (Передайте за проїзд)**
The phrase "Передайте, будь ласка" (Pass it on, please) in marshrutkas involves a complex chain of motion (pass money forward, pass change back). It relies on the community "movement" of currency.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

### Key Learner Errors (B1 Level)
1.  **Unidirectional vs. Multidirectional confusion in Past Tense**:
    *   *Error*: "Вчора я йшов у парк." (Yesterday I was walking to the park - implies process).
    *   *Correction*: "Вчора я ходив у парк." (Yesterday I went to the park and came back - a completed round trip).
    *   *Teaching Tip*: Emphasize that "ходив" in the past often equals "was there and returned".
2.  **Prefix Confusion (При- vs. По-)**:
    *   Learners often mix up "Він прийшов" (He arrived - he is here) and "Він пішов" (He left - he is gone).
    *   *Mnemonic*: **При-** connects (arrival), **По-** pushes away (departure).
3.  **Transitive Motion Verbs (Нести vs. Везти)**:
    *   Confusion between carrying by hand (нести) and transporting by vehicle (везти).
    *   *Tip*: Check if the subject is walking or driving.

### Teaching Sequence Recommendations
1.  **Diagnostic Quiz**: Start immediately with a mix of simple pairs and prefixed forms to identify weak spots.
2.  **Visual Mapping**: Use diagrams for prefixes (arrows in/out/through/across).
3.  **Narrative Integration**: Move away from isolated sentences. Have students describe a full "Day in the Life" or a "Dream Trip" to force the use of sequences ( вийшов -> сів -> поїхав -> приїхав -> зайшов).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope (B1 Checkpoint)
*   **All 14 basic pairs** of motion verbs (unidirectional/multidirectional).
*   **Core directional prefixes**: в-, ви-, при-, від-, під-, до-, пере-, про-, за-, об-, з-.
*   **Aspectual nuances**: Focus on result (Perfective/Prefix) vs. Process/Repetition (Imperfective).
*   **Transitive verbs**: нести, везти, вести (and their prefixes).
*   **Figurative meanings**: Common idioms (час йде, дощ іде, поїзд іде).

### OUT of Scope (Reserved for B2/C1)
*   **Archaic or rare motion verbs**: брести, лізти (unless basic literal), повзти (in depth).
*   **Complex abstract prefixes**: на- (accumulation via movement, e.g., наїздити кілометри), роз- (dispersal in abstract senses).
*   **Stylistic nuances of "блукати" vs "ходити"** (nuance is fine, but not tested rigorously).
*   **Participles created from motion verbs**: (e.g., "прибувший" - generally avoided in modern Ukrainian, usually active participles are limited).
