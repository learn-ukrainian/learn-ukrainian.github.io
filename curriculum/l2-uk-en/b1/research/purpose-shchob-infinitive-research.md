# Дослідження: Підрядні речення мети: щоб + інфінітив

## State Standard Reference
§4.4.3: "складнопідрядні речення з підрядною частиною мети (сполучник щоб): Ми ходимо в музеї Львова, щоб краще знати історію цього старовинного міста"
Alignment: This module directly teaches the B1 requirement for purpose clauses with `щоб`, focusing on the same-subject construction (using infinitive) as illustrated in the Standard's example.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Lesya Ukrainka's Resilience**: The famous line from her poem "Як дитиною, бувало..." — *"Щоб не плакать, я сміялась"*. This perfectly illustrates the grammar (purpose) while introducing a core Ukrainian cultural value: stoicism and inner strength in the face of adversity.
2. **Folk Wisdom**: The proverb *"Щоб рибу їсти, треба у воду лізти"* (To eat fish, you must get into the water). Demonstrates the structure in a timeless context of work ethic.
3. **Lviv Museums**: Building on the State Standard's own example ("Ми ходимо в музеї Львова..."), we can anchor the practice section in a tour of Lviv's cultural sites (e.g., Lviv National Museum, Arsenal), reinforcing the "learn to preserve" narrative.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Conjugated Verb instead of Infinitive**: Learners often say *"Я прийшов, щоб я допоміг"* (calque from English "so that I could help") instead of *"Я прийшов, щоб допомогти"* (same subject = infinitive).
2. **Subject Mismatch**: Using the infinitive when subjects differ. Error: *"Я хочу, щоб ти читати"* (I want you to read). Correction: *"Я хочу, щоб ти читав"* (Requires past tense/subjunctive form).
3. **Confusing `щоб` vs `що`**: Using `щоб` for simple explanatory clauses (*"Я знаю, щоб ти прийшов"* -> *"Я знаю, що ти прийшов"*).
4. **Overuse of `для того, щоб`**: While correct, native speakers often drop `для того` in spoken language for better flow.

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Tone**: The "Lesya Ukrainka" hook provides a slightly serious, poetic undertone suitable for the "Senior Language Specialist" persona.
- **Pedagogy**: Explicitly contrast "same subject" (this module) with "different subject" (next module) to prevent fossilized errors, but focus practice ONLY on the infinitive form here.
- **Style**: Use `аби` in literary examples to show richness, but focus on `щоб` for active production.
