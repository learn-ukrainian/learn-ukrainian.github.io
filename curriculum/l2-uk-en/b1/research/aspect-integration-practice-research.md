## Cultural Hooks
1. **Chumak Way (Чумацький шлях)**: The long, enduring journey of Chumaks is a perfect metaphor for Imperfective aspect (*їхали, везли, співали*), contrasting with the specific events of arrival or incident (Perfective: *приїхали, продали, сталося*). Fits "Ethnographer" persona.
2. **Folk Wisdom**: "Доки грім не гряне, рак не свисне" (Until thunder strikes, a crayfish won't whistle) — illustrates the strict *limit* nuance of **доки** (until a specific Perfective event occurs).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Sequential vs. Simultaneous**: Learner says *"Коли я **читав** (Imp) книгу, я **ліг** (Perf) спати"* (While I was reading, I went to sleep — illogical) instead of *"Коли я **прочитав** (Perf) книгу, я **ліг** спати"* (When I had read the book, I went to sleep).
2. **Habitual vs. One-time**: Using Perfective for habits. Error: *"Кожного дня він **прочитав** новини"* → Correction: *"Кожного дня він **читав** новини"*.
3. **Future Tense Mixing**: Trying to use "буду" with Perfective verbs (*"я буду написати"* instead of *"я напишу"* or *"я буду писати"*).
