# Research: Health and Wellness (B1 Module 63)

**Level**: B1 (Intermediate)
**Topic**: Health, Wellness, Prevention, Lifestyle
**Module**: B1.63 "Здоров'я та самопочуття"

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **State Standard for Ukrainian as a Foreign Language (2024)**, proficiency level B1 requires the ability to communicate in the sphere of "Health and Physical Condition" (Annex B, Lexical Minima).

> **Section B.1.4: Health and Body Care**
> *   "The user can describe symptoms, understand medical advice, discuss healthy lifestyle choices, and express physical and emotional states."
> *   **Grammatical Competence**: Use of complex sentences for advice (subjunctive, imperative), reflexive verbs related to self-care (*почуватися*, *лікуватися*), and noun governance in medical contexts.

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

At the B1 level, students transition from basic "body parts/pain" (A1/A2) to "systemic health/process" vocabulary.

### High Frequency (Core B1)
*   **Concepts**: *самопочуття* (well-being/how one feels), *імунітет* (immunity), *профілактика* (prevention), *дієта* (diet), *шкідлива звичка* (bad habit).
*   **Verbs**: *одужувати/одужати* (to recover), *дотримуватися (режиму)* (to follow a regimen), *уникати (стресу)* (to avoid stress), *записатися (до лікаря)* (to make an appointment).
*   **Adjectives**: *корисний* (healthy/useful), *шкідливий* (harmful), *психологічний* (psychological), *хронічний* (chronic).

### Low Frequency (Contextual/Passive)
*   *обстеження* (medical examination/check-up)
*   *щеплення / вакцинація* (vaccination)
*   *страхування* (insurance)
*   *знеболювальне* (painkiller)

### Common Collocations
*   *вести здоровий спосіб життя* (to lead a healthy lifestyle)
*   *мати шкідливі звички* (to have bad habits)
*   *скаржитися на...* (to complain of...)
*   *проходити обстеження* (to undergo a check-up)
*   *зміцнювати імунітет* (to strengthen immunity)

## 3. Cultural Hook

1.  **"Helsi" and Digital Health**: Ukraine has rapidly digitized its healthcare system. The "Helsi" app is ubiquitous for booking appointments (*записатися до сімейного лікаря*). The concept of a "family doctor" (*сімейний лікар*) is central to the modern reform, acting as the gatekeeper for specialists.
2.  **Self-Medication Culture & Pharmacies**: Ukrainians often treat mild ailments at home (*лікуватися вдома*) using traditional methods (tea with viburnum/raspberry, mustard plasters) or by going directly to a pharmacy (*аптека*). Pharmacists often give medical advice.
3.  **Sanatoriums and Mineral Waters**: "Wellness" in Ukraine historically links to *санаторії* (sanatoriums) and *мінеральні води* (places like Truskavets or Morshyn). "Drinking the water" (*пити воду*) is a medically prescribed ritual.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

*   **Diff: "Feeling" vs. "Touching"**:
    *   *Почуватися* (reflexive) = to feel (state of health/emotion). *Я добре почуваюся.* (I feel well).
    *   *Відчувати* (transitive) = to sense/feel something (pain, joy, cold). *Я відчуваю біль.* (I feel pain).
    *   *Error*: Students often use *відчувати* for general well-being (*Я відчуваю добре* - WRONG).
*   **Diff: "Treat" vs. "Cure"**:
    *   *Лікувати* (process) vs. *Вилікувати* (result).
    *   *Лікуватися* (reflexive) = to undergo treatment/treat oneself.
*   **Diff: "Useful" vs. "Healthy"**:
    *   Ukrainian uses *корисний* (useful) for "healthy food/habits". *Корисна їжа* (Healthy/Beneficial food).
    *   *Здоровий* is usually describing a living being or an organ (*здорова дитина*, *здорове серце*).
*   **Sequence**: Teach *самопочуття* phrases -> Lifestyle collocations (*вести...*) -> Medical advice structures (*Вам варто...*, *Раджу...*).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope (B1)
*   **Topics**: Healthy lifestyle, prevention, basic medical system interaction, mental well-being (stress, rest).
*   **Grammar**:
    *   Imperative mood for advice (*Пийте більше води*).
    *   Subjunctive/Conditional for polite suggestions (*Я б радив...*, *Вам варто було б...*).
    *   Complex sentences with *щоб* (purpose): *Щоб не хворіти, треба...*
    *   Genitive case for negation and absence (*немає апетиту*, *уникати стресу*).

### OUT of Scope (Advanced/Specialized)
*   **Topics**: Detailed anatomy (internal organs beyond basic), specific disease names (pneumonia, diabetes - except cognates), surgical procedures, insurance policy details.
*   **Grammar**:
    *   Passive voice participles in medical reports (*діагноз встановлено*).
    *   Deep stylistics of medical documentation.
