## Cultural Hooks

1. **Буковель — найбільший гірськолижний курорт Східної Європи** (Wikipedia, uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Буковель): 58 km total trail length, 68 individual trails, 19 lifts (capacity ~35,000 persons/hour), located at 920 m (village Polyanytsia, Ivano-Frankivsk oblast), peak Dovha 1372 m. Founded 2001–2002. Recognized as world's fastest-growing ski resort in 2012. Visitor numbers grew from 48,000 (2003/04) to 2.5 million (2024). **PLAN CORRECTION NEEDED**: plan says "68 км трас" — this is incorrect; 68 is the NUMBER of trails (кількість трас: 68), total LENGTH is 58 km. Content must say "68 трас загальною довжиною 58 км."

2. **Драгобрат — найвисокогірніший курорт Українських Карпат** (Wikipedia, uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Драгобрат): located in Svydovets massif, valley at 1300–1400 m, near mountain Stih (1704 m) and Blyznytsya (1883 m), 18 km from Yasinia (Zakarpattia). Snow cover November–May due to the horseshoe geography of the massif. Views of Chornohora (Hoverla 2061 m, Petros 2020 m). Settlement Yasinia founded 1583, near "Centre of Europe" geographical point (45 km away). Wild, minimally developed — contrast with Bukovel's resort infrastructure.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **«підйомник» замість «витяг»** → Preferred: **крісельний витяг**, **бугельний витяг** — "підйомник" exists in Ukrainian but is less precise in ski context and is the Soviet-era generic term; "витяг" is the standard contemporary resort terminology used at Bukovel documentation and ski press.

2. **«кататися на лижах» vs. «кататися з лижами» / «їздити на лижах»** → Correct: **кататися на лижах** (prepositional: на + locative). Learners from English backgrounds may over-apply "with skis" phrasing. Additionally, confusing **кататися** (recreational movement, pleasure) with **їздити** (purposeful travel) — "я катаюся на лижах" (I ski for fun) vs. "я їду до Буковеля" (I'm traveling to Bukovel).

3. **«ски-пас» spelling** — The term "ски-пас" is a direct English borrowing not standardized in VESUM. Learners often misspell as "ски пас" (no hyphen). **Teach alongside native alternative: абонемент на підйомник / скі-пас (абонемент)**. Note: фрірайд IS in VESUM as the standardized form.

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **Factual correction**: Do NOT write "68 км трас" — write "68 трас загальною довжиною 58 км" (source: Wikipedia infrastructure section).
2. **витяг polysemy**: When first introducing "витяг" as ski lift, explicitly gloss the ski-specific meaning with both collocates (крісельний витяг, бугельний витяг, шестикрісельний витяг) to prevent confusion with "bank extract" meaning.
3. **ски-пас**: Present as internationally borrowed term, provide паспортний абонемент / абонемент на витяг as Ukrainian alternative. Note the VESUM gap — this is an emerging loanword.
4. **Textbook anchor**: Grade 3 Ponomarova (2020) has a Bukovel text suitable as a reading comprehension model: "Буковель — найпопулярніший гірськолижний курорт у Карпатах." The textbook even uses it to practice writing (3–4 sentences) — mirrors this module's production section.
5. **Decolonized framing**: Bukovel's development (founded 2001) is entirely post-independence Ukrainian entrepreneurship with Austrian (Plan-Alp) and Canadian (Ecosign) technical partners — no Soviet/Russian framing required. Carpathian ski culture predates Soviet period; the Hutsul tradition of winter mountain life is an authentic Ukrainian cultural frame.
6. **Register**: Plan specifies розмовний register — dialogue-heavy booking and equipment sections are on-target. Mix informal ski-slope register ("пухляк", "фрірайд") with functional transactional register (booking, rental dialogue).
7. **Colour-coded trail system**: Буковель uses international standard — сині (beginner), червоні (intermediate), чорні (advanced), могул, фрі-райд. This is useful vocabulary for reading resort maps authentically.
8. **B1 immersion**: Module 92 is well within B1.1+ (full immersion phase starts M06). No English prose permitted outside vocabulary table translations.
