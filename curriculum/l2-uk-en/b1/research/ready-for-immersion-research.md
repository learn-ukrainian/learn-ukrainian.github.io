Alignment: This module serves as a "Metalanguage Checkpoint," ensuring learners possess the vocabulary to understand grammar instructions (`інструкції`), rules (`правила`), and academic discourse (`освітній процес`) required for the subsequent 100% immersion modules. It directly addresses the "Education" thematic area by treating language learning itself as the subject matter.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Letter "Ґ" as Identity**: The letter "Ґ" was banned from the Ukrainian alphabet in 1933 during Soviet reforms (to align orthography closer to Russian) and only restored in 1990. This makes *правопис* (orthography) not just a set of rules, but a matter of national identity. (Source: "Repressed Renaissance" history).
2. **Proverb on Precision**: *"Слово — не горобець, вилетить — не спіймаєш"* (A word is not a sparrow; once it flies out, you won't catch it). This underscores the cultural value placed on careful speech and precision, relevant to a module about grammatical accuracy.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **False Friends**: Confusing **правило** (rule) with **право** (legal right).
   *   *Error*: "Я маю правило це робити" (I have the *rule* to do this) instead of "Я маю право" (I have the *right*).
2. **Sound vs. Letter**: Confusing **звук** (sound) and **буква** (letter).
   *   *Context*: Learners often ask "How many letters?" when they mean sounds, or vice versa, especially with iotated vowels like 'ї' (2 sounds, 1 letter) or digraphs 'дж' (2 letters, 1 sound).
3. **Stress shifts (Наголос)**: Ignoring stress patterns that distinguish meaning.
   *   *Example*: **пла́чу** (I cry) vs. **плачу́** (I pay); **замóк** (castle) vs. **зáмок** (lock).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Tone**: As a checkpoint before full immersion, the tone should be encouraging but firm on terminology. "You need these tools to survive the next level."
- **Visuals**: Consider a "visual dictionary" or diagram for parts of speech (Tree of Speech?) to make abstract concepts concrete.
- **Immersion Prep**: Explicitly state: "From the next module, explanations will be in Ukrainian. Can you handle: 'Дієслово змінюється за часами'?"
