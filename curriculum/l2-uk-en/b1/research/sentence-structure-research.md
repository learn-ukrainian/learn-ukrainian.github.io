# Дослідження: Структура речення

## State Standard Reference
§4.4: "Синтаксис. Просте речення (розповідне, питальне, спонукальне). Просте ускладнене речення (з однорідними членами, дієприкметниковим та дієприслівниковим зворотами, вставними словами). Складне речення (складносурядне, складнопідрядне)."
Alignment: This module provides the metalanguage (terminology) required to analyze the sentence structures mandated by §4.4, specifically equipping learners to identify and label sentence parts as defined in the standard's examples.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Underlining" Ritual**: Every Ukrainian student learns to analyze sentences visually ("синтаксичний розбір"). There is a rigid standard: Subject = one line, Predicate = two lines, Object = dotted line, Attribute = wavy line, Adverbial = dot-dash line. This visual code is universal in Ukrainian education.
2. **"Головні та другорядні члени"**: The concept of "Principal Members" (Subject/Predicate) vs "Secondary Members" (Object/Attribute/Adverbial) is the fundamental hierarchy taught in Ukrainian syntax, shaping how native speakers view sentence architecture.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Dangling Participles (Дієприслівниковий зворот)** → *Ідучи додому, почався дощ* (Wrong) vs *Коли я йшов додому, почався дощ* (Correct). **Why**: The subject of the main verb must also be the doer of the gerund action.
2. **Case Governance (Керування)** → *Завдавати шкоду* (Wrong) vs *Завдавати шкоди* (Correct, Genitive). **Why**: Learners often default to Accusative for direct objects, missing abstract Genitive requirements.
3. **Numerals as Subjects** → *21 студенти* (Wrong) vs *21 студент* (Correct). **Why**: Complex agreement rules with compound numerals ending in 1, 2, 3, 4.

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Visuals**: Use the standard underlining conventions (CSS or SVGs) to demonstrate sentence analysis. This is the authentic "Ukrainian way" to see syntax.
- **Term-First**: Introduce the term `підмет` before `subject`. The goal is to make them think in Ukrainian grammatical categories.
- **Diagramming**: Show a simple linear diagram of a sentence ( [ ... ], ( ... ). ) to introduce the notation for complex sentences used in Ukrainian schools.
