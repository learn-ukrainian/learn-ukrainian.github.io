# Research Notes: A1 Final Exam

**Track**: A1
**Module**: a1-final-exam (M44)
**Researched**: 2025-01-10
**Type**: COMPREHENSIVE ASSESSMENT MODULE

## Модуль призначення
Фінальна атестація рівня A1: перевірка всіх граматичних структур, лексичних груп, комунікативних навичок згідно з Ukrainian State Language Standard 2024.

## Assessment Methodology (TTT Comprehensive)

### Final Exam Philosophy
This is **summative assessment**:
- **Test ALL A1 competencies**: Grammar, vocabulary, skills (listening, reading, speaking, writing simulated via activities)
- **Teach NOTHING NEW**: Zero new grammar or vocabulary
- **Test AGAIN**: Comprehensive, integrative tasks across all 4 domains

### Assessment Domains (CEFR-Aligned)

#### 1. Grammar Competence (Word Target: ~117 words)
**What's tested:**
- Nouns: Gender recognition (він/вона/воно)
- Verbs: Present, past, future tenses (all conjugations)
- Cases: Nominative, accusative, genitive, locative (all 4 A1 cases)
- Adjective/Pronoun agreement: Gender and case matching
- Modals: могти, хотіти, мусити, треба

**Activity type:** Comprehensive quiz (30 items)

#### 2. Vocabulary Mastery (Word Target: ~100 words)
**What's tested:**
- Thematic groups: їжа, транспорт, покупки, сім'я, здоров'я
- Numbers: 0-100, time expressions
- Basic phrases: greetings, requests, thanks, apologies
- Situational vocabulary: café, store, transport, phone, emergencies

**Activity type:** Mixed quiz + fill-in (integrated with grammar)

#### 3. Listening Comprehension (Word Target: ~100 words + activity)
**What's tested:**
- Understanding simple dialogues (2-3 speaker exchanges)
- Identifying key information (numbers, locations, times)
- Following simple narratives (what happened? who? where?)

**Activity type:** Quiz (15 items, audio comprehension simulation)
**Note**: Real audio not generated in curriculum, but tasks written as if audio exists

#### 4. Reading Comprehension (Integrated)
**What's tested:**
- Understanding short texts (50-100 words)
- Identifying main ideas
- Extracting specific information (names, places, actions)

**Activity type:** Embedded in narrative + comprehension questions

#### 5. Speaking/Production Tasks (Word Target: ~123 words)
**What's tested (simulated):**
- Role-play scenarios: ordering at café, buying ticket, making phone call
- Describing self/family/routine
- Asking/answering basic questions

**Activity type:** Fill-in (10 items, structured production prompts)
**Note**: Speaking assessed via written prompts (oral exam simulation)

## Структура фінального тесту (від плану)

### Part 1: Warm-up (121 words)
**Purpose**: Orient learner to exam structure
- What will be tested (grammar, vocabulary, skills)
- How each part is assessed (points/weighting)
- Tips for success (time management, staying calm, review strategies)

### Part 2: Grammar Review (117 words)
**Coverage**:
- Nouns and gender (він/вона/воно assignment)
- Verbs: теперішній час (present)
- Verbs: минулий час (past, -в/-ла/-ло/-ли)
- Verbs: майбутній час (future, буду + infinitive)
- Cases: називний (nominative), знахідний (accusative), родовий (genitive), місцевий (locative)
- Agreement: adjectives and pronouns matching gender/case

### Part 3: Vocabulary Review (100 words)
**Thematic groups**:
- Їжа та напої (M18, M19, M35, M36)
- Транспорт (M15, M39, M40)
- Покупки (M17, M18, M37, M38)
- Сім'я та люди (M32, M12)
- Здоров'я (M31)
- Час та числа (M17, M23)
- Базові фрази (greetings, requests, M07, M41, M42)

### Part 4: Skills Assessment (100 words)
**Coverage**:
- Аудіювання: розуміння простих діалогів (2-3 exchanges)
- Читання: розуміння коротких текстів (50-100 words)
- Говоріння: рольові ситуації (simulated via structured prompts)

### Part 5: Comprehensive Test (123 words)
**Integrative tasks**:
- Combine grammar + vocabulary in realistic scenarios
- Multi-step practical tasks (buying + ordering + traveling)
- Problem-solving (handle unexpected situations)

### Part 6: Production Narrative (189 words)
**Фінальний наратив**: Подорож Україною
- Use ALL A1 skills in one cohesive story
- Demonstrate range: past/present/future, all cases, rich vocabulary
- Show readiness for A2 (more complex sentence structures, smoother discourse)

## Activities планування

### Activity 1: Quiz - Listening Comprehension (15 items)
**Format**: Short dialogues (written as if audio), comprehension questions
- Dialogue 1: At café (ordering, prices)
- Dialogue 2: Buying ticket (destination, time, platform)
- Dialogue 3: Phone call (who's calling, why, when to meet)
- Dialogue 4: At doctor (symptoms, advice)
- Dialogue 5: Giving directions (where, how to get there)

### Activity 2: Quiz - Grammar and Vocabulary (30 items)
**Format**: Comprehensive mixed quiz
- Gender identification (5 items)
- Case selection (5 items)
- Verb conjugation (10 items: present, past, future)
- Adjective/pronoun agreement (5 items)
- Vocabulary in context (5 items)

### Activity 3: Fill-in - Oral Assessment Tasks (10 items)
**Format**: Structured production prompts (role-play written responses)
- Task 1: Introduce yourself (name, where from, what do)
- Task 2: Describe your family (3-4 sentences)
- Task 3: Order at restaurant (menu, preferences)
- Task 4: Buy train ticket (destination, time)
- Task 5: Make phone call (call friend, arrange meeting)
- Task 6: Ask for directions (polite question, understand response)
- Task 7: Describe your day (routine, activities)
- Task 8: Talk about past weekend (past tense)
- Task 9: Make future plans (future tense)
- Task 10: Handle emergency (lost item, ask for help)

## Scoring and Assessment Notes

### Implicit Scoring Criteria
- **Grammar (30%)**: Accuracy in case, gender, tense
- **Vocabulary (25%)**: Range and appropriateness
- **Comprehension (25%)**: Understanding dialogues and texts
- **Production (20%)**: Ability to form coherent responses

### Pass Threshold (Ukrainian State Standard)
- **60%+**: Pass A1 level
- **80%+**: Strong A1, ready for A2
- **<60%**: More A1 practice needed
