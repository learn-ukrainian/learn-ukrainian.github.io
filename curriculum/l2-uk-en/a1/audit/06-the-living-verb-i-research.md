## Cultural Hook (1-2 facts)

**1. Pro-drop phenomenon in Ukrainian:**
- **Fact:** Unlike English, Ukrainian drops pronouns naturally because verb endings clearly indicate the person. "Читаю" (I read) is complete without "я" — the -аю ending tells you it's first person singular. This makes Ukrainian more concise and contextual.
- **Source:** Comprenders.com Ukrainian verb guide, The Languages Ukrainian verbs reference
- **How to use:** Explain in Presentation section why pronouns are optional — learners can say "Працюю" instead of "Я працюю"

**2. Master key metaphor:**
- **Fact:** First Conjugation is the "master key" — learning this single pattern unlocks hundreds of Ukrainian verbs (танцювати, малювати, співати, гуляти, etc.). It's the most productive verb class in modern Ukrainian.
- **Source:** Ukrainian grammar references, frequency lists
- **How to use:** Motivational note in Warm-up — emphasize that mastering this pattern gives immediate access to expressing dozens of daily activities
