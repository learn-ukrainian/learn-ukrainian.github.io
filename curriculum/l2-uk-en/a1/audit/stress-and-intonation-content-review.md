## Issues Found

### CRITICAL (blocks deployment)

### HIGH (should fix before deployment)

### MEDIUM (fix if possible)
1. **Self-check answers shown inline** — The Summary section lists self-check questions with answers immediately following each question. This eliminates the self-assessment value. Consider separating questions from answers or using a spoiler/collapse pattern.

### LOW (informational)
1. **Plan section naming mismatch** — Plan (meta YAML) uses "Наголос — Stress" for the first section; content uses "Що таке наголос? (What is Stress?)". Both cover the same content. Plan names "Інтонація — Intonation" while content uses "Музика мови: Інтонація". Minor — content titles are actually more engaging.
2. **говорю missing** — Plan's meta YAML mentions "говорЮ" as a last-syllable stress example, but the content does not use this word. Not a required vocabulary item, so this is informational only.
3. **Extensive practice dialogues** — Six dialogues (roughly 150+ words) in the summary section are substantial for a 1200-word module. While pedagogically valuable, they contribute heavily to word count. This is a positive rather than a problem.
