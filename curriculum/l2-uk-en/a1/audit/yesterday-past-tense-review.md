## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Grammar/Style (Russianism Risk)
- **Location**: Section "Міні-діалог 1: Після вихідних", Andriy's line.
- **Original**: "Я гуляв по старому місту."
- **Problem**: "По" + Dative ending (*місту*) is a Russianism pattern or colloquial Surzhyk. Standard Ukrainian uses "по" + Locative (*по місті*) or Instrumental without preposition (*містом*). For A1, Locative is safer but the ending must be correct.
- **Fix**: "Я гуляв у старому місті." or "Я гуляв у центрі." (Simpler).

### Issue 2: Vocabulary Scope in Activities
- **Location**: Activity `fill-in` ("Complete with Past Tense"), Item 11.
- **Original**: "Вчора ми ___ у ресторані. (Answer: обідали)"
- **Problem**: The verb *обідати* (to have lunch) is not taught in this module and "Daily Routine" is Module 25 (future).
- **Fix**: Change answer to "були" (were) or "їли" (ate). If using *їли*, ensure options reflect it. Recommendation: use "були" (options: був, була, було, були).

### Issue 3: Quiz Content Scope
- **Location**: Activity `quiz` ("Time Expressions"), Items 9 and 10.
- **Original**: Questions asking for "last Monday" and "last weekend".
- **Problem**: The specific phrases *минулого понеділка* and *минулого вихідного* are not in the lesson text. While the *rule* is taught, the vocabulary *понеділок* and *вихідний* (in Genitive) adds friction. Also, "минулого вихідного" (singular) is awkward for "last weekend" (usually plural *вихідні*).
- **Fix**: Replace with questions about "last year" (rephrase) or "yesterday". Or simple: "What does 'Вчора' mean?".
