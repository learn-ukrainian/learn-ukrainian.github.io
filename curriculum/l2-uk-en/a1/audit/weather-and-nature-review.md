## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Grammar Scope - Genitive Plural
- **Location**: Section "Examples in Context" / "Природа України"
- **Original**: "У парку багато **дерев** і **квіток**." / "У парку багато **птахів**."
- **Problem**: This uses Genitive Plural (дерев, квіток, птахів). A1 students typically know Nominative Plural (дерева, квіти, птахи). "Багато" + Genitive Plural is complex morphology not yet taught.
- **Fix**: Change to Nominative Plural constructions: "У парку є **дерева** і **квіти**." / "У парку живуть **птахи**."

### Issue 2: Vocabulary Leak in Activities
- **Location**: Activity "Group Sort: Seasons", "Group Sort: Animals vs Plants"
- **Original**: items "лід", "зелено", "кущ", "трава", "ведмідь"
- **Problem**: These words appear nowhere in the content or vocabulary list. A1 students cannot sort words they haven't seen.
- **Fix**: Replace with taught vocabulary.
    - `лід` -> `сніг` (or remove if duplicate) or `холодно`
    - `зелено` -> `тепло` or `квітка`
    - `кущ` -> `дерево`
    - `трава` -> `квітка`
    - `ведмідь` -> `собака` or `кіт`

### Issue 3: Inconsistent Season Adverbs
- **Location**: Section "Examples in Context" / "Погода в Україні"
- **Original**: "**Весною** тепло і йде дощ."
- **Problem**: The module introduces *Влітку, Взимку, Восени* (adverbs). *Весною* is Instrumental case noun. Research notes explicitly state "Instrumental Case... is not yet learned".
- **Fix**: Use the standard adverbial form "**Навесні**" which aligns better with the others pedagogically and avoids introducing a new case ending unexpectedly.
