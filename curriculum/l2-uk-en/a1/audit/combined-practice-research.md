# Research Notes: Combined Practice

**Track**: A1
**Module**: combined-practice (M43)
**Researched**: 2025-01-10
**Type**: CHECKPOINT MODULE (Assessment-focused)

## Модуль призначення
Інтеграційна практика всіх практичних навичок A1.4 (M35-42): кафе, ресторан, магазин, квитки, транспорт, телефон, екстрені ситуації.

## Assessment Methodology (TTT + Integration)

### Checkpoint Philosophy
Unlike regular teaching modules, this is a **practice checkpoint**:
- **Test**: Check if learners can combine multiple skills
- **Teach**: Provide linking language (connectors, sequencing)
- **Test**: Verify ability to handle multi-step scenarios

### Assessment Focus Areas

#### 1. Multi-Step Scenarios (Sequential Actions)
Learners must demonstrate ability to:
- Plan multiple activities in sequence
- Use connectors: спочатку, потім, нарешті
- Switch contexts smoothly (cafe → transport → phone)

#### 2. Problem-Solving Integration
Learners must handle:
- Unexpected changes (forgot wallet, bus is late)
- Adaptations (Plan B)
- Combining practical solutions (call friend, find ATM)

#### 3. Contextual Flexibility
Learners must show:
- Appropriate register switching (formal/informal)
- Vocabulary recall across contexts
- Grammar application in new combinations

## Key Linguistic Features

### Connectors and Sequencing (NEW for M43)
- **спочатку** (first, at first)
- **потім** (then, afterwards)
- **нарешті** (finally, at last)
- **тому що** (because) - причина
- **якщо** (if) - умова
- **але** (but) - протиставлення
- **також** (also) - доповнення
- **разом** (together) - спільна дія

### Problem-Solving Language
- **проблема** (problem)
- **план** (plan)
- **вирішити** (to solve)
- **змінити** (to change)

## Integrated Scenarios (від плану)

### Scenario 1: Ранок → кафе → магазин → транспорт
**Context**: Morning routine with multiple errands
- Wake up, go to café for breakfast
- Stop at store for groceries
- Take transport to meet friend
**Skills tested**: M35, M37, M40
**Complications**: Café card machine broken (need cash), bus delayed (phone friend)

### Scenario 2: Проблема → рішення → допомога
**Context**: Problem arises, must solve it
- Lost wallet at restaurant (M36)
- Call restaurant (M41)
- If not found, call friend for help or go to police (M42)
**Skills tested**: M36, M41, M42
**Complications**: Restaurant doesn't answer phone (must go in person)

### Scenario 3: Телефон → зустріч → ресторан
**Context**: Social planning with adjustments
- Call friend to plan meeting (M41)
- Buy tickets for event (M39)
- Go to restaurant together (M36)
**Skills tested**: M41, M39, M36
**Complications**: Event sold out (plan B: just restaurant)

## Activities планування

### Activity 1: Fill-in (8 items)
Extended scenario with blanks (all connectors, context-switching vocabulary)

### Activity 2: Fill-in (10 items)
Multi-part conversations (3-4 exchanges in different locations)

### Activity 3: Quiz (6 items)
Handle unexpected situations (comprehension + appropriate response selection)

### Activity 4: Quiz (10 items)
Complex interactions (integrate vocabulary from 2-3 different modules)

## Assessment Criteria (Implicit)

### Successfully Completed If:
✅ Learner can sequence 3+ activities using connectors
✅ Learner can switch contexts without confusion
✅ Learner can adapt when plans change
✅ Learner can integrate vocabulary from different practical domains
✅ Learner can solve simple problems using A1 resources

### Common Challenges to Address:
⚠️ Connector overuse (too many "потім" in a row)
⚠️ Context confusion (using café language at market)
⚠️ Rigidity (can't adapt when situation changes)
⚠️ Grammar breakdown under multi-step pressure

## Pedagogical Structure

### Warm-up (149 words)
- Introduce concept of "планування" (planning)
- Teach connectors in isolation first
- Show 1-2 simple examples of multi-step plans

### Complex Scenarios (187 words)
- Present 3 full scenarios with commentary
- Highlight where connectors appear
- Show how vocabulary integrates across contexts
- Point out problem-solving moments

### Practice (191 words)
- 3 dialogues with complications:
  1. Plans change due to problem
  2. Combination of shopping + eating out
  3. Surprise element requires adaptation

### Production Narrative (223 words)
- **Long narrative**: A day with many tasks
- **Integration**: All A1.4 practical skills appear
- **Problem-solving**: At least 2 unexpected complications resolved
- **Tone**: Realistic, relatable, encouraging

## Cultural and Practical Notes

### Ukrainian Daily Life Realities
- **Multi-tasking**: Combining errands (магазин + кафе на одному маршруті)
- **Flexibility**: Plans change often due to infrastructure, weather
- **Community**: Calling friends for help is normal, expected
- **Problem-solving**: Resourcefulness valued ("вихід завжди є")
