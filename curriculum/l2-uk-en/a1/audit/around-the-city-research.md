## Grammar Analysis

### Core Structures (Required)
1. **Directional adverbs**: прямо (straight), наліво (left), направо (right), назад (back)
2. **Location question**: Де знаходиться...? (Where is...?)
3. **Direction question**: Як дістатися до...? (How to get to...?)
4. **Prepositions of location**: біля (near), навпроти (opposite), за (behind), перед (in front)
5. **Locative case in context**: у парку, на площі, в центрі (applied from M13)

### Pedagogical Sequencing
- Start with basic places in Ukrainian city
- Introduce directional vocabulary (прямо, наліво, направо)
- Teach location prepositions (біля, навпроти)
- Practice giving and following directions
- End with production (give directions to own home)

## Cultural Hooks

### Ukrainian City Layout
1. **Central площа (майдан)**: Every Ukrainian city has central square (майдан)
   - Kyiv: Майдан Незалежності (Independence Square)
   - Lviv: Площа Ринок (Market Square)
   - Odesa: Потьомкінські сходи (Potemkin Stairs area)
2. **Аптека culture**: Pharmacies (аптеки) are everywhere, trusted for minor ailments
3. **Пошта (Укрпошта)**: Traditional post offices still widely used (bills, packages)
4. **Зупинка**: Bus stops often have informal names (not just numbers)

### Giving Directions (Cultural Notes)
1. **Landmarks over street names**: Ukrainians often use landmarks
   - "Біля Макдональдса" (near McDonald's)
   - "Навпроти банку" (opposite the bank)
   - Street names sometimes changed post-independence → landmarks more reliable
2. **Gesture culture**: Pointing and gesturing common when giving directions
3. **Informal help**: Strangers often walk with you part-way to show route
4. **Formal address**: Use Ви when asking strangers for directions

## Activity Design Notes

1. **Follow directions on a map** (10 items)

2. **Place → description** (15 items)
   - аптека → "Тут купують ліки"
   - пошта → "Тут відправляють листи"

### Fill-in Activities (×2)
3. **Ask for and give directions** (8 items)

4. **Complete with prepositions** (15 items)
   - Аптека ___ (біля) парку.
   - Банк ___ (навпроти) школи.
   - Preposition + genitive practice

## Production Task Ideas

### Give Directions to Own Home
- Learner explains route from city center to their home
- Uses directional adverbs and prepositions
- Example: "Від центру їдьте прямо. Потім поверніть наліво біля банку."

### Tourist and Local Role-Play
- Role-play asking for and giving directions
- Practices both sides of the conversation
- Incorporates politeness (Ви) and authentic language

## Common A1 Errors to Address

1. **Preposition + case confusion**: *біля аптека → біля аптеки (genitive!)
2. **Left/right confusion**: наліво vs ліворуч (both correct, наліво more common)
3. **Motion vs location**: Я йду в банк (accusative) vs Я в банку (locative)
4. **Overgeneralization of в**: *в площі → на площі (fixed expression)
