## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Linguistic Accuracy (IPA Consistency)
- **Location**: Presentation / Tables "Numbers 0-20" vs "Tens (20-100)"
- **Problem**: Inconsistent IPA notation. The 0-20 table uses `/ɔ/`, `/dʋ/`, and `/d͡z/` (e.g., 20: `/ˈdʋad͡zʲatʲ/`), while the Tens table uses `/ɑ/`, `/v/`, and `/dt͡s/` (e.g., 20: `/ˈdvɑdt͡sʲɑtʲ/`). Ukrainian standard /дз/ is an affricate /d͡z/, and stress/vowel quality symbols should be consistent.
- **Fix**: Harmonize the IPA symbols across both tables. Use `/ɔ/` and `/d͡z/` consistently as per standard Ukrainian phonology.
