## Pedagogical Quality

**Lesson arc:** WELCOME (cultural hook about vyshyvanka) -> PRESENT (color table) -> PRESENT (agreement) -> PRESENT (clothing) -> PRACTICE (носити + dialogue) -> SUMMARY (self-check). Adequate but mechanical.

**Cognitive load:** CONCERN. The introduction section (lines 13-92) is excessively fragmented. It consists of very short Ukrainian sentences (2-5 words each) followed by English translations, creating a choppy, list-like reading experience. For example:
```
Привіт! Це кольори.
Hello! Here are the colors.

Це наш одяг.
Here is our clothing.

Кольори й одяг.
Colors and clothing.

Це нова тема.
The topic is new.
```
This pattern continues for ~40 lines. It reads like a word list, not a lesson. The beginner feels like they are reading flash cards, not being taught by a patient tutor.

**Warmth:** LOW. The module reads mechanically. Missing encouraging phrases like "Great job!", "Don't worry if this feels tricky", "You're making amazing progress". The opening "Welcome back! Today we learn colors and clothing." is flat. No preview of what the learner will be able to do.

**Cultural hooks:** Vyshyvanka symbolism (intro) with червоний/чорний/білий meaning. Good cultural anchor but delivered in the same choppy format.

**носити teaching:** The Accusative case chunk teaching (lines 289-328) is actually well done — clear pattern shown (червона сукня -> червону сукню), explicit contrast, and a practical dialogue. This is the strongest section.

## Grade Justification

**Grade: C**

The module covers all 4 plan objectives and has adequate activity variety. The носити + Accusative section is well-executed, and the cultural hooks around vyshyvanka symbolism are genuine and relevant.

However, multiple issues prevent a higher grade:
- 2 Russianisms ("красивий/красива") — a project hard-fail criterion. While the audit system passed these (it checks a limited list), they are explicitly on the banned list.
- Numerous imperative and conjugated verb violations that survived the full validation pipeline
- Mechanical, choppy writing style in the first half of the module that fails the "patient tutor" voice
- Section word count violations beyond tolerance

The module needs a manual fix pass focusing on: (1) replacing красивий/красива with гарний/гарна, (2) replacing imperatives and conjugated verbs with English or noun phrases, (3) rewriting the choppy intro/colors sections into flowing tutoring prose.

The C grade (not F) is because: no more than 2 Russianisms (the "3+ Russianisms = F" threshold), all 4 objectives are met, and the core grammar teaching is accurate.
