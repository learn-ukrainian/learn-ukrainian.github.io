## Cultural Hooks

### Ukrainian Café Culture

**Historical Context**:
- **Lviv Tradition**: Coffee capital of Ukraine since Austro-Hungarian times
- **Kaviarnya vs. Kav'yarnia**: Both spellings used, regional variation
- **Social Space**: Cafés as meeting places, not just quick service

**Modern Café Scene**:
- **Specialty Coffee Movement**: Third-wave coffee in major cities (Kyiv, Lviv, Kharkiv)
- **Local Roasters**: Lviv Coffee Manufacture, Чорна Кава (Black Coffee)
- **Unique Concepts**: Themed cafés (literary, art, vintage)

### Lviv Coffee Legends
- **Georg Franz Kolschitzky**: Legend of first coffee house (disputed history)
- **Kamianka (Кам'янка)**: Historic coffee house, operating since 18th century
- **Coffee Festivals**: Annual coffee festivals in Lviv

### Ordering Customs
- **Waiter Service**: Not self-service like Western chains
- **Table Seating**: Wait to be seated or choose table yourself (context-dependent)
- **Menu Reading**: Often laminated, sometimes chalkboard specials
- **Payment**: Usually pay at table, not counter

### Tipping Culture (Чайові)
- **Standard**: 5-10% for good service
- **Optional**: Not obligatory but appreciated
- **Cash Preferred**: Even if paying by card, tip in cash
- **Rounding Up**: Common to round bill (138₴ → 150₴)

### Coffee Terminology
- **Кава по-львівськи**: Lviv-style coffee (with spices, honey, chocolate)
- **Раф-кава**: Raf coffee (espresso with cream, vanilla sugar)
- **Американо**: Americano (but eспресо is most traditional)

### Typical Menu Items (A1-Level)
- **Drinks**: кава, чай, сік, компот, мінеральна вода
- **Pastries**: круасан, тістечко, штрудель, чізкейк
- **Light Meals**: сендвіч, салат, суп

## Grammar Notes

### Case Integration Practice
1. **Accusative**: Direct object ordering
   - Я візьму каву (I'll take coffee)
   - Принесіть меню (Bring the menu)

2. **Genitive**: Quantity/specification
   - Чашка чаю (cup of tea)
   - З молоком (with milk)

3. **Locative**: Location context
   - У кав'ярні (in the café)

### Polite Imperatives
- **Plural Form**: Принесіть, візьміть (formal/polite)
- **Singular Form**: Принеси, візьми (informal)
- **Softeners**: Будь ласка, можна

### Future Tense Preview
- **Візьму**: (I will take) - natural in ordering context
- **Not formal teaching**: Just exposure for formulaic use
