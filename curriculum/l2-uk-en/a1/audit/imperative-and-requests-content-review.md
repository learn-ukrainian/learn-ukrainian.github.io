## Issues Found

### CRITICAL (blocks deployment)

None.

### HIGH (should fix before deployment)

1. **"Не скажіть!" in prohibition match-up activity (pair 8)** — Perfective negative imperative misleads A1 learners. In Ukrainian, prohibitions typically use imperfective aspect: "Не кажіть!" (don't say), not "Не скажіть!" (be careful not to say accidentally). At A1, aspect is not yet introduced, so this teaches a form whose nuance the learner cannot understand. **Fix:** Replace with imperfective "Не кажіть!" or substitute a different verb pair (e.g., "Не давайте!" / "Do not give! (formal)").

### MEDIUM (fix if possible)

1. **Warmth deficit** — Only 1 encouragement phrase ("Practice makes perfect") in the entire module. Tier 1 rubric requires >=3 encouragement markers. Add 2-3 encouraging moments: after the first grammar table ("These patterns will feel natural quickly!"), before the practice section ("You're ready to practice!"), and strengthen the closing ("Look how much you can do now!").
2. **"Прошу вас" pattern mismatch** — Plan specifies "Прошу вас + infinitive" (e.g., "Прошу вас сісти") but module teaches "Прошу вас + imperative" ("Прошу вас, візьміть це"). The prose should either demonstrate the infinitive pattern as well or acknowledge both constructions.
3. **Missing classroom commands** — Plan specifies "Читайте текст", "Пишіть у зошиті", "Слухайте уважно", "Дивіться на дошку" as authentic classroom contexts. Module uses generic objects ("ці слова", "тут"). Adding 2-3 real classroom phrases would strengthen practical value.
4. **"Не іди там!"** (line 190) — Semantically awkward. "Там" is locative, but context implies direction. Replace with "Не іди до них!" (vocabulary in scope) or accept the constraint.
5. **"Negative Commands" fill-in activity too mechanical** — All 8 answers are just "Не" or "не". Low cognitive engagement. Redesign to test full negative imperative forms (e.g., blank = "Не читай" rather than just the particle).

### LOW (informational)

1. **Activities 4 and 5 overlap** — Both true-false, both test form-pronoun matching. Consider converting "Form Check" to a different type (e.g., group-sort: sort forms into ти vs ви).
2. **"Ви не ідіть до них"** (fill-in activity, item 6) — Includes redundant subject pronoun before negative imperative, slightly unnatural.
3. **LLM stock phrases** — "It is important to remember," "incredibly simple," "Practice makes perfect" could be rewritten for more natural tutor voice.
4. **No stress marks** — Would benefit pronunciation (чита́й vs пиши́) but not required at A1.
5. **Plan's varied prohibition examples missing** — Plan includes "Не кури! Не біжи! Не чіпай!" for variety; module uses only the 8 core verbs. Keeps scope tight but less varied.
