## Cultural Hook (1-2 facts)

**1. Ukrainian хата (traditional house) and magical objects:**
- **Fact:** In traditional Ukrainian homes, even everyday objects like brooms (віник) held magical significance — they were protection amulets placed bristles-down to sweep away bad spirits. The stove (піч) was considered sacred, housing ancestors' souls.
- **Source:** https://newsukraine.rbc.ua/news/7-items-in-a-ukrainian-house-that-held-magical-1756077044.html
- **How to use:** Brief mention in Cultural Insight section — connects vocabulary to Ukrainian folk beliefs about household objects

**2. Rušnyk (рушник) embroidered towels:**
- **Fact:** The embroidered towel (рушник) hanging above icons or windows wasn't just decoration — each pattern encoded family stories, protection wishes, and spiritual blessings. Empty shelves were a sign of poverty, so families kept dishes (мисник shelf) visibly full and decorated.
- **Source:** https://vzhe-vzhe.com/en/blog/fakti-pro-ukransku-hatu/
- **How to use:** Example in Practice section — "Цей рушник дуже гарний" (This rushnyk is very beautiful), connecting demonstratives to cultural objects
