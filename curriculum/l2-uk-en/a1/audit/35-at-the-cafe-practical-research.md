## Ukrainian Café Culture
### Lviv Café Tradition
- **Lviv** = café capital of Ukraine
- Historic coffee culture (Austrian influence)
- Modern cafés blend tradition with contemporary style
- Important social spaces, not just "coffee to go"

### Café vs. Restaurant
- **Кав'ярня** (café): drinks + light snacks (pastries, sandwiches)
- **Ресторан** (restaurant): full meals
- Cafés more casual, often Wi-Fi friendly

### Service Style
- Seated service (not self-service) in traditional cafés
- Waiter brings menu
- Order taken at table
- Pay at table (not at counter)

## Common Phrases & Etiquette
### Ordering Sequence
1. **Greeting**: Добрий день! / Привіт!
2. **Getting menu**: Меню, будь ласка
3. **Asking advice**: Що ви порадите? (What do you recommend?)
4. **Ordering**: 
   - Мені, будь ласка, каву (I'll have coffee, please)
   - Я візьму капучино (I'll take a cappuccino)
5. **Clarifications**: 
   - З молоком? (With milk?)
   - Великий чи маленький? (Large or small?)
6. **Payment**: 
   - Можна рахунок? (Can I have the bill?)
   - Готівкою чи карткою? (Cash or card?)
7. **Thanks & goodbye**: Дякую! До побачення!

### Common Requests
- Wi-Fi: Який пароль від Wi-Fi?
- Socket/outlet: Де розетка? / Можна тут зарядити?
- Toilet: Де туалет?
- Take-away: З собою (to go)

## Grammar in Context
- **Accusative case**: Мені каву (coffee - Acc.)
- **Polite requests**: Можна...? (Can I...?)
- **Future tense**: Я візьму (I'll take - perfective future)
- **Instrumental**: З молоком (with milk - Instr.)

## Cultural Tips for Learners
- Tipping: ~10% common but not mandatory
- Table service expected in traditional cafés
- Lingering over coffee is normal (not rushed)
- Wi-Fi usually available, ask for password
