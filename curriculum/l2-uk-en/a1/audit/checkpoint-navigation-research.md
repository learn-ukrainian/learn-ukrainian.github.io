## Cultural Hooks

### Navigation Skills in Ukrainian Context
- **City Layout**: Soviet-era planning vs. historic centers
- **Address System**: вулиця (street), проспект (avenue), площа (square)
- **Public Transport**: Need to know directions for trams, buses, metro
- **Asking for Help**: Ukrainians generally helpful with directions

### Checkpoint as Cultural Milestone
- **Self-Sufficiency**: Can survive basic Ukrainian urban life
- **Real-World Skills**: Not just academic grammar
- **Confidence Building**: Checkpoint success → motivation for past tense
- **Progress Marker**: From total beginner to functional navigator

## Pedagogical Strategy (TTT Adapted)

### Traditional TTT (Teaching Modules)
1. **Test**: Diagnostic (what do you know?)
2. **Teach**: Instruction (new material)
3. **Test**: Assessment (did you learn?)

### Checkpoint TTT Approach
1. **Test**: Present skill area (what should you know?)
2. **Teach**: Brief review/reminder (not full lesson)
3. **Test**: Assessment activities (demonstrate mastery)

**Key Difference**: Checkpoints have minimal "teach" — mostly review + assessment
