## Checkpoint Scope (A1.0 - A1.3)
This checkpoint tests cumulative knowledge from modules 01-33.

### Core Skills Tested
1. **Cyrillic Reading**
   - Fluent reading without transliteration
   - Complex letter combinations (щ, ї, ь)
   - Stress patterns (awareness, not perfection)

2. **Noun Gender & Agreement**
   - Gender recognition by endings (-consonant/-о/-а)
   - Adjective agreement (гарний/гарна/гарне)
   - Possessive pronoun agreement (мій/моя/моє)

3. **Verb System**
   - Present tense (I conjugation: -у,-еш,-е,-емо,-ете,-уть)
   - Present tense (II conjugation: -у,-иш,-ить,-имо,-ите,-ять)
   - Past tense (був/була/було/були)
   - Future tense (буду + infinitive, perfective future)

4. **Case System (4 cases)**
   - **Називний** (Nominative): subject
   - **Знахідний** (Accusative): direct object, direction
   - **Родовий** (Genitive): possession, absence, "from"
   - **Місцевий** (Locative): location "at/in/on"

5. **Practical Integration**
   - Café ordering
   - Shopping phrases
   - Asking directions
   - Combined scenarios
