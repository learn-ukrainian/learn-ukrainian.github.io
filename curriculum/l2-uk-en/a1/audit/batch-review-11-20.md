## Action Required

### M14: Mine and Yours ✅ KEEP AS-IS

**Decision:** English hints for gender agreement testing are **acceptable** and pedagogically necessary.

**Rationale:** The fill-in activity "Complete with Possessives" uses hints like `(my)`, `(his)`, `(her)` to indicate WHICH possessive the student should use. The student then must select the correct GENDER form (мій/моя/моє/мої). Without these hints, sentences like `Це ___ машина` with options [його, її, їхній] would be ambiguous.

**Rule Clarification:** English hints are disallowed when they give away the answer. English hints are **allowed** when:

- They indicate the possessive person (my/his/her/our/their) for gender agreement testing
- They provide context that doesn't exist in Ukrainian (e.g., speaker gender)
- They disambiguate between grammatically valid options that test different skills
