## Ukrainian Restaurant Structure
### Menu Organization (Traditional)
1. **Закуски/Салати** - Appetizers/Salads
2. **Перше** - First course (typically soup: борщ, юшка)
3. **Друге** - Main course (meat/fish + гарнір)
4. **Десерт** - Dessert
5. **Напої** - Drinks

### Service Style
- Seated by host/hostess
- Waiter presents menu
- Order taken in stages (drinks → courses)
- Courses served sequentially
- Bill brought to table

## Common Phrases & Scenarios
### Making Reservation
- Я хотів би замовити столик (I'd like to book a table)
- На скільки осіб? (For how many people?)
- На яку годину? (For what time?)
- На завтра на вісімнадцяту годину (For tomorrow at 6 PM)

### Arriving at Restaurant
- Добрий вечір! (Good evening!)
- Столик на двох, будь ласка (Table for two, please)
- У мене резервація на прізвище... (I have a reservation under...)

### Reading Menu & Ordering
- Що ви порадите? (What do you recommend?)
- Що таке...? (What is...?) - asking about unfamiliar dish
- З чого це? (What's it made of?)
- Мені, будь ласка, борщ і вареники (I'll have borscht and varenyky)
- На перше... на друге... (For the first course... for the main...)

### Special Needs
- Без м'яса (Without meat - vegetarian)
- Без глютену (Gluten-free)
- У мене алергія на... (I'm allergic to...)
- Менш гостро (Less spicy)

### During Meal
- Смачно! (Delicious! - compliment to server)
- Можна ще води? (Can I have more water?)
- Все добре, дякую (Everything's fine, thanks)

### Complaints (Polite)
- Вибачте, але... (Excuse me, but...)
- Це холодне (This is cold)
- Я не це замовляв/замовляла (This isn't what I ordered)

### Payment
- Можна рахунок? (Can I have the bill?)
- Скільки з мене? (How much do I owe?)
- Ми платимо разом чи окремо? (Together or separately?)
- Решту не треба (Keep the change - as tip)
- Готівкою/Карткою (Cash/Card)

## Restaurant Etiquette
### Tipping Culture
- **10-15%** is standard in mid-range restaurants
- Cash tip often preferred (even if paying by card)
- Can round up bill or leave on table
- Not mandatory but expected for good service

### Table Manners
- Wait to be seated (don't seat yourself)
- Call waiter politely: Вибачте! (Excuse me!)
- Not rude to linger after eating (not rushed)
- Splitting bills is acceptable

## Grammar in Context
- **Accusative**: ordering dishes (Мені борщ)
- **Genitive**: portion requests (Склянку води - glass of water)
- **Future tense**: Я візьму... (I'll take...)
- **Conditional**: Я хотів би... (I would like... - polite)
- **Instrumental**: З чим? (With what? - ingredients)

## Common Ukrainian Dishes (A1 Recognition)

- **Вареники** - dumplings (sweet or savory)
- **Деруни** - potato pancakes
- **Голубці** - stuffed cabbage rolls
- **Салат Олів'є** - Olivier salad (popular)
- **Київський торт** - Kyiv cake (dessert)

## Cultural Notes for Learners
- Restaurant culture more formal than cafés
- Reservations recommended for popular places
- Service can be slower than Western fast-casual
- Enjoying meal and conversation is valued
- Ukrainian cuisine hearty and flavorful
