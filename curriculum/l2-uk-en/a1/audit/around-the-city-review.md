## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Activity Scope Creep (Instrumental Case)
- **Location**: Activity `quiz` "Directional Sentence Order", Item 11
- **Original**: "Музей між театром і парком"
- **Problem**: This sentence uses the preposition `між` (between) which requires the Instrumental case (`-ом`). This module focuses on Locative/Accusative. Neither `між` nor the Instrumental endings have been taught.
- **Fix**: Replace with `Музей біля театру` (Genitive "chunk") or `Музей навпроти парку`.

### Issue 2: Activity Scope Creep (Vocabulary/Case)
- **Location**: Activity `quiz` "Directional Sentence Order", Item 7
- **Original**: "Іди вздовж вулиці Шевченка"
- **Problem**: `Вздовж` (along) is new vocabulary not taught in the module.
- **Fix**: Replace with `Іди прямо по вулиці Шевченка`.

### Issue 3: Register Mismatch in Dialogue
- **Location**: Content / Scenario 2
- **Original**: "Анна: Привіт! Ти де? ... Анна: Виходьте на зупинці «Площа Ринок»."
- **Problem**: Anna addresses Oleg informally (`Ти`, `Сідай`), but then gives a formal/plural command (`Виходьте`). This is inconsistent.
- **Fix**: Change `Виходьте` to `Виходь` (informal singular). Update the grammar note to explain that `Виходьте` is the formal version used in announcements, while `Виходь` is for friends.

### Issue 4: Ambiguous Activity Options
- **Location**: Activity `fill-in` "Complete the Directions", Item 7
- **Original**: "Я їду до університету ___." Options: `на трамваї`, `на автобусі`, `на машині`, `пішки`.
- **Problem**: All options are grammatically correct and logically possible. A student could drive or walk.
- **Fix**: Change options to eliminate ambiguity (e.g., `на трамваї`, `вулиця`, `стіл`, `аптека` - mixing parts of speech) OR add context to the sentence (e.g. "Я не маю машини, тому їду до університету на трамваї").

### Issue 5: Ambiguous Activity Options (Turn)
- **Location**: Activity `fill-in` "Complete the Directions", Item 10
- **Original**: "Поверніть ___ біля світлофора." Options: `праворуч`, `ліворуч`...
- **Problem**: Both "turn left" and "turn right" are valid. Guessing games are not educational.
- **Fix**: Provide a visual cue description or context, or ensure only one direction is in the options (replace `ліворуч` with `прямо` which doesn't fit "Поверніть" as naturally as a direction, though "turn straight" is impossible, "Go straight" is `Ідіть`). Better: Replace `ліворуч` with `назад` (Turn back? Possible but less likely) or a noun.
