# Lesson Content

## warm-up

Now you are starving.
You enter a "Puzata Hata" (popular bistro chain).

You want Borscht. But not just any Borscht.
You want Borscht **with** sour cream.
You want tea **with** lemon.

In Module 16, we learned "Without" (Bez + Genitive).
Today, we learn "With" (Z + Instrumental).
Life is better with things.
