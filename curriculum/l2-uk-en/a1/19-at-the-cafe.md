# Lesson Content

## warm-up

You are in Lviv. The smell of roasted coffee is everywhere.
You enter "Svit Kavy" or "Lviv Croissants".
You don't just want coffee. You want an experience.
You need to find a table. You need to ask about the cheesecake.
You need to pay without panic.
In Module 18, you learned the words.
In Module 19, we put them together into a **Conversation Flow**.
This module is 100% practical. No new grammar rules. Just survival skills.
