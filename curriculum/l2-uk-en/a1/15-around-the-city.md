# Lesson Content

## warm-up
**The Urban Jungle**

You are in Kyiv. It is huge. It is loud. It is beautiful.
But you are lost.
Your phone battery is dead. No Google Maps.
You see a grandmother (Babusya) selling flowers.
You summon your courage.
"Excuse me... where is the Metro?"
She points a shaking finger. "Straight. Then left."
You survive.

This module is about **Urban Survival**. Reading signs, asking questions, and understanding the answers.
