## Cultural Hooks
1. The "Apostol" (1574) by Ivan Fedorov, the first printed book in Ukraine, linking the verb 'читати' to deep literacy traditions.
2. Proverb: "Птицю пізнати по пір'ю, а людину по мові" (A bird is known by its feathers, a person by their speech).

## Common Learner Errors
1. Infinitive abuse → "Я читаю" instead of "Я читати" — English speakers are not used to verbs changing form for every person.
2. Pronoun overuse → "Читаю" instead of "Я читаю" — English requires the pronoun, but Ukrainian allows pro-drop for natural flow, as the verb ending already conveys the subject.
