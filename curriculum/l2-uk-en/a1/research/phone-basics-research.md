# Research Notes: A1 M41 "Phone Basics"

**Track**: A1 Core
**Module**: M41 "Phone Basics"
**Subtitle**: Making Calls and Texting
**Researched**: 2026-02-08

## 1. Grammar: State Standard 2024 Reference

According to the **Державний стандарт української мови як іноземної (2024)**, the topic of phone communication and messaging is introduced at the A1 level primarily through communicative intentions and basic text types.

*   **§ 1.2.2 (Типи текстів)**: Explicitly lists `смс-повідомлення` (SMS messages) as a type of text students should understand.
*   **§ 1.3.1.1 (Письмо - Загальний перелік умінь)**: Defines the ability to `написати чи переписати ... короткі, прості тексти на листівці чи в смс-повідомленні` (write or rewrite short, simple texts on a postcard or in an SMS message).
*   **§ 1.1.2.2 (Слухання - Типи текстів)**: Includes understanding emergency numbers: `Який номер поліції? Швидка допомога – 103`.
*   **§ 4.3.2 (Синтаксис - Складне речення)**: Uses phone-related examples for complex sentences at A1: `Олена зателефонувала Вікторові, але він не відповів` (Note: Dative nouns like "Вікторові" are often introduced as lexical chunks or through pronouns at this level).

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency

### High Frequency (Core A1)
*   **телефон** (phone)
*   **номер** (number)
*   **смс / повідомлення** (SMS / message)
*   **дзвонити / телефонувати** (to call)
*   **писати / надіслати** (to write / to send)
*   **алло** (hello - on the phone)
*   **слухаю** (I'm listening/speaking)
*   **так / ні** (yes / no - confirming identity)
*   **зараз** (now - "I can't talk now")
*   **хто це?** (who is this?)

### Medium/High Frequency (Technical/Practical)
*   **зарядка / акумулятор** (charger / battery)
*   **мережа / зв'язок** (network / connection)
*   **додаток** (app)
*   **передзвонити** (to call back)
*   **зачекати** (to wait)

### Common Collocations
*   **набирати номер** (to dial a number)
*   **надіслати смс** (to send an SMS)
*   **немає зв'язку** (no signal)
*   **телефон сів** (phone died/battery is out)

## 3. Cultural Hook

1.  **Phone Etiquette**: Ukrainians answer the phone with a neutral "Алло". However, in formal contexts or when the caller is known, "Слухаю" (I'm listening) or "Так" (Yes) is very common. Identifying oneself immediately is polite but not always mandatory if the caller starts with "Добрий день".
2.  **Messenger Dominance**: While "смс" is the standard term in textbooks, in daily life, Ukrainians almost exclusively use **Telegram**, **Viber**, and **WhatsApp**. A common phrase is `Пиши у Вайбер` (Write [to me] on Viber).
3.  **Emergency Numbers**: Ukraine transitioned to a European-style system but still maintains the traditional numbers: 
    *   **101** – Пожежна (Fire)
    *   **102** – Поліція (Police)
    *   **103** – Швидка допомога (Ambulance)
    *   **112** – Єдиний номер (Unified emergency number)

## 4. Pedagogical Notes

*   **"Calling" Grammar**: Learners often struggle with the Dative case for "calling someone" (телефонувати комусь). At A1, focus on **"дзвонити на номер"** (calling a number - Accusative) or use **Dative pronouns** (мені, тобі, нам, вам), which are covered in the A1 standard (§ 4.2.1.4).
*   **Verbs of Motion/Action**: Distinguish between "писати" (writing) and "надіслати" (sending). "Надіслати" is Perfective but essential for "I will send it now".
*   **Listening Challenges**: Phone conversations are notoriously difficult for beginners due to lack of visual cues and potential audio distortion. Keep A1 dialogues short (4-6 exchanges per § 1.4.2).
*   **Vocabulary Trap**: "Телефон" usually refers to the device, but "номер" is the focus of the action. Ensure students don't confuse "Я дзвоню телефон" (wrong) with "Я маю телефон" (right).

## 5. Scope Boundaries

### IN Scope
*   **Accusative Case**: For the object of messages (`Пишу повідомлення`) and the destination of calls (`Дзвоню на цей номер`).
*   **Dative Pronouns**: `Подзвони мені`, `Я пишу тобі`. (Covered in § 4.2.1.4).
*   **Future Tense**: `Я передзвоню` (Perfective future) or `Я буду дзвонити` (Imperfective future).
*   **Past Tense**: `Хто дзвонив?`, `Я писав тобі`.
*   **Modal Verbs**: `Я не можу говорити`, `Я хочу подзвонити`.
*   **Numbers**: Standard 10-digit Ukrainian mobile format (e.g., 097-xxx-xx-xx).

### OUT of Scope
*   **Dative Nouns**: `Дзвоню мамі`, `Пишу братові`. Unless treated as fixed lexical chunks, full Dative declension for nouns is typically prioritized in late A1/early A2.
*   **Conditional Mood**: `Я б подзвонив, якби...` (I would call if...).
*   **Complex Participles**: "The missed call", "The sent message".
*   **Formal/Business Phone Protocols**: Keep it to informal and basic service interactions (e.g., calling a taxi or a doctor).
