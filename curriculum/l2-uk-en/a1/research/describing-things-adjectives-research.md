# Дослідження: Describing Things - Adjectives

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.1.2: "Adjective declension — hard/soft groups, gender agreement"
Alignment: This module directly fulfills the requirement to teach basic adjective declension paradigms (hard/soft) and enforces gender/number agreement in the Nominative case.

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Notes | Key collocations |
|------|-------|------------------|
| новий | Top 100 high-frequency adjective. | новий рік, новий день, нове життя |
| гарний | High-frequency social descriptor. | гарна погода, гарний день, гарна дівчина |
| великий | Essential for scale and physical description. | велике місто, велика родина, великий привіт |
| синій | Primary example for soft stem (-ій); practical anchor. | синя лінія (Kyiv Metro) |

## Cultural Hooks
1. St. Sophia's Cathedral (Софійський собор) in Kyiv is an excellent anchor for teaching "старий" (ancient) and "великий" (grand).
2. Mavka (Мавка), a famous figure from Ukrainian folklore (specifically Lesya Ukrainka's "Forest Song"), is a great context for teaching feminine adjectives like "молода", "гарна", and "зелена".

## Common Learner Errors
1. *новий машина* → *нова машина* — Learners often default to the masculine dictionary form for all nouns, forgetting to agree gender.
2. *синий* → *синій* — English speakers misspell soft stem endings after soft consonants, influenced by simpler hard phonetic patterns.
3. *гарна дівчата* → *гарні дівчата* — Learners use singular adjective forms with plural nouns, failing to realize adjectives must agree in both gender and number.

## Notes for Content Writing
- Use English for all grammatical scaffolding (explaining what "adjective agreement" means).
- Keep Ukrainian example sentences short (max 8-10 words).
- Use visual tables or color-coding to emphasize the endings (-ий, -а, -е, -і).
- Clearly contrast attributive ("гарна погода") and predicative ("Погода гарна") positions, pointing out the omission of the "to be" verb ("є") in Ukrainian.
- Use the "Real Estate Agent" persona to create practical roleplay scenarios (e.g., describing a "нова квартира").
