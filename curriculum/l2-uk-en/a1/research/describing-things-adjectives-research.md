# Дослідження: Describing Things - Adjectives

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.1.2: "Adjective declension — hard/soft groups, gender agreement"
Alignment: This module introduces adjective agreement in gender and number for hard and soft stems in the Nominative case, establishing the foundation for all future noun-adjective pairings.

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Notes | Key collocations |
|------|-------|------------------|
| новий | Top 100 high-frequency adjective | новий рік, новий день, нове життя |
| старий | Key for describing historical landmarks | старий будинок, стара книга |
| гарний | High-frequency social descriptor | гарна погода, гарний день, гарна дівчина |
| великий | Essential for scale and physical description | велике місто, велика родина |
| синій | Primary soft stem anchor for beginners | синя лінія (Kyiv Metro) |
| добрий | Essential for greetings and character | добрий день, добрий вечір, добра людина |

## Cultural Hooks
1. St. Sophia's Cathedral (Софійський собор) in Kyiv as a prime example of something "старий" (ancient) and "великий" (grand).
2. The folklore figure Mavka (Мавка) from Lesya Ukrainka's "Forest Song" to illustrate feminine adjectives like "молода", "гарна", and "зелена".
3. The Kyiv Metro's "синя лінія" (Blue Line) as a practical, real-world anchor for the soft stem.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Dictionary Form Default** → *новий машина* instead of *нова машина* — Learners often forget to inflect and use the masculine dictionary form for all nouns.
2. **Hard/Soft Spelling Confusion** → *синий* instead of *синій* — Phonetic approximation causes learners to write hard endings instead of soft ones.
3. **Plural Disagreement** → *гарна дівчата* instead of *гарні дівчата* — Failing to shift to the universal plural `-і` marker regardless of the noun's gender.

## Notes for Content Writing
- Use English for the core grammar scaffolding (explaining agreement) but transition to Ukrainian for examples to meet the 25-35% immersion target for this phase.
- Use explicit visual color-coding for gender endings (Blue/Red/Yellow/Green).
- Address attributive vs. predicative placement early so learners don't try to insert "є" (e.g., *Погода гарна*).
