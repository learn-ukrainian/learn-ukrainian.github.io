## Cultural Hooks
1. **Bread Traditions**: Bread (*хліб*) is sacred in Ukraine. It's a symbol of hospitality (*хліб-сіль*) and life. Throwing bread away is traditionally considered a sin. (Source: ukrainian-recipes.com).
2. **The "Palianytsia" Identity**: The word *паляниця* (a traditional round loaf) is a famous "shibboleth" in Ukraine—a word that is famously difficult for non-native speakers (especially Russians) to pronounce correctly, becoming a symbol of national identity. (Source: kiddle.co).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Nominative Overuse**: Learners often forget to change feminine -а/-я to -у/-ю in the Accusative. Example: *Я хочу картопля* → *Я хочу картоплю*.
2. **Missing Genitive after Quantities**: Using Nominative instead of Genitive after words like "кілограм" or "пляшка". Example: *Пляшка вода* → *Пляшка води*.
3. **Number-Case Confusion**: Misapplying Genitive plural for numbers 2-4 (which require Nominative plural). Example: *Два кілограмів* → *Два кілограми*.
