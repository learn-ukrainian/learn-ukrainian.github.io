# Дослідження: Prepositions of Place

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.3.3: "Locative case — location (в/у, на), topic (по)" (lines 690–695)
Also §4.1.7: "Euphony devices (у/в, і/й, з/із/зі)" (lines 590–594)
Alignment: This module directly addresses the two core locative prepositions (в/у, на) at A1 level, plus introduces біля/навпроти + Genitive as supplementary orientation prepositions. The euphony rule (§4.1.7) is explicitly drilled via the в/у alternation.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Хрещатик as navigational anchor**: Kyiv's main boulevard is the real-life reference point Ukrainians use for giving directions — "на Хрещатику", "біля Майдану", "навпроти ЦУМу". Using these landmarks grounds the location question/answer pattern in lived Ukrainian urban experience, not invented street names.
2. **На кухні (not в кухні)**: This idiomatic exception reflects the historical association of the kitchen as a working/social space rather than a purely enclosed room — linguistically preserved from when на marked activity spaces. The same logic applies to на ринку, на пошті, на роботі — places where you do something, not just exist inside them.
3. **Народна загадка (riddle) for location practice**: "Стою на місці, а дорогу вказую." (I stand in place, but I show the way.) — a riddle about a signpost (дорожній знак / вказівник). Can be used as a warm-up for directional vocabulary. The answer requires: "Він стоїть на вулиці / біля дороги."

## Common Learner Errors

1. **В/У confusion before consonant clusters** → Correct: «У Львові» (not «В Львові») — the льв-cluster triggers euphonic у per Pravopys §23; English speakers default to в regardless of phonetic context.
2. **На vs. в/у for institution interiors** → Correct: «на пошті», «на роботі», «на кухні» — English speakers expect "in the post office" → в пошті (wrong). The на + institution rule has no English equivalent and must be explicitly listed.
3. **Forgetting consonant alternations in Locative** → Correct: «у руці» (not «у руки» / «у руку») — learners carry Nominative/Accusative stem into Locative; к→ц, г→з, х→с are non-obvious from the base form.
4. **Mixing Genitive and Locative after біля** → Correct: «біля школи» (Gen) NOT «біля школі» (Loc) — learners overapply Locative after learning в/у + Loc and apply it to all location prepositions.

## Teaching Strategy

- **Discovery exercise**: Show a simple apartment floor plan (or a short city map sketch described in English). Present 4 Ukrainian caption labels without explanation: «Книга на столі.» / «Ключі у шафі.» / «Кіт біля дверей.» / «Машина навпроти будинку.» Ask the learner: what do you notice about the form of the noun after each location word? They'll spot that the noun endings change (столі, шафі, дверей, будинку). Then ask: which words take -і endings vs. -у/-ю endings? This elicits the Locative vs. Genitive split before the rule is stated. Modelled on Grade 7 litvinova Вправа 258 — error-correction approach adapted: instead of correcting mistakes, learners identify patterns from correct examples.
- **Best context**: Asking for directions / describing a neighbourhood. Scenario: "You just arrived in Kyiv. You need to find the pharmacy. A local tells you where it is." Requires all four preposition types in natural combination: «Аптека знаходиться на вулиці Хрещатик, навпроти банку, біля метро.»
- **Contrastive pairs** for disambiguation:
  - «в кімнаті» (inside a room) vs. «на кухні» (in the kitchen — traditional exception)
  - «в театрі» (inside the theatre building) vs. «на виставі» (at the exhibition/performance)
  - «біля магазину» (near the store — Genitive) vs. «у магазині» (inside the store — Locative)
  - «у Львові» vs. «в Одесі» — euphonic alternation with identical meaning, different phonetic context

## Notes for Content Writing

- The Instrumental preview (поруч з, між) must be framed explicitly as "learn this as a whole phrase, not a case rule yet" — the A1 quick-ref forbids Instrumental case teaching until A2.
- Знаходиться: introduce as a vocabulary item / useful phrase, not as a conjugation drill — learners don't need the full reflexive paradigm here.
- All example sentences must stay under 10 words (A1 constraint). Avoid compound sentences.
- Section headers should follow M15+ pattern: Ukrainian with English in parentheses, e.g., «В/У + Місцевий (In + Locative)».
- Do NOT use imperative verbs in Ukrainian text — IMPERATIVE BAN applies (taught at M47). Use English instructions instead (e.g., "Notice the ending change").
- Kyiv landmarks (Хрещатик, Майдан, Золоті Ворота) are culturally grounded references — use them in the Де знаходиться section for authentic flavour.
