## Cultural Hooks
1. **Proverb «Копійка береже гривню»** ("A kopeck saves a hryvnia") — Ukrainian folk wisdom about thrift; parallels English "a penny saved is a penny earned." Directly teaches both currency units.
2. **Etymology of гривня** — derives from *гри́ва* (mane/neck), referring to silver neck-ornaments and ingots used as currency in Kyivan Rus. The word predates Russian monetary history by centuries — authentic decolonization-free cultural grounding.
3. **₴ symbol** — a cursive italic **г** with two horizontal stripes, analogous to €, $, ¥. Introduced with the 1996 revival of the hryvnia as Ukrainian state currency. Concrete everyday writing hook.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **два vs. дві** → English speakers default to два for all genders. Correct: **дві гривні** (f.), **два долари** (m.). Triggered by missing gender awareness for гривня.
2. **Soft sign in 50–80** → Learners write \*п'ятьдесят, \*шістьдесят. Correct: **п'ятдесят, шістдесят** (no soft sign mid-compound). Pattern is counterintuitive after learning п'ять with soft ь.
3. **Apostrophe in п'ять** → English speakers omit or mispronounce the glottal break. Correct pronunciation: [p'jat'] — the apostrophe signals hardening, not mere spelling.
4. **Genitive plural form** → Learners reuse nominative: \*п'ять гривня. Correct: **п'ять гривень**. 1-2-5 pattern must be drilled as chunks, not derived from case rules (those come at M32).

## Teaching Strategy
- **Discovery exercise**: Show learner a simple Ukrainian market receipt (хліб — 25 гривень, молоко — 42 гривні, яйця — 1 гривня/шт). Ask: "What changes about the word гривня? Can you spot the three forms?" Let learner notice 1/2/5 pattern before naming it. Model: textbook arithmetic exercises (Grade 5 style — read numbers, complete sums).
- **Best context**: Supermarket / bazaar / café cashier — "Скільки коштує?" is the natural trigger question. Shopping role-play scaffolded with price tags.
- **Contrastive pairs**: два долари / дві гривні (gender contrast); п'ять гривень / п'ятдесят гривень (soft-sign contrast); одна гривня / десять гривень (singular vs Gen Pl).

## Notes for Content Writing
- Teach 1-2-5 rule as **three memorized chunk patterns** (table), NOT as case grammar — scope note in plan is explicit.
- Teen numbers (11–19) stress falls on **-на́дцять** suffix — include stress marks.
- All dialogues should use imperfective **коштує** only (no perfective aspect at A1).
- Avoid imperative forms per A1.2 sequence constraints — use "Let's practice..." (English) as drill intro.
- Current prices (2024): хліб ~25 грн, молоко ~40 грн, кава ~50 грн — use for realistic practice items.
