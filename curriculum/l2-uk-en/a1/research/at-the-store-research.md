# Дослідження: At the Store

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.3.2: "Знахідний відмінок: Без прийменника (об’єкт дії): Віра читає цікаву книжку."
§4.2.3.3: "Місцевий відмінок: у, в, на (позначення місця): ... Студент працює в новій лабораторії."
Alignment: This module addresses the core A1 competency of shopping and store navigation (Catalogue B: §3 "купівля"), applying the accusative case for direct objects (purchased goods) and locative case for spatial orientation (store departments).

## Cultural Hooks
1. Themed Supermarkets: Ukrainian chains (like Silpo) often feature elaborate, distinctive themes (e.g., Steampunk, GTA, Little Prince), turning grocery shopping into an immersive cultural experience rather than a standardized chore.
2. The "Bag Ritual" & Receipt Predictions: The quintessential interaction at any checkout is "Пакет потрібен?" (Do you need a bag?). Additionally, some supermarket chains print "передбачення" (predictions or fortunes) on receipts, turning a simple transaction into a moment of engagement.

## Common Learner Errors
1. Destination vs. Location case confusion → "Я йду в магазині" (Incorrect) vs "Я йду в магазин" (Correct - Accusative destination) / "Я в магазині" (Correct - Locative location).
2. Masculine inanimate over-correction → Applying animate -а/-я endings to inanimate objects in the Accusative case (e.g., "Я купую сира" instead of the correct "Я купую сир").
3. Lexical interference (Russism) → Using the Surzhyk/Russian term "покупка" instead of authentic Ukrainian "закупи" (shopping trip) or "купівля" (process of buying).

## Notes for Content Writing
- English Scaffolding: Since this is an A1 module (10-50% immersion), use English scaffolding to clearly explain grammar concepts (Accusative direct objects and Locative places), but transition to Ukrainian for examples and dialogues.
- Decolonized Framing: Highlight authentic Ukrainian vocabulary like "закупи" positively, asserting it as standard usage without centering it as a "correction to Russian". Celebrate modern Ukrainian retail culture's creativity (e.g., themed stores) as a hallmark of the contemporary Ukrainian experience.
- Treat "Готівкою чи карткою?" (Cash or card?) as fixed instrumental chunks to avoid overwhelming the learner with full Instrumental declension at A1.
