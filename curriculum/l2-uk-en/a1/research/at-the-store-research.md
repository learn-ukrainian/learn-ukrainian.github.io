The grammar taught in this module typically utilizes:
- **§ 4.2.3.2. Знахідний відмінок (Accusative Case):** "Без прийменника (об’єкт дії): Віра читає цікаву книжку." (Applied here to: *Я купую хліб, молоко, воду*).
- **§ 4.2.3.2.2. Знахідний відмінок (Prepositions):** "на (указівка на кінцевий пункт руху або переміщення): Степан заходить у кімнату." (Applied to: *Я йду в магазин/супермаркет*).
- **§ 4.2.1.1. Іменник (Noun):** Gender recognition and basic pluralization for goods (*яблука, овочі*).

## 2. Vocabulary Frequency
For A1 shopping, we prioritize high-frequency "functional" words and basic food categories.

### High Frequency (Crucial for A1)
- **Verbs:** купувати (to buy), платити (to pay), коштувати (to cost), дати (to give - imperative *дайте*).
- **Nouns:** магазин (store), супермаркет (supermarket), продукт (product), ціна (price), гроші (money), пакет (bag), чек (receipt), термінал (card terminal), каса (checkout).
- **Interrogatives:** Скільки? (How much?), Що? (What?).
- **Food Categories:** хліб (bread), молоко (milk), вода (water), сир (cheese/curd), яблуко (apple), кава (coffee), чай (tea).

### Lower Frequency / Contextual
- **Specifics:** ваги (scales), решта (change), знижка (discount), кошик (basket), візок (cart).
- **Specific Goods:** банан, апельсин, картопля, яйця.

### Common Collocations
- **Скільки коштує...** (How much does ... cost?)
- **Можна карткою?** (Can I pay by card?)
- **Ваш пакет.** (Your bag.)
- **Пакет потрібен?** (Do you need a bag?)

## 3. Cultural Hook
**The "Packet" Culture & Themed Supermarkets:**
1.  **The "Packet" Question:** In Ukrainian supermarkets, the cashier almost always asks "*Пакет потрібен?*" (Do you need a bag?) before starting the checkout process. In recent years, there is a strong shift toward *еко-торби* (eco-bags) due to environmental laws.
2.  **Themed Aesthetics:** Ukraine is home to some of the most uniquely designed supermarkets in Europe, particularly the **Silpo (Сільпо)** chain. Each store often has a specific theme (e.g., Steampunk, Vincent van Gogh, GTA, Circus, or Space). Mentioning that a grocery trip can be a "museum-like" experience adds high engagement.
3.  **Self-Weighing (Зважте самі):** In most supermarkets, customers are expected to weigh their own fruits and vegetables at digital scales in the produce section and stick the printed barcode label on the bag before reaching the checkout.

## 4. Pedagogical Notes
- **"Скільки коштує" Usage:** Students often want to say "How much is this?" as "*Скільки це?*". While understood, the standard A1 phrase is "*Скільки коштує (це/хліб)?*".
- **Grammar Trap (Numbers):** Students already know numbers, but the Genitive case used with numbers (e.g., *2 гривні, 5 гривень*) can be complex. At A1, focus on rote-memorized prices or the nominative forms for simplicity, or reinforce the basic Genitive for "5+" if already taught.
- **Animacy in Accusative:** Shopping is the perfect place to practice **Inanimate Accusative** (where the form equals Nominative for Masc/Neut: *Я купую хліб, яблуко*). Contrast this with **Feminine Accusative** (*Я купую воду*).
- **Word Order:** In Ukrainian, "Скільки це коштує?" and "Скільки коштує це?" are both acceptable, but the latter is more common when pointing.

## 5. Scope Boundaries
### IN SCOPE
- **Accusative Case (Inanimate):** Direct objects of "купувати".
- **Locative Case:** For location (*в магазині, на ринку*).
- **Present Tense:** Conjugation of Group 1/2 verbs (*купую, купуєш*).
- **Numbers 1-100:** For prices.
- **Modal Verbs:** *Я хочу купити...* (I want to buy...).

### OUT OF SCOPE
- **Genitive Case for Quantities (Complex):** Avoid "a kilo of..." (*кілограм цукру*) if the Genitive ending is irregular or hasn't been fully mastered. Stick to "I buy milk" vs "I buy 1 liter of milk".
- **Instrumental Case:** "Paying *with* a card" (*карткою*) is useful as a set phrase but the grammar of the Instrumental case should not be the focus yet.
- **Future/Past Tense in Shopping:** Keep the dialogue in the immediate present ("I am buying", "How much does it cost now").
- **Case Agreement with Adjectives (Complex):** Limit to simple Nominative/Accusative agreement (*червоне яблуко*).
