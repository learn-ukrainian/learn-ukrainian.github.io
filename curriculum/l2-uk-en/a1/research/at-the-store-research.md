# Дослідження: At the Store

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.3.2.1: "Знахідний відмінок: Без прийменника (об’єкт дії): Віра читає цікаву книжку."
§4.2.3.3: "Місцевий відмінок: у, в, на (позначення місця): Спортивний костюм у великій сумці... Гроші лежать на столі."
Alignment: This module precisely satisfies these linguistic competencies by teaching the Accusative case for direct objects (shopping items: купувати воду, хліб) and the Locative case for store navigation (у магазині, на касі, у відділі).

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Thematic Supermarkets ("Сільпо"):** Ukraine boasts a uniquely vibrant retail culture where supermarket chains like "Сільпо" are individually themed (e.g., Steampunk, GTA, Alice in Wonderland, Little Prince). This transforms daily grocery shopping into an immersive aesthetic and cultural experience, highlighting modern Ukrainian design innovation.
2. **The Receipt Predictions (Передбачення на чеках):** A beloved modern Ukrainian tradition where supermarket receipts print a short, positive fortune or prediction at the bottom. This provides a delightful, authentic micro-reading exercise for language learners.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Destination vs. Location confusion:** Learners often mix Accusative and Locative cases for places. (Error: *Я купую в магазина.* → Correct: *Я купую в магазині.* vs. *Я йду в магазин.*) — Happens due to conflating directional motion with static location.
2. **Inanimate Masculine Over-correction:** Applying animate endings to inanimate objects in the Accusative. (Error: *Я купую хліба / сира.* → Correct: *Я купую хліб / сир.*) — Happens when learners overgeneralize the "-а" Genitive/animate rule.
3. **Lexical Interference (Surzhyk):** Using the Russism *покупка* instead of authentic Ukrainian terminology. (Correct: *робити закупи* [shopping trip] or *купівля* [the process of buying]).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing:** Highlight the modern, vibrant nature of Ukrainian retail. Frame "закупи" as the authentic standard without directly validating the Russian interference. 
- **Scaffolding:** As an A1 module, English scaffolding is MANDATORY for all grammar explanations (Accusative/Locative rules).
- **Chunking:** Teach "карткою чи готівкою" purely as fixed lexical chunks to guarantee immediate communicative success at the checkout, rather than introducing the full Instrumental case paradigm.
- **Voice:** Adopt the "Patient Supportive Tutor" (Supermarket Manager) persona.
