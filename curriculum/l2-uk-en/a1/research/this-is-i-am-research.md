# Дослідження: This Is / I Am

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.3.1: "Nominative case — subject, predicate nominative"
§4.2.1.4: "Personal pronouns — basic declension"
Alignment: This module introduces fundamental Subject + Predicate identity statements in the nominative case using personal pronouns and the demonstrative pronoun "це", directly fulfilling the State Standard requirements for initial communicative competence.

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Notes | Key collocations |
|------|-------|------------------|
| це | Top 10 frequency; demonstrative pronoun | це [noun], хто це?, що це? |
| я | Top 20 frequency; 1st person singular | я студент, я тут |
| ти / ви | Informal vs. Formal/Plural 'you' | ти хто?, ви тут |
| він / вона | Replaces masculine/feminine nouns | він там, вона вчителька |
| хто / що | Used for identifying persons/objects | хто це?, що це? |
| студент / студентка | High frequency thematic nouns | я студент, це студентка |

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Ви" Safety Net:** Using "Ви" (formal you) is a mandatory default when addressing strangers, elders, or officials in Ukrainian culture to show respect. 
2. **The "Bruderschaft" Transition:** Shifting from the formal "Ви" to the informal "ти" is a significant social milestone (often marked by the phrase «Давай на ти?»).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **The Phantom "Is"** → *Я студент* (not *Я є студент*) — English speakers often mistakenly insert the verb "to be" (є) into present tense identity statements.
2. **The "It" Trap** → *Стіл? Він там.* (not *Воно там.*) — English speakers overuse "воно" (it) for all inanimate objects, ignoring that every noun in Ukrainian has a gender and requires "він" or "вона".
3. **Register Mix-up** → *Ви хто?* (not *Ти хто?* to a stranger) — Due to the lack of a T-V distinction in English, learners may accidentally offend by using "ти" prematurely.

## Notes for Content Writing
- Rely heavily on English scaffolding for grammar explanations (Core A pedagogy).
- Use the visual "Ø" symbol to emphasize the missing copula (Я [Ø] студент).
- Introduce IPA only on the first occurrence of new vocabulary words.
- Ensure all identity sentences remain strictly in the nominative case.
