- §4.1.1: Український алфавіт — full 33-letter alphabet with upper/lowercase

## Vocabulary Check
- Core words: центр, чай, школа, гарний, жити, день, Європа, яблуко — all high-frequency
- борщ, дякую, привіт, будь ласка — essential survival phrases
- Frequency source: All confirmed high-frequency via everyday usage; international cognates widely recognized
- Ґ words are rare in Ukrainian (ґанок, ґума, ґудзик) — this is expected and pedagogically noted

## Cultural Hook (2 facts)
- **Fact 1:** UNESCO inscribed Ukrainian borscht culture on its List of Intangible Cultural Heritage in Need of Urgent Safeguarding in July 2022, during the ongoing Russian invasion.
  - Source: https://ich.unesco.org/en/USL/culture-of-ukrainian-borscht-cooking-01852
  - How to use: Already integrated in "Did You Know?" callout about Щ and борщ

- **Fact 2:** The Valuev Circular (1863) and Ems Ukaz (1876) banned Ukrainian-language publications and education. Taras Shevchenko's poetry kept the language alive.
  - Source: https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Валуєвський_циркуляр
  - How to use: Already integrated in "History Bite" callout in Cultural Insight section
