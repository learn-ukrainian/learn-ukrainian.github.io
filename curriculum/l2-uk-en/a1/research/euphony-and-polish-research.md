# Дослідження: Euphony and Polish

## State Standard Reference
§4.1.7: "Euphony devices (у/в, і/й, з/із/зі)" — A1 phonology strand
Alignment: This module directly addresses the A1 euphony standard — introducing all three core alternation rules and building automatic selection through pattern recognition and drills.

## Vocabulary Frequency

| Word | Notes | Key collocations |
|------|-------|------------------|
| у/в (in/at) | High-frequency preposition; у мене, в школі | у Києві, в Одесі, у вагоні |
| і/й (and) | Most common conjunction; alternates on phonological environment | батько і мати, мама й тато |
| з/із/зі (from/with) | Three-way alternation; Pravopys §25 | з Києва, із Львова, зі школи |
| мене (me, Gen.) | У мене є... construction (I have); taught from M15 | у мене є книга |
| школа (school) | Locative у школі, zі школи — canonical practice noun | у школі, зі школи |
| Київ (Kyiv) | Most practiced proper noun; у Києві, з Києва | у Києві, з Києва |
| Львів (Lviv) | Triggers із/зі alternation; teaches both variants | із Львова, зі Львова, у Львові |
| батько (father) | Models consonant-final context → і after о | батько і мати |
| мама (mother) | Models vowel-final context → й after а | мама й тато |

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Radio announcer style**: Ukrainian radio/TV announcers are famous for crisp euphonic speech — у вагоні, зі Львова sound polished and professional. Framing the module through the "radio announcer" persona (already in the plan) makes the register feel aspirational, not pedantic.

2. **Grade 5 textbook exercise (Avramenko, p.117)**: Directly models the discovery exercise — two sentences shown side by side: *Брат й син ввечері привітали з святом* vs *Брат і син увечері привітали зі святом*. Students notice which is easier to say aloud. This is the exact discovery hook to use.

3. **Скоромовка pair**: The euphony rules exist to prevent consonant pile-ups like *втвр* or vowel collisions like *аіа*. A playful contrasting pair — try saying *брат й сестра* vs *брат і сестра* — demonstrates the rule bodily before explaining it.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Always using і regardless of context** → Correct: *мама й тато* not *мама і тато* — happens because English "and" has no alternation, so learners default to і everywhere.
2. **Always using у regardless of context** → Correct: *я в школі* not *я у школі* after vowel — same transfer: English "in" has one form.
3. **Using з before Львів** → Correct: *із Львова* / *зі Львова* not *з Львова* — English "from" has one form; the three-way з/із/зі split has no parallel. Textbook exercise (Avramenko Grade 6, p.226) directly targets this confusion.
4. **Confusing з preposition with з- prefix** → Correct: з- prefix in *зробити* is FIXED, does NOT alternate — learners sometimes apply euphony to prefixes.

## Teaching Strategy
- **Discovery exercise**: Show two parallel sentences (follow Avramenko Grade 5 model): *Брат й син ввечері привітали з святом* vs *Брат і син увечері привітали зі святом*. Ask: "Which sounds smoother? Try saying both out loud." Learner notices the second flows better — then the rule is named.
- **Best context**: Radio announcer announcing train departures — "Поїзд із Львова прибуває о сьомій. Пасажири у вагонах!" — uses all three alternations naturally in one context.
- **Contrastive pairs**: у мене / в мене (pause vs. flow), батько **і** мати (consonant ends) / мама **й** тато (vowel ends), **з** Києва / **із** Львова / **зі** школи. The pairs let learners feel the phonetic motivation before memorizing the rule.
- **Note on А1 constraint**: No Ukrainian grammar terms (приголосний, голосний) in body text per A1 quick-ref — use "ends in a consonant sound" / "ends in a vowel sound" in English explanations.

## Notes for Content Writing
- The Avramenko Grade 5 p.117 exercise is a perfect model for the discovery section — use its two-sentence format verbatim as inspiration.
- Grade 6 Avramenko p.226 provides the clearest rule table for у/в and і/й — adapt its rule structure (not copy).
- Keep з/із/зі to the three canonical examples (з Києва, із Львова, зі школи) — don't introduce new cities or clusters.
- "Та" as escape hatch for і/й confusion: briefly mention *та* as an alternative conjunction that sidesteps the choice entirely — useful communicative tip for learners who freeze.
- Prefix vs. preposition distinction for з- is A1-appropriate and prevents a very common error in exam tasks.
- Udень/вдень and увечері/ввечері are listed in the plan but are edge cases — keep brief (one bullet, one example box).
