Alignment: Module covers A1 thematic area "місто/подорожі" with Locative and Accusative for direction/location. Imperative for requests is in-scope at module 57.

**⚠️ SCOPE FLAG — Instrumental case**: The plan's vocabulary_hints include "їхати автобусом (Instrumental)." Per the state standard mapping, Instrumental is **A2-only** (forbidden at A1). Content writer must use ONLY the Locative construction: "їхати на автобусі / на метро / на трамваї." Drop Instrumental forms entirely.

| Word | Notes | Key collocations |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| метро | Indeclinable noun; very high spoken frequency in Kyiv | їхати на метро, станція метро, лінія метро |
| зупинка | Core transport noun, f. (зупинки); highest frequency for bus/tram | наступна зупинка, автобусна зупинка, на зупинці |
| маршрутка | Colloquial f. noun; ubiquitous in Ukrainian daily speech | зупиніть на зупинці, повна маршрутка, передайте за проїзд |
| пересадка | Derived from пересідати; medium frequency, essential for multi-leg trips | робити пересадку, станція пересадки, без пересадок |

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Arsenalna station depth**: Arsena´lna (Арсена´льна) is the world's deepest metro station at 105.5 m — the escalator ride takes ~3 minutes. Commuters read books, listen to music, or chat. A genuine discovery moment: "Why is the escalator so long?"
2. **"Передайте за проїзд!"**: In marshrutkas, cash passes hand-to-hand from passenger to driver. Social protocol requires "За одного´, будь ла´ска" (for one, please) and trusting strangers to relay your money. Creates immediate real-world dialogue.
3. **Kyiv Digital (Ки´їв Цифрови´й)**: Since 2019, Kyiv has shifted from metal жетони (tokens) to contactless bank cards and QR codes validated on touchscreen validators. This is a genuine generational contrast — older Kyivans may say "жетон" while younger speakers say "прикла´сти ка´ртку."

## Common Learner Errors

1. **іти´ vs. ї´хати**: "Я йду´ на метро´" → **Я їду´ на метро´** — English "go" covers both walking and riding; Ukrainian splits by mode of movement. High priority; plan flags this explicitly.
2. **зупи´нка vs. ста´нція**: Learners use зупи´нка for metro → **ста´нція метро´** — bus/tram have зупи´нки; metro/train have ста´нції. A simple table drill with photos fixes this.
3. **Omitting preposition with vehicle**: "Я їду автобус" → **Я їду на авто´бусі** — calque from English "I'm on the bus." At A1 use only Locative (на + Loc.). Do NOT introduce Instrumental forms (they are A2).
4. **Imperative confusion — asking the driver**: "Зупини´" (informal) vs. "Зупині´ть" (formal/plural) — marshrutkas use the plural polite imperative. Learners default to informal.

## Teaching Strategy

- **Discovery exercise**: Show a short Kyiv metro map snippet with 3 stations and ask: "You are at Хреща´тик and need to get to Арсена´льна. What do you say to your friend?" Give two options: "Я йду´ на Арсена´льну" vs. "Я їду´ на Арсена´льну." Learner picks → rule emerges from contrast.
- **Best context**: Marshrutka scenario — the learner is passenger #3, needs to pass 20 UAH, wants to get off at the next stop. Requires "Передайте за проїзд!", "За одного!", "Зупині´ть на зупи´нці!". All core phrases arise naturally in 30 seconds.
- **Contrastive pairs for Де/Куди**:
  - "Де ви?" — "На зупи´нці." (Locative — location)
  - "Куди ви їдете?" — "На Хреща´тик." (Accusative — destination)
  These two questions side-by-side make the case contrast concrete.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Instrumental is A2-only** — use ONLY "їхати на [Locative]" throughout. The plan's vocabulary_hints mention "їхати автобусом" but this must be omitted or replaced with "їхати на авто´бусі."
- Module 57 — imperative is in-scope (from M47). Use 2nd-person polite plural imperative (Зупині´ть, Переда´йте, Покажі´ть) consistently in the marshrutka dialogue.
- "Наступна зупинка..." announcement pattern is great for a blockquote container — mimics real metro/bus audio.
- Keep metro/bus/marshrutka vocabulary in a 3-column table for fast visual contrast.
- The Kyiv Digital / жетони contrast is a natural engagement box: "Did you know? Before 2019, Kyivans used metal tokens (жето´ни)…"
