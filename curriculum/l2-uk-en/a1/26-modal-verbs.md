# Lesson Content

## warm-up
**The Forbidden Fruit**

I eat pizza. (Statement).
I **want** to eat pizza. (Desire).
I **can** eat pizza. (Ability).
I **must** eat pizza. (Necessity / Addiction).

Verbs are actions.
Modals are attitudes.
In this module, we will learn how to add "Attitude" to your sentences.
You change the world not by doing, but by **wanting** to do.
