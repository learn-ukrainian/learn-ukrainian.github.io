# Weather & Nature

> **Note:** Weather and nature are everyday topics! Learn to describe what you see outside and talk about seasons.

## Warm-up: What's the Weather Like?

Look outside! Is it sunny or cloudy? Warm or cold? What season is it?

Weather is one of the most common conversation topics worldwide. In Ukraine, weather varies dramatically ‚Äî hot continental summers, cold winters, beautiful springs and autumns. Learning weather vocabulary lets you participate in small talk and plan activities.

Ukrainian weather expressions often use IMPERSONAL constructions. Instead of "It is cold" (with "it" as subject), Ukrainian says "–•–æ–ª–æ–¥–Ω–æ" ‚Äî just the state, no subject! This is a fundamental difference from English and makes Ukrainian weather expressions very direct.

Nature vocabulary is also essential for appreciating Ukraine's beautiful landscapes ‚Äî the Carpathian mountains, vast steppe, and famous sunflower fields!

By mastering this module, you'll be able to discuss weather, describe seasons, and talk about the natural world around you.

> üí° **Thinking Trick:** Ukrainian weather is SUBJECTLESS. Don't look for "it"! Just say the condition: –•–æ–ª–æ–¥–Ω–æ. –¢–µ–ø–ª–æ. –°–æ–Ω—è—á–Ω–æ. Direct and elegant!

## Cultural Insight: Nature in Ukrainian Culture üá∫üá¶

**Nature in Ukrainian identity:**
Ukraine is called "the breadbasket of Europe" for its fertile soil. Nature symbolism is everywhere:

- **–°–æ–Ω—è—à–Ω–∏–∫ (Sunflower):** Symbol of Ukraine, famous fields in summer
- **–ö–∞–ª–∏–Ω–∞ (Guelder rose):** National symbol, appears in folk songs
- **–°—Ç–µ–ø (Steppe):** The vast grasslands, celebrated in poetry
- **–î–Ω—ñ–ø—Ä–æ (Dnipro River):** "Father Dnipro" ‚Äî central to Ukrainian history

**Weather proverbs:**
- "–Ø–∫ –≥—Ä—ñ–º —É —Ç—Ä–∞–≤–Ω—ñ, –±—É–¥–µ —Ö–ª—ñ–± –¥–æ–±—Ä–∏–π." (Thunder in May means good bread.)
- "–ó–∏–º–æ—é —Å–Ω—ñ–≥ ‚Äî –≤–ª—ñ—Ç–∫—É —Ö–ª—ñ–±." (Winter snow ‚Äî summer bread.)

**Pet culture:**
Ukrainians love pets! Cats (–∫–æ—Ç–∏) and dogs (—Å–æ–±–∞–∫–∏) are popular. Many Ukrainians rescued pets during war evacuations ‚Äî pets are family!

> üìú **History Bite:** The steppe was homeland to Cossacks who fought for Ukrainian freedom. Poems by Taras Shevchenko celebrate the steppe's beauty. Russian destruction now threatens Ukraine's nature ‚Äî but Ukrainians protect their land!

> üìú **Myth Buster:** Some claim Ukraine is "just flat plains." FALSE! Ukraine has the Carpathian Mountains (up to 2061m), beautiful Black Sea coast, and diverse ecosystems. Its nature rivals Western European countries!
