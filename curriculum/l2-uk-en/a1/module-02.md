## cultural insight

### The Curse of Italics
You walk down the street and see a handwritten chalkboard menu. You panic.
Why does **Ğ¢** look like **m**?
Why does **Ğ”** look like **g**?

Ukrainian Italic (Cursive) has evolved differently from Print.
*   Print **Ğ¢** -> Italic *Ñ‚* (looks like *m*)
*   Print **Ğ”** -> Italic *Ğ´* (looks like *g*)
*   Print **Ğ˜** -> Italic *Ğ¸* (looks like *u*)

> âš ï¸ **Traveler Warning**
