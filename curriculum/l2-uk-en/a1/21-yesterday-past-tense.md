# Lesson Content

## warm-up
**The Time Traveler**

Until now, you lived in the "Now".
"I read." "I eat." "I sleep."
But life has a history.
To tell stories, you need the **Past Tense**.
In English, you add "-ed" (Walk -> Walked) or break the rules (Go -> Went).
In Ukrainian, it is easier.
You just check the gender of the **doer**.
- **He** -> -Ğ’.
- **She** -> -Ğ›Ğ.
- **It** -> -Ğ›Ğ.
- **They** -> -Ğ›Ğ˜.
