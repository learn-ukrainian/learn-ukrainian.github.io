# Lesson Content

## warm-up
**The GPS System**

You have a laser beam to point at Objects (Accusative).
You have a Soul Detector to point at People (Animate Accusative).
But you are floating in space.
"Is the passport **in** the bag?"
"Is the phone **on** the table?"
"Is Ivan **in** Kyiv?"

To anchor yourself in the universe, you need the **Locative Case**.
It answers the question **Ğ”Ğµ?** (Where?).
It acts like a GPS coordinate.

## presentation

### Section 1: The Magic Ending (-I)

- **Ğ¡Ñ‚Ñ–Ğ»** (Table) -> ĞĞ° ÑÑ‚Ğ¾Ğ»**Ñ–**.
- **ĞšÑ–Ñ‚** (Cat) -> ĞĞ° ĞºĞ¾Ñ‚**Ñ–** (On the cat).

### Section 2: In vs On (Ğ’ vs ĞĞ°)

**Logic:**

- **ĞĞ°** ÑÑ‚Ğ¾Ğ»Ñ– (On the table) - surface.

- **ĞĞ°** ĞºĞ¾Ğ½Ñ†ĞµÑ€Ñ‚Ñ– (At a concert) - event.
