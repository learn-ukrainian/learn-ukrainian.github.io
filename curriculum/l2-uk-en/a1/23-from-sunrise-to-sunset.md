> ğŸš‚ **Culture Corner: The Train Schedule (Ukrzaliznytsia)**
> Ukrainian trains (Ukrzaliznytsia) are religious about the 24-hour clock.

- But being 30 minutes late is rude.
- "Academic Quarter" (15 mins) is generally forgiven socially.
