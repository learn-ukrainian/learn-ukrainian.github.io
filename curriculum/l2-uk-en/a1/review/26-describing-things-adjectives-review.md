### Example 3: Soft Stem Distinction (lines 68-82)
> **Soft Stem Adjectives (-ій/-я/-є/-і)**
>
> Some adjectives have soft stems ending in -ій:
>
> | Gender | Ending | Example | English |
> |--------|--------|---------|---------|
> | masculine | -ій | синій светр | blue sweater |
> | feminine | -я | синя сумка | blue bag |
> | neuter | -є | синє небо | blue sky |
> | plural | -і | сині квіти | blue flowers |

**Why it works:** Explains soft stem as distinct pattern. Uses color adjective (синій) familiar from previous learning. Shows parallel structure to hard stems (only vowels differ). Prevents error of using -ий with soft-stem adjectives. Notes that plural -і is same for both stem types.

### Example 4: Stem Identification Rule (lines 99-105)
> **How to Find the Stem**
>
> To find the adjective stem, remove the -ий ending:
> - новий → нов- (stem) → нова, нове, нові
> - старий → стар- (stem) → стара, старе, старі
> - великий → велик- (stem) → велика, велике, великі

**Why it works:** Provides algorithm for generating all forms. Shows three examples demonstrating process. Empowers learners to conjugate any adjective. Prevents memorization overload (learn pattern, not 4 forms per adjective).

### Example 5: Antonym Pairs (lines 109-130)
> **Common Antonym Pairs**
>
> - великий ↔ малий (big ↔ small)
> - новий ↔ старий (new ↔ old)
> - гарний ↔ поганий (good/beautiful ↔ bad/ugly)
> - дорогий ↔ дешевий (expensive ↔ cheap)
> - важкий ↔ легкий (heavy/difficult ↔ light/easy)

**Why it works:** Organizes vocabulary by semantic opposition. Creates memorable pairs. Doubles vocabulary efficiency (learn two related words together). Shows practical descriptive range. 15 pairs cover essential description needs (size, quality, age, weight, difficulty, temperature, speed, etc.).

### Example 6: Stem Type Explanation (lines 132-136)
> **Why Some Adjectives Are Soft**
>
> Adjectives with stems ending in **ж, ч, ш, щ, й** use soft endings (-ій, not -ий). Examples:
> - свіжий (fresh) — свіж + ій
> - гарячий (hot) — гаряч + ій

**Why it works:** Explains phonetic reason for soft stems. Lists the five palatalized consonants (ж, ч, ш, щ, й). Provides two examples showing stem + ending. Teaches pattern recognition (if stem ends in these consonants, expect soft endings). Linguistic depth prevents confusion.

**Why it works:** Natural apartment description conversation. Shows multiple adjectives with correct agreement (новий будинок m., велика кухня f., маленька ванна f.).
