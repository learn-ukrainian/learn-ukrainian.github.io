The paragraph after Dialogue 1 states:

> "This is the key to Ukrainian past tense: the ending tells you the **speaker's** gender, not who they are grammatically."

This is **factually imprecise**. The ending tells you the **subject's** gender, not the speaker's. In the dialogue itself, Оксана (female speaker) says "Він гуляв у парку" — the -в ending marks Тарас's (the subject's) gender, not Оксана's.
