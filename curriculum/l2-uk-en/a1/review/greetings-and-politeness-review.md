## Discrepancies

### Discrepancy 1: Incorrect Base Word for Grandfather
- **Module says:** "- **Дідусю!** (Grandpa!) — from бабусь"
- **Reference says:** `бабусь` is the genitive plural of "бабуся" (grandma). "Дідусю" derives from "дідусь" (grandpa).
- **Source:** VESUM Dictionary Verification
- **Severity:** HIGH
- **Suggested fix:** Change "бабусь" to "дідусь".

### Discrepancy 2: Latin Script Transliteration in Target Language Texts
- **Module says:** "Ukrainian uses **pane (Mr.)** and **pani (Ms.)**: Добрий день, pane Petre! Добрий день, pani Oksano!"
- **Reference says:** The module plan specifies Cyrillic forms (`пане`, `пані`). Hard rules prohibit Latin transliteration.
- **Source:** Plan YAML
- **Severity:** HIGH
- **Suggested fix:** Use Cyrillic "пане" and "пані".

### Discrepancy 3: English Translation Hallucinations Replacing Target Vocabulary
- **Module says:** "- **Goodbye!** (informal, to one person you address as ти)" and in dialogues: "My name is Taras."
- **Reference says:** The plan specifies the Ukrainian target vocabulary to be taught: `Бувай/Бувайте` for informal goodbyes and `Мене звати...` for introductions. The LLM generated English instead of Ukrainian in several spots.
- **Source:** Plan YAML
- **Severity:** HIGH
- **Suggested fix:** Replace the hallucinated English phrases with the correct Ukrainian vocabulary (Бувай, Мене звати, Давай на ти).
