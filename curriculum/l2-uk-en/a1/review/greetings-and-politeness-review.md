## 2. Issues Found

**Section: Basic Greetings (Вітання)**
No major issues found. Good use of phrases like "Приві́т! Як спра́ви?". The "Casual Hello" provides simple context.

**Section: Formal vs. Informal: 'Ти' and 'Ви'**
The distinction is clearly laid out, but the phrase "Як ти?" is slightly colloquial compared to "Як твої справи". The Golden Rule of Respect sets the right tone.

**Section: Politeness Magic Words (Ввічливість)**
"Будь ласка" is well explained. Giving Thanks has a solid list of alternatives to "Дякую".

**Section: Introductions (Знайомство)**
The module correctly replaces direct translation of "My name is". The phrase "Як вас зва́ти?" is taught accurately.

**Section: Summary and Self-Check**
The summary is concise. The vocabulary section serves as a solid recap for all previous points.

## 3. Recommendation
**Status:** PASS
The module effectively introduces polite greetings and the crucial T-V distinction for A1 learners. The content is accessible and well-structured.
