## Findings
[Linguistic accuracy] [Critical]
Location: Екстрені ситуації (Emergencies) — "Because you are addressing an emergency operator or a random stranger on the street, you consistently use the formal plural imperative ending in **-іть**."
Issue: Factually incorrect grammar rule. The formal plural imperative also frequently ends in "-те" (e.g., "Викличте" which is taught immediately after). Stating it consistently ends in "-іть" is wrong and will confuse learners.
Fix: Change the rule to include both common endings "-іть" and "-те".

[Linguistic accuracy] [Minor]
Location: Екстрені ситуації (Emergencies) — "The number 101 connects directly to the пожежна допомога (fire service)."
Issue: "Пожежна допомога" is a non-standard collocation, likely calqued from "швидка допомога" or "медична допомога". The standard term is "пожежна служба" or "пожежна охорона".
Fix: Replace "пожежна допомога" with "пожежна служба".

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is very strong in pedagogy and tone, but the factual error regarding imperative endings must be corrected to prevent learners from being confused by the "Викличте" example.
