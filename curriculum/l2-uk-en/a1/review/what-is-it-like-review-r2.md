2. `У мене́` used instead of `У ме́не` (prepositions shift the stress on personal pronouns to the first syllable).

## Findings

[2. Linguistic accuracy] [CRITICAL]
Location: "**Тарас:** Так, але́ вона́ доро́га. *(Yes, but it is expensive.)*"
Issue: The stress mark on `доро́га` is incorrect. The word `доро́га` with stress on the penultimate syllable is the noun "road". The feminine adjective for "expensive" has the stress on the final syllable: `дорога́`.
Fix: Change `доро́га` to `дорога́`.

[2. Linguistic accuracy] [CRITICAL]
Location: "you can proudly say **У мене́ є великий стіл** (I have a big table)."
Issue: The preposition "у" forces a stress shift onto the first syllable of the pronoun "мене". It must be pronounced and written as `У ме́не`. `У мене́` is a phonetics error.
Fix: Change `У мене́` to `У ме́не`.

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is exceptionally well-written, engaging, and structurally perfect. However, two critical linguistic errors regarding stress placement (`доро́га` and `У мене́`) must be corrected to prevent teaching learners the wrong pronunciation.
