## Findings
[Engagement & tone] [minor]
Location: Section "Звуки і літери" — "This distinction is the absolute foundation of the Ukrainian language." and "In Ukrainian, this boundary between the spoken sound and the written letter is treated as a sacred distinction from the very first day of school. You cannot truly learn the language without understanding that your mouth makes sounds, and your pen writes letters."
Issue: Overly dramatic and gamified language; telling instead of showing.
Fix: Tone down the dramatic phrasing to be more professional and grounded.

[Engagement & tone] [minor]
Location: Section "Голосні звуки" — "The vowel is the powerful motor of the word, providing all the necessary energy and rhythm."
Issue: Generic enthusiasm and overly metaphorical language.
Fix: Simplify to describe the phonetic function without hyperbole.

[Engagement & tone] [minor]
Location: Section "Приголосні звуки" — "Mastering this hard and soft distinction is the ultimate key to developing a natural native Ukrainian accent."
Issue: Promotional and cliché tone ("ultimate key").
Fix: Remove the hyperbole.

[Pedagogical quality] [minor]
Location: Section "Голосні звуки" — "Watch Anna Ohoiko's pronunciation videos for each vowel letter — watch, listen, and repeat."
Issue: The instruction to watch the videos is given in the Vowels section, but the actual activity marker (`watch-and-repeat`) is not injected until the end of the Consonants section.
Fix: Change the text to inform the learner that they will practice with the videos for all letters after covering consonants.

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is factually, pedagogically, and linguistically excellent. However, there are multiple instances of overly dramatic phrasing and generic enthusiasm that violate the engagement and tone guidelines. A few minor text replacements are required to correct the tone and adjust the flow of the video activity instruction.
