## Findings

[3. Pedagogical quality] [Major]
Location: Section "Десятки і сотні — Tens and Hundreds", paragraph starting with "These larger numbers are indispensable..."
Issue: The explanation for currency endings based on the final digit ("amounts ending in one", "ending in two, three, or four") fails to exclude the teens (11-14), which use the genitive plural form. This omission could lead learners to incorrectly form "одинадцять гривня" or "дванадцять гривні", unlike the Age section which correctly notes the exception.
Fix: Update the rule to explicitly exclude the teens.
