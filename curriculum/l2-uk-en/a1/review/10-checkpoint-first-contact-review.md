### Example 5: Cultural Synthesis - Ukrainian Honesty (lines 341-350)
> **Culture Corner: "Як справи?"**
> In English, "How are you?" is often automatic - people don't expect a real answer. In Ukrainian, **"Як справи?"** is a genuine question. Ukrainians will tell you the TRUTH - good, bad, complicated. And they expect you to listen!
>
> Common honest answers:
> - **Нормально.** (So-so. / It's okay.)
> - **Непогано.** (Not bad.)
> - **Бувало й краще.** (I've had better days.)
> - **Чудово!** (Wonderfully!)

**Why it works:** Prevents culture shock, gives authentic responses (not just "добре"), prepares learners for genuine interaction.
