## Findings

**[EXERCISE QUALITY] [minor]**
Location: Fill-in #5 "Complete the conflict sentence"
Issue: The exercise decomposes one sentence ("Я хочу гуляти, але не можу — мушу працювати") into 6 single-word blanks. This is mechanical reconstruction rather than productive practice. The plan hint suggests "Complete: Я ___ гуляти, але не ___ — ___ працювати" with 6 items — implying multiple varied sentences using the pattern, not one sentence split 6 ways.
