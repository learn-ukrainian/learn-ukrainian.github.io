## Findings

Location: Читання (Reading Practice), last paragraph: `**«Я зараз в Києві! Хочу їхати у Львів!»**`
Issue: Euphony rule violation. The word "зараз" ends in a consonant ('з'), and "Києві" starts with a consonant ('К'). According to Ukrainian euphony rules (taught explicitly in Pattern 1 of this very module), between two consonants, the preposition must be "у" to avoid a consonant cluster. It should be "у Києві".
Fix: Change `в Києві` to `у Києві`.
