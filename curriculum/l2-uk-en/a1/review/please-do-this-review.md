## Findings

[PEDAGOGICAL QUALITY] [minor]
Location: Dialogue 2 — "Слухай, ходімо в кафе!"
Issue: "ходімо" is a 1st person plural imperative (let's go), which is outside the stated scope of "2nd person ти and ви forms only." While natural in dialogue, a learner might wonder what form this is since it's not explained.
Fix: Add a brief parenthetical note after the dialogue analysis mentioning that "ходімо" means "let's go" and is a different form they'll learn later.

[VOCABULARY COVERAGE] [minor]
Location: Vocabulary hints — recommended word "показати (to show)"
Issue: "показати" from the recommended vocabulary list does not appear anywhere in the module.
Fix: Could be worked into a dialogue or example, but this is a recommended (not required) word, so non-blocking.

[DIALOGUE QUALITY] [minor]
Location: Dialogue 2 — "Скажи, що ти хочеш?"
Issue: Slightly unnatural for a café setting — a friend would more likely say "Що будеш?" or "Що замовиш?" However, this construction serves the clear pedagogical purpose of modeling "скажи" in context, and it is grammatically correct.
Fix: No fix needed — pedagogical value outweighs naturalness concern here.
