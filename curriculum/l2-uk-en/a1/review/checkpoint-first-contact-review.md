## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Linguistic/Activity Error
- **Location**: Activity 1 (Quiz), Item 4
- **Original**: `-яті` (marked as `correct: true`)
- **Problem**: The correct 3rd person plural ending for "говорити" is "-ять". "-яті" is a typo and non-existent ending.
- **Fix**: Change `-яті` to `-ять`.

### Issue 2: Grammar Scope Creep
- **Location**: Activity 3 (Fill-in), Item 5
- **Original**: `Він ... листа.` (Answer: `пише`)
- **Problem**: "листа" is the Genitive case form. For an inanimate masculine noun like "лист", the Accusative (which is the object of "пише") should be "лист" (identical to Nominative). Genitive is not taught in this module.
- **Fix**: Change to `Він пише лист.` or `Він пише текст.`
