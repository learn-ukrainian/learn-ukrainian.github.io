## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Incomplete Model Example
- **Location**: Section "Skill 4: Питальні речення"
- **Original**: «**Тому що...** — Because...»
- **Problem**: This is a sentence fragment. A beginner needs a complete model to see how "тому що" connects to a reason. Leaving it hanging is pedagogically weak.
- **Fix**: Provide a full sentence example, e.g., "Тому що я голодний" or "Тому що я турист".

### Issue 2: Incomplete Conjugation Paradigm
- **Location**: Section "Skill 3: Дієвідміна", under "Practice"
- **Original**:
«*   Я **пишу** (s → sh)
    *   Ти **пишеш**
    *   Вони **пишуть**»
- **Problem**: The stem change (s → sh) is introduced, but skipping the 3rd person singular ("Він/Вона") creates a gap. A learner might wonder if it reverts to "Він писає" (incorrect).
- **Fix**: Add "Він/Вона **пише**" to confirm the stem change persists in all forms.

### Issue 3: Missing IPA for High-Frequency Phrase
- **Location**: Section "Skill 5: Замовлення їжі"
- **Original**: «2.  **Я буду...** — I will have... (Literally: I will be...)»
- **Problem**: This is a key phrase for the module ("Your superpower"). Unlike "Можна", "Будь ласка", and "Дякую", it lacks IPA. "Я буду" is high frequency and pronunciation guidance helps.
- **Fix**: Add IPA [jɑ ˈbudu].
