**Factual grammar error found:**

The content states: *"The literal structure is 'at me hurts head' — that's a dative construction, and you'll study it properly at A2."*

VESUM confirms: **мене** = `v_rod` (genitive) / `v_zna` (accusative). **Мені** = `v_dav` (dative). The construction "у мене" uses the **genitive** of "я," NOT the dative. Calling this "dative" is factually wrong and will confuse learners at A2 when they encounter actual dative constructions like "Мені погано."

Later in the text: *"And **Мені погано** uses **мені** (another dative form)"* — the word "another" implies "у мене" was also dative, compounding the error. "Мені" IS dative, but "мене" is not.
