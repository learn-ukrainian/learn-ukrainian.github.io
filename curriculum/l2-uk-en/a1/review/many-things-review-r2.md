**One misleading claim:**
The module states: *"The word річ (thing) becomes речі (things) — not a standard pattern."* VESUM confirms `речі` as the regular nominative plural of `річ` (noun:inanim:f:v_naz → noun:inanim:p:v_naz). The ending **-і** is standard for feminine soft-sign nouns (cf. ніч → ночі, сіль → солі). What's notable is the **stem vowel alternation** (і→е), not the ending itself. Calling this "not a standard pattern" may mislead A1 learners into thinking it's irregular when it's actually predictable for this noun class.
