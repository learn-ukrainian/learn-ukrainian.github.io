## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Missing IPA for New Food Vocabulary
- **Location**: Sections "Зразок Меню" and "Опис страв"
- **Original**: `Гречка`, `Сметана`, `Цибуля`, `Бульйон` (and others in lists)
- **Problem**: These are new A1 words introduced without IPA. A beginner cannot guess how to pronounce `бульйон` [bulʲˈjɔn] or `гречка` [ˈɦrɛt͡ʃkɐ]. The prompt explicitly requires IPA for the "first occurrence of each new vocabulary word".
- **Fix**: Add IPA to the menu items and description words.

### Issue 2: Imperative Tone
- **Location**: Section "Крок 3: Меню та запитання"
- **Original**: «Дайте, будь ласка, меню.»
- **Problem**: While grammatically correct, "Дайте" (Give) is a direct imperative. Even with "please", it can sound blunt coming from a learner who might miss the intonation.
- **Fix**: Replace with «Можна меню, будь ласка?» (May I have...) or «Принесіть, будь ласка, меню» (Bring...). "Можна" is already there, so just removing the blunt option is safer.

### Issue 3: Unnatural Vocative
- **Location**: Діалог 3
- **Original**: «Офіціанте, можна рахунок, будь ласка?»
- **Problem**: Calling a waiter "Офіціанте!" is archaic or overly formal/theatrical in modern Ukraine. It is more natural to use «Перепрошую» (Excuse me) or just make eye contact.
- **Fix**: Change to «Перепрошую, можна рахунок, будь ласка?»
