## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Pragmatic Utility (Pedagogy)
- **Location**: Section "Чергування приголосних: Правило К-Г-Х", Subsection "Mutation Х → С"
- **Original**: «Муха на **мусі**» — A fly on a fly (rare in real life, but grammatically correct!).
- **Problem**: While funny, this is confusing for a beginner trying to grasp the concept. It distracts from the grammar rule with a semantic puzzle. A learner needs a clear, visualizable example.
- **Fix**: Replace with "Черепаха (turtle) -> На черепасі". "The cat is on the turtle" is a classic, clear image.

### Issue 2: English Phrasing (Language)
- **Location**: Section "Масштаб міста: Архітектура", Paragraph 2
- **Original**: "...you likely live in a dormitory suburb/sleeping district..."
- **Problem**: "Sleeping district" is a direct translation of *спальний район* and sounds unnatural in English. "Dormitory suburb" is technical.
- **Fix**: Use "residential area" which is the standard English equivalent, perhaps keeping "sleeping district" as a literal translation in parentheses for cultural color.

### Issue 3: Definition Precision (Pedagogy)
- **Location**: Section "Основні локативи (Basic Locatives)", Item 2
- **Original**: "Work is treated as an activity."
- **Problem**: Slightly too narrow; it's also a place of employment or event.
- **Fix**: "Work is treated as an activity or event." (Aligns better with later explanation of "Activities/Events").
