## Findings
[Pedagogical quality] [Major]
Location: `**Pattern 6: Directions (M33)** — imperative forms for giving directions.`
Issue: The text misclassifies adverbs of direction (`направо`, `наліво`) as "imperative forms", which is pedagogically inaccurate and confusing for learners.
Fix: Change the description to "imperative verbs and direction words".

[Pedagogical quality] [Major]
Location: `**Я іду до музе́ю.** *(I go to the museum.)* **Потім хо́чу ї́хати до Лаври.**`
Issue: The text introduces the `до + genitive` construction for direction (`до музею`, `до Лаври`), which breaks the pedagogical scope of A1.5. Pattern 3 explicitly teaches learners that direction (Куди?) uses `в/на + accusative` (`в музей`, `в Лавру`). Introducing a new case for direction without explanation creates confusion.
Fix: Change `до музею` to `в музей` and `до Лаври` to `в Лавру`.

[Structural integrity] [Minor]
Location: `*(«Excuse me, where is the National Museum?»)*`, `*(«The museum is on the square. Go straight, then left.»)*`, `*(«I'm in Kyiv now! I want to go to Lviv!»)*`
Issue: The writer used Ukrainian guillemets (« ») for the English translations inside parentheses. Standard English typography requires double quotes (" ").
Fix: Replace the Ukrainian guillemets with standard English double quotes in the English translations.

## Verdict: REVISE
The module is highly comprehensive and engaging, but it has major pedagogical flaws that break the PPP flow (misclassifying adverbs as imperative verbs and introducing out-of-scope grammatical cases for direction). It requires a REVISE to correct these issues before publishing.
