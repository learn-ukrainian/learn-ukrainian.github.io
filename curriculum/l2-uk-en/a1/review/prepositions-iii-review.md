### Lesson Arc Analysis

*   **WELCOME**: Excellent. «Привіт, сусіде! Заходьте, двері відчинені.» creates immediate warmth.
*   **PREVIEW**: Clear objectives about space and movement.
*   **PRESENT**: Logical progression from "Where" (static) to "Whither/Whence" (dynamic).
*   **PRACTICE**: 10 activities covering various aspects of prepositions.
*   **CELEBRATE**: Encouraging closing: «Це ваш шлях до успіху.»

## Scores/Assessment

### 1. Linguistic Accuracy & Naturalness
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: Ukrainian is simple, natural, and appropriate for A1. IPA is accurate.
*   **Quote**: «Ваше перше питання: «Де капці?» [dɛ ˈkapt͡sʲi]» — very authentic cultural touch.

### 2. Pedagogical Depth (PPP)
*   **Score: 10/10**

### 3. Immersion Balance
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: 36.2% is perfect for late A1 (Module 30). English scaffolding is consistent.

### 4. Activity Richness
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: 10 activities is excellent density for A1. Variety of types (quiz, group-sort, fill-in, unjumble).

### 5. Humanity & Warmth
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: The "tutor voice" is very strong. Frequent use of «ви», «ти», and «ми».

## Issues/Critique

### 1. Activity Redundancy
*   **Issue**: There is a high density of `fill-in` activities (4 separate blocks). While repetition is good for A1, the instructions «Choose the correct preposition...» become a bit repetitive by the third instance.
*   **Quote**: «Choose the correct preposition for the location.» (used in multiple activities).
*   **Suggested Fix**: Vary the instructions slightly to keep the learner engaged, e.g., "Help Andriy find his way by choosing the right preposition."

### 2. IPA Consistency
*   **Issue**: In the sentence «Студент йде в школу» the IPA for «йде» is `[jde]`, but in «Студент йде в університет» it is `[jidɛ]`. While both can occur in fast speech/sandhi, it's better to stick to one form for A1 learners to avoid confusion.
*   **Quote**: «Студент йде в університет [stuˈdɛnt jidɛ ʋ uniʋɛrsɪˈtɛt].»

## Recommendation/Verdict

**Verdict: PASS**
The module is exceptionally well-crafted for an A1 learner. It is warm, logical, and culturally rich. The minor issues noted do not block the pedagogical value.
