## Issues/Critique
- Minor issue: The transition between "Мені подобається (I like)" and "Я люблю (I love)" could be slightly smoother with an extra bridging sentence.
- Section "Я хочу (I want)" is great but could use one more example.
- "Порівняння (Comparing Likes)" perfectly summarizes everything.

## Recommendation/Verdict
Verdict: PASS

The module successfully teaches students how to express their likes, loves, and wants. It correctly uses simple phrasing without introducing complex Dative or Instrumental grammar prematurely, which fits the A1 constraints.
