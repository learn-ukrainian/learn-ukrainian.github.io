**6. Factual error — у/в euphony explanation is WRONG:**

The module states:
> "The word **суботу** ends in a vowel, so the next word naturally starts with a consonant to keep the speech flowing smoothly. If the previous word ends in a vowel, you use **в** before a consonant. If it ends in a consonant, you use **у** before a consonant."

This is factually incorrect. Per Правопис §23 and Avramenko Grade 5–6 textbooks, the у/в alternation depends on the sounds **surrounding the preposition itself** — what comes BEFORE у/в and what comes AFTER it:
- **у** between consonants: `він у домі`
- **в** between vowels or after a vowel before a consonant: `риба в акваріумі`, `Ольга в салоні`
- **у** before в, ф, льв, зв, св, дв, тв, гв, хв: `він у вагоні`
- **У** at the start of a sentence before a consonant: `У лісі волого`

The claim that "суботу ends in a vowel" is irrelevant — суботу comes AFTER the preposition, not before it. The choice between "у суботу" and "в неділю" depends on context (what word precedes the preposition), not on any fixed rule about these specific days. Both "у неділю" and "в неділю" are correct depending on the preceding sound. Teaching this as a fixed pairing at A1 level implants a wrong mental model.
