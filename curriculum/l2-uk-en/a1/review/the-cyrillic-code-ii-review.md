## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Missing Apostrophe Explanation
- **Location**: Presentation Section / Plan Requirement
- **Original**: (Missing)
- **Problem**: The plan requires teaching 'сім'я' and 'читання слів з апострофом', but the text never explains what the apostrophe (') symbol means or how to pronounce it. The "Soft Sign" is explained, but its "hard" counterpart (apostrophe) is absent.
- **Fix**: Add a subsection or callout in Presentation (e.g., "The Anti-Softener: Apostrophe") explaining it separates the consonant from the iotated vowel.

### Issue 2: Ungrammatical Sentence in Activity 7
- **Location**: Activity 7 (Unjumble) / Item 4
- **Original**: «Київ центр» (from words: Київ, центр)
- **Problem**: "Київ центр" is not a grammatically correct sentence in Ukrainian. It is two nouns. It implies "Kyiv Center" (proper name?) or a broken "Kyiv is the center".
- **Fix**: Change to «Київ — це Європа» (Kyiv is Europe) using taught vocabulary, or «Це центр» (This is the center).

### Issue 3: Untaught Vocabulary in Activity 7
- **Location**: Activity 7 (Unjumble)
- **Original**: «Ми любимо літо», «Це смачне яблуко»
- **Problem**: The words 'любити' (to love) and 'смачне' (tasty) appear nowhere in the text or vocabulary list. A1.1 learners cannot unjumble words they haven't seen.
- **Fix**: Replace with sentences using known vocabulary.
    - Change «Ми любимо літо» → «Це гарне літо» (This is a beautiful summer).
    - Change «Це смачне яблуко» → «Це гарне яблуко» (This is a beautiful apple).

### Issue 4: Missing 'сім'я' in Text
- **Location**: Content Body
- **Original**: (Missing)
- **Problem**: 'сім'я' is in the vocabulary list and recommended in the plan, but appears nowhere in the text.
- **Fix**: Add «сім'я» to the "Практика" reading list (after adding the Apostrophe explanation).
