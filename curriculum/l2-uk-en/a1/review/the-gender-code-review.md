## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Cognitive Overload (Pedagogy)
- **Location**: Section "Практика: Чотири відміни (Practice: Four Declensions)"
- **Original**: «Ukrainian nouns belong to four distinct "families" called відміни...»
- **Problem**: This is Lesson 3. Students just learned the alphabet. Introducing the *concept* of declension families (and all 4 of them, including "changelings") creates massive cognitive friction. They need to know *Gender*, not *Morphological Class*. This belongs in A2 or late A1.
- **Fix**: Replace this entire section with a simple "Exceptions" note. "Most words follow the rules, but some are special. We will learn them later."

### Issue 2: Activity Misalignment (Beginner Safety)
- **Location**: Activities file, `group-sort` titled "Declension Families"
- **Original**: «Sort the words into their declension groups (Family 1-4).»
- **Problem**: Asks students to categorize "теля" and "ім'я" into "Family 4". This tests memory of the lecture, not language ability. It's a meta-grammar test, not a language test.
- **Fix**: Replace with a "Gender Sorting" activity or "Noun vs Name" recognition.

### Issue 3: Vocabulary Density
- **Location**: Section "Чоловічий рід (Masculine)" through "Середній рід (Neuter)"
- **Original**: Lists 12+ new nouns (брат, дім, стіл, хліб, сестра, мама, книга, земля, вікно, місто, море, сонце) in rapid succession.
- **Problem**: Too many new words introduced purely as grammatical examples without context or practice.
- **Fix**: Reduce the list or weave them into the "Mini-dialogue" section earlier to give them context.
