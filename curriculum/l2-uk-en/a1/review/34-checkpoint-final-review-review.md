### Example 2: Self-Assessment Rubric (lines 84-106)
> **Rate Your Skills (1-5)**
>
> For each competency above, rate yourself:
>
> **5 - Excellent:** I can do this confidently and easily
> **4 - Good:** I can do this with minor effort
> **3 - Fair:** I can do this but need more practice
> **2 - Limited:** I struggle with this
> **1 - Just Beginning:** I need significant review
>
> **Example:**
> "I can introduce myself" → Rate: 4 (I can do it but sometimes forget vocabulary)

**Why it works:** Provides clear 5-level scale. Shows descriptors for each level (confident → struggling). Includes example demonstrating how to use scale. Promotes honest self-assessment. Creates data for tracking progress. Shows what scores mean qualitatively.
