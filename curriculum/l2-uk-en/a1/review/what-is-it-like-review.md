## Findings
[9. Dialogue & conversation quality] [minor]
Location: Dialogue 2 — Window Shopping ("<div class="dialogue-line"><span class="speaker">Марія:</span> А це вікно? Яке воно?")
Issue: The dialogue takes place while "window shopping." One character asks about the "window" (вікно) and evaluates it as "чисте і світле." It is unnatural for shoppers to evaluate the physical shop window pane for cleanliness when they are supposedly looking at goods to buy. The writer chose "вікно" to have a neuter noun, but "крісло" (armchair) makes much more sense as an actual shopping item.
Fix: Replace the window with an armchair (`крісло`), and use adjectives that make sense for a shopping item (`велике і дороге`).

The module is exceptionally well-written and pedagogically sound. It just requires one minor logical fix in Dialogue 2 to make the context of "window shopping" completely natural.
