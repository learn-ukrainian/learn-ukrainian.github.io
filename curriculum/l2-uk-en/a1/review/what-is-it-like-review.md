**[PEDAGOGICAL QUALITY] [minor]**

Issue: ~150 words of English metalinguistic framing precede the first Ukrainian dialogue. In strict PPP, the "present" phase should lead with the target language input. The explanation-first approach is more deductive than the textbook-inspired inductive approach the plan implies.
Fix: Not actionable as a find/replace — would require restructuring. The current approach is still effective and within A1 L2 norms.

**[EXERCISE QUALITY] [minor]**
Location: §2 quiz, all items use format "___ це кімната?"
Issue: The construction "Яка це кімната?" is grammatically correct but less common as a standalone question than "Яка кімната?" The "це" adds a demonstrative nuance ("What kind of room is THIS?") that isn't quite what the exercise is testing.
Fix: Minor — the exercise is unambiguous and functional as-is.
