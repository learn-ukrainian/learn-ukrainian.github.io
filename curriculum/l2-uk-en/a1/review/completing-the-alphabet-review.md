## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Misspelled Distractors in Quizzes (VESUM Failure)
**Location**: `completing-the-alphabet.yaml`, Activity: `quiz`, "Apostrophe Rules"
**Problem**: The activity uses non-existent, deliberately misspelled words as distractors (e.g., `мясо`, `мьясо`, `м'асо`, `пять`, `пьять`). The pre-screen correctly flagged these as `[ACTIVITY_VESUM_FAIL]`. Showing incorrect spellings to A1 beginners is a pedagogical anti-pattern as they may inadvertently memorize the wrong visual forms.
**Fix**: Redesign the quiz to test the apostrophe rule without displaying misspellings. Use a fill-in-the-blank format, ask conceptual questions (e.g., "Why does the word `м'ясо` have an apostrophe?"), or use valid distractor words that do not take an apostrophe (like `день` or `сіль`).

**Fix**: Remove the comparison to `кін` and focus purely on the pronunciation shift of `кінь`, or replace it with a more common minimal pair (e.g., `син` / `синь` or `рис` / `рись`).
