## Findings
[2. Linguistic accuracy] [critical]
Location: Section "Майбутній час (Future Tense)", paragraph 4: "Note that the synthetic future (like **прочитаю**) exists in the language..."
Issue: The text incorrectly identifies "**прочитаю**" as the "synthetic future". In Ukrainian grammar, "прочитаю" is the simple future (проста форма) of a perfective verb. The synthetic future (складна форма) is formed with the infinitive + -му (e.g., читатиму).
Fix: Change "synthetic future" to "simple future".

[4. Vocabulary coverage] [minor]

Issue: The recommended vocabulary word "план" was not introduced in Ukrainian, although the English concept was used frequently to set up the lesson context.
Fix: Add the Ukrainian word in parentheses: "We make plans (**плани**)..."
