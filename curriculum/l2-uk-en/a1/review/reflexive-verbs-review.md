### Issue 2: Incorrect IPA Stress
- **Location**: Vocabulary Table / `–≤—З–Є—В–Є—Б—П`
- **Original**: `[ЋИ Лt Г…™t…™s ≤…Р]`
- **Problem**: The IPA uses `/ЋИ Нt Г…™t…™s ≤a/` (which uses a non-standard ` Н`) and in text `[ЋИ Лt Г…™t…™s ≤…Р]`. The primary stress in Ukrainian `–≤—З–Є—В–Є—Б—П` is on the first syllable, but the phoneme `/ Н/` is not a standard Ukrainian phoneme.
