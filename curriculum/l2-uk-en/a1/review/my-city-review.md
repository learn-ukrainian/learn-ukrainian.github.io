## Findings

**[EXERCISE QUALITY] [MAJOR]**
Location: Quiz "В or на? Choose the correct preposition" — all 8 items
Issue: The quiz offers three options: ["на", "в", "у"]. This conflates two distinct skills: (1) в/на prepositional choice (which preposition does this place take?) and (2) в/у euphonic alternation (which variant sounds better after the preceding word?). The plan says "В or на? Choose preposition for city places" — this should be a binary в/на decision. An A1 learner who correctly knows пошта takes "на" but банк takes "в" might still get Q5 wrong by choosing "в" instead of "у" after "Він". This tests euphony, not prepositional knowledge.
Fix: Simplify to two options ["в/у", "на"] so the quiz tests only the intended skill (preposition choice). Or restructure the sentences so euphony is not a confounding factor.

**[ENGAGEMENT & TONE] [MAJOR]**
Location: "Місця в місті" section, opening paragraph: "Every city (**місто**) is made up of different functional spaces. Whether you are running errands, seeking entertainment, or dealing with travel, you need to know the names of these essential city places."
Issue: The prose is functional but dry. "functional spaces" and "running errands" read like a textbook glossary introduction, not an engaging lesson for teens and adults. The module lacks cultural texture — what makes Ukrainian cities distinctive? What's a typical Ukrainian мікрорайон like? The plan doesn't demand this explicitly, but engagement is a scored dimension.
Fix: Add 2-3 sentences of cultural context — e.g., how Ukrainian cities are organized, the role of центр vs мікрорайон, the ubiquity of аптеки on every corner, the importance of площа as a civic gathering space.

**[PLAN ADHERENCE / STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY] [MINOR]**
Location: "Підсумок — Summary" section (~200 words vs 300 target)
Issue: Summary is ~33% under its 300-word section target, which exceeds the ±10% tolerance. The content is mostly reference tables with minimal prose.
Fix: Expand the Summary with a short practice paragraph — e.g., a model "tour of my city" text that a learner can use as a template, demonstrating all vocabulary and structures from the module in connected prose.

**[PEDAGOGICAL QUALITY] [MINOR]**
Location: "Місця в місті" section — vocabulary introduction as bullet lists
Issue: The 18 city places are introduced as a bare list with translations. While the plan's `content_outline` implies listing, pedagogically it would be stronger to group them thematically (errands: аптека, магазин, супермаркет, банк, пошта; culture: музей, театр, кінотеатр, бібліотека; etc.) and wrap each group in a sentence or two of context.
Fix: Minor improvement — not blocking. Could group vocabulary into 3-4 thematic clusters with a connecting sentence for each.
