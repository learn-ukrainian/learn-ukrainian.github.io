### Issue 4: Ambiguous Explanation
- **Location**: Section "A Note on 'Doors'"
- **Original**: «Це двері. (This is a door / These are doors.)»
- **Problem**: Confusing translation. "Це двері" primarily means "This is a door/These are doors" (Identification). It does NOT mean "These doors" (Specification), which is what the slash might imply to a beginner confusing it with "Ці двері".
- **Fix**: Clarify: «Це двері. (This is a door.)» vs «Ці двері. (These doors.)»
