## Strengths
- **Metaphorical Explanations**: The concept of "Pointing Words" as a "Verbal Finger" and the "Soul of the Room" make abstract grammar and culture concrete and memorable.
- **Pedagogical Foresight**: The "Great Trap" section explicitly addresses the confusion between `Це` as a particle (This is) vs. `Це` as a neuter demonstrative. This prevents a very common beginner error.
- **Cultural Integration**: Explaining `Покуття` and the difference between `Хата/Дім/Квартира` adds significant depth beyond just grammar.
