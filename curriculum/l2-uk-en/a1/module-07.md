# The Art of Asking

## warm-up

You can state facts: "I read."
But conversation is ping-pong. You need to hit the ball back.
**"Do you read?"**
**"Where do you read?"**
**"Why do you read?"**

In English, you need auxiliary verbs: "Do", "Does", "Are".
In Ukrainian, you just need a particle or an intonation.
