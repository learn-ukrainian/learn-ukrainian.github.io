## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: False Typographical Word Division Rule
**Location**: `## Правила переносу — Word Division Rules` -> `### Common Mistakes for English Speakers`
**Problem**: The text claims that dividing between consonants in "сестра" is an English mistake: "Splitting like English: ~~сес-тра~~ instead of **се-стра**". This is factually incorrect. According to Ukrainian Orthography (Правопис 2019, § 49), dividing as `сес-тра` is perfectly valid for line breaks (перенесення). Conflating phonetic syllabification with line-break rules creates a false grammatical restriction.
**Fix**: Remove the claim that `сес-тра` is an illegal hyphenation, and clarify that while spoken syllables are pushed forward (`се-стра`), written line breaks offer more flexibility.

### Issue 2: Impossible Syllable Fragments in Activities
**Location**: `activities/syllables-and-word-division.yaml` (`Syllable Boundaries 1 & 2`)
**Problem**: The `fill-in` activity asks the learner to complete words using splits that result in vowelless syllables, which directly violates the module's "Golden Rule" ("every vowel creates exactly one syllable"). For example, `се-ст___` with the answer `ра` implies the split is `се-ст-ра` (leaving `ст` without a vowel). `сіль-ськ___` with the answer `ий` implies `сіль-ськ-ий` (leaving `ськ` without a vowel).
**Fix**: Rewrite the blanks to split at valid syllable boundaries: `се-___` (answer: `стра`), `сіль-___` (answer: `ський`).

### Issue 3: Hallucinated Duplicate Options in Activities

### Issue 4: Contradictory Consonant Split Rules
**Location**: `### Consonant Clusters — Where Do You Split?` vs `### What You CAN Do`
**Problem**: The text creates a pedagogical trap. It teaches that "Ukrainian pushes as many consonants as possible to the next syllable" (`се-стра`). But then, in the very next section under Word Division, it states you CAN split "Between two consonants" and gives `ав-то-бус` as an example. The text tries to justify this by saying "two different consonant groups meet", which is meaningless to a beginner (`с` and `т` are also two different consonants).
**Fix**: Clarify that sonorant consonants (like `в` in `ав-то-бус`) often close a syllable, explaining the difference naturally rather than creating an unexplained contradiction.
