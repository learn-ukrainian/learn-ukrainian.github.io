## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Pedagogical Confusion (Case Mixing)
- **Location**: Section "Mutation Г → З" (Line ~198)
- **Original**: «* **Подруга** (female friend) → **У подруги** — Exception! Sometimes it stays hard in Genitive...»
- **Problem**: This module teaches Locative case (ending -і). Introducing a Genitive construction («у подруги» = at friend's place) with an -и ending confuses the learner. It looks like a Locative exception but is actually a different case.
- **Fix**: Replace with a clear Locative example for Г→З mutation, e.g., «Підлога → На підлозі» (which is used later in Dialogue 3).

### Issue 2: Low Immersion
- **Location**: Entire Module
- **Original**: (N/A - General)
- **Problem**: The module relies too heavily on English for explanations and setup. 27% immersion is below the A1.2 standard of 40-60%.
- **Fix**: Convert simple instruction lines to Ukrainian or bilingual. E.g., change "Let's look at three fixed phrases" to "Розглянемо три фрази (Let's look at three phrases)".

### Issue 3: Confusing Explanation
- **Location**: Section "The Concept of Location" (Line ~89)
- **Original**: «If you say "Я в концерті" ... it sounds like you are physically inside a piano or an instrument.»
- **Problem**: This is misleading. "In a concert" implies being part of the performance or inside the hall, not inside an instrument. It confuses the literal meaning.
- **Fix**: "It sounds like you are inside the abstract concept, or physically inside the hall itself, which is unnatural. Events take **на**."
