## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Tense Consistency in Narrative
- **Location**: Section "Історія: Мій похід на Привоз"
- **Original**: «Була субота. Ранок. Ринок гудів... Я бачу багато людей.»
- **Problem**: The text jumps from Past ("Була", "гудів") to Present ("бачу", "говорять") and back to Past ("пішов", "стояла"). For A1, this is confusing and breaks the timeline.
- **Fix**: Standardize to Present Tense for vividness and simplicity (matches the rest of the story).

### Issue 2: Testing Untaught Vocabulary
- **Location**: Activities file, `match-up` "Одиниці виміру"
- **Original**: Pairs for "літр" and "пляшка".
- **Problem**: These units were not taught in the "Units of Measurement" section (only kg, gram, bunch, piece were).
- **Fix**: Remove these pairs to strictly test what was taught.

### Issue 3: Confusing Gloss (Gender)
- **Location**: Section "Бессарабський ринок"
- **Original**: «Він дуже старий. 100 років. (He is very old. 100 years.)»
- **Problem**: Translating "Він" (referring to "ринок") as "He" in English reinforces a common learner error. In English, a market is "It".
- **Fix**: Change gloss to "(It is very old. 100 years.)"
