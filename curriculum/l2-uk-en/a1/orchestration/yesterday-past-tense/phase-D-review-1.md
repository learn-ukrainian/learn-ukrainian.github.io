## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Natural Lexical Choice (Weather)
- **Location**: Section "Розповідь: Моє вчора"
- **Original**: «Погода була добра.»
- **Problem**: "Добра погода" sounds like "kind weather". Standard collocation is "гарна погода" or "хороша погода".
- **Fix**: Change to «Погода була гарна.»

### Issue 2: Natural Lexical Choice (Actor)
- **Location**: Section "Діалог 1"
- **Original**: «Актор був добрий?»
- **Problem**: Similar to above. "Добрий" usually means kind-hearted. For professional skill/quality, "хороший" is better.
- **Fix**: Change to «Актор був хороший?»

### Issue 3: Styistic/Calque (Law)
- **Location**: Section "Закон ЗУНР (1919)"
- **Original**: «ЗУНР ... прийняла закон про українську мову.»
- **Problem**: While "прийняти закон" is common, "ухвалити закон" is the standard literary/legal term and avoids the calque from Russian "принимать".
- **Fix**: Change to «ухвалила закон».

### Issue 4: Jargon Overload
- **Location**: Section "Irregular Verb: Йти (To Go)"
- **Original**: «It undergoes a complete change in the stem, a phenomenon called suppletion... You must use the suppletive root **іш-**»
- **Problem**: "Suppletion" and "suppletive root" are linguistics jargon unnecessary for A1.
- **Fix**: Simplify explanation to "special form".

### Issue 5: Purple Prose / Metaphor Overload
- **Location**: Section "Контекст: Вчора куратора"
- **Original**: «Do you see how the endings dance? ... The rhythm of the past tense creates the music of the story.»
- **Problem**: Typical LLM fluff. It's confusing.
- **Fix**: Simplify to "Notice how the endings change to match the subject."
