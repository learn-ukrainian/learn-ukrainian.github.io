## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Linguistic Accuracy (IPA)
- **Location**: Section "Cultural Hook: The First Book (1574)"
- **Original**: «друкував [drukuˈu̯ɑu̯]»
- **Problem**: The IPA transcription suggests a `w` sound between vowels (`u` and `a`). In standard Ukrainian, `v` between vowels is realized as `[ʋ]` (labiodental approximant), not `[u̯]`. `[u̯]` usually occurs at the end of a syllable/word or before a consonant.
- **Fix**: Change to «[drukuˈʋɑu̯]»

### Issue 2: Naturalness (Collocation/Calque)
- **Location**: Section "Контекст: Вчора куратора"
- **Original**: «Я **робила** екскурсію.»
- **Problem**: "Робити екскурсію" sounds like a calque from English "do a tour" or overly simplified "foreigner talk". The natural collocation is "проводити екскурсію".
- **Fix**: Change to «Я **проводила** екскурсію.» (or «Я показувала музей.» if "проводити" hasn't been introduced).

### Issue 3: Linguistic Accuracy (Case Usage)
- **Location**: Section "Time Marker: Минулий (Last)"
- **Original**: «Минулого року я був студент.»
- **Problem**: Using Nominative case ("студент") with past tense "бути" sounds unnatural for a profession/role, especially in the context of "last year". The Instrumental case ("студентом") is standard. While Nominative is sometimes accepted in colloquial speech or early A1, it risks fossilizing an error.
- **Fix**: Change example to «Минулого року я був **у школі**.» (avoids case issue) OR «Минулого року я **не працював**.»

### Issue 4: Linguistic Accuracy (Aspect)
- **Location**: Section "Практика: Спогади про вчора"
- **Original**: «Вчора я **прокидався** не дуже рано.»
- **Problem**: Using Imperfective "прокидався" for a single, completed action in a sequence ("Yesterday I woke up...") sounds unnatural, implying "I was in the process of waking up" or "I used to wake up". Native speakers would use Perfective "прокинувся" or simply "встав".
- **Fix**: Change to «Вчора я **встав** не дуже рано.» (Perfective "встати" fits the -в pattern nicely: вста-в).
