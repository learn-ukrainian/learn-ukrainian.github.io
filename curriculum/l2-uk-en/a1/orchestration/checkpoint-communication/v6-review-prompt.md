## Граматика (Grammar Summary)

Here are the key patterns from the Communication phase. Use these rules as a quick reference when building your own sentences.

### Кличний відмінок (Vocative Case)

The vocative case is used exclusively when addressing someone directly. It replaces the nominative case for names and titles, showing respect and engagement. The ending changes based on the last letter of the word.

- **Олена** → **Олено!** (Words ending in -а change to -о)
- **Тарас** → **Тарасе!** (Words ending in a hard consonant add -е)
- **Андрій** → **Андрію!** (Words ending in a soft consonant add -ю)
- **Мама** → **Мамо!** (Mom!)
- **Брат** → **Брате!** (Brother!)
- **Друг** → **Друже!** (Friend!)

### Наказовий спосіб (Imperative Mood)

When you need to give an instruction or make a request, use the imperative mood. Ukrainian distinguishes between informal (singular) and formal/plural requests. Always add **будь ласка** (please) to be polite.

- **ти** (informal singular): **читай** (read), **дай** (give), **скажи** (tell), **слухай** (listen)
- **ви** (formal or plural): **читайте** (read), **дайте** (give), **скажіть** (tell), **слухайте** (listen)

### Сполучники сурядності (Coordinating Conjunctions)

Conjunctions link your ideas together. Without them, communication is fragmented.

- **і** / **та** (and): used for simple addition. «Хліб і вода» (Bread and water).
- **а** (and/but): used for a soft contrast or comparison. «Я читаю, а ти пишеш» (I read, and you write).
- **але** (but): used for a strong contrast. «Я хочу, але не можу» (I want to, but I cannot).
- **бо** (because): used to provide a reason. «Я вдома, бо холодно» (I am home, because it is cold).

### Підрядні сполучники (Subordinating Conjunctions)

These conjunctions link a main thought to a dependent thought. In Ukrainian, these clauses are ALWAYS separated by a comma. If you write **що** (that), you must place a comma before it.

- **що** (that): «Я знаю, що це правда» (I know that this is true).
- **де** (where): «Скажи, де ти» (Tell [me] where you are).
- **коли** (when): «Я не знаю, коли він тут» (I do not know when he is here).

- **З Новим роком!** (Happy New Year!)
