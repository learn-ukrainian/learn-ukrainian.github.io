## Вступ: Час в Україні

### Чому час важливий
Time is vital for daily life in Ukraine. We value punctuality in business and travel. Imagine you are at the station. **Укрзалізниця** [ukrzaliznɪt͡sʲa] (Ukrainian Railways) is famous for precision. You must know the exact minute.

> [!culture]
> **The Iron People**
> Ukrainian trains run on time, even during difficult times. 90% punctuality is our standard. Being late for a meeting or a train is not polite!

_Приклади:_
*   **Потяг прибуває вчасно.** (The train arrives on time.)
*   **Ми поважаємо час.** (We respect time.)
*   **Це важливо сьогодні.** (This is important today.)

### Як запитати про час
You do not have a phone or a watch. You are on a street in Kyiv. You need to ask a stranger for the time. Be polite and use the phrase **«Котра година?»** [kotra ɦodɪna].

_Приклади:_
*   **Вибачте, котра година?** (Excuse me, what time is it?)
*   **Скажіть, будь ласка, котра година?** (Tell me please, what time is it?)
*   **Котра зараз година?** (What time is it now?)
