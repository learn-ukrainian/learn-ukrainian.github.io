## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Pedagogical Confusion (Labial L)
- **Location**: Section "Губний Л: Звук-помічник"
- **Original**: «The **Л** appears ONLY in the "I" form (**я**). ... **Вони роблять** ... (Wait! The **Л** reappears in "They" for some verbs...)»
- **Problem**: The text explicitly states a rule ("ONLY in I") then immediately contradicts it in parentheses. This confuses beginners. The rule for Type 2 labials is that *L* appears in 1st Sg (`я`) and 3rd Pl (`вони`).
- **Fix**: State the rule correctly and simply: "The **Л** appears in the forms for 'I' (**я**) and 'They' (**вони**)." Remove the confusing aside.

### Issue 2: LLM Fingerprint / Metalanguage
- **Location**: Various sections
- **Originals**:
  - «Now, let's look at the machinery under the hood.»
  - «This is a fancy grammatical term...»
  - «Now we meet a fascinating quirk of Slavic phonetics.»
- **Problem**: This meta-commentary distances the learner and sounds artificial (LLM-ish). "Machinery under the hood" is a cliché. "Quirk of Slavic phonetics" is too academic for A1.
- **Fix**: Simplify to direct instruction. "Let's look at the structure." "This means..." "Notice a special sound change."

### Issue 3: Missing Vocabulary
- **Location**: Plan vs Content
- **Problem**: The verb **просити** (to ask) is listed as `required` in the plan but appears nowhere in the text or vocabulary list.
- **Fix**: Add **просити** to the "Common verbs" list or practice section.
