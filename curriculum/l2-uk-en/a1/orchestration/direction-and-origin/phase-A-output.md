# Дослідження: Direction and Origin

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.3.2.2: "Із прийменниками: в, у, на (указівка на кінцевий пункт руху або переміщення): Степан заходить у кімнату. Я кладу книжку на твій стіл;"
Alignment: This module fulfills the A1 State Standard requirement for expressing destination using the Accusative case with prepositions в/у/на. It also introduces basic spatial uses of the Genitive case (до/з/від) to complete a functional spatial movement paradigm appropriate for beginner communication.

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Frequency / Source | Key collocations |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| іти / їхати | Core verbs (Top 100) | іти на роботу, їхати в магазин |
| до | High frequency prep. | до лікаря, додому, до Києва |
| з / від | High frequency prep. | з України, зі школи, від мами |

## Cultural Hooks
1. "Додому" (homeward) is a deeply resonant concept in Ukrainian culture, associated with safety, family hearth, and—especially in the current context—resilience and return during times of displacement. 
2. Traditional hospitality places a strong emphasis on direction and origin in greetings: welcoming guests "до хати" (into the home) or asking travelers "звідки шлях тримаєте?" (where is your path from?).

## Common Learner Errors
1. Mixing destination and location (e.g., *Я іду в школі* instead of *в школу*) — stemming from mental translation of English "I am going to school / I am at school."
2. Using "в" for visiting people (e.g., *в лікаря* instead of *до лікаря*) — a literal translation error.
3. Translating "to Ukraine" literally as *до України* instead of the correct spatial entry *в Україну*.

## Notes for Content Writing
- Decolonized Framing: Explicitly teach "в Україну" and "з України". Warn learners against the imperial Russian construct "на Україну" / "з на України", which frames Ukraine as a territory rather than a sovereign, independent state. Emphasize that Ukrainian uses independent spatial logic.
- Scaffolding Note: As an A1 module, English scaffolding is required to bridge the conceptual difference between entering a place (в + Accusative) versus visiting a person (до + Genitive).
