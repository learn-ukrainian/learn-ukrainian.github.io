## Cultural Insight

### Ukrainian Names and Identity

Ukrainians take names seriously. When introducing yourself, use your first name only among peers. In formal settings, you might hear a patronymic‚Äîa middle name from the father's name. For example:

- –ê–Ω–Ω–∞ –ü–µ—Ç—Ä—ñ–≤–Ω–∞ (Anna, daughter of Petro)
