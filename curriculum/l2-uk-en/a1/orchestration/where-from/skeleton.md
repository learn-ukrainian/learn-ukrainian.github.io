## Звідки? (Where From?) (~330 words)
- P1 (~60 words): Introduce the completion of the spatial location trio. Contrast "Де ти?" (В Україні — Locative, where you ARE), "Куди ти їдеш?" (В Україну — Accusative, where you're GOING), and "Звідки ти?" (З України — Genitive, where you're FROM).
- P2 (~80 words): Explain that "Звідки ти?" translates to "Where are you from?" but operates differently from English. It is a complete sentence (no verb "to be" is needed), and the interrogative word "Звідки" cannot be split. The answer is simply "Я з [place]".
- P3 (~100 words): Detail the pattern `з` + memorized Genitive chunks. Give examples of origins from cities (з Києва, зі Львова, з Одеси, з Харкова) and everyday places (з роботи, зі школи, з магазину, з банку). Note that at A1, learners should memorize these as fixed chunks.
- P4 (~90 words): Briefly explain the euphony rules for `з / із / зі`. Show that `з` is used before most sounds (з Канади, з України), `із` helps with pronunciation (із Торонто), and `зі` is used before difficult consonant clusters like 'с', 'ш', 'лв' (зі Львова, зі школи, зі США).

## Підсумок — Summary (~330 words)
- P1 (~100 words): Recap the three fundamental questions of spatial relations and their corresponding prepositions: Де? → в/на + locative (В Україні); Куди? → в/на + accusative (В Україну); Звідки? → з/із/зі + genitive chunk (З України).
- P2 (~80 words): Summarize the use of the `з/із/зі` prepositions for origin and remind learners that Ukrainian spatial questions are single words (Звідки) unlike English prepositional phrases (from where).
- P3 (~150 words): Self-check questions:
  * Where are you from? (Звідки ти?) — Think of your country and city using `з/із/зі`.
  * Where do you live now? (Де ти зараз живеш?) — Think of your current location using `в/на`.
  * Where are you going after this lesson? (Куди ти йдеш після цього уроку?) — Think of your destination using `в/на` or `додому`.
