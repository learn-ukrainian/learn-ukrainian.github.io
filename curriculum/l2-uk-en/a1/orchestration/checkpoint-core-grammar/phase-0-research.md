## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Third Melodious**: In 1934, at a language contest in Paris, Ukrainian was ranked the third most melodious language in the world (after French and Persian), based on phonetics and rhythm. (Source: Ukrainian Language Institute / General Cultural Lore).
2. **Case Richness**: Ukrainian is the only Slavic language of its neighbors (Russian/Belarusian) to have fully preserved the ancient **Vocative case** (кличний відмінок), used for direct addresses like *Мамо!*, *Друже!*, or *Україно!*. This adds a unique emotional layer to communication.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **"To be" Overuse**: English speakers often try to use the word "є" (is) in every sentence. → *Я студент* (Correct) instead of *Я є студент* (Technically correct but often redundant in A1).
2. **Gender Mismatch**: Forgetting that adjectives must match the noun's gender. → *Гарна книга* (Correct) vs *Гарний книга* (Error: Masculine adjective with Feminine noun).
3. **Case Confusion (In vs. To)**: Mixing Locative and Accusative with prepositions 'в'/'на'. → *Я йду в магазин* (Accusative: Direction) vs *Я в магазині* (Locative: Location).
