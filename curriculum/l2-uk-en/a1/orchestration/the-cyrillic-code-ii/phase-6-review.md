## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Extreme Verbosity (Beginner Safety)
- **Location**: Entire Module
- **Original**: 5506 words
- **Problem**: The target is ~1500 words. At 5500 words, this is a book chapter, not a lesson. A beginner cannot process this amount of English explanation for 14 letters.
- **Fix**: Ruthlessly cut English prose. Reduce metaphors. Shorten "Imagine..." sections. Aim for <2500 words.

### Issue 2: Unnatural Phrasing (Calque)
- **Location**: Section "Soft Sign (Ь)", Example 2
- **Original**: «Тут є гарний день.»
- **Problem**: "There is a beautiful day" translated literally. "Тут є" implies existence/location of an object. To say "It's a beautiful day here", use "Сьогодні гарний день" or "Тут гарно".
- **Fix**: «Сьогодні гарний день.»

### Issue 3: Strange Syntax
- **Location**: Section "Warm-up", Example 1
- **Original**: «Там тут.»
- **Problem**: "There here." It lacks a conjunction or punctuation to make sense as a list or contrast.
- **Fix**: «Там і тут.» (There and here) or «Там. Тут.»

### Issue 4: LLM Pattern - Metaphor Overload
- **Location**: Throughout
- **Original**: "special forces", "masquerade", "building a house", "dance the hopak", "soul of pronunciation", "phoenix letter", "hammer a nail", "flying beetle", "level boss".
- **Problem**: While engaging, the density is too high (LLM characteristic). It distracts from the core phonetics.
- **Fix**: Remove 50% of metaphors. Keep the visual ones (beetle for Ж, chair for Ч), remove the abstract ones (special forces, soul, level boss).
