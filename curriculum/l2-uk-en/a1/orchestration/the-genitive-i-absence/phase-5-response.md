## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Pedagogical Inconsistency (The "Знання" Trap)
- **Location**: Example Sentence 11 / Activities
- **Original**: "У них немає знання."
- **Problem**: The module teaches the rule "Neuter: -о → -а" (вікно → вікна). The word "знання" ends in -я. Its genitive is "знання" (ends in -я). This violates the visual pattern taught to the beginner ("change ending to -a"). Furthermore, "знання" is often used in plural ("знань") for "knowledge", making the singular form slightly unnatural in this generic context.
- **Fix**: Replace "знання" with a regular neuter noun ending in -о, such as "молоко" (milk) -> "молока", "вино" (wine) -> "вина", or "пиво" (beer) -> "пива".

### Issue 2: Activity Categorization Error
- **Location**: Activities file / group-sort "Genitive Endings"
- **Original**: Group "Neuter -а" includes "знання" and "значення".
- **Problem**: "Знання" and "значення" do NOT end in -а in Genitive (they end in -я). Asking a student to sort them into a "-а" group is technically incorrect and confusing.
- **Fix**: Remove these items. Replace with "вікна" (already there), "права" (from право), "слова" (from слово), "молока" (from молоко).
