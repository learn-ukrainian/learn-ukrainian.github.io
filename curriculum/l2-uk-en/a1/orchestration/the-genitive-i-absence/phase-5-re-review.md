## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Linguistic Accuracy (Genitive Ending)
- **Location**: Line 65 / Section "Genitive Endings for Absence"
- **Original**: `| **Masculine** | стіл       | столу    | /ˈstɔlu/  | немає столу |`
- **Problem**: The genitive singular of **стіл** (table, concrete furniture) is **стола**. The ending **-у** is used for abstract nouns (час -> часу), substances (цукор -> цукру), or specific spatial concepts (дому). Teaching `столу` for a generic table is incorrect standard Ukrainian.
- **Fix**: Replace the example with a masculine noun that correctly takes **-у**, such as **цукор** (sugar) or **чай** (tea), which fits the "absence" theme perfectly.
    - Replacement: `| **Masculine** | цукор      | цукру    | /ˈt͡sukru/ | немає цукру |`

### Issue 2: Activity Reinforcing Error
- **Location**: Activities file, Item `type: quiz`, `title: Understanding Genitive`, Question 5
- **Original**: `question: What is the genitive form of «стіл»? ... correct: true` for `text: столу`
- **Problem**: Reinforces the incorrect grammar point mentioned above.
- **Fix**: Change the question to ask about "цукор" (if changed in text) or change the correct answer to "стола" (and update text). Recommendation: Switch to "цукор" to illustrate the -u ending consistently.

### Issue 3: Genitive Ending Nuance
- **Location**: Line 103 / Section "Example Sentences"
- **Original**: `Тут немає телефону.`
- **Problem**: While `телефону` is acceptable for "phone service/number", a physical phone device usually takes **-а** (`телефона`). For A1 learners, it is safer to stick to clear categories (Device = -a).
- **Fix**: Change to **Інтернету** (Internet), which unambiguously takes **-у** (`немає Інтернету`) and is a very high-frequency phrase for A1 travelers.
