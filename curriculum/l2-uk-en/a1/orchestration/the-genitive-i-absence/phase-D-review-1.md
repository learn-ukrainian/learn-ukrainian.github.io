## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Misplaced Grammar Example
- **Location**: Section "Genitive Endings: Masculine Hard Nouns (-а)"
- **Original**: «**Сон** (dream/sleep) → немає **сну** (The 'о' disappears).»
- **Problem**: This item is listed under the header for **-а** endings, but the example explicitly shows the ending **-у** (*сну*). This contradicts the section header and confuses the learner.
- **Fix**: Move this example to the "Masculine Abstract/Substance Nouns (-у)" section.

### Issue 2: Misleading Rule about Loanwords
- **Location**: Warning Box "[!warning] Увага: Типова помилка (-а vs. -у)"
- **Original**: «Is it a substance (sugar, tea), concept (time), or foreign loanword (телефон)? Use **-у**.»
- **Problem**: The rule implies foreign loanwords generally take **-у**. However, "паспорт" (used extensively in this lesson) is a foreign loanword but takes **-а**. "Автобус", "комп'ютер", "студент" are also loanwords taking **-а**. The rule as stated creates a contradiction with the lesson's own examples.
- **Fix**: Refine the rule to focus on technology/concepts rather than "foreign loanwords" broadly.
