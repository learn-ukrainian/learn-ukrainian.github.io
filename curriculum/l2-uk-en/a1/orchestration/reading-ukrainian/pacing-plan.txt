Section 1 "Склади (Syllables)": ~270 words — Teach the golden syllable rule (vowels = syllables), the 4-step method for reading new words, and the звуковий аналіз approach from Большакова.
Section 2 "Голосні літери (Vowel Letters)": ~320 words — All 10 vowel letters mapped to 6 sounds, simple vs iotated vowels, Ї as uniquely Ukrainian, И/І minimal pairs.
Section 3 "Читання слів (Reading Words)": ~310 words — Syllable-by-syllable reading strategy, common CVCV/CVCCV/CVC patterns, preview of Щ, Ь, and apostrophe.
Section 4 "Читаємо разом (Reading Together)": ~200 words — Progressive word reading from 2 to 4+ syllables, then a short Це-based reading passage.
Section 5 "Підсумок — Summary": ~150 words — Self-check questions covering all key concepts.
Total: ~1250 words
