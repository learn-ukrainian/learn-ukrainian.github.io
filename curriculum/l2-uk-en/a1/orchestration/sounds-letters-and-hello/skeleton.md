## Звуки і літери (Sounds and Letters) (~280 words total)
- P1 (~90 words): Introduce the Golden Rule from textbook Grade 1/5: "Ми чуємо і вимовляємо звуки, а бачимо і пишемо літери." Explain the numbers: 33 letters (літери), 38 sounds (звуки). Clarify the difference between the physical symbols on paper and the spoken sounds we hear.
- P2 (~100 words): Introduce the first family: Голосні (vowels). Explain that they are made with voice only, mouth open, with no obstruction. Quote the poem from Большакова: "Голосні почуєш в пісні." List the 6 vowel sounds: [а], [о], [у], [е], [и], [і] and the 10 vowel letters used to write them: А, О, У, Е, И, І, Я, Ю, Є, Ї.
- P3 (~90 words): Introduce the second family: Приголосні (consonants). Explain that they are made with voice plus noise or noise only, using lips, teeth, and tongue to create an obstruction. Mention there are 32 consonant sounds. Quote Большакова: "Приголосні деренчать, і тихенько шелестять."
- Exercise: quiz, focus: 'Distinguish between sounds (звуки) and letters (літери). Example questions: "Що ми чуємо і вимовляємо?" → "звуки" | "Що ми бачимо і пишемо?" → "літери" | "Скільки літер в абетці?" → "33" | "Скільки звуків в українській мові?" → "38".', 6 items

## Перші слова (First Words) (~340 words total)
- P1 (~90 words): Introduce Cyrillic letters that look AND sound familiar to English speakers: А, О, К, М, Т. Show how to read them immediately using words: мама, тато, кома, атом, мак, око. Note that Ukrainian Т is dental (tongue touches teeth) and К/Т are unaspirated (no puff of air).
- P2 (~100 words): Highlight the "False Friend" letters—the biggest trap: В sounds [в] (not 'b'), Н sounds [н] (not 'h'), Р sounds [р] (rolled, not 'p'), С sounds [с] (not 'c/k'), У sounds [у] (not 'y'), Х sounds [х] (not 'x'). Provide practice words: вода, рука, сон, ніс, хата.
- Exercise: match-up, focus: 'Match false friend Cyrillic letters to their REAL sounds. Pairs: В ↔ [в] (not 'b'), Н ↔ [н] (not 'h'), Р ↔ [р] (not 'p'), С ↔ [с] (not 'c/k'), У ↔ [у] (not 'y'), Х ↔ [х] (not 'x').', 6 items
- P3 (~100 words): Introduce new shapes with no Latin equivalent: Б, Г, Ґ, Д, Ж, З, И, Й, Л, П, Ф, Ц, Ч, Ш, Щ. Teach through words: банк, дім, зима, книга, школа. Explain that Щ always makes TWO sounds [шч] and Ь has NO sound (it just softens the preceding consonant).
- P4 (~50 words): Introduce special letters: Ї always equals [йі] and never softens (unique to Ukrainian). Briefly mention that Я, Ю, Є can represent two sounds or soften a consonant, to be fully explored in M02.
- Exercise: group-sort, focus: 'Sort Cyrillic letters into Голосні (vowels) and Приголосні (consonants). Голосні: А, О, У, Е, И, І. Приголосні: К, М, Т, В, Н, Р, С, Х.', 8 items

## Привіт! (Hello!) (~280 words total)
- P1 (~100 words): Introduce your first conversation (following Anna Ohoiko ULP Episode 1). Teach the informal greeting: "Привіт!" (Hi!). Teach "Як справи?" (How are you?) and standard responses: Добре (good), Чудово (great), Нормально (okay). Introduce the return question: "А у тебе?" (And you?).
- P2 (~100 words): Teach the phrase "Glad to see you!" noting the gendered forms: "Рада тебе бачити!" (female speaker) and "Радий тебе бачити!" (male speaker). Explain that this is the first encounter with gender in Ukrainian, an important concept that will become a major topic starting in M08.
- P3 (~80 words): Break down the word "Привіт!" as a reading practice exercise. Sound out each letter and identify its group: П (new), р (false friend), и (new vowel), в (false friend), і (vowel), т (familiar). Blend them into syllables: При-віт!
- Exercise: fill-in, focus: 'Complete a basic greeting dialogue with blanks. EXACT pattern: "— {Привіт}! Як {справи}?" / "— {Добре}. А у {тебе}?" / "— {Чудово}." Options per blank: Привіт / справи / Добре / тебе / Чудово / Нормально.', 4 items

## Читаємо (Reading Practice) (~280 words total)
- P1 (~100 words): Introduce environmental reading using common signs seen in Ukraine. Words to read: Аптека (pharmacy), Банк (bank), Кафе (cafe), Метро (metro), Пошта (post office), Школа (school), Зупинка (bus stop). Guide the learner to sound out each letter and blend syllables.
- P2 (~90 words): Practice reading major Ukrainian city names: Київ, Львів, Одеса, Харків, Дніпро, Полтава. Encourage learners to recognize both familiar letters and new shapes while sounding out the locations.
- P3 (~90 words): Form your first actual sentences using "Це" (this is). Present sentences: Це мама. Це банк. Це Київ. Introduce the two basic question words for objects and people: Що це? (What is this?) and Хто це? (Who is this?).

## Підсумок — Summary (~160 words total)
- P1 (~160 words): Recap the fundamental difference between sounds and letters (33 літери, 38 звуків). Review the two sound families: голосні (vowels) and приголосні (consonants). Remind learners of the false friend letters (В, Н, Р, С, У, Х) to watch out for. Summarize the conversational phrases: "Привіт!", "Як справи?", and how to answer with "Добре" or "Чудово." Encourage them to keep practicing reading street signs and city names to build their Cyrillic decoding speed before moving on to the next module.
