## Підсумок (Summary)

You have taken your first step into the Ukrainian language by mastering its phonetic foundation. Test your memory with a few self-check questions before you move forward. How many letters are in the Ukrainian alphabet? There are exactly thirty-three letters. How many sounds exist in the language? There are thirty-eight sounds. Why are they different? Because some letters represent two sounds, and the soft sign represents no sound at all.

What are **голосні** (vowel sounds)? They are sounds made purely with your voice, with no physical obstruction in the mouth. What are **приголосні** (consonant sounds)? They are sounds made with noise or a combination of noise and voice, utilizing your lips, teeth, or tongue. Can you say the phrase «голосна літера»? No, because sounds are vowels or consonants, not letters. Letters only represent the sounds we make.

What does the word **привіт** mean? It means an informal hello used among friends and peers. How do you answer the question **як справи**? You can simply say **добре**, **нормально**, or **чудово**. Your phonetic journey has officially begun.
