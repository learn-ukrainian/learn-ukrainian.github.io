## Grammar Rules
- Phonetics Basics: Правопис § 1-25 (General Phonetics) — Ukrainian phonetics distinguishes between sounds (38) and letters (33). Vowels (голосні) are produced by voice alone (6 sounds: [а], [о], [у], [е], [и], [і]), while consonants (приголосні) involve noise or a combination of voice and noise (32 sounds).
- Gender Agreement: Standard rule for predicative adjectives — "Радий тебе бачити!" (Masculine) vs "Рада тебе бачити!" (Feminine) follows the rule that adjectives and some pronouns agree with the subject in gender, number, and case.
- Letter Γ vs Ґ: Правопис § 5-6 — "Г" represents the voiced glottal fricative [ɦ], while "Ґ" represents the voiced velar plosive [g].
