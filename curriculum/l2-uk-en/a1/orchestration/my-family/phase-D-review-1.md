## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Unfair Assessment (Activities)
- **Location**: Activities file, `type: match-up` (title: "Кличний відмінок") and `type: fill-in` (title: "Звертання до рідних").
- **Original**: Items include pairs like `Брат/Брате`, `Сестра/Сестро`, `Андрій/Андрію` and fill-ins for `Олено`.
- **Problem**: The content module ONLY teaches 4 forms: `Мамо`, `Тату`, `Бабусю`, `Дідусю`. It explicitly says "You don't need to learn the whole grammar table yet, but you MUST know these four forms". The learner has zero way to know that `Андрій` becomes `Андрію` (soft declension) or `Сестра` becomes `Сестро`. Testing untaught material is a pedagogy failure.
- **Fix**: Remove the untaught items from the activity. Add `Син/Сину` (which appears in the dialogue).

### Issue 2: Plan Deviation (Age)
- **Location**: Section "Вік (Age)"
- **Original**: "Talking about exact age in Ukrainian requires a specific grammar case... We will master this in A2."
- **Problem**: The meta plan explicitly asked for "Basic age construction reinforcement: simple number phrases (25 років)". While avoiding Dative is safe, completely skipping the plan requirement is a deviation.
- **Fix**: Add a simple fixed-phrase introduction to Age without deep grammar explanation.
