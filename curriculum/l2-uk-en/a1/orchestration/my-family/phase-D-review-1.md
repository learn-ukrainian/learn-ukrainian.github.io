### Issue 1: Russianism «красива» (CRITICAL — Auto-Fail Item)

**Location:** Lines 241, 251, 252, 464, 476 in content; line 476 in useful phrases
**Type:** RUSSIANISM
**Severity:** Critical

### Issue 2: Unjumble Activity Punctuation Errors (MAJOR)

**Location:** Activities YAML, activity 7 ("Запитання та відповіді"), lines 148, 154, 158
**Type:** ACTIVITY_ERROR
**Severity:** Major

Three unjumble answers have incorrect punctuation that teaches wrong Ukrainian writing norms:

**Fix:** Add comma/question-mark tokens to the word arrays, or add them to the answer strings if the activity engine handles punctuation separately.

**Location:** Intro block (line 13), plan section 1
**Type:** PLAN_DEVIATION
**Severity:** Moderate

**Fix:** Add a brief callout or paragraph in section «Розминка: Члени сім'ї» explaining: «Сім'я» — the people who live together (parents + children), «Родина» — your wider family and ancestors.
