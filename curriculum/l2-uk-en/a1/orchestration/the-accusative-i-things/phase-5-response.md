### Issue 1: Broken S.T.A.L.K.E.R. Quote
- **Location**: Line 46-48 / Section "Common Verbs That Take Accusative"
- **Original**: "The iconic Ukrainian game S.T.A.L.K.E.R. has a famous phrase: «Іди своєю дорогою, сталкер». But notice **аномалію** — that is the accusative case..."
- **Problem**: The quoted phrase «Іди своєю дорогою, сталкер» does NOT contain the word **аномалію**. The explanation claims "notice аномалію", which confuses the learner who looks for it in the quote and fails.
- **Fix**: Replace the quote with one that actually uses the accusative, or change the explanation to be a general reference.

### Issue 2: Unnatural Calque in Dialogue
- **Location**: Line 100 / Section "Mini-Dialogue 3"
- **Original**: "Хочеш бачити?"
- **Problem**: Calque from English "Do you want to see?". In Ukrainian, `бачити` is physical ability/perception. "Do you want to look?" should be `Хочеш подивитися?` or `Хочеш глянути?`. For A1, simple `Хочеш?` (Do you want [it]?) is safer.
- **Fix**: Change to "Хочеш?" or "Хочеш подивитися?".
