## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Broken Activity Design (Multiple Correct Answers)
- **Location**: Activity 2 ("Complete with Numbers")
- **Original**: Sentence: "У мене ___ гривень." Options: десять, п'ять, двадцять, сто.
- **Problem**: All options are grammatically correct (require Genitive Plural) and semantically plausible. A student can choose "п'ять" and be marked wrong because the arbitrary answer is "десять". This happens in items 1, 2, 5, 6, 8, 9, 11, 12.
- **Fix**: Redesign the activity. Either use unique grammatical cases to force a specific number (e.g. mix 1, 2, 5), or provide context (e.g. "image shows 10 UAH"), or change to a Match-up/Quiz format where context is provided.

### Issue 2: Grammar Error in Answer Key
- **Location**: Activity 2, Item 3
- **Original**: "Вона має ___ років." Answer: "двадцять три".
- **Problem**: "років" is Genitive Plural. "23" (двадцять три) requires Nominative Plural ("роки"). The sentence "Вона має двадцять три років" is grammatically incorrect.
- **Fix**: Change sentence to "Вона має ___ роки." OR change answer to a number ending in 5-9/0 (e.g. "двадцять").

### Issue 3: Unnatural Age Construction (Calque)
- **Location**: Content, Tens (20-100), Example 2; Compound Numbers, Example 7.
- **Original**: "Вона має тридцять років." / "Я маю двадцять три роки."
- **Problem**: This is a direct calque from English "She has 30 years". The natural Ukrainian construction is Dative + number + years ("Їй тридцять років", "Мені двадцять три роки"). While "мати" is understandable, teaching it as the primary model promotes unnatural speech patterns ("Polonism/Anglicism").
- **Fix**: Use the natural Dative construction "Мені X років", "Їй Y років". If Dative hasn't been taught, teach this as a set phrase chunk or stick to Nominative constructions that avoid the person (e.g. "Мій вік - 20 років").

### Issue 4: Scope Creep (Instrumental Case)
- **Location**: Activity 12 (Quiz: Currency and Numbers), Q8 Explanation
- **Original**: "Explanation: Карткою is instrumental case."
- **Problem**: The module text claims to use Genitive (Module 16) but introduces Instrumental forms "карткою/готівкою" as vocab. The quiz explanation then references "instrumental case" which confuses a student who hasn't learned it.
- **Fix**: Remove the grammatical explanation "is instrumental case" from the quiz. Just say "Set phrase for 'by card'".
