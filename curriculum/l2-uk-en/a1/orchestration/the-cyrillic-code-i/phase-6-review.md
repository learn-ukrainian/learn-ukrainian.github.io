## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Untaught Material (The "Ghost Letter" Y)
- **Location**: Section "Презентація: Літери-друзі та літери-пастки" (Missing) vs Activities 2, 3, 4, 8 (Present)
- **Original**: (Content is missing the letter explanation entirely)
- **Problem**: The Activities explicitly test the letter «У» (False Friend "Y" -> [u]), but this letter is **never introduced** in the text. It is missing from the "False Friends" list which only covers В, Н, Р, С, Х.
- **Fix**: Add «У» to "Group 2: False Friends".
  *Draft*:
  #### The "Y" Trap: У
  - **Symbol**: **У у**
  - **Sound**: [u] (like "m**oo**n" or "b**oo**t").
  - **The Trap**: It looks like a "Y", but it makes the "oo" sound.
  - **Mnemonic**: Think of "Y" as a slingshot shooting the moon (**oo**).
  - **Example**: **суп** (soup) — See? It’s S-OO-P.

### Issue 2: Confusing Mnemonic
- **Location**: Section "The 'H' Trap: Н"
- **Original**: «Or, visualize the connecting bar in the middle of the "N" flipping to become horizontal.»
- **Problem**: This visualization is geometrically confusing for a beginner trying to map H to N. The "Hen" mnemonic is sufficient and better. The flipping bar overcomplicates it.
- **Fix**: Remove the second sentence. Keep it simple: «It looks like an "H", but think of a **H**en. The letter H stands for **H**en, which ends in N.»

### Issue 3: Inconsistent Activity Grouping
- **Location**: Activity 9 "Нові знайомі" (New Acquaintances)
- **Original**: Options include **М** and **К**.
- **Problem**: The title says "New Acquaintances" (referring to Group 3: New Letters), but the quiz includes **М** and **К**, which were explicitly taught as "True Friends" (Group 1). This blurs the categorization logic established in the lesson.
- **Fix**: Rename the activity to "Letter Check" or remove the True Friends from this specific "New Letters" drill.
