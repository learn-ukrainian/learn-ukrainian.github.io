## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Missing Plan Content (Cultural)
- **Location**: Section "Cultural Context: Чому українська абетка особлива"
- **Original**: Mentions `І` and `Ї`.
- **Problem**: The plan explicitly requested coverage of "Unique Ukrainian letters (І, Ї, Є, Ґ)". The current text omits `Є` and `Ґ`. While `Ї` and `І` are the most iconic, strictly following the plan is required.
- **Fix**: Add a brief sentence about `Є` (Ye) and `Ґ` (Ghe) to the "Unique letters" paragraph.

### Issue 2: Phonetic Trap (Soft L)
- **Location**: Line 223 / Section "Вправа 3"
- **Original**: «**Лак** (varnish) — **Ліс** (forest)»
- **Problem**: The student was taught that `Л` = `[l]` (hard). In `Ліс`, `Л` becomes soft `[lʲ]` due to `і`. This phonetic change hasn't been explained yet and contradicts the "True Friend" simplicity established earlier.
- **Fix**: Replace `Ліс` with `Лом` (crowbar), `Лот` (lot), or `Лото` (lotto) where `Л` remains hard. Or use `Лампа` vs `Лапа` (paw). Ideally, keep the `a` vs `i` contrast but use a consonant that doesn't soften as drastically, or accept the nuance. Better replacement: `Лак` vs `Лом` (checking vowel diff). Or `Там` vs `Тім` (Tim).

### Issue 3: Unknown Vocabulary & Grammar
- **Location**: Activities / Fill-in / Item 6
- **Original**: «Це ________ міста.» (Answer: план)
- **Problem**: The word `міста` is the Genitive case of `місто` (city). Neither `місто` nor the Genitive case has been introduced. A beginner cannot predict or understand this form.
- **Fix**: Change the sentence to «Це ________ метро.» (This is the metro plan) or just «Це мій ________.» (This is my plan).
