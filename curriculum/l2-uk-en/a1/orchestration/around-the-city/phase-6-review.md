## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Calque from English (Time/Distance)
- **Location**: Line 229 / Section "Landmarks Over Distance"
- **Original**: «Це 5 хвилин.»
- **Problem**: Direct translation of "It is 5 minutes". In Ukrainian, distance expressed in time usually uses the verb "to go" or "it takes".
- **Fix**: «Йти 5 хвилин.» (To walk 5 minutes) or «Це за 5 хвилин.» (It is 5 minutes away).

### Issue 2: Unnatural Existence Verb
- **Location**: Line 189 / Section "Опишіть ваш маршрут"
- **Original**: «Там є школа.»
- **Problem**: Calque of "There is a school". In Ukrainian, the copula "є" is often dropped for location/existence unless emphasizing availability. "Там школа" is more natural for pointing out a landmark.
- **Fix**: «Там школа.» or «Там знаходиться школа.»

### Issue 3: Pronoun Overuse
- **Location**: Line 189 / Section "Опишіть ваш маршрут"
- **Original**: «Потім я повертаю наліво.»
- **Problem**: Unnecessary repetition of the subject "я" after "Я йду прямо". Ukrainian often drops the pronoun in a sequence of actions by the same subject.
- **Fix**: «Потім повертаю наліво.»
