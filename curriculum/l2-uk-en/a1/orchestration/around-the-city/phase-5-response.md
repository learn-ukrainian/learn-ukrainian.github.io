## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Grammar Scope Creep (Instrumental Case)
- **Location**: Scenario 2 and Scenario 3
- **Original**: "Там, прямо за тим будинком." (Scenario 2) / "Велосипедом дуже зручно." (Scenario 3)
- **Problem**: This uses the Instrumental case (`за будинком`, `велосипедом`). Module 15 is A1; Instrumental is typically A2. M13 taught Locative (`на велосипеді`), which is used elsewhere. Mixing both confuses the learner who hasn't learned Instrumental endings.
- **Fix**: Change to Locative or Genitive constructs known to A1.
    - "за тим будинком" -> "біля того будинку" (Genitive - usually introduced earlier/implicit) or "біля магазину".
    - "Велосипедом" -> "На велосипеді".

### Issue 2: Incorrect Grammar Explanation
- **Location**: Section "Pro Tip: Preposition Power"
- **Original**: "These combine with locative or accusative cases depending on motion vs. location."
- **Problem**: The listed prepositions (`До`, `Від`, `Вздовж`) govern the **Genitive** case, NOT Locative or Accusative. `Через` governs Accusative. Grouping them all under "Locative or Accusative" is linguistically false and misleading.
- **Fix**: Update explanation to correctly identify cases or remove the specific case claim if trying to simplify (just say "These are essential prepositions").

### Issue 3: Vocabulary Inconsistency (Text vs Activities)
- **Location**: Text vs Activities (Quiz 1)
- **Original**: Text teaches **"наліво / направо"**. Activity items use **"ліворуч / праворуч"**.
- **Problem**: The student is tested on words (`ліворуч`) that were not explicitly presented in the text. While synonyms, A1 students need consistency. `Ліворуч/Праворуч` is stylistically better in Ukrainian, but the text must match the quiz.
- **Fix**: Standardize to **"ліворуч / праворуч"** throughout the text (Presentation & Dialogues), as they are more standard/elegant, and update the table.

### Issue 4: Ambiguous Activity Distractors
- **Location**: Activities (Fill-in: Transportation Phrases)
- **Original**: Question: "Вони приїхали ___." Options: `на машині` (correct), `машиною`.
- **Problem**: `Машиною` (Instrumental) is also grammatically correct in Ukrainian. Since the student shouldn't know Instrumental yet, seeing it as a distractor is confusing, or worse, if they know it from outside sources, they will be penalized for a correct answer.
- **Fix**: Remove valid alternative cases from distractors. Change `машиною` to `машина` (Nominative) or `у машині`.
