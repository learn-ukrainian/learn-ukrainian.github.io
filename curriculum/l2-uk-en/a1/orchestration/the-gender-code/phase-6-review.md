### Issue 1: Structural Coherence
- **Location**: Line 229 / Section "Сім'я 2: Приголосний та -О/-Е"
- **Original**: «The "Dog" Distinction: Consider the word собака... actually, собака belongs to Family 1 grammatically»
- **Problem**: The discussion of `собака` (Family 1 exception) is placed under the header for **Family 2**. This contradicts the header and confuses the structural logic.
- **Fix**: Move the "The 'Dog' Distinction" block to be a subsection of **Family 1** (as an exception) or a standalone "Traps" subsection.

### Issue 2: Linguistic Precision / Pedagogy
- **Location**: Line 278 / Section "Культурний Контекст"
- **Original**: «**Укриття** ... Ends in 'я' (Double consonant + ya). This is often a sign of a special **Neuter** group.»
- **Problem**: Phrasing suggests `укриття` belongs to a "special" group (often implying Family 4/4th Declension like *ім'я/теля* which also end in -я). In reality, `укриття` is a standard 2nd Declension soft group neuter. The "special" label is vague and potentially misleading for future grammar.
- **Fix**: Clarify that double consonant + -я is a **soft neuter** pattern (Family 2), distinct from the "Baby Neuter" (Family 4).

### Issue 3: Missing IPA
- **Location**: Lines 276-277 / Section "Культурний Контекст"
- **Original**: «**Артефакт** (Artifact)... **Зона** (The Zone)...»
- **Problem**: New vocabulary introduced without IPA transcription.
- **Fix**: Add IPA: `**Артефакт**` and `**Зона**`.
