## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Russianism & Imperative Violation
**Location**: Практика та підсумок (Practice and Summary)
**Problem**: The dialogue line `— Давай **планувати** вихідні!` uses "Давай" + infinitive, which is a calque from Russian ("давай планировать"). Furthermore, it violates the A1 morphology scope since the imperative form "давай" is not taught until M47 (as caught by the pre-screen).
**Fix**: Change the phrase to a natural, A1-appropriate alternative such as `— Час планувати вихідні!` (Time to plan the weekend!).

### Issue 2: English Calque (Anglicism)
**Location**: Лексика та культурний контекст (Vocabulary and Culture)
**Problem**: The dialogue line `— Ти маєш **плани** на завтра?` is an English calque ("You have plans"). While grammatically permissible, it is highly unnatural. Native speakers overwhelmingly use "У тебе є...".
**Fix**: Change the sentence to `— У тебе є плани на завтра?`

### Issue 3: Out-of-Scope Grammar in Proverb
**Location**: Лексика та культурний контекст (Vocabulary and Culture)
**Problem**: The `[!culture]` box introduces the proverb `«Не кажи "гоп", поки не перескочиш»`. This exposes A1 learners to the imperative (`кажи`) and perfective future (`перескочиш`), both of which are way out of scope and explicitly flagged by the pre-screen as violating beginner scaffolding.
**Fix**: Remove the proverb to maintain beginner safety. Keep the excellent cultural explanation about Ukrainians being cautious with plans.

### Issue 4: LLM Hallucinated Cultural Claim
**Location**: Вступ (Introduction)
**Problem**: The text claims: `There's a phrase Ukrainians use that captures the spirit of looking forward: **«Завтра буде новий день.»**` This is a classic LLM hallucination—taking a global/English idiom ("Tomorrow is a new day") and framing it as a profound, culturally unique Ukrainian saying.
**Fix**: Remove the exaggerated "captures the spirit" claim and present the sentence simply as a good, encouraging example of the future tense.

### Issue 5: Unnatural Phrasing in Dialogue
**Location**: Лексика та культурний контекст (Vocabulary and Culture)
**Problem**: The question `— О, добре! А **скоро** їдеш?` (Oh, nice! Going soon?) sounds clunky and translated.
**Fix**: A native speaker would naturally ask `— О, добре! **Коли** їдеш?` (When are you going?).
