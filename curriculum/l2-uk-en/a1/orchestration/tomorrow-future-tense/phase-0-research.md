# Дослідження: Tomorrow - Future Tense

## State Standard Reference
§4.2.4.1: "дієвідмінювання дієслова бути в майбутньому часі: буду, будеш, буде, будемо, будете, будуть... утворення складеної форми майбутнього часу дієслів недоконаного виду: буду читати, будеш сидіти, буде сміятися, будемо гуляти, будете працювати, будуть відпочивати; утворення форми майбутнього часу деяких дієслів доконаного виду: зможу, скажете, прочитають."
Alignment: The module focuses on the compound future tense (буду + infinitive) and the conjugation of "бути", directly aligning with the A1 morphology requirements. It also incorporates "завтра" as a core temporal concept from Catalogue A (Communicative intentions).

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Приказка: "Не кажи «гоп», поки не перескочиш."** (Don't say "hop" until you've jumped over). This is the Ukrainian equivalent of "Don't count your chickens before they hatch," reflecting a cultural attitude of caution towards future plans and premature celebration. (Source: speakua.com)
2. ** Resilience Idiom: "Завтра буде новий день."** (Tomorrow will be a new day). A common phrase used to encourage someone facing difficulties, emphasizing optimism and the hope that the future brings fresh opportunities. (Source: quora.com/cultural idioms)

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Aspect Clash:** Attempting to form the compound future with perfective verbs (e.g., *буду прочитати instead of прочитаю or буду читати). Learners must be taught that `буду` only pairs with infinitives of imperfective verbs.
2. **Conjugation Drift:** Mixing up the endings of the auxiliary verb "бути" (e.g., *ви будемо instead of ви будете), especially between the 1st person plural and 2nd person plural.
3. **Literal "Will" Translation:** Using "бути" for all future actions as a direct translation of English "will", neglecting the simple future (perfective) which is required for completed results (though simple future is only introduced for "select verbs" at A1).

## Notes for Content Writing
- Focus heavily on the **буду + інфінітив** structure as it is the most regular and productive for A1.
- Limit the mention of the synthetic future (читатиму) to a brief "FYI" to avoid overwhelming the learner, as per the plan's instruction.
- Use **наступного тижня** and **наступного року** to practice the change of endings in temporal expressions (genitive/locative usage, though case rules shouldn't be over-explained here).
- Ensure high immersion (35-40%) since this is M22. Transliteration should only be used for first occurrences.
