## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Test Before Teach (Genitive Masculine)
- **Location**: Section "Одиниці виміру" vs Activity 9
- **Problem**: The text explicitly teaches patterns for Feminine (`-а` → `-и`), Neuter (`-о` → `-а`), and Plural. It completely omits Masculine Singular Genitive. However, Activity 9 asks learners to fill in: `сік` → `соку`, `чай` → `чаю`, `сир` → `сиру`, `хліб` → `хліба`. A learner has zero information to solve this.
- **Fix**: Add a "Masculine (Consonant)" rule or a list of set expressions to the "Pattern for Beginners" block in the text. Also add `Шматок` (Piece) to vocabulary as it appears in activities but not the text.
