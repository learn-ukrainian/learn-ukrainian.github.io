## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: LLM Fingerprint (Repetitive Phrasing)
- **Location**: Multiple Sections
- **Original**: 5+ instances of "Let's..." (Let's explore, Let's learn, Let's see) and 5 instances of "Imagine...".
- **Problem**: Creates a robotic, formulaic cadence. Real tutors vary their transitions.
- **Fix**: Replace with direct instructions or varied transition phrases.

### Issue 2: Pedagogical Gap (Neuter Ambiguity)
- **Location**: Section "CRITICAL: 'Це' vs 'Цей' (The Identification Trap)"
- **Original**: The table contrasts "Це книга" (Identification) vs "Ця книга" (Specification).
- **Problem**: It fails to show that for **Neuter** nouns (e.g., *вікно*), both forms look identical ("Це вікно" vs "Це вікно"). A beginner might ask: "Wait, the table says they are different, but for 'window' they are the same?"
- **Fix**: Add a specific note about this overlap to the warning box.
