## Пори року

### Чотири сезони (The Four Seasons)
We don't just ask about today. We plan for the whole year. In Ukraine, nature changes dramatically four times. It is a cycle every neighbor knows.

Here are the four players in this story:

*   **зима** [zɪˈmɑ] — winter
*   **весна** [vɛsˈnɑ] — spring
*   **літо** [ˈlʲitɔ] — summer
*   **осінь** [ˈɔsʲinʲ] — autumn

> [!fact]
> Ukraine has four very distinct seasons. **Зима** is truly snowy, and **літо** is truly hot. We get the full experience!

### Коли? (When?)
To say "in winter," we use a special time word (an adverb). We don't use a preposition like "in." Look at how the stress moves in two of these. This is key for sounding natural.

| Noun (What?) | Adverb (When?) | IPA |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **зима** | **взимку** | [ˈwzɪmku] |
| **весна** | **навесні** | [nɑvɛsˈnʲi] |
| **літо** | **влітку** | [ˈwlʲitku] |
| **осінь** | **восени** | [ʋɔsɛˈnɪ] |

_Приклади:_
*   **Взимку холодно.** (In winter [it is] cold.)
*   **Навесні гарно.** (In spring [it is] beautiful.)
*   **Влітку спекотно.** (In summer [it is] hot.)
