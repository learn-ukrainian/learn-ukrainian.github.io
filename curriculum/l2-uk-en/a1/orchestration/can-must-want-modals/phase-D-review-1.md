## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Confusing Grouping
- **Location**: Section "Треба and Потрібно", list under "How it works"
- **Original**: «Ми **повинні** купити хліб».
- **Problem**: Included in a list titled "**Треба/Потрібно + Infinitive**". This confuses impersonal (Dative) and personal (Nominative) constructions.
- **Fix**: Remove or move to the "Повинен" section.

### Issue 2: Future Perfective Scope Creep
- **Location**: Section "Практика: Дозвіл та Етикет", Situation 3
- **Original**: «Так. Я **візьму** її.»
- **Problem**: "Візьму" is Future Perfective. A1 learners haven't seen this.
- **Fix**: Change to «Я хочу її купити» (fits the module theme).

### Issue 3: Beginner Safety (Dative Scare)
- **Location**: Section "Треба and Потрібно"
- **Original**: "The person is in the **Dative case**."
- **Problem**: Uses the grammatical term without showing the forms (Мені, Тобі, Нам), leaving beginners to guess or fear.
- **Fix**: Add a small gloss/reminder of the forms.
