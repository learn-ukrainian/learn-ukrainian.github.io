Section 1 "Діалоги (Dialogues)": ~350 words — Introduce talking about the day in the past and future as fixed vocabulary chunks through two dialogues.
Section 2 "Мій типовий день (My Typical Day)": ~400 words — Read a model text of a daily routine and learn the core adverbs for the five parts of the day.
Section 3 "Від ранку до вечора (From Morning to Evening)": ~550 words — Learn sequence words and basic daily activity verbs to connect events into a narrative.
Section 4 "Підсумок — Summary": ~250 words — Review combining time, sequence, and activities to tell a coherent story, followed by a self-check.
Total: ~1550 words
