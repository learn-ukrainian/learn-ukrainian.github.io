## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Linguistic Error (Case Agreement)
- **Location**: Line 89 / Section "Describing the World in Context"
- **Original**: "Вона дуже мелодійна та **виразною**."
- **Problem**: "Виразною" is Instrumental case, but "мелодійна" is Nominative. They are homogeneous predicates referring to "Вона" (subject). They must match in case (Nominative).
- **Fix**: "Вона дуже мелодійна та **виразна**."

### Issue 2: Grammar Scope Creep (Past Tense)
- **Location**: Line 106 / Section "Practice"
- **Original**: "Так, я **купив** його вчора."
- **Problem**: Past tense (минулий час) is typically taught in later A1 modules (around M30+). Module 26 focuses on adjectives.
- **Fix**: Change to a structure using known grammar or an adjective. E.g., "Так, він зовсім новий." (Yes, it is quite new).
