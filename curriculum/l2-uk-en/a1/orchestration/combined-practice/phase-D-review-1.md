## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Linguistic Accuracy (Incorrect Punctuation)
- **Location**: Throughout (Sections "Причини: «Тому що» проти «Бо»", "Вирішення проблем: «Якщо»", "Висновок")
- **Original**: «Я п'ю воду, бо — хочу пити.», «Якщо — ринок не працює, ми йдемо в супермаркет.»
- **Problem**: The module systematically inserts an em dash (—) immediately following the conjunctions "бо", "тому що", and "якщо". This is grammatically incorrect in Ukrainian, breaks the natural rhythm of the sentence, and directly contradicts the module's own claim that "бо" is fast-paced.
- **Fix**: Remove the em dashes from all these examples to ensure accurate grammar and natural prosody.

### Issue 2: LLM Fingerprint (Mechanical Transitions)
- **Location**: Throughout the English text
- **Original**: Multiple instances of "Let us..." ("Let us look at a simple scenario", "Let us explore", "Let us learn", "Let us fix", "Let us apply", "Let us look at a multi-turn").
- **Problem**: This repetitive framing sounds like a robotic lecturer rather than a warm, natural tutor, violating the persona guidelines and triggering the LLM fingerprint check.
- **Fix**: Replace the repetitive "Let us..." phrases with more varied, conversational transitions.

### Issue 3: Beginner Safety (Unscaffolded Wall of Text)
- **Location**: Section "Додаткова практика читання"
- **Original**: Three large paragraphs starting with «Ось історія про день Олени.»
- **Problem**: This block contains over 300 words of continuous Ukrainian text without any English translations, vocabulary pop-outs, or structural breaks. For an A1 learner, this is an intimidating "wall of text" that can cause overwhelm and violates the principle of graduated immersion.
- **Fix**: Add a strong, encouraging English preface reminding them it's okay not to understand every word, framing it as an optional challenge rather than a strict requirement.
