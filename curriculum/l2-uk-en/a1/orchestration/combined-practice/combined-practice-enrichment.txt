## Vocabulary Frequency

## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Na Kavu" (For Coffee) Ritual**: In Ukrainian culture, particularly in Lviv, "піти на каву" is the universal starting point for any plan, meeting, or decision-making session. It serves as the logical "Warm-up" for a day of errands.
2. **The Power of "Базар" (The Market)**: Despite modern supermarkets, the local market remains a hub where multiple practical skills (negotiating, identifying produce, handling cash) are combined. A "Combined Practice" scenario in Ukraine often centers around a trip to the market followed by other daily tasks.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **"Також" Word Order**: English speakers often place "також" (also) at the end of the sentence. Correct: "Я також хочу каву" (I also want coffee) vs incorrect/unnatural "Я хочу каву також".
2. **Over-reliance on "Потім"**: Beginners often string sentences together with "потім... потім... потім" (then... then... then). The lesson should encourage using "спочатку" (first) and "нарешті" (finally) for a more structured narrative.
3. **Register of "Бо" vs "Тому що"**: Learners may not realize "бо" is shorter and more colloquial than "тому що", though both are valid for A1.

## Cross-References
