## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Pedagogy / Consistency
- **Location**: Section "Чим ви їдете? (By what means?)" / Table
- **Original**:
  «*   Я їду **таксі**. — I go by taxi.
  *   Я їду **метро**. — I go by metro.»
- **Problem**: This contradicts the specific warning box earlier ("Correct: **Я їду на метро.**") and general usage. While grammatically Instrumental is possible, teaching beginners to say "Я їду метро" is unnatural compared to "Я їду на метро".
- **Fix**: Modify the section to explicitly acknowledge that for these indeclinable nouns, the Locative construction ("на метро") is preferred/standard, and update the table examples.

### Issue 2: Linguistic Style (path)
- **Location**: Section "Основні види транспорту", under Tram
- **Original**: «Трамвай їде по рейках.»
- **Problem**: "По рейках" is common but less idiomatic than the Instrumental of path ("рейками") in literary/standard Ukrainian. Avoiding "по" + Locative for movement is a mark of high-quality Ukrainian.
- **Fix**: «Трамвай їде рейками.»

### Issue 3: Activity Ambiguity
- **Location**: Activities file, Quiz "Граматика: Де чи Куди?", Item 1
- **Original**: «Зараз я йду _____ (вхід у метро).»
- **Problem**: The parenthetical prompt "(вхід у метро)" implies the destination is the *entrance* (noun 'вхід'), which would require "до входу" or "на вхід". However, the correct answer is "в метро". This misleads the learner.
- **Fix**: Change prompt to «(метро)» or «(станція метро)».
