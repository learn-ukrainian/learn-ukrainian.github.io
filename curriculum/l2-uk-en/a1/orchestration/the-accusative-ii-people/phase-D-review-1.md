## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Linguistic Error (Gender)
- **Location**: Section "Common Mistakes Gallery", Row 2
- **Original**: «Dog is feminine (**-а** → **-у**).»
- **Problem**: `Собака` is grammatically **masculine** (e.g., *мій собака*), even though it follows the I declension (ending in -a). Teaching it as feminine causes future errors with adjectives/pronouns.
- **Fix**: Clarify that it acts like a feminine noun *in declension* due to the -a ending, but is masculine.

### Issue 2: Pedagogical Confusion (Vowel Magic)
- **Location**: Section "Vowel Magic: Fleeting & Changing", Table Row 3
- **Original**: «**Батько** | Я бачу **батька** | **-о** drops → **+а**»
- **Problem**: `Батько` → `Батька` is a standard II declension ending change (-о to -а), NOT a fleeting vowel or root change like `кіт/кота`. Grouping it here confuses the learner about what "vowel magic" (root alternation) actually is.
- **Fix**: Remove this row from the "Vowel Magic" table.
