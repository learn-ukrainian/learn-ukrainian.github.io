## Critical Issues Found

### Issue 1: Untaught Grammar (Reflexive Possessive)
- **Location**: Practice Pattern 4 / Quiz Q5 / Dialogue 3
- **Original**: "Ти любиш **свого** друга", "Він любить **свою** вчительку"
- **Problem**: The pronoun `свій` (свого, свою) is used in practice and tested in quizzes, but the "Pro Tip: Pronouns Change Too!" table only lists `мій, твій, цей, той, наш, ваш`. A1 students will be confused why it's not "твого" or "його".
- **Fix**: Either add `свій` to the presentation table OR replace it with `твій/мій` in practice to match the taught material. (See Fix Plan).
