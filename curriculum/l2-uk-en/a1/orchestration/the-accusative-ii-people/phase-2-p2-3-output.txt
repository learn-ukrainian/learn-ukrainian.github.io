### Syntactic Roles: The Object

Let's put on our "grammar glasses" for a moment. Every sentence has parts that do different jobs. We already know the **–ü—Ė–ī–ľ–Ķ—ā** (Subject)‚ÄĒthat is the person doing the action (like "I" or "He").

### The Big Picture: People vs. Things

Now, let's mix it all together. Your life is full of both people and things. A good speaker switches automatically between the two rules.

- **Things** (Inanimate) usually do not change in the Masculine.
- **People** (Animate) DO change in the Masculine.
- **Feminine** nouns change to **-—É** either way!
