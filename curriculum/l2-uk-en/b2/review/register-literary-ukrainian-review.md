# Review: B2 M22 — Регістр — Літературна українська

## 1. Executive Summary
The module "Регістр — Літературна українська" is an excellent and comprehensive guide to the Ukrainian literary register for B2 students. It successfully navigates the complex history of the language's standardization and provides practical, hands-on examples of stylistic devices, tropes, and syntax. The pedagogical approach is sound, moving from historical context to specific tools like metaphor, epithet, and inversion, and finally to practical application and common pitfalls like "kantselyaryt".

## 2. Detailed Assessment

### 1. Historical Context and Accuracy (10/10)
The text does a fantastic job explaining the role of Kotliarevsky and Shevchenko. The section on Kulish is also a vital addition that explains the phonetic alphabet: «Цей правопис базувався на геніально простому принципі».

### 2. Linguistic Depth and Tropes (10/10)
The explanations of metaphor, metonymy, and epithet are clear and vivid. The examples provided are excellent and help the student understand the difference.

### 3. Syntax and Inversion (9/10)
The explanation of stylistic inversion is solid, demonstrating how word order changes emphasis: «Прийшла весна красна».

### 4. Immersion and Tone (10/10)
The module maintains a strictly professional yet engaging pedagogical tone throughout. There is no English contamination.

### 5. Vocabulary Integration (9/10)
The vocabulary choices are appropriate for B2, introducing essential literary terms like 'епітет', 'метафора', 'алегорія'.

### 6. Activity Quality (10/10)
The activities are diverse, ranging from multiple-choice to sorting and essay response, all tailored to test the specific concepts taught in the prose.

### 7. Avoidance of Calques (10/10)
The module actively fights against Russian calques, successfully demonstrating why «ухвалювати рішення» is correct while «приймати рішення» is a calque.

### 8. Use of Persona (9/10)
The persona of a knowledgeable Ukrainian literature professor shines through the text.

### 9. Decolonization Perspective (10/10)
The module emphasizes the independent and organic development of the Ukrainian literary language, debunking myths about external creation.

### 10. Engagement and Callouts (10/10)
The use of callouts like `[!fact]`, `[!dialogue]`, and `[!warning]` breaks up the text beautifully and highlights key information.

### 11. Readability and Flow (9/10)
The transitions between historical facts and linguistic analysis are smooth. 

### 12. Overall Aesthetic (9/10)
A highly polished and professional module. 

## 3. Issues Found
1. While the explanation of tropes is excellent, the module could provide slightly more examples of metonymy in everyday use. The phrase «читати Франка» is good, but another example would solidify the concept.
2. In the "Символ та алегорія" section, the distinction between allegory and symbol could be blurred for a B2 student. More contrasting pairs might help.

## 4. Final Recommendation
PASS. The module is structurally sound, pedagogically effective, and linguistically rich. The minor issues noted do not detract from the overall high quality of the lesson.
