## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Ви" Capitalization Nuance**: In Ukrainian business correspondence, capitalizing "Ви" (and "Вас", "Вам") is a strict marker of respect when addressing a *specific individual*. 2. **Response Time Etiquette**: Unspoken rule: "Telegraphic" answers expected within 3 days; full letters within 1-2 days. If a request takes longer, an intermediate "receipt acknowledgment" (лист-підтвердження отримання) is mandatory within 3 days. Silence > 3 days is considered unprofessional.

1. **Tautologies (Pleonasm)**: "спільне співробітництво" (common mistake) → "співробітництво" (correct); "вільна вакансія" → "вакансія".
