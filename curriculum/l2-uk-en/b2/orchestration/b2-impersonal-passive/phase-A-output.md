# Дослідження: Безособові конструкції на -но/-то

## State Standard Reference
§4.3.3.1: "Односкладні речення з головним членом – дієсловом... безособові речення: Було приємно дивитися на веселих дітей. Там усе правильно записано."
Alignment: This module directly teaches the syntactic structure and stylistic application of single-member impersonal sentences with the main member expressed by an invariable -но/-то form, fulfilling the B2 syntax requirement for one-member sentences.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Fatalism in Literature:** The use of "-но/-то" constructions historically reflects a cultural sense of fate or higher powers at work (e.g., «Так судилося», «Так написано» in the works of Shevchenko and Franko), contrasting with its modern administrative use.
2. **Contemporary Reality:** The form is ubiquitous in modern Ukrainian wartime media and alerts, focusing purely on the factual outcome without naming the actor: «Оголошено повітряну тривогу», «Відбій скасовано», «Ціль знищено».

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Case Mismatch (Nominative instead of Accusative):** *Книга написано* → **Книгу написано**. Learners intuitively treat the object as the subject because it's a "passive" thought.
2. **Forced Agent (Instrumental Case Intrusion):** *Книгу написано мною* → **Я написав книгу** (or just **Книгу написано**). Learners try to translate "The book was written by me" directly. Ukrainian strongly rejects the Instrumental agent with -но/-то forms.

## Cross-References
- Builds on: b2-02 (Past Passive Participles)
- Prepares for: b2-04 (Reflexive Passive)

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing:** Specifically highlight the rejection of the Instrumental agent. While Russian freely allows "написано мной", Ukrainian preserves agency: if you know who did it, use the active voice («Ми зробили», not «Було зроблено нами»). The -но/-то form is strictly for when the agent is irrelevant or unknown.
- **Immersion:** 100% Ukrainian. Do not use English scaffolding in the prose.
- **Stylistic Range:** Balance the strict, emotionless legal/bureaucratic register with the stark reality of news reports.
