# Дослідження: Вставні слова та вирази

## State Standard Reference
§4.3.2: "просте речення зі вставними словами: Може, то птахи приносять на своїх крилах весну?;"
Alignment: Module explicitly teaches identification and punctuation of parenthetical words (вставні слова) as required for B2 syntax competence.

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Frequency / Notes | Key collocations / Synonyms |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| **мабуть** | High frequency. Marks uncertainty/politeness. Note: standard spelling is with "т" (not "будь"). | мабуть, так; мабуть, ні; це, мабуть, найкращий... |
| **на жаль** | High frequency. Emotional evaluation. Always written separately. | на жаль, не зможу; на превеликий жаль; як на жаль |
| **на щастя** | Medium frequency. Relief/positive evaluation. | на щастя, все добре; на моє щастя |
| **по-перше / по-друге** | High frequency in formal/structured speech. Always hyphenated. | по-перше, я хочу...; а по-друге... |
| **звісно / звичайно** | High frequency. Agreement/certainty. | звісно, що так; звичайно, ні |
| **кажуть** | High frequency in colloquial speech. Source of info. | кажуть, що...; люди кажуть |
| **до речі** | High frequency. Topic switching. | до речі, ти знаєш...; до речі, про... |

## Cultural Hooks
1.  **The "Polite Uncertainty"**: Ukrainians often use **мабуть** or **напевно** even when they are fairly sure, to avoid sounding arrogant, categorical, or aggressive. It softens the statement and invites the listener's opinion (e.g., "Це, мабуть, найкращий варіант" vs "Це найкращий варіант").
2.  **Collective Wisdom ("Кажуть")**: The phrase **кажуть** (they say) is a culturally specific marker of "shared knowledge" or "rumor," functioning similarly to the passive voice in other languages but retaining a sense of community "voice." It appeals to the authority of the "collective."

## Common Learner Errors
1.  **False Parentheticals**: Learners often put commas around words that *feel* parenthetical but are particles in Ukrainian: **навіть** (even), **майже** (almost), **адже** (after all), **принаймні** (at least). *Correction:* These are NEVER separated by commas.
2.  **Homonymy (Member vs. Parenthetical)**: Confusing the word as a sentence member vs. a parenthetical.
    *   *Error:* "Він, може, це зробити." (He, maybe, to do it.)
    *   *Correct:* "Він може це зробити." (He can do it - verb) vs "Він, може, прийде." (He, maybe, will come - parenthetical).
3.  **Hyphenation**: Writing **по перше** (wrong) instead of **по-перше** (correct), or **нажаль** (wrong) instead of **на жаль** (correct).

## Cross-References
- **Builds on**: b2-11 (Multi-clause Sentences), Comma usage basics
- **Prepares for**: b2-13 (Emphasis and Inversion), b2-14 (Stylistic Connectors)

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Visuals**: A decision tree for "Is it parenthetical?" (Can you remove it? Does it change the fact or just the attitude?) would be very effective for the "Homonymy" error.
- **Emphasis**: Explicitly list the "False Parentheticals" (навіть, майже, адже) in a warning box, as this is a persistent B2 error.
