## Cultural Hooks
1. **Hierarchy & Formality**: Ukrainian business culture retains a higher degree of formality than American culture. Using "Ви" (capitalized when addressing a specific person) is standard and expected in formal correspondence to show respect.
2. **The "Доброго дня" Myth**: While popular, "Доброго дня!" is gramatically reserved for farewells (genitive of wish). The correct formal greeting is "Добрий день!" or "Вітаю!". This is a marker of high literacy.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Calques (Surzhyk)**: "приймати участь" → "брати участь" (to participate); "на протязі" → "протягом" (during); "приймати міри" → "вживати заходів" (to take measures).
2. **Subject Line Vagueness**: Using empty headers like "Питання" or "Важливо" instead of specific summaries (e.g., "Запит щодо контракту №123").
3. **Register Confusion**: Using semi-formal greetings ("Привіт", "Доброго часу доби") in first-contact emails.

## Cross-References
- Builds on: B1 formal register introduction (if present), A2-B1 grammar (imperative, conditional).
- Prepares for: B2-PRO modules, C1 Professional Writing, Business Negotiations.

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Tone**: Emphasize the difference between "dry/bureaucratic" (bad) and "professional/courteous" (good).
- **Structure**: Teach the "Subject -> Salutation -> Context -> Call to Action -> Sign-off" strict structure.
- **Nominalization**: Briefly introduce how verbs become nouns in formal style (e.g., "ми вирішили" -> "прийняття рішення"), referencing §4.2.6 of the Standard.
