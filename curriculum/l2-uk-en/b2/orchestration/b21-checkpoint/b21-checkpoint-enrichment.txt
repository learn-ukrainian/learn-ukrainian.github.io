2. **Diia (State in a Smartphone)**: A modern Ukrainian digital governance revolution. Ukraine became the first nation to grant digital passports the same legal status as physical ones. ## Common Learner Errors
1. **Overuse of "було + participle"** → *Incorrect:* "Рішення було прийнято." → *Correct:* "Рішення прийняли" (active) or "Рішення прийнято" (impersonal). *Why:* Direct translation of English "The decision was made." Ukrainian prefers active constructions or impersonal forms without "було" in the present/past resultative.
2. **Agent confusion in reflexive passive** → *Incorrect:* "Будинок будується робітниками." → *Correct:* "Робітники будують будинок." *Why:* Reflexive verbs (-ся) are rarely used with an explicit instrumental agent. They imply the action happens "by itself" or generally.
3. **Register mismatch** → *Error:* Using heavy passive structures in casual speech (sounding bureaucratic) or colloquial active forms in formal legal writing.
