## Cultural Hooks
1. **Kazimir Malevich & Ukraine**: Born in Kyiv, Malevich explicitly stated his "Suprematism" was rooted in the Ukrainian folk art (stove paintings, embroidery) he saw in villages as a child. He taught at the Kyiv Art Institute (1928-1930) and his favorite song was "Гуде вітер вельми в полі".
2. **Petrykivka Painting (UNESCO)**: Unique tool usage — the "kotyachka" (brush made of cat fur) for finest details. Symbolism: Viburnum (kalyna) = feminine beauty; Oak = strength/sun; Cuckoo on viburnum = flow of time.
3. **Mykhailo Boichuk**: Founder of "Neo-Byzantinism" (Boychukism). He aimed to revive the monumental style of Kyivan Rus' frescoes but was destroyed during the "Executed Renaissance" (Rozstriliane Vidrodzhennia).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Calque "present"**: *На картині присутні яскраві кольори* → *На картині є / використано / переважають яскраві кольори* — "Присутній" is usually for people/attendance.
2. **Wrong verb for mood**: *Картина викликає настрій* → *Картина створює / навіює / передає настрій* — "Викликати" is more for reactions/reflexes.
3. **Tautology**: *Художник зобразив зображення* → *Художник змалював / відтворив / показав* — Avoid repetitive roots.
