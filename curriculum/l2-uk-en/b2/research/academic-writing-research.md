## Cultural Hooks
1. **Academic Integrity Law (2026)**: Ukraine enacted a new Law "On Academic Integrity" effective Feb 1, 2026, which explicitly regulates the use of AI in education and research. This is a hot topic for students.
2. **Kyiv-Mohyla Academy**: The historical predecessor of modern Ukrainian academic tradition (1615), emphasizing rhetoric and philosophy, contrasting with the Soviet style of "dry" science.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Calques from English/Russian**: *приймати участь* (wrong) → *брати участь* (correct); *приводити приклад* (wrong) → *наводити приклад* (correct).
2. **Pleonasm**: *вільна вакансія* (redundant) → *вакансія*; *своя власна думка* → *власна думка*.
3. **Preposition Misuse**: *при написанні* (calque) → *під час написання* or *коли пишемо*.
4. **Passive Overuse**: *нами було досліджено* (stiff/passive) → *ми дослідили* (active/agency).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Tone**: Professional, encouraging, emphasizing "integrity" as a moral value, not just a rule.
- **AI Context**: Briefly mention that using AI tools is allowed *if disclosed*, fitting the 2026 context.
- **Terminology**: Use *покликання* (citation/reference) rather than *посилання* (link/hyperlink) in the context of bibliography, though *посилання* is often used interchangeably. Standardize on *покликання* for bibliography items.
