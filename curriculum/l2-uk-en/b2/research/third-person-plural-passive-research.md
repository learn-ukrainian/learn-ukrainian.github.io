# Дослідження: Пасив третьою особою множини (Неозначено-особові речення)

## State Standard Reference
§4.3.3.1: "Односкладні речення з головним членом – дієсловом: [...] неозначено-особові речення: По радіо повідомили про погіршення погоди;"
Alignment: This module directly addresses the B2 requirement for mastering one-member sentences (односкладні речення), specifically the indefinite-personal type (неозначено-особові), which functions as a colloquial passive equivalent.

## Vocabulary Frequency
| Word | Frequency / Source | Key collocations |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| **чутка** (rumor) | High (Spoken) | *чутка пішла/пройшла* (rumor spread), *пустити чутку* (start a rumor), *за чутками* (rumor has it), *глуха чутка* (vague rumor) |
| **плітка** (gossip) | High (Spoken) | *розпускати плітки* (spread gossip), *плести плітки* (weave gossip), *пуста плітка* (idle gossip) |
| **казати** (to say) | Very High | *кажуть, що...* (they say that...), *мені казали* (I was told) |
| **повідомити** (to inform) | High (Media/Formal) | *повідомили про...* (it was reported about...), *нам повідомили* (we were informed) |

## Cultural Hooks
1.  **Gossip as Social Control**: Historically in Ukrainian villages, *плітки* (gossip) were not just idle talk but a mechanism for maintaining social norms and community cohesion ("social glue").
2.  **Folk Wisdom**: The proverb "Чужий рот не хлів — не зачинити" (A stranger's mouth is not a barn — you can't shut it) perfectly captures the cultural attitude towards the inevitability of rumors. Another relevant one: "Сорока летить, а собака на хвості сидить" (news spreads fast).

## Common Learner Errors
1.  **Over-explicit Subject**: Using "Вони" explicitly (*Вони кажуть*) instead of the more natural indefinite form (*Кажуть*).
    *   *Correction*: Omit the pronoun to achieve the true "indefinite" or "passive" effect.
2.  **Subject Re-introduction**: Attempting to add a grammatical subject to a sentence that should remain subjectless to focus on the action.
    *   *Correction*: Maintain the one-member structure (only predicate).
3.  **Register Confusion**: Using formal passive participles (-но/-то) in casual gossip contexts where 3rd person plural is more appropriate.
    *   *Correction*: Use *Вкрали гаманець* (colloquial) instead of *Гаманець було вкрадено* (formal/police report).

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Terminology**: Briefly introduce the term "неозначено-особові речення" but focus on the functional description "3rd Person Plural Passive" for English speakers.
- **Contrast**: Explicitly contrast with English "They say..." (where 'They' is present) vs Ukrainian *Кажуть...* (where 'They' is absent).
