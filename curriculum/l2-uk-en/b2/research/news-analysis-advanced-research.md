## Common Learner Errors

1. **«гарячі новини»** → **свіжі/останні/оперативні новини** — Direct calque from English "hot news" / Russian «горячие новости». Ukrainian uses «свіжі новини» (fresh), «останні новини» (latest), «оперативна інформація» (breaking/urgent).

2. **«зробити інтерв'ю»** → **взяти інтерв'ю** (journalist) / **дати інтерв'ю** (interviewee) — The verb «зробити» is used for physical creation of objects; journalistic Ukrainian observes the precise distinction between «взяти» (take/conduct) and «дати» (give).

3. **«на ефірі»** → **в ефірі** — Preposition error. «Бути в ефірі» (to be on air); «виходити в ефір» (to go on air). «На ефірі» is non-standard and sounds like direct Russian/colloquial influence.

4. **«опублікувати» used for all disclosure** → distinguish: **опублікувати** (publish an article/text in press) vs. **оприлюднити** (disclose/make public — used for official documents, data, reports). Conflating these is a register error common at B2.

## Notes for Content Writing

- The **«джинса»** term deserves a full explanatory callout box: Ukrainian-specific slang for editorial content disguised as news, used by journalists since the 1990s. No equivalent in other Slavic journalistic traditions.
- **Whataboutism** (ватнікізм/пересмикування) — the plan uses the English term; consider introducing the Ukrainian journalistic term «ніщо нікого ні до чого» or simply «пересмикування» as stylistically more authentic.
- The **CRAAP test** is U.S. academic (Meriam Library, CSU Chico, 2004); it is widely taught in Ukrainian university journalism programs and can be presented without further qualification.
- **Framing via passive voice** (Section 5 of plan): this maps directly onto §4.1.3.1 (B2 passive voice constructions) and §4.3.5 (direct/indirect speech transformation) — both of which are B2-level grammar already introduced in the module sequence. This section can leverage prior knowledge explicitly.
- **Register**: публіцистичний register should be modeled throughout — nominalized verbs (висвітлення подій, замовчування фактів), passive constructions, hedging modals (можливо, очевидно, нібито, мовляв).
- The plan's §1.1.2.2.2 reference is a State Standard communicative intention index for argumentative discussion — this is about the ability to "express and defend opinions, evaluate others' arguments." Phase B should embed discussion scaffolds (мовленнєві формули) such as «на мою думку», «варто зазначити», «як свідчать дані», «попри це».
