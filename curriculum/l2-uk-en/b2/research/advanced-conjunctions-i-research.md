## State Standard Reference

**§4.3.4.4** — Складнопідрядні речення з відношеннями причини: *«Хлопці боялися поворухнутися, щоб не злякати пташки.»*
**§4.3.4.6** — Складнопідрядні речення з відношеннями допусту: *«Хоч не одного там калічили ті скали, ми далі йшли.»*
(Lines 3437–3445, UKRAINIAN-STATE-STANDARD-2024.txt)

**Alignment**: Module directly addresses both §4.3.4.4 (causal relations) and §4.3.4.6 (concessive relations) at B2 depth — expanding beyond the A2 introduction of "тому що / хоча" (§4.4.2, lines 1403–1416) into stylistic differentiation (бо/тому що/оскільки register ladder) and full concessive paradigm (хоча / незважаючи на те що / попри те що / дарма що). The State Standard itself quotes Франко's "Каменярі" as the canonical concessive example — the plan's literary anchor is Standard-validated.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **«Каменярі» (1878) — Іван Франко** — The poem was written in 1878 and appeared in the almanac «Дзвін» the same year. Its concessive structure is so emblematic that the State Standard 2024 quotes it directly as the canonical illustration of §4.3.4.6: *«Хоч не одного там калічили ті скали, ми далі йшли.»* — The poem gave Franko his enduring nickname «Каменяр» (The Stone-Cutter). Source: Ukrainian Wikipedia, «Каменярі (вірш)».

2. **Textbook canonicity** — Avramenko Grade 7 (NUS 2024, tier 1) explicitly lists the full causal conjunction ladder in a single table: *адже, бо, тому що, через те що, у зв'язку з тим що, оскільки* — confirming the pedagogical sequence the plan follows. Voron Grade 11 adds «завдяки тому що» with the important note that it belongs with the causal group. Source: RAG chunks `7-klas-ukrmova-avramenko-2024_s0196`, `11-klas-ukrajinska-mova-voron-2019_s0286`.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **«із-за» for causal meaning** → correct: **«через»** — «із-за» exists in Ukrainian VESUM as a preposition, but its correct Ukrainian function is **spatial** only ("from behind": *вийшов із-за рогу*). Using it for cause (*«із-за дощу не пішли»*) is a direct Russianism. The plan's framing (Standard §4.3.4.4) mandates «через» for cause. Karaman Grade 10 lists «з-під» and «у зв'язку з» in the same exercise, reinforcing the spatial/causal distinction.

2. **«дякуючи» with negative causes** → correct: **«через»** for negative outcomes — *«дякуючи хворобі»* is a calque error; «дякуючи» (VESUM: advp) is semantically restricted to positive/beneficial causes. For illness, accidents, failures: *«через хворобу», «через аварію»*. Voron Grade 11 notes «завдяки тому що» in the positive causal group — contrasting with «через те що» for negative causes.

3. **«тому що» written as one word / «томущо»** → correct: **«тому що»** always two words — This is a direct orthographic Russianism (Russian «потому что» sometimes prompts merged writing). Avramenko Grade 7 explicitly lists it as a multiword composed conjunction (*складений сполучник*). The plan correctly flags this under §4.3.4.4 content.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **The §4.3.4.6 Франко quote is Standard-certified**: use *«Хоч не одного там калічили ті скали, ми далі йшли»* as the literary anchor for the concessive section — it is not merely a cultural hook but an official pedagogical example.
- **Register ladder is corpus-validated**: бо (514 IPM) > оскільки (211 IPM) > хоча (171 IPM) > попри (49 IPM) ≈ незважаючи (46 IPM). This frequency hierarchy maps cleanly to the stylistic progression colloquial → neutral → formal → analytical.
- **«тому що» split punctuation** (the *«..., тому, що...»* nuance) has no dedicated textbook source found — treat carefully; cite as a grammar rule observation, not a textbook quote.
- **дарма що**: Karaman Grade 10 confirms the form but treats it as a stylistic/literary marker — consistent with the plan's positioning.
- **Vocabulary verification**: All key vocabulary confirmed in VESUM. «тому що» is correctly not in VESUM as a single entry (it is a two-token conjunction — no issue).
- **Decolonized framing**: Frame «із-за» avoidance not as "unlike Russian" but as "Ukrainian spatial/causal distinction governed by Правопис §4.3.4.4"; frame «дякуючи» restriction as a semantic precision rule of Ukrainian, not a contrast with another language.
