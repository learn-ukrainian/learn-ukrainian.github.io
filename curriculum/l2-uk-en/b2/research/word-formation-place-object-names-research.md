# Дослідження: Словотворення: назви місць та об'єктів

## State Standard Reference

**§4.2.8** (lines 3366–3367):
> "Утворення іменників – назв місць за виконуваною дією: спати – спальня, приймати – приймальня, читати – читальня, лікувати – лікарня."

**Alignment**: The module directly addresses this standard. The Standard examples (спальня, приймальня, читальня, лікарня) are all -ня/-льня formations. The module expands the B1 foundation (§4.3.5: "-ня, -ище (пекарня, кладовище)") to include the full system: -ня/-льня/-альня, -ище (neuter locatives vs augmentatives), and -арня (professional establishments). The Standard cites only deverbal -ня; coverage of -ище and -арня at B2 is the planned "expanded" treatment justified by the B2 label on this entry.

**Note**: The B1 Standard §4.3.5 first introduced -ня and -ище. This B2 module builds on that by adding: (a) the morphological trigger rules for -льня vs. -альня, (b) the augmentative trap with -ище, and (c) the full -арня paradigm.

## Cultural Hooks

**1. Просвіта читальні — centers of national revival (1868–1920s)**
Wikipedia confirms Всеукраїнське товариство «Просвіта» was a "культурно-освітянська" organization with a national mission. **Critical factual correction for plan**: The term "хата-читальня" (Wikipedia: "сільська культурно-освітня установа, заснована за перших років радянської влади в Україні як перша форма сельбуда, після ліквідації «Просвіти»") was actually a Soviet institution established *after* Просвіта was liquidated — not a Просвіта institution. The Просвіта organization ran "читальні" (reading rooms), not "хати-читальні." Content writing must distinguish: Просвіта **читальні** = Ukrainian national awakening (Galicia, 1860s–1910s); Soviet **хата-читальня** = mass literacy campaign post-1920, different ideology. The module plan conflates these; the "хата-читальня" hook should be reframed as Просвіта's **читальні** specifically.

**2. Кав'ярня as a Ukrainian-native word**
Wikipedia confirms: "Кав'я́рня, кава́рня, кафе́, розм. кофе́йня" — кав'ярня is the pitomо Ukrainian form, with кафе as the international borrowing. The Lviv kava culture makes кав'ярня a living example of Ukrainian word-formation productivity. The compound книгарня-кав'ярня is a modern hybrid attested in urban discourse that illustrates -арня/-ярня productivity.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Hypergeneralization of -ня** → *"робільня"* instead of **майстерня** — happens because learners correctly learn "verb stem + -ня = place" but not all verbs take this suffix; майстерня derives from майстер (noun), not роб-ити. Content should include a negative exemplar set.

2. **-ище gender trap**: Learners write "вітрище — середнього роду" (by analogy with locative сховище, горище) when вітрище is masculine (augmentative of вітер, чол. р.). The -ище suffix has two functions — place (neuter) and augmentative (inherits source noun gender) — and these are visually identical. This is the highest-risk error for the -ище section.

3. **-льня vs. -альня morphological trigger confusion**: Learners write *"читальня"* correctly but then produce *"вбирльня"* instead of **вбиральня**. The rule: if the verb stem ends in a consonant cluster or truncates, the connecting -а- is inserted (вбир-а-ти → вбиральня). Contrast: чита-ти (stem чита-) → читальня (no connecting vowel needed). This requires explicit attention.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Factual correction required**: Reframe "хата-читальня" as Просвіта **читальні** (Galician national awakening, 1868+). Soviet хата-читальня is a different institution. Use "читальня" as the word; cultural anchor = Просвіта reading rooms.
- **Decolonized framing**: The suffix -ня is a productive native Ukrainian formation — present it on its own terms, not via contrast with any other language. The -ище augmentative vs. locative distinction is unique to Ukrainian morphology and can be presented as a richness of the language.
- **Register note**: -арня words (пекарня, перукарня, кав'ярня) are neutral/everyday; -ище words (сховище, родовище) skew toward formal/technical/scientific register — align with the module's "науковий регістр" goal.
- **Frequency gradient for pedagogical ordering**: лікарня (highest, 16.77 IPM) → кладовище (4.50) → сховище (2.68) → кав'ярня (0.96) → пекарня (0.47) → читальня (0.43). Lead with known words (лікарня) for TTT diagnosis.
- **майстерня**: This word must appear in error-correction section as a *non-deverbal* -ня word (from майстер, noun) to prevent hypergeneralization trap.
- **Word count strategy**: 4000-word target requires substantive depth in each section. Section on -ище (850 words) must handle the gender-ambiguity problem carefully with multiple worked examples.
