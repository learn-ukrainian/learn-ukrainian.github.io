## Cultural Hooks

1. **Михайло Кравчук (1892–1942)** — Wikipedia (uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Кравчук_Михайло_Пилипович): Born 10 October 1892 in Chovnytsia, Volhynia. Academician of the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences (1929), youngest ever elected at 37. Co-author of the first three-volume Ukrainian mathematical terminology dictionary. His students included Serhiy Korolev (rocket designer, father of Soviet space programme) and Archip Liulka (jet engine designer). His Kravchuk polynomials remain globally cited in coding theory and image processing. He insisted on writing mathematics in Ukrainian — labelled a "nationalist," arrested February 21, 1938, sentenced in a half-hour trial to 20 years. Died in a GULAG camp on March 9, 1942. Rehabilitated 1956. Added to UNESCO's list of most outstanding people in 2002. Quote: *"Моя любов — Україна і математика."*

2. **Тритомний словник математичної термінології** — Wikipedia (via Kravchuk article): In the late 1920s, the mathematical subsection of the Institute of Ukrainian Scientific Language (Інститут української наукової мови), chaired by Kravchuk, produced a three-volume mathematical dictionary — a foundational act of Ukrainian scientific language building during the cultural renaissance (*Розстріляне відродження*). The dictionary was suppressed after Kravchuk's arrest; the Soviet project of Russifying Ukrainian scientific vocabulary continued for decades. This makes word-formation suffixes (-нн-, -енн-, матричний, поліноміальний) directly historically charged in this module.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Declension of tens 50–80:** Learners decline both morphological parts (*п'яти-десяти*), but only the second part declines: **п'ятдесяти** (not *п'ятидесяти*), шістдесяти, сімдесяти, вісімдесяти. The plan explicitly flags this as a correction target. State Standard example: "шістдесятьома (шістдесятьма) двома."

2. **Numeral–noun agreement rule "2/3/4 vs. 5+":** After 2, 3, 4 → nominative plural (два дослідження, три обчислення); after 5+ → genitive plural (п'ять досліджень, сто доведень). Learners frequently overgeneralise the genitive (п'ять *дослідження*). Compound numbers require the final digit to govern: двадцять три рядки (not *рядків*).

3. **One-member vs. two-member sentences:** Learners add an explicit subject to безособові sentences (*Кравчука було заарештовано* → *НКВС заарештував Кравчука*), losing the stylistic effect. In scientific and official-administrative register, the impersonal passive construction signals authoritative/bureaucratic tone — learners must be trained to read and produce it intentionally, not eliminate it as a grammatical "gap."

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Kravchuk as thematic spine:** All five sections can reference Kravchuk's biography naturally — numeral-heavy chronology (dates, publication counts, congress locations), scientific vocabulary in word-formation section, impersonal passives for his arrest/rehabilitation in one-member syntax section, epistolary texts from actual known correspondence with colleagues for the workshop. This gives the module organic cohesion rather than five disconnected grammar drills.
- **Decolonized framing critical here:** Kravchuk was explicitly persecuted for writing mathematics in Ukrainian. Word-formation and terminology content should be framed as Ukrainian scientific language development, not as "adapting" or "translating from" anything. The polyterminus decision (матричний, поліноміальний) belongs to a Ukrainian tradition he helped build.
- **Low-IPM vocabulary needs density:** синтез and обчислення are technical but low-frequency. Use them in multiple sentence contexts across sections to achieve productive retention. Minimum 3 full example sentences per term.
- **50–80 declension correction:** State Standard confirms variant forms are accepted — шістдесятьома / шістдесятьма; вісімдесятьома / вісімдесятьма. The module should present both and clarify when each is stylistically preferred (literary vs. colloquial).
- **Epistolary register:** Use brief excerpts modelled on 1920s–30s academic correspondence style (formal Ukrainian, dated forms of address like *Шановний колего*, closing *З повагою*). Contrast with scientific (безособові, nominalized verbs) systematically in the workshop.
- **Word target 4000:** With five content sections and a biographical throughline, the Kravchuk context provides excellent expansion material — biographical narrative for section 1, data-rich numeral tables + passage for section 2, word-formation paradigm tables + scientific text for section 3, annotated sentence types + analysis for section 4, and a contrastive transformation task for section 5.
