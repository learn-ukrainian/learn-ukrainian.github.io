## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Art of Silence**: "Слово — срібло, а мовчання — золото". In Ukrainian professional and cultural contexts, high-context communication often relies on knowing when not to speak. Silence is not necessarily agreement; it can indicate contemplation or strategic withholding in negotiations.
2. **Directness vs. Politeness**: Ukrainian can be very direct, but in formal or power-imbalanced situations (like speaking to superiors or older people), the choice of verb softens the interaction (e.g., using "цікавитися" instead of "питати").

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Aspect Confusion (сказати vs. говорити)**: Learners often say "Я хотів сказати з тобою" instead of "Я хотів поговорити з тобою". *Why:* Over-generalizing the English "to talk/speak" to the perfective resultative "сказати".
2. **Translation Trap (просити vs. питати)**: Using "питати" for requests: "Я питаю тебе допомогти" instead of "Я прошу тебе допомогти". *Why:* English "ask" covers both inquiring for information and requesting an action.
