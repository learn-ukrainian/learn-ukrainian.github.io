# Дослідження: Умовний спосіб, частки та вигуки

## State Standard Reference

**§4.1.3.3 (B2):** "Умовний спосіб. Утворення форм умовного способу: знав би, придумала б, відслужили б, якби закричав, -ла, -ло, -ли."
*(Lines 3323–3324, UKRAINIAN-STATE-STANDARD-2024.txt)*

**Alignment:** The module directly addresses the B2 requirement for conditional mood formation with particle б/би. The standard gives paradigmatic examples across all genders/numbers, which the module expands with syntactic patterns (якби…то, коли б…тоді) and the orthographic distinction між якби / як би.

**Note on частки та вигуки:** The terms *частка* and *вигук* do not appear as standalone morphological topics in the B2 section of the State Standard. The B2 preamble (lines 3068–3077) explicitly states: "Обсяг граматичних умінь рівня В2 охоплює всі аспекти рівнів А1, А2, В1" — so particles and interjections are carried forward from A2/B1 scope. The only explicit B2 mention of *частка* is in §4.3.5 (line 3446): "частка чи в разі заміни прямої мови непрямою" (indirect speech). The module's extension to modal, emphatic, and negative particles addresses the B2 stylistic competence requirement (§4.4, lines 3453–3479) — phonetic, lexical, and syntactic expressive devices.

**B1 foundation (§4.2.3.3, lines 2297–2298):** "Умовний спосіб — якби + минулий час." This module deepens and extends that foundation.

**Key collocation note:** *навряд чи* is a fixed phrase (adverb + particle); GRAC does not return frequency for multi-word tokens. ## Cultural Hooks

1. **Іван Котляревський (1769–1838)** — засновник сучасної української літератури (Ukrainian Wikipedia). His *Енеїда* is the first major work in modern literary Ukrainian and is saturated with emotional interjections (ой, ого, овва) and emphatic particles (аж, таки, же) drawn from живе народне мовлення. The module's examples from Котляревський in the interjections section are well-grounded: *Енеїда* is canonical precisely because it democratized these expressive markers.

2. **Іван Франко** — the plan also cites Франко for interjections in художній стиль. Franko was a prolific stylist who used particles like *таки*, *же*, *аж* to convey narrative urgency and folk register. Both authors are firmly in the B2 canon and serve as non-trivial literary anchor points for the module.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **б vs би confusion** — *сказала б* (after vowel) vs *сказав би* (after consonant). Rule confirmed in Gr. 7 Avramenko (tier 1, 2024): "Частку б уживаємо після голосного, а би — після приголосного." English speakers default to *би* uniformly because they have no equivalent phonetic rule.

2. **якби (разом) vs як би (окремо)** — *якби* = conditional conjunction ("if"); *як би* = adverb + particle ("how would"). Gr. 7 Avramenko confirms: "якби = коли б / як би — прислівник + частка (Як би не проспати?)". This is a top-5 orthographic error for B2 learners.

3. **хіба vs невже** — both translate as "really?" in English but carry opposite emotional charges: *хіба* = skepticism/doubt ("is it really possible?"), *невже* = genuine surprise/disbelief ("can it really be?"). GRAC shows *хіба* is 6.5× more frequent than *невже* (59.84 vs 9.14 IPM), suggesting *хіба* is the default and *невже* is marked. Learners conflate them.

4. **Double negation** — Ukrainian requires "нічого не знаю" (double negative = grammatically correct standard); English speakers omit the second negative, producing the ungrammatical "*нічого знаю*".

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Textbook grade level for this topic:** Ukrainian school grammar places умовний спосіб, частки, and вигуки firmly in Grade 7 (Avramenko and Litvinova, both tier 1, 2024). The B2 module deepens this with: syntactic positioning, stylistic register, and контрастивний аналіз with English — not covered at Grade 7 level.
- **Particle typology for content:** Use the Gr. 10 Karaman (tier 2) classification table as the structural backbone: модальні → (вказівні, підсилювальні, стверджувальні, питальні, заперечні, спонукальні, порівняльні, видільні, означальні); словотворчі; формотворчі. This is the most complete textbook-sourced taxonomy.
- **VESUM note on *навряд*:** VESUM classifies it as adverb, not particle. When writing the vocab table, label *навряд чи* as a fixed modal phrase rather than a standalone particle.
- **Decolonized framing:** Ukrainian double negation (нічого не знаю) is often miscategorised by Soviet-era sources as a "Slavic feature shared with Russian." This framing should be avoided. Describe it as a Ukrainian grammatical norm (as confirmed by Avramenko and Litvinova, 2024), without any reference to Russian.
- **Particle *таки*:** Its post-verbal hyphenated form (*зробив-таки*, *прийшов-таки*) is a high-register authenticity marker — emphasise this as distinctively Ukrainian. Do not frame it through any Russian parallel.
- **Word budget check:** 800+800+800+700+900 = 4000 words — exactly at target. Apply the 1.5× overshoot rule: aim for 5500–6000 words in draft to compress to 4000+ after editing.
