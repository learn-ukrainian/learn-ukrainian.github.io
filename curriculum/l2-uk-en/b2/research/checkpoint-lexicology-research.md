## Cultural Hooks

1. **Іван Пулюй (1845–1918)** — Ukrainian physicist and electrical engineer; first developer of the X-ray tube (прототип сучасних рентгенівських апаратів), researcher of cathode and X-rays at the German Technical College in Prague where he served as professor and rector. Co-translated the Bible into Ukrainian (with Panteleimon Kulish and Ivan Nechui-Levytsky). One of the founding members of the Shevchenko Scientific Society. Source: Ukrainian Wikipedia — *Пулюй Іван Павлович*. Decolonized framing: Puliui's contribution to X-ray science predates and parallels Röntgen's Nobel work — erased from Soviet-era historiography because he was Ukrainian and worked in the West.

2. **Українська діаспора** — As of 2025, 15–20 million Ukrainians and their descendants live outside Ukraine — approximately one quarter of the entire Ukrainian people. The diaspora spans four waves of emigration (economic, political, refugee). Source: Ukrainian Wikipedia — *Українська діаспора*.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Фразеологічна калька**: "приймати участь" → **брати участь** — direct calque from Russian "принимать участие"; "приймати" is wrong in this context. Similarly: "відігравати роль" is correct; "грати роль" and "мати значення" are separate, not interchangeable.

2. **Паронімічна плутанина**: "ефектний" (spectacular, impressive visually; IPM 0.48) vs "ефективний" (effective, producing results; IPM 5.87). Learners conflate these because they look similar and both borrowed from Latin/French. Also: "громадський" (civic, relating to community/public — громадська активність; IPM 16.16) vs "громадянський" (civil, relating to citizenship — громадянське суспільство; IPM 1.71).

3. **Синтаксична калька з "так як"**: Learners write "так як" (from Russian "так как") instead of correct "оскільки" or "тому що". "Оскільки" has IPM 211.93 — extremely common in formal Ukrainian writing, making this a high-value correction.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Ivan Puliui framing**: Present him as a Ukrainian scientist who worked in the Austro-Hungarian Empire — NOT as a peripheral figure of European science. His X-ray tube work is independently significant. His Bible translation is a landmark of Ukrainian literary language standardization.
- **Diaspora section**: Use vocabulary like *ідентичність*, *збереження традицій*, *громадська активність*, *світове українство* — all register-appropriate for B2 academic style. Avoid Soviet-era terminology like "закордонні українці" as a euphemism that downplays diaspora agency.
- **Paronyme section**: The ефектний/ефективний pair is a genuine high-stakes error in professional writing (a "spectacular" report vs an "effective" one). Justify both with real collocations.
- **Checkpoint structure note**: This is an assessment module — the content outline mixes review material + integrative tasks. Each section should include a diagnostic component (self-check), not just new instruction.
- **Conjunction "завдяки тому, що"**: For the Puliui case study section — this is a causal conjunction expressing positive causation ("thanks to the fact that"), important to distinguish from "через те, що" (neutral/negative causation). Both are §4.3.4 territory.
