# Дослідження: Видові нюанси I: Вторинна імперфективація

## State Standard Reference

§4.2.3 (B2, lines 3348–3350): "Утворення видових пар дієслів: писати – написати, робити – зробити, темніти – потемніти, вивчати – вивчити, говорити – сказати, захищати – захистити, сполучати – сполучити."

Alignment: The Standard frames this as recognizing and forming aspectual pairs. This module extends that to the *secondary imperfective* layer — the derivation of a new НДВ from a prefixed ДВ — which is the logical next step beyond basic pair recognition. The State Standard lists only primary ДВ↔НДВ pairs; secondary imperfectivization is the B2 deepening of §4.2.3 where learners must handle three-member aspect triads (писати → написати → записувати) and understand the added iterative/process meaning the secondary НДВ introduces.

Also relevant: §4.1.3.1 (lines 3297–3317) shows the Standard's expectation for full imperfective conjugation at B2, including compound future of НДВ (висловлювати → висловлюватиму), which presupposes learner fluency with -ува-/-юва- forms.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **Василь Стус — поет-шістдесятник і палімпсест** (Wikipedia verified): Стус — Ukrainian poet, translator, dissident, political prisoner of the USSR, member of the Ukrainian Helsinki Group, Hero of Ukraine, laureate of the Shevchenko State Prize. His manuscripts were literally suppressed, confiscated, and "overwritten" by Soviet censorship — making him an authentic embodiment of the palimpsest metaphor. His collection *Палімпсести* (the title itself) uses the image of layered texts to express the survival of Ukrainian identity beneath Soviet erasure. This is an ideal cultural anchor: not decorative, but structurally meaningful for the lesson's central metaphor.

2. **Деколонізація топонімів** (Wikipedia: перейменування verified; перейменовувати frequency 865/0.43 IPM): Post-2022 Ukraine undertook large-scale renaming of streets, cities, and public spaces to remove Soviet and Russian colonial names. This produces exactly the aspectual contrast the module targets: **перейменували** (одиничний факт, ДВ) vs. **перейменовують** / **перейменовували** (тривалий суспільний процес, вторинний НДВ). Authentic, contemporary, politically meaningful — directly mirrors the grammar point.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Гіперкорекція -овува-**: виробляти → *виробовувати* instead of нормативного **виробляти** / **виробити → виробляти**; переписати → *переписовувати* instead of **переписувати**. Root cause: learners over-apply the -ува- suffix analogically from verbs like записувати, without noticing that переписувати already contains the suffix -ува- after consonant alternation. The module must provide explicit negative examples.

2. **Наголос-плутанина у мінімальних парах**: ви́різати (ДВ, perfective — "to cut out once") vs. виріза́ти (НДВ, imperfective — "to keep cutting"). Learners at B2 frequently neutralize stress, treating both as НДВ or vice versa. The error is acoustically invisible in casual speech but semantically crucial for aspect choice. Stress drills are mandatory for this section.

3. **Змішування первинного і вторинного НДВ**: писати (primary НДВ, base verb) vs. записувати (secondary НДВ, derived from записати). Learners sometimes use писати where записувати is required (e.g., in contexts of habitual specific-direction writing), or produce over-general statements where the secondary НДВ's iterative specificity is lost. The functional distinction — повторюваність конкретної дії vs. загальний процес — needs explicit attention.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Palimpsest metaphor must do grammatical work**: introduce it in section 1 not as decoration but as a functional model — ДВ = final inscribed text (результат), вторинний НДВ = the act of repeatedly inscribing/overwriting (ітеративний процес). Стус's *Палімпсести* gives this authentic literary grounding.
- **Three-member triads are the pedagogical spine**: писати (base НДВ) → написати (ДВ) → записувати (secondary НДВ). Section 2 should build the paradigm table around multiple triads.
- **Consonant alternation in -ува- derivation** is well-documented in Glazova (Grade 10): зарядити → заряджати, знизити → знижати/знижувати, погасити → [погашати]. These textbook examples may be cited or adapted.
- **дуративний**: replace with **тривалий** in all Ukrainian-language explanations; дуративний may appear once with parenthetical gloss.
- **Pereymenuvanna case study**: use authentic Kyiv/Lviv renaming examples for the decolonization section — streets renamed from Soviet figures to Ukrainian cultural ones. This grounds the grammar in lived Ukrainian reality.
- **Academic register collocations**: дослідження (122.77 IPM) and аналіз (33.94 IPM) are high-frequency; their secondary НДВ derivatives (досліджувати, аналізувати) should appear in academic-text transformation exercises.
- **Fill-in blanks**: per B2 spec, use `___` format (no brackets).
- **Word count allocation check**: plan sets 4000 words total across 5 sections (600+1000+700+1000+700=4000). The 1.5× overshoot principle means writing toward 5500–6000 words and trimming is preferred over writing to the minimum.
