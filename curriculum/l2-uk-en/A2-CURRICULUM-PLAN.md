# A2 Curriculum Plan: Ukrainian for English Speakers

## Prerequisites from A1

Before starting A2, learners have completed:
- Cyrillic alphabet (print + italic awareness)
- Noun gender system
- Three cases: Nominative, Accusative, Genitive, Locative
- Present, Past, and Future tense
- Modal verbs (можу, треба, хочу)
- Adjective agreement
- Possessive pronouns
- ~750 vocabulary words

---

## CEFR A2 Can-Do Alignment

### CEFR A2 Descriptor (Council of Europe)
> Can understand sentences and frequently used expressions related to areas of most immediate relevance (e.g. personal and family information, shopping, local geography, employment). Can communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring a simple and direct exchange of information on familiar and routine matters.

### Our A2 Can-Do Checklist
By module 65, learners can:

**Understanding (Listening/Reading)**
- [ ] Understand short, simple texts on familiar matters
- [ ] Find specific information in simple everyday material (ads, menus, timetables)
- [ ] Understand short personal letters
- [ ] Follow short, simple news items

**Speaking (Production/Interaction)**
- [ ] Describe education, present/past job, and personal interests
- [ ] Describe events, experiences, hopes, and ambitions
- [ ] Give reasons and explanations for opinions and plans
- [ ] Handle routine situations in shops, banks, post offices
- [ ] Make and respond to invitations, suggestions, apologies
- [ ] Express feelings and reactions

**Writing**
- [ ] Write short, simple notes and messages
- [ ] Describe experiences and events
- [ ] Write personal letters describing experiences and impressions

### Module Content Specifications A2.2

#### Module 41: Aspect - The Big Picture
**Grammar:** Understanding perfective vs imperfective

**Aspect basics:**
- Imperfective: ongoing, repeated, general action
- Perfective: completed, single, result-focused

**Imperfective uses:**
- Що ти робиш? (What are you doing? - now)
- Я читав книгу. (I was reading a book. - process)
- Вона завжди допомагає. (She always helps. - habitual)

**Perfective uses:**
- Я прочитав книгу. (I read the book. - finished)
- Він допоміг мені. (He helped me. - completed act)

**Vocabulary (30 words):**
робити/зробити, читати/прочитати, писати/написати, говорити/сказати, вчити/вивчити, готувати/приготувати, відкривати/відкрити, закривати/закрити, починати/почати, закінчувати/закінчити, забувати/забути, знаходити/знайти, губити/загубити, брати/взяти, давати/дати

**Activities:**
1. Match-up: Imperfective ↔ Perfective pairs
2. Quiz: Choose aspect for context
3. Gap-fill: Complete with correct aspect

#### Module 52: Because and Although
