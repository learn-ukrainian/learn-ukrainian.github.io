### Narrative Arc Analysis

*   **HOOK**: Strong. Starts with the "phenomenon" of Cucuteni-Trypillia and sets the chronological stakes (5500–2750 BCE).
*   **TENSION**: The conflict between the "primitive tribes" myth and the reality of 15,000-person cities.
*   **JOURNEY**: Logical flow from discovery (Khvoyka) -> urbanism -> economy -> spirituality -> decolonization.
*   **CLIMAX**: The "Geomagnetic Revolution" revealing the true scale of Nebelivka and Talyanky.
*   **RESOLUTION**: Reclaiming Trypillia as part of the Ukrainian "agrarian code" and mental decolonization.
*   **CALL TO ACTION**: Encouragement to visit museums and participate in "Toloka".

## Summary/Verification
The module provides a comprehensive and decolonized overview of the Trypillian civilization. It successfully weaves primary sources (Khvoyka) with modern archaeological findings (geomagnetic surveys).

## Scores/Assessment

### 1. Linguistic Accuracy & Naturalness
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: Native-level, academic yet accessible Ukrainian. No Russianisms.
*   **Quote**: «Ми не учні, що запізнилися на урок історії; ми були в класі, коли урок тільки починався.» — Powerful, natural metaphor.

### 2. Historiographical Depth & Primary Sources
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: Excellent use of Vikentiy Khvoyka's quotes. The transition from 19th-century archaeology to modern geomagnetic surveys is well-documented.
*   **Quote**: «Глина випалена до дзвінкості, а поверхня вкрита складним, ритмічним розписом» — precise citation.

### 3. Decolonization Perspective

### 4. Immersion & Complexity (B2)
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: 98.3% immersion is perfect for B2-HIST. Complexity is appropriate for "Seminar" track.

### 5. Humanity & Teacher Voice
*   **Score: 10/10**
*   **Justification**: The teacher's voice is passionate and direct («Ми маємо право пишатися...»).

## Issues/Critique

### 1. Vocabulary Density
*   **Issue**: The vocabulary YAML list contains 28 items. For a module of nearly 8000 words covering specialized topics like "geomagnetic survey", "slash-and-burn agriculture", and "archaeological strata", the list could be slightly more comprehensive to support the learner's independent reading.
*   **Quote**: «Для історика, який звик працювати з текстами, Трипільська цивілізація (Trypillian civilization) становить унікальний виклик...»

### 2. Activity Distribution
*   **Issue**: While the module is "Content-heavy", 5 activities for 8000 words means long stretches of reading without interaction (some sections are over 1000 words).
*   **Quote**: «Протоміста» section is 1048 words with no intermediate check.
*   **Suggested Fix**: Add a few "Think about it" questions or mini-tasks between the major sections to maintain focus.

## Recommendation/Verdict

**Verdict: PASS**
This is a flagship module for the B2-HIST track. It is academically rigorous, emotionally engaging, and politically vital for decolonizing Ukrainian history. The depth of content far outweighs the minor issues in activity distribution.
