## 2. Issues Found
Overall the module is quite strong, but there is a minor issue with the flow in the introduction. The sentence «Згодом ця спільнота еволюціонувала та перетворилася на цілком окремий соціальний стан, який сучасники шанобливо називали «лицарським людом» (knightly people).» could be improved. The inline English translation, while helpful for learners, disrupts the immersive flow of the B2 historical narrative slightly. A better approach might be to keep it strictly to the vocabulary table or explain the concept entirely in Ukrainian. 

Additionally, the text can feel overly dense at times, lacking sensory engagement. Adding more descriptions of the battlefield experience would help bring the history to life and make it more engaging for B2 learners.
