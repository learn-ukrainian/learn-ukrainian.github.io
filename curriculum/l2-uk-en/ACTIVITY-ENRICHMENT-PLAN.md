# CEFR-Compliant Activity Enrichment Plan

## Overview

This plan defines activity requirements for each CEFR level with progressive complexity in both activity types and sentence structures.

---

## Level Requirements Summary

| Level | Modules | Min Activities | Sentence Length | Clauses | Key Grammar Focus |
|-------|---------|----------------|-----------------|---------|-------------------|
| A1 | 1-30 | 6 | 3-6 words | 1 | Gender, Basic cases, Present tense |
| A2 | 31-60 | 8 | 6-10 words | 1-2 | All cases, Past/Future, Aspect intro |
| A2+ | 61-80 | 10 | 8-14 words | 2 | Aspect mastery, Subordinate clauses |
| B1 | 81-140 | 12 | 12-18 words | 2-3 | Conditionals, Participles, Indirect speech |
| B2 | 141-190 | 15 | 15-25 words | 3-4 | Literary style, Register, Advanced syntax |

## A1 Level (Modules 1-30) — Breakthrough

### CEFR A1 Can-Do Statements
- Can understand and use familiar everyday expressions
- Can introduce themselves and others
- Can ask and answer simple questions about personal details

### Required Activities: 6 per module

| # | Activity Type | Cognitive Level | Purpose |
|---|---------------|-----------------|---------|
| 1 | **fill-in** | Production | Complete sentences with correct form |
| 2 | **match-up** | Recognition | Connect Ukrainian ↔ English |
| 3 | **unjumble** | Synthesis | Build sentences from scrambled words |
| 4 | **quiz** | Comprehension | Test grammar rules |
| 5 | **true-false** | Recognition | Verify statements |
| 6 | **group-sort** | Analysis | Classify by category |

### A1 Sentence Complexity

**Structure:** Simple SVO (Subject-Verb-Object)
**Length:** 3-6 words
**Clauses:** 1 only
**Connectors:** None required

**Example Sentences by Module Type:**

**Modules 1-10 (Alphabet/Phonetics):**
```
Це буква А.
Я бачу слово.
Він читає текст.
```

**Modules 11-20 (Basic Grammar):**
```
Це моя книга.
Я бачу студента.
Вона в школі.
Немає часу.
```

**Modules 21-30 (Practical Vocabulary):**
```
Я люблю каву.
Мій брат вдома.
У мене є сестра.
Вона працює тут.
```

#### 5. quiz #1: Grammar Concepts (10 questions)
```markdown
## quiz: Case Usage

1. Which case follows the preposition "без"?
   - [ ] Accusative
   - [x] Genitive
   - [ ] Dative
   - [ ] Instrumental
   > "Без" always requires genitive: без часу, без грошей

2. What determines the choice between imperfective and perfective?
   - [ ] The subject of the sentence
   - [x] Whether the action is completed or ongoing
   - [ ] The time of day
   - [ ] The gender of the noun
   > Aspect shows completion (pf) vs. process/habit (impf)
```

#### 6. quiz #2: Applied Reasoning (10 questions)
```markdown
## quiz: Why This Form?

1. "Я бачу студентА" — why accusative = genitive here?
   - [x] Because "студент" is animate masculine
   - [ ] Because it's a direct object
   - [ ] Because of the verb "бачити"
   - [ ] Because it's singular
   > Animate masculine accusative always matches genitive

2. "Вона читаЛА книгу" — why -ла ending?
   - [ ] Because "книга" is feminine
   - [x] Because the subject "вона" is feminine
   - [ ] Because it's past tense
   - [ ] Because the action is completed
   > Past tense agrees with subject gender: він читав, вона читала
```

#### 7. true-false (10 items)
```markdown
## true-false: Aspect and Tense

- [x] Imperfective verbs can be used in all three tenses
  > Correct! читати: читаю, читав, буду читати

- [ ] Perfective verbs have a present tense form
  > Incorrect. Perfective present forms have future meaning: прочитаю = I will read

- [x] "Він писав лист" means the action was in progress
  > Correct! Imperfective past shows ongoing action

- [ ] "Я буду читати" and "Я прочитаю" mean the same thing
  > Incorrect. "буду читати" = process, "прочитаю" = completion
```

   > [!answer] студента

   > [!answer] книги

   > [!answer] Я читав/читала книгу.

#### 15. error-correction (subtle errors, 10 items)
```markdown
## error-correction: Subtle Style Errors

> Find the stylistic/grammatical error and correct it.

1. "Внаслідок того, що він не прийшов, ми пішли без нього."
   > [!answer] Оскільки / Через те, що він не прийшов, ми пішли без нього.
   > Error: "Внаслідок" implies consequence, not cause; use "оскільки" or "через те, що"

2. "Письменник написав дуже гарний і красивий роман."
   > [!answer] Письменник написав чудовий роман.
   > Error: Pleonasm — "гарний" and "красивий" are synonymous; remove redundancy

3. "Згідно результатів дослідження, існує зв'язок..."
   > [!answer] Згідно З результатами дослідження...
   > Error: "Згідно" requires "з" + instrumental, not genitive

4. "Він є талановитим письменником."
   > [!answer] Він талановитий письменник. / Він — талановитий письменник.
   > Error: "є" as copula is a Russicism in such constructions; omit or use dash

5. "Цей текст являється прикладом постмодернізму."
   > [!answer] Цей текст є прикладом постмодернізму.
   > Error: "являтися" is bureaucratic/Russian influence; use "бути" or "становити"
```
