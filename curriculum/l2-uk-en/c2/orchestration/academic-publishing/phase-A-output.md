# Дослідження: Академічне публікування українською мовою

## State Standard Reference
§4.3.8: "Уміння утворювати вторинні тексти, зокрема реферати, повідомлення, довідково-інформаційні тексти."
Alignment: This module directly addresses the C2 State Standard requirement to produce advanced secondary texts (вторинні тексти) by teaching learners how to write structured abstracts (анотації), extended summaries (реферати), peer reviews (рецензії), and conference theses (тези доповіді), applying flawless academic register.

## Cultural Hooks
1. **Decolonization of Academic Standards**: The adoption of ДСТУ 8302:2015 marked a significant step in standardizing Ukrainian bibliographic references, consciously distancing them from older Soviet GOST standards and establishing an independent national academic identity.
2. **The Era of Academic Integrity**: Post-2014 educational reforms placed "академічна доброчесність" (academic integrity) at the forefront of Ukrainian academia, leading to the mandatory integration of anti-plagiarism tools (like Unicheck and StrikePlagiarism) in national universities and academic journals.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Confusing «анотація» and «реферат»** → Translating "abstract" to either interchangeably. *Correct form*: «Анотація» is a brief 150-300 word overview for an article, while «реферат» is an extended summary or review paper.
2. **Calquing Russian passive constructions** → Using phrases like "в даній статті розглядається". *Correct form*: Use active voice or natural Ukrainian impersonal constructions: «у цій статті проаналізовано», «автор досліджує», «у роботі висвітлено».
3. **Direct translation of English abstracts** → Failing to adapt to Ukrainian stylistic norms, which often require specific introductory clichés and a more formal, detached academic register than their English counterparts.

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing**: Present the Ukrainian academic publishing ecosystem as self-sufficient, resilient, and actively integrating with global databases (Scopus/WoS), driven by the agency of Ukrainian scholars. Do not use Russian systems as a point of comparison.
- **Immersion**: Strict 100% Ukrainian immersion. English is strictly limited to the "translation" column in the vocabulary YAML file.
- **Tone**: The persona is a Senior Specialist/Research Mentor. Maintain a highly professional, academic tone utilizing modal hedging («ймовірно», «можна стверджувати»).
- **Practicality**: Provide highly specific, concrete examples of ДСТУ 8302:2015 formatting and review templates to ensure the content serves as a functional reference for C2 learners.
