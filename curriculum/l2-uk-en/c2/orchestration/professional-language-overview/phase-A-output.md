## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Golden Age" of Terminology (1920s)**: In the 1920s, the Institute of Ukrainian Scientific Language created dozens of dictionaries for specific fields (from mechanics to botany). This flourishing was brutally repressed in the 1930s ("terminological sabotage"), and modern Ukrainian professional language is often a restoration of that lost organic development.
2. **National Commission on State Language Standards**: The contemporary body responsible for the codification of professional standards, reflecting the state's active role in language policy today.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Prepositional Calques**: "по питаннях економіки" (Russian influence) → **з питань економіки** (Normative).
2. **Lexical Calques**: "приймати участь" (calque) → **брати участь**; "приймати міри" → **вживати заходів**.
3. **Register Mixing**: Using diminutives or slang in formal reports (e.g., "договірчик", "передзвони") instead of neutral professional vocabulary.
