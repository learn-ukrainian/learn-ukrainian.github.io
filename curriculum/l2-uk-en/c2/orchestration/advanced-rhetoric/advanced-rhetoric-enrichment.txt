## Cultural Hooks

2. **Literary Oratory**: Ivan Franko's public speeches and characters' monologues (e.g., in "Zakhar Berkut") demonstrate the power of rhythmic prose and rhetorical questions in nation-building.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Redundancy vs. Repetition**: Learners often confuse *pleonasm* (accidental redundancy: "пам'ятний сувенір") with *rhetorical repetition* (intentional emphasis: "слова, слова, слова").
   *Correction*: Pleonasm weakens; rhetorical repetition strengthens.
2. **Passive Voice Overuse**: Attempting to sound "formal" by using passive constructions ("Було вирішено нами..."), which kills persuasive power.
   *Correction*: High rhetoric in Ukrainian prefers strong active verbs ("Ми вирішили...").
3. **Calquing English Structures**: Using English-style cleft sentences ("It is he who...") instead of Ukrainian word order inversion for emphasis.
   *Correction*: Use word order: "Саме він..." or place the emphasized element at the end of the sentence.
