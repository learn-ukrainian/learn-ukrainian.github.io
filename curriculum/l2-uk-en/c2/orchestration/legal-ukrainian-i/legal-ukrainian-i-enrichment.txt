## Cultural Hooks
1. **Constitution of Pylyp Orlyk (1710)**: Often cited as one of the world's first democratic constitutions, establishing a separation of powers long before Montesquieu. A point of national pride in legal history.
2. **Ruska Pravda**: The ancient legal code of Kyivan Rus under Yaroslav the Wise, laying the foundation for Slavic legal tradition.
3. **The Constitutional Court's Role**: The modern significance of the Constitutional Court (Конституційний Суд України) as the sole body of constitutional jurisdiction, often at the center of political checks and balances.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Prepositional Governance**: Using *згідно* with Genitive instead of Instrumental (*згідно закону* ❌ → *згідно із законом* ✅).
2. **Lexical Confusion**: Confusing *закон* (a specific statute) with *право* (law as a system or a right). "Study law" is *вивчати право*, not *вивчати закон*.
3. **False Friends/Anglicisms**: Using *адвокат* for all lawyers (it specifically means a litigator/bar member), whereas *юрист* is the general term.
4. **Bureaucratic Clutter**: Overusing passive voice constructions (*було вирішено*) where active official style prefers impersonal or direct forms (*суд ухвалив*).
