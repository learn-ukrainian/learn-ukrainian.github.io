## Cultural Hooks
1. **The 2019 Orthography Reform (Новий правопис)**: The reintroduction of "deleted" norms from the 1928 Kharkiv Orthography (e.g., *индик*, *ирій*, feminine names like *імені*, *радості* ending in -и in Genitive). This is the ultimate test of C2 awareness — knowing *why* these forms exist and how they connect to the Executed Renaissance.
2. **Borys Antonenko-Davydovych ("Як ми говоримо")**: His classic work on "cleansing" the language from bureaucratic calques is a cornerstone for C2 stylistic polish.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Passive Voice Overuse**: Using «Питання було вирішене...» instead of active «Ми вирішили питання...» (Bureaucratic/Soviet legacy style vs. Ukrainian Active Agency).
2. **Preposition "По" Abuse**: Using «по справам», «по вихідним» instead of «у справах», «виҳідними».
3. **Mismatched Aspect/Tense**: Failing to use the Pluperfect (давноминулий час) «був зробив» in complex narratives where it adds temporal clarity and stylistic flavor.
