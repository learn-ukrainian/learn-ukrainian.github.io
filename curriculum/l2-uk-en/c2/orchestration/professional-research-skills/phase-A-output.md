## Cultural Hooks
1. **Vernadsky National Library (NBUV)**: The largest library in Ukraine and one of the largest in the world, serving as the main information center for researchers. Mention its massive card catalogs and digital archives (irbis-nbuv.gov.ua).
2. **Academic Integrity Movement**: Since 2014, Ukrainian universities have aggressively adopted Western standards of "академічна доброчесність" to combat plagiarism, moving away from Soviet-style formalism.
3. **Scopus/Web of Science requirements**: Modern Ukrainian PhD requirements mandate publications in these international databases, making English-language abstract writing and international citation standards critical skills for Ukrainian scholars.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **"Зробити дослідження" vs "Провести дослідження"**: Learners often translate "do research" literally. The correct collocation is *проводити/провести дослідження*.
2. **"Вірне джерело" vs "Надійне джерело"**: *Вірний* primarily means "loyal" (like a friend). For "correct/reliable source", use *правильне* or *надійне/достовірне джерело*.
3. **"Цитата" vs "Цитування"**: *Цитата* is the quote itself; *цитування* is the process or system of citation. "Правила цитування" (not "правила цитати").

- Emphasize the **DSTU 8302:2015** standard for bibliography, as it differs from APA/MLA but is mandatory for Ukrainian thesis defense.
