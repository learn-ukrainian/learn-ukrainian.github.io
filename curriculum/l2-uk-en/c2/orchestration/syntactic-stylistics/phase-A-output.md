## Cultural Hooks
1. **Marusia Churai & Folk Songs**: Syntactic parallelism is the heartbeat of Ukrainian folk poetry. Example: «Як ми кохалися, як зерно в горісі, / Тепер розійшлися, як туман по лісі!» (Psychological parallelism).
2. **Impressionism & Parceling**: Mykhailo Kotsiubynsky ("Intermezzo") and Vasyl Stefanyk are masters of parceling (chopped syntax) to convey psychological stress or dynamic action. Example: «А повз неї тупали тисячі ніг... Великі, середні й маленькі...»
3. **Shevchenko's Gradation**: Taras Shevchenko used ascending gradation masterfully to build emotional pressure: «І в'яне, сохне, гине, гине твоя єдиная дитина.»

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Overuse of direct word order (SVO)**: Learners often stick to rigid English-like SVO structure, missing the nuance of Ukrainian "Theme-Rheme" inversion for logical stress.
2. **Choppy Syntax (Lack of flow)**: Avoiding complex sentences results in "telegraphic style". Correction: Use subordinate clauses and participial constructions to create rhythmic "periods".
3. **Misuse of Parceling**: Breaking sentences randomly instead of logically isolating the rheme (new information) for emphasis.
