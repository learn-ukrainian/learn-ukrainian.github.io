## Cultural Hooks
1. **Peresopnytsia Gospel (1556-1561):** The first translation of the Gospel into Old Ukrainian language. It is a national symbol upon which Presidents of Ukraine swear their oath of office.
2. **"Prayer for Ukraine" (Боже великий, єдиний):** Spiritual anthem of Ukraine (music by Mykola Lysenko, lyrics by Oleksandr Konysky). Perfect example of high religious style in national context.
3. **Ivan Ohienko (Metropolitan Ilarion):** His 1962 translation of the Bible is considered the standard for literary Ukrainian, balancing exactness with the aesthetics of the Ukrainian language.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Vocative Omission:** Praying "Ісус, допоможи" instead of "Ісусе, допоможи". In Ukrainian religious discourse, the Vocative case is mandatory and strictly enforced.
2. **Russisms in Terminology:** Using *батюшка* (Russian style) instead of *отець* or *священник*.
3. **Calques:** "Спаси і сохрани" (Russian *Спаси и сохрани*) instead of the Ukrainian canonical "Спаси і помилуй".
4. **Capitalization:** Confusion between *Церква* (as the Body of Christ/Institution - capitalized) and *церква* (building - lowercase in general contexts, though variable by tradition).
