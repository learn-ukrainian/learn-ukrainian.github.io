## Cultural Hooks
1. **Normocontrol (Нормоконтроль)**: In Ukrainian universities, there is often a specific official or department ("нормоконтролер") dedicated solely to checking if the thesis meets formal DSTU standards (margins, fonts, spacing). It is a distinct gatekeeping stage before the actual defense.
2. **The "Binder" (Папка/Тверда палітурка)**: The physical presentation of the thesis (hardcover binding with gold lettering) is a ritualistic part of the process, often done at specialized print shops near universities during "defense season".

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Confusion of Editing Levels**: Learners often mix up `редагування` (improving flow/logic) with `коректура` (fixing typos/grammar). *Correction*: Finish editing before starting proofreading to avoid wasted effort.
2. **Anglicisms in Syntax**: Overusing passive voice constructions typical of English academic writing (e.g., "було зроблено аналіз") instead of the preferred Ukrainian active "personal" constructions (e.g., "ми проаналізували", "автор дослідив").
3. **Bibliography Formatting**: Mixing citation styles (APA, MLA) with the specific Ukrainian DSTU 8302:2015 standard, which has unique punctuation rules (e.g., usage of `//` for journals).
