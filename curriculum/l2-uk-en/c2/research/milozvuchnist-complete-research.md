## Cultural Hooks

**1. Рильський's "Мова" — canonical anchor for the солов'їна мова myth**
Максим Тадейович Рильський (1895–1964) — академік АН УРСР (1943) та АН СРСР (1958), поет-неокласик, перекладач, мовознавець. His poem "Мова" ("Як парость виноградної лози...") is the most cited literary text in Ukrainian language pedagogy when teaching milozwuchnist'. It frames the language not as a natural phenomenon but as a *cultural garden* requiring active cultivation — "виполювання бур'янів" (weeding) becomes the metaphor for conscious language care. This directly supports the C2 framing that euphony is a *skill*, not a birthright.
*(Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Рильський_Максим_Тадейович)*

**2. Солов'їна мова — myth anatomy**
The phrase "солов'їна мова" (nightingale language) is a culturally loaded metaphor for Ukrainian's perceived musicality. Wikipedia defines евфонія as «привабливість і виразність звуків і звукосполучень мови або мовлення» — a definition rooted in perception and culture, not objective acoustic measurement. The "world's most musical language" claim circulates widely online but lacks verifiable scientific basis. The decolonized C2 framing is: Ukrainian's phonological architecture (law of the open syllable, vowel-consonant alternations, absence of consonant clustering pressure) creates *conditions* for euphony, but euphony is realized through conscious choice, not automatically. This is the productive tension for the Вступ section.
*(Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Евфонія)*

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Зіяння (hiatus) — double vowel collision**
*Error*: «Вона уміє» / «він і інші» → *Correct*: «Вона вміє» / «він й інші»
*Why*: Learners apply the basic rule (у after consonant, в after vowel) but miss that the *following* word's initial sound also triggers alternation. The rule is bidirectional — both the preceding and following phonetic environment govern choice.

2. **З/зі/із — consonant cluster failure before шиплячі**
*Error*: «З школою», «з шумом» → *Correct*: «Зі школою», «із шумом»
*Why*: Learners memorize the basic з/із rule but fail to apply it before complex initial clusters (шк-, зн-, ств- etc.). The шиплячі (ш, ж, ч, щ) trigger is particularly high-error. Grade 5 textbooks note this explicitly (Авраменко).

3. **Frozen у/в in fixed expressions**
*Error*: Learners use learned collocations with fixed у/в and fail to apply alternation when syntactic context changes — e.g., always writing «у місті» even when preceded by a vowel: «жила у місті» vs the correct «жила в місті».
*Why*: Chunk-learning overrides the positional rule. At C2, the correction requires not just rule knowledge but phonological automaticity.

**Bonus error (C2-specific):** Overcorrection — hypercorrect application of euphony rules in poetic contexts where the author *deliberately* violates them for rhythmic/rhyme effect (Glazova Grade 10 explicitly warns: «У поетичному мовленні... порушення правил чергування у–в, і–й цілком припустиме»). C2 learners must distinguish normative application from authorial license.

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **благозвучність is real but VESUM-absent** — Use it as a literary register term (Рильський, Пономарів usage) with a brief gloss; do not list in the vocabulary YAML without flagging it as a stylistic variant rather than a dictionary form. Consider using the VESUM-verified **милозвучність** as the primary term.

2. **The "солов'їна мова" myth needs careful framing**: The Вступ section should *engage* the myth honestly — it's culturally significant and emotionally resonant — but the C2 pedagogical move is to analyze its phonological *basis* rather than either uncritically celebrating or dismissively debunking it.

3. **Градація складності across sections**: The textbook evidence shows у/в and і/й are introduced at Grade 5–6 (A1–A2 equivalents) and reviewed through Grade 10. C2 content must operate strictly at the *стилістичний* and *авторський вибір* level — mechanical rules must be assumed internalized.

4. **Poetic violation as C2 competency**: Glazova Grade 10's note about допустимі відступи in poetry is the pedagogical seed for Section 4 (свідоме порушення). Content writer should develop this into a full analytical framework.

5. **Зіяння is a terminus technicus** (8 GRAC hits) — introduce it formally with definition before using it analytically.

6. **Пономарів О. "Культура слова"** (listed as primary source) is the canonical reference. The r2u.org.ua link in the plan is the R2U corpus portal — the actual Пономарів text may need to be sourced from a textbook reference or direct citation rather than live URL lookup.
