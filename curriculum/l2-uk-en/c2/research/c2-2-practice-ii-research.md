**§4.3.6** (line 5733): *"Уміння створити текст певного стилю за планом."*
Alignment: This module directly operationalizes §4.3.6 — learners practice portfolio-as-plan execution across four functional styles (художній, публіцистичний through essays, розмовний through dialogue subtext, науковий through literary analysis). The portfolio frame integrates §4.3.1.1 (all 7 styles), §4.3.3 (lexical stylistics: канцеляризми, metaphor, epithet, periphrasis, neologisms), and §4.3.4 (syntactic stylistics).

- **§4.3.3** (lines 5682–5712): Lists канцеляризми (*згідно з…, відповідно до…, у зв'язку з…*) as a lexical stylistic category — directly supports the plan's point on eliminating *канцеляризми* from художній style. Lists авторські неологізми (*злотоцінно, синьожовтеня*) — supports poetry section.
- **§4.3.7** (lines 5735–5736): Style transformation — relevant to the essay section's work on adapting voice across registers.

## Cultural Hooks

1. **«Коронація слова»** (founded 1999 by Тетяна та Юрій Логуші): Ukraine's leading international literary competition for novels, plays, screenplays, song lyrics, and children's literature. Single explicit language requirement: works must be written in Ukrainian. Has launched 250+ authors, including 145 first-time novelists who collectively published 432 novels. Juried by major cultural figures (Джамала, Іван Дзюба, Тамара Гундорова). Directly usable as the "professional benchmark" frame for the portfolio section. (Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Коронація_слова)

2. **Оксана Забужко** — письменниця, феміністська літературознавиця, поетеса, есеїстка, публіцистка. Ключові теми: українська національна ідентичність і гендер. Her essays model the *суб'єктивний авторський голос* that §Есеїстика section targets — she is an exemplar of Ukrainian essayistic subjectivity independent of any external (Western or Russian) framing. (Source: uk.wikipedia.org)

3. **Сергій Жадан** — поет, прозаїк, есеїст, перекладач, фронтмен гуртів «Жадан і Собаки» та «Лінія Маннергейма». Author of *Депеш Мод*, *Ворошиловград*, *Інтернат*, *Список кораблів*. Since March 2024: military service, бригада «Хартія» Національної гвардії. His essays demonstrate the synthesis of political engagement and literary voice referenced in the plan. Paired with Забужко = two models of distinctly Ukrainian essayistic subjectivity. (Source: uk.wikipedia.org)

## Common Learner Errors

1. **Канцеляризм infiltration into художній style** — *здійснити написання* (calque of bureaucratic verb + verbal noun) → correct: *написати*; *провести аналіз* → *проаналізувати*. Happens because learners default to formal register when writing "seriously." The plan explicitly targets this: content phase should provide a short contrastive table (канцеляризм → жива мова).

2. **Focalizer drift in prose** — mixing narrator's omniscient voice with character's internal perspective within the same paragraph, producing register confusion. Manifests as: omniscient framing sentence (*Микола знав, що…*) followed by character thought in third person but with authorial vocabulary. Correct practice: commit to one focalizer per scene unit; flag shifts with clear markers.

3. **"Show don't tell" calqued from English** — learners translate the Anglo-American creative writing maxim literally, producing an alien metalanguage. The Ukrainian tradition foregrounds *зоровий образ* (visual/sensory image) and *художня деталь* as native concepts. Content phase must reframe the principle using Ukrainian-native terminology, not the English loan phrase.

4. **Intertextuality as decoration** — learners insert quotations/allusions without integrating them into argument or emotional logic, producing a "quotation collage" rather than genuine *інтертекстуальний діалог*. Model correct integration from Забужко's essay practice.

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **Станіславський феномен** (Андрухович, Іздрик) cited in plan for the poetry cycle section — this refers to the Ivano-Frankivsk literary scene that emerged in the late 1980s–1990s, centered around Bu-Ba-Bu (Андрухович, Забужко-era positioning) and Meridian Czernowitz aesthetics. Content phase should verify specific names/context before presenting as factual — the plan's framing is accurate as a cultural touchstone but should not overstate a formal "school."

2. **Хронотоп** (Bakhtin term, freq. 0.23 IPM): Use in the prose section to frame narrative time-space architecture. It entered Ukrainian literary criticism organically via Бахтін — no Soviet/Russian reframing needed; it is a standard international term.

3. **§4.3.6 wording** ("за планом") maps directly to portfolio architecture: the plan IS the artistic concept. Content phase can use this as a pedagogical reframe — *творче портфоліо як реалізація власного плану*.

4. **Деколонізований фрейм**: Ukrainian literary essay tradition (Забужко, Жадан) should be presented as a self-sufficient tradition, not as derivative of Russian essayistic culture (Rozanov, etc.). The plan's example list already achieves this correctly.

5. **Канцеляризми list from §4.3.3** (*згідно з…, відповідно до…, у зв'язку з…*) — the Standard itself names these as marked lexical items. Use this as the authoritative source when presenting the канцеляризм → художній мова corrective table.

6. **Activity type compliance**: Plan specifies essay-response, authorial-intent, critical-analysis (min 6 items each, total 30 min). These are all legitimate C2-level activity types per the level constraint doc. No fill-in, match-up, or error-correction at C2.
