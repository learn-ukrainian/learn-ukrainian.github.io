## Cultural Hooks

**1. Микола Амосов (1913–2002) — Ukraine's cardiac surgery pioneer**
Ukrainian cardiac surgeon at Kyiv Institute of Cardiovascular Surgery. Developed Ukraine's first artificial heart valve prosthesis. Known beyond medicine for his "система Амосова" — a philosophy of preventive health through physical exercise and caloric discipline, which he practiced to age 89. Textbook Grade 9 (Voron, tier 2) references him explicitly as a model of Ukrainian medical achievement. Strong decolonized framing: Amosov represents Ukrainian-born world-class medicine developed in Kyiv, not in Moscow.

**2. Реформа охорони здоров'я 2017–2020 — Ukraine's Semashko → НСЗУ transition**
Ukraine dismantled the Soviet Semashko model (state-owned vertical hierarchy, no individual insurance) and replaced it with НСЗУ as a single national payer (Wikipedia confirmed). НСЗУ "укладає договори із закладами охорони здоров'я та закуповує у них послуги з медичного обслуговування населення." The eHealth system (електронна медична картка) was introduced alongside. This reform is historically significant: it broke from Soviet institutional legacy that had been unreformed since 1917. Strong relevance to C2.4 thematic discourse module.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **"больниця" → "лікарня"** — Direct calque from Russian "больница." Ukrainian is "лікарня" (лікувати + -ня). "Больниця" is a Russianism; some speakers use it colloquially in eastern dialects but it is non-standard. Same pattern: "лечити" → "лікувати," "лечення" → "лікування."

2. **Aspect selection in medical contexts** — Learners default to imperfective when describing completed procedures: ❌ "лікар оглядав пацієнта і знайшов пухлину" (parallel imperfectives imply repeated, not completed action) → ✓ "лікар оглянув пацієнта і виявив пухлину." Medical reports use perfective for documented outcomes. Ongoing treatment protocols use imperfective correctly.

3. **"укол" as a Russianism** — "Укол" (рос. укол = ін'єкція) is widely used in Ukrainian colloquial speech but the standard medical term is "ін'єкція" or "введення препарату." Similarly, "рецепт" is correct but "виписка рецепту" (via рос. "выписка") should be "виписування рецепту" or "рецептурний бланк." At C2, learners should control register: colloquial "укол" acceptable in informal contexts, "ін'єкція" required in medical documentation register.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Амосов as structural anchor:** Open the intro section with Amosov's aphorism about preventive health to establish Ukrainian medical identity before engaging with institutional/policy content. Frames healthcare as a Ukrainian intellectual tradition, not a Soviet legacy.
- **НСЗУ framing:** The reform narrative should foreground Ukrainian agency (2017 decision by Верховна Рада, reform designed by команда МОЗ under Уляна Супрун) — not frame it as "post-Soviet transition." Ukrainian policymakers made an active choice to adopt a European insurance model.
- **Біоетика low frequency (0.03/M):** This is genuine specialist vocabulary. Content should introduce it explicitly as such — "термін, що належить до фахового медичного та філософського дискурсу" — and provide sufficient context so learners understand its register level.
- **Folk medicine decolonized framing:** Ukrainian знахарство has pre-Christian ethnographic depth (Поліський регіон particularly well-documented). Frame it as a distinct cultural tradition with its own epistemology, not as primitive precursor to "real" (implicitly Western/European) medicine.
- **Wartime medicine dimension:** The plan's mention of "wartime medicine and resource scarcity" under деонтологія is appropriate and important. Since 2022, Ukrainian medical ethics discourse has been substantially shaped by battlefield triage, trauma surgery volumes, and цивільно-військова медицина. This should be treated with appropriate gravity — not exploited rhetorically, but acknowledged as real contemporary context for Ukrainian медична деонтологія.
- **Register differentiation table:** The intro section should include a concrete register comparison: the same concept (e.g., addiction) rendered in науковий (залежність від психоактивних речовин), офіційно-діловий (особи з розладами вживання психоактивних речовин), публіцистичний (наркомани, люди, що борються із залежністю), and побутовий registers — shows C2-level register command.
- **Word budget note:** Plan allocates 600 words to intro — this should be expanded given the density of register and framing content needed at C2. Phase B writer should aim for 700+ on the intro section and compress slightly elsewhere to remain within total target.
