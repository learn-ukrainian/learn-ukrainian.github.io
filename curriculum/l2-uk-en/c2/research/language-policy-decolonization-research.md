## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Target on Linguists**: During the Executed Renaissance (Розстріляне відродження), Soviet authorities did not just target writers; they specifically repressed linguists and lexicographers (like Hryhorii Holoskevych) to artificially alter Ukrainian grammar and vocabulary, forcing it closer to Russian (зближення мов).
2. **The 2019 Language Law**: The "Закон про забезпечення функціонування української мови як державної" is a landmark decolonial milestone. Unlike historical imperial decrees designed to suppress, it acts as a protective framework to secure the linguistic rights of Ukrainian speakers within their own sovereign state.

## Common Learner Errors
1. "згідно закону" → "згідно із законом" / "відповідно до закону" — A Russian syntactic calque commonly used by learners attempting the official-business register.
2. "приймати міри" → "вживати заходів" — A direct lexical calque often mistakenly used in policy and administrative discussions.
3. Naturalization of Linguicide — Failing to recognize the systemic, violent enforcement of Russification, instead mistakenly framing historical linguistic shifts as a "natural societal preference" or "organic evolution."

## Notes for Content Writing
- **Decolonized Framing**: Never use Russian as a comparative baseline. Frame Ukrainian linguistic resilience positively. Address "суржик" not merely as a corruption, but as a direct colonial trace left by unequal historical prestige.
- **Agency**: Ensure Ukrainians are portrayed as active defenders, planners, and revitalizers of their language (мовна мобілізація), rather than passive victims.
- **Register**: Maintain a highly formal academic and legal-administrative register (C2 level) throughout the module.
