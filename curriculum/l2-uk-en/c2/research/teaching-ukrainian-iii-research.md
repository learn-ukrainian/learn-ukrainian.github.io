## Cultural Hooks

**1. Борис Грінченко (1863–1910) — pedagogue, lexicographer, pioneer of Ukrainian-language education**
Source: Ukrainian Wikipedia, article «Грінченко Борис Дмитрович»

Грінченко was the author of *Рідне слово* — a pioneering Ukrainian-language reading textbook — and compiler of the four-volume *Словар української мови* (1907–1909). His core pedagogical principle: balance literary norm with living folk speech (*жива народна мова*). This is directly applicable to the module's discussion of avoiding "sterilization" — Грінченко preserved idioms, proverbs, and folk expressions as pedagogically valuable, not obstacles to standardize away. He campaigned for Ukrainian as the medium of school instruction at a time when Russian Imperial policy actively suppressed it — making his materials-development work simultaneously pedagogical and decolonial resistance.

**2. Василь Сухомлинський (1918–1970) — «Школа під голубим небом»**
Source: Ukrainian Wikipedia, article «Сухомлинський Василь Олександрович»

Distinguished Ukrainian pedagogue from Павлиш (Кіровоградська обл.). Сухомлинський's «Школа під голубим небом» (*Pavlysh School*) was a methodology of emotional engagement: lessons in nature, student-authored short texts, materials built from learners' lived experience. He wrote over 40 books and 600 articles on pedagogy. His approach — using the learner's emotional reality as the raw material for content — directly informs Section 4 of this module on cultural context and authorial vision.

## Common Learner Errors

**1. "Стерилізація тексту" — over-simplification that kills cultural content**
→ Correct approach: preserve cultural markers, idioms, and referential anchors; use glossaries and linhvokrainoznavchi notes to support comprehension instead of deleting complexity
→ Why it happens: learners (and beginning teachers) conflate *accessibility* with *cultural neutrality*; they remove the very elements that make a text engaging and authentic

**2. Instruction–task register mismatch (помилка невідповідності інструкції)**
→ Correct: instruction language must be at or below the target level of the task
→ Why it happens: writers default to their own competence register when writing instructions, unconsciously raising complexity; а B1-level fill-in-the-blank exercise should not have C1-level instructions

**3. Вторинний текст treated as generic "short version"**
→ Correct: реферат, анотація, повідомлення, конспект are distinct genres with different structures, audiences, and purposes (per Grade 7 Litvinova textbook: «Вторинні тексти створені на базі первинного: у них передано основний зміст і авторський задум, але не дослівно. Різновидами вторинного тексту є план, реферат, конспект, переказ»)
→ Why it happens: learners and teachers treat all compression tasks as equivalent; failure to distinguish genre leads to badly structured products

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **Грінченко as the decolonial anchor:** Frame his work as Ukrainian-language education under imperial suppression — not just historical curiosity but the founding act of the Ukrainian pedagogical tradition. His insistence on *жива мова* in teaching materials is the original answer to "стерилізація."

2. **Сухомлинський: use carefully.** He was a Soviet-era figure who navigated censorship. Frame his *pedagogical methodology* (emotional engagement, learner-authored content) positively without rehabilitating the Soviet institutional context. His Павлиш school is a legitimate Ukrainian pedagogical landmark.

3. **«Вторинний текст» is a textbook-confirmed Ukrainian term** (Grade 7 Litvinova 2024, Grade 6 Golub 2023). Use it confidently; no need to hedge.

4. **Style transformation glossary** (from textbooks, for use in examples):
   - Науковий → науково-популярний (most common pair in §4.3.7 per State Standard)
   - Офіційний → публіцистичний
   - Художній → науковий (analysis/critique direction)

5. **AI ethics section (Section 5):** This is timely and under-addressed in Ukrainian pedagogy. Frame it positively — AI as a tool that accelerates drafting, with the методист-редактор as the irreplaceable quality gate. Emphasize that AI-generated exercises require human verification for grammatical correctness, cultural appropriateness, and level accuracy.

6. **Avoid Soviet-era pedagogical frameworks** (e.g., "didactics" as a Soviet discipline name) — use сучасна методика, методологія, педагогічна майстерність as framing terms.

7. **«Пілотування» vs. «апробація»:** Both correct; *апробація* is more common in official Ukrainian Ministry documents and should appear as synonym/collocate.
