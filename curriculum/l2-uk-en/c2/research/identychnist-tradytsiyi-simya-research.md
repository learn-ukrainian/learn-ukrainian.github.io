## Cultural Hooks

1. **Писанка as living ritual object** (Wikipedia verified): *Пи́санка — яйце, декороване традиційними символами, які пишуться за допомогою воску й барвників. Писанки робили з сирого яйця і використовували для ритуального дарування, тоді як крашанки — з варених яєць для ігор та їжі.* The distinction писанка/крашанка anchors a C2 discussion of authentic ritual vs. commodified folklore. Ukrainian писанкарство predates Christianization, demonstrating the syncretic pagan-Christian layering that underlies most calendar traditions. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Писанка

2. **Олекса Воропай as primary ethnographic anchor** (Wikipedia verified): Voropai (1913–1989) — Ukrainian ethnographer, folklorist, biologist, memoirist in emigration. Doctor of Slavic Ethnology (Univ. of Munich, 1958). His *Звичаї нашого народу: Етнографічний нарис* (1958–1966) was written in the diaspora precisely because Soviet ethnography systematically distorted or suppressed Ukrainian folk practices by assimilating them into a "common Soviet" framework. This political context is essential for C2 analytical framing: reading Voropai today requires acknowledging (a) his diaspora perspective, (b) the Soviet-era lacunae he was filling, and (c) what contemporary Ukrainian ethnography has updated. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Воропай_Олекса_Іванович

## Common Learner Errors

1. **іменини ≠ день народження** → Correct distinction: *іменини* = name day (feast of one's patron saint, tied to the church calendar); *день народження* = birthday (date of birth). English has no name-day tradition, so learners conflate these. Error pattern: *"Коли твої іменини?" used as "When is your birthday?"* C2 nuance: in contemporary Ukraine, іменини are observed primarily in religious contexts; secular usage is declining among younger generations — itself a cultural data point worth analyzing.

2. **Синтаксис висловлення співчуття** → Correct form: *Щиро співчуваю Вашій втраті* (dative). Learners frequently produce *\*Я співчуваю Вашу втрату* (accusative) by analogy with English "I feel your loss." The dative construction is idiomatic and non-negotiable at C2; the formal/informal register split (Вашій vs. твоїй) must also be explicit.

3. **Ментальність** — false friend + essentialist trap → The word exists in Ukrainian (✅ VESUM), but C2 learners (and even native speakers in unreflective use) often deploy it essentializingly: *"Українська ментальність — це X"*, which collapses regional, generational, class, and historical variation. C2 analytical register requires the learner to frame any appeal to ментальність critically — *"поняття «менталітет» та його критика"* is correctly flagged in the plan. Ensure content models this meta-awareness explicitly.

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **Voropai framing is pedagogically rich**: Phase B should build the "Практикум" section around a real Voropai excerpt with modern anthropological commentary. The contrast Soviet-suppression → diaspora preservation → contemporary revival is a strong narrative thread.

2. **Calendar reform hook**: Ukraine's shift from January 7 to December 25 Christmas (Orthodox Church of Ukraine, effective 2023) is a live cultural event, not historical background. It concretely illustrates how identity, religion, and decolonization intersect — more engaging than abstract discussion.

3. **War as identity crucible**: The plan correctly flags war's impact on gender roles and funeral culture. At C2, this must be handled analytically, not emotionally. Model prose should use sociological register (*перерозподіл ґендерних ролей*, *трансформація обрядів прощання з воїнами*) while maintaining sensitivity.

4. **Decolonization framing note**: Soviet ethnography systematically presented Ukrainian folk traditions as local variants of "pan-Slavic" or "Eastern Slavic" practices, with Russian variants as the default. Content must actively counter this — e.g., писанка is not a variant of Russian крашанки; they are categorically different ritual objects with different functions.

5. **Register differentiation is core, not peripheral**: The module covers formal співчуття formulae, intimate побажання, academic аналіз, and casual забобони conversation. Phase B content should explicitly contrast registers within each section, not only in the "Практикум" section.

6. **Activity constraint reminder**: Plan includes `fill-in` (12 items) in activity_hints, but C2 quick-ref explicitly excludes fill-in. Phase B/activities generation should substitute with a formulas-in-context task using `essay-response` or `critical-analysis` format — e.g., analyzing register-appropriateness of given formulae rather than filling blanks.
