## Cultural Hooks

1. **Епістолярний стиль як окрема категорія** (Ukrainian Wikipedia / State Standard): The State Standard 2024 §4.3.1.1 recognises *епістолярний* as one of seven distinct Ukrainian language styles — a status it does not hold in all linguistic traditions. Some Ukrainian linguists debate whether it is an independent style or a substyle of conversational language, but its presence in the State Standard gives it institutional standing. This makes Ukrainian epistolary culture an active pedagogical category, not just a historical artifact. **Source:** uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Епістолярний_стиль_мовлення; State Standard §4.3.1.1.

2. **Дебюрократизація та боротьба з канцеляритом** (Ukrainian Wikipedia): *Канцелярит* — the term coined for the bureaucratic deformation of literary language through an overload of *канцеляризми* — is a Soviet-era inheritance. In Ukrainian public administration after 1991, and especially from 2014 onward, conscious reform movements have promoted «людиноцентричне» («human-centred») communication. This is the context for the plan's framing of "перехід від радянського канцеляриту до концепції human-to-human комунікації." The word *канцелярит* itself is highly specialized (0.01 IPM in GRAC) — a metalinguistic term used in language reform discourse, not in documents themselves. **Source:** uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Канцелярит

3. **Діловодство / справочинство** (Ukrainian Wikipedia): Ukrainian has its own native term for document management: *справочинство* (alongside the international *діловодство*). This reflects the broader project of Ukrainian lexical self-sufficiency — maintaining a native-rooted administrative vocabulary. The coexistence of both terms is itself a teaching point about Ukrainian professional register. **Source:** uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Діловодство

## Common Learner Errors

1. **«згідно з» + Genitive → must be Instrumental**: Learners (and native speakers under Russian/Soviet influence) write *«згідно наказу»* (Genitive, calque from Russian *«согласно приказа»*) instead of the normative *«згідно з наказом»* (Instrumental). The State Standard §4.3.3 explicitly lists *«згідно з…»* as a канцеляризм requiring correct case governance. **Textbook authority:** Grade 8 Zabotnyi 2025 (§9, p. 43) covers case governance errors systematically.

2. **Closing formula calque — «Щиро Ваш» → «З повагою»**: The closing *«Щиро Ваш»* is a direct calque of English "Sincerely yours" and is non-normative in Ukrainian official correspondence. The normative closing is *«З повагою»* (optionally with the sender's name and position). English-speaking learners transfer this pattern automatically. The plan's workshop section (§6) must address this explicitly.

3. **Lowercase «ви» in formal address → must be capitalised «Ви», «Ваш»**: Ukrainian formal correspondence requires capitalised *«Ви»* and *«Ваш»* when addressing a single person as a mark of respect — this is a distinctive feature of Ukrainian business etiquette absent in English. English-speaking learners consistently write lowercase, not recognising the functional distinction. Grade 8 textbooks (Avramenko 2025 §67) model this in лист-запрошення exemplars.

4. **«Я пишу, щоб запитати…» → «Звертаюся до Вас із запитанням»**: The English frame "I am writing to enquire / ask" produces a direct calque that sounds unnatural in Ukrainian official correspondence. The normative Ukrainian opening uses a deverbal noun (*звертання*, *запит*) rather than an infinitive purpose clause. This is precisely the type of calque prevention the plan's §4 and §6 target.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Register architecture**: The module spans *two* related but distinct registers — офіційно-діловий (ДСТУ-regulated documents, official letters) and *епістолярний* (broader correspondence culture). Phase B content must keep these distinct, especially in §1 (Вступ) where the evolutionary narrative moves *from* Soviet канцелярит *toward* human-centred communication while *retaining* official-style markers where required.
- **«Ви» capitalisation** is a high-engagement teaching moment at C2 — frame it as a feature of Ukrainian epistolary culture, not a rule borrowed from elsewhere.
- **ДСТУ 4163:2020 references**: Wikipedia has no dedicated article on ДСТУ 4163. The plan's source notes (dnaop.com) should be cited within the content as the normative reference. Do not fabricate ДСТУ article numbers beyond those in the plan (§1.3.2, §4.3.1.1 as referenced in plan).
- **Decolonized framing of канцелярит**: The Soviet administrative language was imposed on Ukrainian institutions — Ukrainian's dебюрократизація movement is an assertion of linguistic self-determination, not just stylistic preference. Frame the shift to «human-to-human» communication as a *cultural and political* act of language normalization.
- **Closing formulas**: Provide a full table of normative vs. non-normative closings in §6 (Практикум). Include: «З повагою» ✅, «Щиро Ваш» ❌ (English calque), «З глибокою повагою» ✅ (more formal), «Сподіваємося на подальшу співпрацю» ✅ (relationship-building).
- **Frequency note for activities**: *запит* (26.29 IPM) is the highest-frequency target word — ensure it receives dense treatment in reading/essay activities. *претензія* (0.74 IPM) is lower-register; frame it as domain-specific legal-professional vocabulary, not general correspondence vocabulary.
