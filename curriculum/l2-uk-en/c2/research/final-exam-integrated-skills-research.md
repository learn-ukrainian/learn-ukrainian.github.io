## Cultural Hooks

1. **ЗНО → НМТ transition (2022):** Ukraine's standardised testing system underwent forced rapid reform when Russia's full-scale invasion in February 2022 made the traditional Зовнішнє незалежне оцінювання (ЗНО) impractical to administer uniformly. The Національний мультипредметний тест (НМТ) was introduced as a temporary replacement (2022–2026), administered digitally, covering Ukrainian language, mathematics, and history of Ukraine. The НМТ became a focal point for discussions about assessment integrity, standardisation, and educational continuity under wartime conditions. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Національний_мультипредметний_тест

2. **НАЯО and diaspora certification:** The Національне агентство із забезпечення якості вищої освіти (НАЯО) is a permanent, collegial state body authorised to implement state policy on higher education quality. It provides language proficiency certification relevant to the diaspora and international recognition contexts — making C2 certification in Ukrainian a genuine credentialing pathway for heritage speakers and international learners. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Національне_агентство_із_забезпечення_якості_вищої_освіти

## Common Learner Errors

1. **складати vs скласти vs здати екзамен** → correct: **скласти іспит** in formal register for the completed act of passing; **складати іспит** for the process; **здати іспит** is colloquial and avoidable in formal academic writing. Error pattern: learners often default to "здати" universally (influenced by colloquial speech) or confuse aspect roles. Plan vocabulary_hints explicitly flags this; content must demonstrate skласти in a formal context.

2. **оцінювання (process) vs оцінка (result)** → English "assessment/evaluation" maps onto both words but they are not interchangeable in Ukrainian academic discourse. "Оцінювання" = the act/process of assessing; "оцінка" = the resulting grade or mark. Learners write "отримати оцінювання" (incorrect) instead of "отримати оцінку" or "пройти оцінювання."

3. **флуентність → плинність мовлення** → "флуентність" is a zero-corpus English borrowing. C2 academic Ukrainian uses "плинність мовлення," which is also the term in §1.4.1.1 of the State Standard itself. Learners at high levels may mistakenly import the English-derived form from Ukrainian language-learning meta-discourse online. Content must model the correct term.

4. **оціночна сітка → рубрика / рубрика оцінювання** → "Оціночна сітка" is a Russianism (*оценочная сетка*). The plan correctly flags this. Content should use "аналітична рубрика" or "рубрика оцінювання" consistently.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Replace флуентність with плинність мовлення throughout all content and vocabulary tables.** Zero GRAC frequency + VESUM absence = not standard literary Ukrainian.
- The C2 level definition (lines 4604–4609) provides a rich quotable anchor for the introduction — "тематично необмеженого... максимально можливого діапазону виражальних засобів" is the operative C2 standard and should frame the module's purpose.
- The НМТ/ЗНО transition is pedagogically useful as a cultural hook: it grounds abstract exam discourse in a real Ukrainian institutional context that has evolved under wartime conditions — a potent decolonized frame (Ukrainian testing as an independent, evolving system, not a Soviet holdover).
- **Aspect pair for the key exam verb:** скласти (pf, result: passed the exam) / складати (ipf, process: taking the exam). The perfective "скласти" is the formal register choice when describing successful completion.
- Section word budgets total 4,750 per the plan (750 + 1000 + 1125 + 875 + 1250 = 5,000 — actually sums to 5,000, which matches the C2 target). No redistribution needed.
- Mock exam section (1,250 words) is the richest section — ensure it models authentic C2 task formats including extended self-assessment with rubric language.
