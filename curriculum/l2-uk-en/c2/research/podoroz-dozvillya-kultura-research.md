## Common Learner Errors

1. **туристичний vs туристський** — Textbook (Grade 7 Заболотний, Grade 8 Авраменко, tier 1) explicitly teaches this paronims pair. «Туристичний» = стосується туризму/подорожі (туристичний сезон, туристичний маршрут); «туристський» = стосується туриста (туристська група, туристська путівка). C2 learners writing analytically must use the correct form — content errors in essays often stem from conflating these.

2. **бронювання ↔ резервування** (false synonymy) — Both valid VESUM words with 4:1 frequency ratio. Learners use them interchangeably; at C2 register precision requires the distinction: бронювання = hospitality/travel pre-commitment; резервування = broader administrative pre-allocation. Collocation errors: *«забронювати стіл»* ✅ vs *«зарезервувати номер»* ✅ — both exist, but mixing collocations (e.g., *«зарезервувати готель»*) sounds marked.

3. **мандрівка vs подорож** (register mismatch) — подорож (13.86 IPM) is neutral/high frequency; мандрівка (1.24 IPM) carries a literary, adventurous or romantic connotation (ближче до «journey» ніж «trip»). C2 learners writing publicistic texts default to подорож even where мандрівка would elevate the register; and vice versa, over-use мандрівка in formal academic contexts where it sounds stylistically jarring.

4. **Надтуризм discourse** — Grade 8 Заболотний 2025 textbook includes a canonical text on «НАДТУРИЗМ» (overtourism), directly relevant to the debate activity. The word надтуризм (calque from «overtourism») should be introduced as a neologism in the workshop section; learners may not know it exists in Ukrainian or may default to English.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Register-switching is the core C2 skill here.** Every section should demonstrate the difference between publicistic tourist text (рекламна брошура), academic analysis (культурологічна стаття), and personal narrative (подорожній щоденник). The Практикум section should model this explicitly with side-by-side examples.

- **Санаторний відпочинок** is a culturally specific phenomenon with Soviet roots — present it as a Ukrainian/post-Soviet cultural phenomenon without framing it as «Russian» or «Soviet legacy» — it has been indigenized and remains popular as a form of health tourism. Трускавець (мінеральні води), Моршин, Миргород are authentically Ukrainian locations.

- **Агросадиба** has near-zero GRAC frequency (2 hits) — this is expected for a specialist-register term that appears mainly in policy documents and government programs. At C2, low corpus frequency ≠ wrong; it signals specialist register, which is pedagogically appropriate to teach.

- **Decolonized framing:** Ukrainian guesthood traditions (хліб-сіль, гостинність) have deep Slavic and Cossack roots independent of Russian hospitality narratives. Frame «гостинність» through Cossack-era and ethnographic Ukrainian sources, not through pan-Slavic or Russian-derived generalizations. Regional variation (Гуцульщина, Полісся, Поділля) is the authentic lens.

- **ЮНЕСКО sites:** Софія Київська and Львівський історичний центр are confirmed UNESCO World Heritage Sites and can be cited by name without hedging.

- **Textbook anchor available:** The Grade 8 Заболотний 2025 «НАДТУРИЗМ» text (chunk `8-klas-ukrmova-zabolotnyi-2025_s0308–s0309`) provides authentic C2-level publicistic Ukrainian text on overtourism that directly grounds the debate activity. Content writers may use it as a style model.
