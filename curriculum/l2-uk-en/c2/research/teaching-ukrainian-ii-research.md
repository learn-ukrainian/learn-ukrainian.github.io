## Cultural Hooks
1. **Vasyl Sukhomlynsky**: The legacy of humanistic pedagogy («Серце віддаю дітям») as a philosophical foundation for student-centered course design.
2. **Kyiv-Mohyla Academy**: The historical context of structuring courses (rhetoric, poetics) in Ukraine's first higher education institution vs. modern European standards.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Lexical Confusion**: `Оцінка` (the grade/mark itself) vs `Оцінювання` (the process/assessment).
   *Correction*: "Система оцінювання включає різні види контролю." vs "Він отримав високу оцінку."
2. **False Friends**: `Курикулум` (rare/jargon) vs `Навчальна програма` (standard term).
   *Correction*: Use `навчальна програма` for the official document and `зміст навчання` for the content.
3. **Verb Usage**: `Вчити` (to teach someone OR to learn something - colloquial) vs `Вивчати` (to study a subject) vs `Навчати` (to teach someone a skill).
   *Correction*: "Ми плануємо, як навчати студентів (teach students), а не як вивчати студентів (study students)."

## Notes for Content Writing
- The tone should be highly professional and academic (Scientific/Official style), reflecting C2 mastery.
- Use the "Spiral Approach" (спіральний підхід) as a key structural concept to demonstrate complex planning.
- Differentiate clearly between `силабус` (student-facing, contract-like) and `робоча програма` (administrative, detailed).
