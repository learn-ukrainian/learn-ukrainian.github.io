## Cultural Hooks
1. **Премія імені Георгія Ґонґадзе**: Founded in 2019 to honor independent journalists. Named after Georgiy Gongadze, whose murder sparked the "Ukraine without Kuchma" protests and became a turning point for freedom of speech.
2. **StopFake**: A world-renowned Ukrainian fact-checking project founded in 2014 to debunk Russian propaganda. It highlights Ukraine's frontline role in the global information war.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Passive Voice Overuse**: "Було повідомлено про подію..." → *Active*: "ЗМІ повідомили про подію..." (Ukrainian publicistic style values active agency).
2. **Lexical Calques**: "Приймати міри" (from Rus. принимать меры) → *Correct*: "Вживати заходів"; "Згідно закону" → *Correct*: "Згідно із законом".
3. **Register Confusion**: Mixing bureaucratic clichés (kantseliaryzmy) into lively reportage (e.g., using "з метою здійснення" instead of "щоб зробити").

## Notes for Content Writing
- Ensure distinction between "standard news" (neutral) and "authorial column" (subjective) is clear, as Ukrainian media landscape blends them often.
- Emphasize "Standards of Journalism" (BBC/IMI standards) to counteract low-quality "clickbait" examples students might encounter online.
