## Cultural Hooks

1. **Григорій Сковорода і «сродна праця»** — Сковорода (1722–1794): Ukrainian philosopher-mystic, theologian, poet, pedagogist. Wikipedia describes him as "філософ-містик, богослов, поет, педагог" who "мав значний вплив на сучасників і подальші покоління своїми байками, піснями, філософськими творами, а також способом життя." His concept of **«сродна праця»** (congenial/vocation-matched labor) holds that genuine mastery is achievable only when work aligns with one's inner nature — a foundational Ukrainian contribution to philosophy of vocation, predating modern Western career theory by over a century. This is a genuine Ukrainian-origin intellectual anchor, not a derivative concept. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Сковорода_Григорій_Савич

2. **Prometheus — Ukrainian EdTech pioneer** — Launched **2014**, Prometheus is a Ukrainian civic project offering free university-level MOOC courses online. Purpose: "безкоштовне надання онлайн-доступу до курсів університетського рівня всім бажаючим." Founded by Ivan Prymachenko and Oleksiy Molchanovskiy. As of 2014 launch, one of the earliest and largest MOOC platforms in Eastern Europe. Alongside EdEra and Projector, it forms the core of the Ukrainian EdTech ecosystem. Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prometheus

## Common Learner Errors

1. **"взяти курс" → "пройти курс" / "записатися на курс"** — Direct calque from English "take a course." Correct Ukrainian uses "пройти курс" (completed action) or "записатися на курс" (enrollment act). Also acceptable: "відвідувати курс" (ongoing attendance). The plan explicitly flags this for correction in §Стратегії опанування галузі.

2. **"експертиза" (false friend)** — Ukrainian "експертиза" primarily means *forensic appraisal* or *official examination* (судова експертиза, будівельна експертиза). Using it to mean "domain expertise" (English) sounds legalistic to native speakers. Correct equivalents: "фахова компетентність," "глибокі знання у галузі," "фахова майстерність," or contextually "досвід." The plan correctly treats "будувати експертизу" as an intentional semantic extension and flags the distinction explicitly — content must teach this disambiguation clearly.

3. **"освіта" vs "навчання" — register confusion** — "Освіта" = the system, institution, and result (вища освіта, система освіти). "Навчання" = the ongoing process (процес навчання, навчання впродовж життя). The calque "life-long education" (навчання впродовж життя) is correct; "освіта впродовж життя" is incorrect in process contexts. The plan flags this at §3.13 context.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **"Сродна праця" framing**: Lead with Сковорода authentically — his concept is not merely an illustrative anecdote but the genuine philosophical grounding for the module's entire argument. The 18th-century Ukrainian origin of vocation philosophy should be stated explicitly and positively, not presented as a curiosity.
- **Low-frequency vocabulary risk**: "наставництво" (0.16/M) and "самоосвіта" (0.12/M) are specialist terms with real-world use in professional HR/edu contexts but will feel elevated to learners. Content must contextualise these with enough examples that they feel reachable.
- **"експертиза" disambiguation** requires a dedicated, careful explanation. A side-by-side example box (юридичне значення vs. сучасне бізнес-значення) will help learners remember the distinction. Do not merely mention it in passing.
- **Prometheus/EdEra/Projector**: All three platforms should appear with brief characterisations. Prometheus (2014, free MOOCs), EdEra (interactive learning), Projector (design/tech/business skills) — these are verified Ukrainian EdTech names.
- **НРК України §3.12**: The Національна рамка кваліфікацій is directly cited in the State Standard thematic section for C2 "освіта." Reference it by its full name at first mention, then abbreviate to НРК.
- **Style register (академічний)**: Content should consistently model the науковий style features from §4.3.3 — appropriate use of канцеляризми ("згідно з", "відповідно до"), professional terminology, and nominalization (-ння deverbal nouns). Activities should ask learners to *transform* between registers per §4.3.7.
- **Word budget note**: At 5000 target with 6 sections, the two 1000-word sections (§2, §3, §4) give the bulk. Practical Exercises (500) is a floor — stay close to it. Avoid padding in §5 (750) — keep it tight and high-density.
