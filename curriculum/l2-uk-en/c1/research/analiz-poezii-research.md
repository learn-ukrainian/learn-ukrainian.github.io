## Cultural Hooks

1. **Микола Зеров та Неокласики** (Wikipedia, verified):
   - Зеров (1890–1937): *"лідер «неокласиків», майстер сонетної форми та перекладач античної поезії"*; *"Представник розстріляного відродження"* — executed by Soviet regime.
   - Неокласики (group): Зеров, Филипович, Драй-Хмара, Бургардт (Юрій Клен), Рильський — *"позиціювали себе як естетів і жорстко протиставляли себе народництву і романтизму"*
   - Hook angle: Зеров's mastery of the classical sonnet form — an act of cultural resistance — connects formal poetics directly to Ukrainian literary identity. The сонет was not merely an aesthetic choice but an assertion of Ukrainian belonging in world literary tradition.

2. **Ліна Костенко** (Wikipedia, verified):
   - *"поетеса-шістдесятниця, письменниця, дисидентка. Лауреатка Шевченківської премії (1987)"*
   - Hook angle: Костенко's complex strophic structures and use of both traditional forms and innovative verse in collections like *Берестечко* (historical poem-novel) demonstrate how versification choices carry ideological weight — meter and strophic complexity as acts of cultural sovereignty, not merely aesthetic preference.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **вірш vs. строфа confusion** → Correct: *вірш* = the entire poetic work; *строфа* = a unit/stanza within the poem; *рядок* = a single line.
   — Why it happens: In casual Ukrainian (and English), "verse" is used loosely for both a poem and a line, causing terminological bleed. The plan explicitly targets this (Section 1).

2. **Ямб/Хорей inversion** → English-speaking learners frequently reverse rising/falling labels.
   — Why it happens: English iambic pentameter is the dominant school exposure, but English has different stress patterns; the Ukrainian words *ямб* and *хорей* give no phonetic mnemonic. The plan's "Я йду" (ямб, ta-DA) vs. "Холод" (хорей, DA-ta) mnemonic directly addresses this.

3. **Syllable counting vs. stress scanning** → Learners count syllables to identify meter rather than marking stress positions.
   — Why it happens: Transfer from syllabic poetry systems (French, Japanese); English schools also often teach syllable counting. Ukrainian силабо-тонічна система requires stress-based scanning. Textbook evidence (Avramenko Grade 6, Zabolotnyi Grade 6) confirms this is taught from middle school using stress-marking diagrams (— ∪ notation) — C1 module should assume familiarity with the symbols but deepen analytical application.

4. **Treating верлібр as "poetry without rules"** → Correct: верлібр rejects regular meter but retains other poetic devices (звукопис, образність, строфічна будова can still be present).
   — Why it happens: The etymology (fr. vers libre "free verse") suggests total freedom; learners underanalyze free verse poems.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Textbook alignment confirmed**: Versification (ямб/хорей/стопа) is introduced in Grade 6 (Avramenko, Zabolotnyi — tier 1 trust), алітерація defined formally in Grade 8 (Avramenko). A C1 module should explicitly reactivate this prior knowledge, then layer on analytical methodology — not re-teach basics from scratch, but integrate them into a unified philological framework.
- **Decolonized framing note**: Ukrainian силабо-тонічна versification has distinct stress patterns from Russian accentuation — Ukrainian's more mobile stress (especially in folk poetry) creates different rhythmic textures. Do not frame Ukrainian metrics as a borrowing from Russian prosody. Ukrainian versification theory developed from direct engagement with Greek, Latin, and Western European traditions (via Зеров and Неокласики).
- **Section 3 cultural depth**: The Неокласики group was deliberately destroyed by Soviet terror (Зеров, Драй-Хмара, Филипович all killed). Teaching сонет form through Зеров therefore carries historical weight; the content should acknowledge this without over-dramatizing — the focus remains on the literary analysis skills.
- **Section 4 (Звукопис)**: Textbook Grade 8 (Avramenko) gives solid definition of алітерація with Shevchenko example ("Реве та стогне Дніпр широкий") — this canonical example can anchor the section.
- **§4.4.3 звуконаслідування** is explicitly listed in the State Standard (7 VESUM matches confirms high currency) — integrate into Soundscapes section, not just the vocabulary table.
- **Word target strategy**: 4000 words for 5 sections averaging 800 words each; the plan allocates 600 to the intro and 1000 to the ямб/хорей architecture section. The 1000-word Section 2 allocation is justified given the multiple drills required (mnemonic, binary vs. ternary meters, identification exercises embedded in prose).
