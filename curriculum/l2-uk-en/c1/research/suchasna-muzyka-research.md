## Cultural Hooks
1. **Festival "Chervona Ruta" (1989)**: The turning point for Ukrainian music. It wasn't just a concert but a political manifestation where the Ukrainian anthem was publicly sung, sparking the independence movement.
2. **Eurovision as a Platform**: Ukraine is the only country to win Eurovision three times in the 21st century (Ruslana 2004, Jamala 2016, Kalush Orchestra 2022), often with songs carrying deep political or cultural messages ("1944", "Stefania").
3. **Music in the Metro**: During the 2022 invasion, legendary bands like Okean Elzy played acoustic concerts in metro stations used as bomb shelters, symbolizing spiritual resilience ("Music of the underground" taking a literal meaning).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Instrument Case Usage**: Learners often say "грати гітару" (direct object) instead of the correct **"грати на гітарі"** (Locative case).
   - *Fix*: Reinforce `грати на + [Locative]` for instruments.
2. **"Listen to" Preposition**: Learners transfer English "listen to" as "слухати до музики".
   - *Fix*: Reinforce **"слухати музику"** (Direct Accusative, no preposition).
3. **"Music" Plurality**: Learners may try to pluralize "музика" (musics) or use it as a countable noun.
   - *Fix*: Treat "музика" as an abstract singular noun. Use "пісні", "композиції", "треки" for countable units.
