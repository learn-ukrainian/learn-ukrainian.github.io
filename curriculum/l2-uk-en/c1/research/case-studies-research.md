## Cultural Hooks
1. **Law on State Language (2019)**: The "Movnyi zakon" is a perfect real-world case study for language policy, balancing state building with minority rights.
2. **Surzhyk vs. Dialects**: The persistent cultural debate about whether Surzhyk is "impure" language to be eradicated or a natural sociolinguistic phenomenon, contrasted with respected dialects (Hutsul, Boyko) which are culturally celebrated.
3. **Ukrainian Media Landscape**: The phenomenon of "Dzhynsa" (paid hidden advertising in news) as a classic media analysis topic in Ukrainian journalism schools.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Calques in terminology**: Using "приймати рішення" (calque from Russian/English) instead of **"ухвалювати рішення"** (official) or **"вирішувати"**.
2. **Surzhyk confusion**: Labeling legitimate dialectal words (e.g., "файний", "ґаздиня") as "surzhyk".
3. **Passive voice overuse**: Using "Було проаналізовано ситуацію..." instead of active agency **"Ми проаналізували ситуацію..."** (violates Agency Pass rule).
