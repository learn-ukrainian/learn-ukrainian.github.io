## Cultural Hooks

**1. Аліпій Печерський — correct Ukrainian form** (plan uses "Алімпій" — Wikipedia article: **Аліпій**):
- Orthodox saint, Kyiv-Pechersk Monastery icon painter, mosaicist, jeweller, healer, monk
- **First known named Old Rus artist** in the chronicles (Kyiv-Pechersk Paterikon)
- Learned from Greek masters — establishing the Kyiv school as a synthesis, not a copy
- Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Аліпій_Печерський
- ⚠️ **Flag for content writer**: correct the plan's "Алімпій" → "Аліпій"

**2. «Живописна Україна» (1844) — verified facts:**
- Shevchenko's etching series, technique: офорт
- Planned 12 prints/year; only 6 published due to financial hardship
- Published titles: «У Києві», «Видубицький монастир», «Судна рада», «Старости», «Казка», «Дари в Чигирині»
- Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Живописна_Україна

**3. Олександра Екстер — verified:**
- Born Grigorovych; Ukrainian artist of Greek-Jewish origin
- Pioneer of European cubism, futurism, art deco; became abstractionist — "на що тоді не зважилися її друзі Пікассо, Брак, Леже"
- Called "амазонка авангарду" by Benedikt Livshyts
- Source: uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/Екстер_Олександра_Олександрівна

**4. Казимир Малевич — decolonization note:**
- Wikipedia Ukrainian: "украї́нський і росі́йський авангардист... засновник супрематизму"
- Born 1879 in Kyiv; trained at Kyiv Drawing School; Ukrainian folk art (вишивка, писанки, народний розпис) explicitly cited in scholarship as influence on suprematist geometry
- Decolonized framing: present as Ukrainian artist who became a global figure — **not** as "Russian" artist with Ukrainian roots

## Common Learner Errors

1. **"картина маслами"** → **"олійна картина"** or **"картина, написана олією"**
   — Calque from Polish/Russian "картина маслом/маслами". Ukrainian uses олійна картина (adjectival) or written с олія (prepositional). Plan already targets this correction.

2. **"малювати ікону"** → **"писати ікону"**
   — The verb писати for icons derives from Greek γράφω (grapho) — the same root as "writing". This is not metaphor but etymology: священний текст. Learners default to малювати (drawing/painting mundane objects). Contrast: малювати картину ✓ vs писати ікону ✓.

3. **Зворотна vs пряма перспектива** — confusion direction:
   — In Western art: пряма перспектива (vanishing point away from viewer). In icons: зворотна перспектива (vanishing point toward viewer, drawing sacred into viewer's space). Learners often reverse or conflate the terms.

4. **Gender of термін "авангард"** in prepositional phrases:
   — "в авангарді" (locative, masc.) is correct; learners produce "в авангарді мистецтва" ✓ but sometimes write "авангардне мистецтво" conflating the movement noun with the adjective авангардний.

## Notes for Content Writing

1. **Name correction**: Use **Аліпій** Печерський throughout, not "Алімпій" (plan has a typo).

2. **Малевич decolonization**: Lean into his Kyiv birth and Ukrainian folk-art influence on suprematism. Avoid framing him as "Russian avant-garde artist who also worked in Ukraine." The geometry of вишивка, the nested diamonds of писанки, and the flat colour fields of народний розпис are legitimate precursors — this is a defensible scholarly position, not revisionism.

3. **Боровиковський** (Wikipedia: "Ukrainian and Russian painter, icon painter and portraitist, academic of St. Petersburg Academy of Arts") — born in Мирhorod (Poltava region). Frame his career arc as a Ukrainian artist who navigated imperial institutions while retaining spiritual-cultural roots.

4. **Архипенко** — "від'ємний простір" (negative space / void as sculptural element) is a genuinely revolutionary concept; confirm this Ukrainian-sourced term is used over any calques.

5. **Bogomازов** (Богомазов): "Ukrainian graphic artist, painter, pedagogue, art theorist" — his theoretical work *Живопис та елементи* (1914) is a key Kyiv avant-garde theoretical text, predating many Western equivalents.

6. **«Живописна Україна»**: Only 6 prints were actually published (not 12 as Shevchenko planned). Content should reflect historical accuracy: the series was cut short, which itself becomes a commentary on the material conditions of Ukrainian cultural production under empire.

7. **Prepositional correction drill**: картина олією (not маслами) — "олією" is the correct instrumental form: написана олією, виконана олією.
