## Cultural Hooks
1. **Тіні забутих предків** (М. Коцюбинський / С. Параджанов): The definitive text/film for Hutsul demonology (molfars, spirits).
2. **Лісова пісня** (Леся Українка): Encyclopedia of Volyn mythology (Mavka, Lisovyk, Perelesnyk).
3. **Двовір'я (Dual Faith)**: The unique Ukrainian phenomenon where pagan rituals (Ivana Kupala) seamlessly merged with Christian holidays (Birth of John the Baptist).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Міф vs Казка**: Learners often use *казка* (fairy tale, fiction) when they mean *міф* (sacred narrative explaining the world).
   * *Error*: Це просто стара казка. -> *Correction*: Це давній міф про створення світу.
2. **Gender of spirits**: Assuming *русалка* corresponds to the Western "Mermaid" (Disney style). Ukrainian *русалка* is often a dangerous spirit of a drowned girl, not necessarily with a fish tail.
3. **Відьма connotation**: Viewing *відьма* only negatively. Etymologically connected to *відати* (to know) — a wise woman, though often feared.

## Notes for Content Writing
- Maintain a respectful, ethnographic tone (Persona: Ethnographer).
- Distinctly separate "Lower Mythology" (spirits of nature/home) from the "Higher Pantheon" (Perun, Dazhboh), focusing on the former as it survived better in folklore.
- Emphasize the *protective* function of many rituals, not just fear.
