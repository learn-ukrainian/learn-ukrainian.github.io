## Cultural Hooks

1. **Оксана Забужко, «Notre Dame d'Ukraine: Українка в конфлікті міфологій» (2007)** — Winner of «Книга року 2007». This 680-page non-fiction work is celebrated for its signature extended sentence architecture: Забужко constructs layered subordinate chains using concessive (попри, хоча), causal (оскільки, позаяк), and additive (водночас, натомість) connectors to sustain complex intellectual argumentation across paragraph-long periods. The book's title-level question — what does Lesya Ukrainka mean to Ukrainian identity? — is answered through precisely this logical-connective web. Source: Ukrainian Wikipedia, «Книжка року 2007».

2. **Іван Вишенський (c. 1550 – c. 1621), полемічна проза** — The plan mentions Вишенський and Смотрицький as historical anchors. Вишенський's treatises (e.g., «Викриття диявола-світодержця», «Послання до єпископів») are the founding texts of Ukrainian intellectual prose and demonstrate proto-logical connectors in Церковнослов'янсько-Ukrainian hybrid syntax. His causals and concessives (the proto-forms of оскільки, однак) ground the lesson in a continuous native tradition — not a borrowing from any other language tradition. This is crucial for decolonized framing: Ukrainian has its own unbroken intellectual discourse tradition predating any external influence.

## Common Learner Errors

1. **«так як»** → нормативно: **«оскільки»** або **«бо»** — Calque of Russian «так как». Has no literary pedigree in Ukrainian; appears in informal/translated texts. Textbook Авраменко gr. 7 (2024) explicitly lists «оскільки» as the standard causal for written argumentation. Correction: Він зачинив вікна, так як було холодно → Він зачинив вікна, **оскільки** було холодно.

2. **«тим не менш»** → нормативно: **«утім»**, **«проте»**, **«однак»** — Calque of Russian «тем не менее». Commonly found in amateur translations and informal academic writing. The Ukrainian alternatives carry stronger stylistic differentiation: «проте» (neutral formal), «однак» (slightly more emphatic), «утім» (literary-publicistic, signals authorial voice). Correction: Тим не менш, висновки залишаються актуальними → **Утім**, висновки залишаються актуальними.

3. **«завдяки» у негативному/нейтральному контексті** → **«через»** — «Завдяки» in Ukrainian carries a strictly positive connotation (benefactive). Using it with negative causes is a Russian-influenced calque (Russian «благодаря» allows neutral/negative contexts more freely). Error: «Завдяки хворобі він пропустив іспит» → «**Через** хворобу він пропустив іспит». Both «завдяки» and «через» are high-frequency and VESUM-verified; the distinction is semantic-stylistic.

## Notes for Content Writing

- **Textbook grounding confirmed**: Grade 7 Авраменко (2024) and Grade 8 Заболотний (2025) cover the foundational taxonomy. C1 module must push significantly beyond — into register differentiation, stylistic nuance, and productive academic writing, not mere identification.
- **VESUM status**: All 14 core vocabulary items verified present in dictionary. «крім того» and «дарма що» are multi-word units (no single entry expected); use confidently in examples.
- **§4.4.4 framing**: The State Standard explicitly treats connector selection as a *stylistic* competency at C1, not a grammatical one. Phase B content should emphasize *why* a writer chooses «натомість» over «але» — it's a stylistic act, not just a synonym swap.
- **Decolonized framing strategy**: Lead all comparisons with native Ukrainian tradition (Вишенський, Забужко). The Russianisms (так як, тим не менш) are presented as *errors to correct*, not as evidence of proximity to another language. Never frame Ukrainian connectors as "similar to" or "different from" Russian equivalents.
- **Register differentiation table** (ready for Phase B):
  | Function | Neutral | Formal/Academic | Literary/Publicistic |
  |----------|---------|-----------------|----------------------|
  | Cause | бо | оскільки | позаяк |
  | Result | тому | отже | звідси |
  | Concession | хоча | попри те що | дарма що |
  | Contrast | але | натомість | утім |
  | Addition | і | крім того | водночас |
- **Word target note**: 4000 words across 6 sections. Sections 2 and 3 (800 words each) are the heaviest and carry the core connector pairs — ensure density of authentic examples (min. 3 per connector presented). Section 5 (Забужко/Вишенський) can use extended quotation-and-analysis format to sustain word count organically.
