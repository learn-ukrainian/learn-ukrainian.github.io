## Cultural Hooks
1. **Molfars of the Carpathians**: The phenomenon of "мольфари" (Carpathian shamans), popularized by Kotsiubynsky's "Shadows of Forgotten Ancestors" and the real-life figure Mykhailo Nechai. They are seen as controllers of forces of nature (clouds, hail).
2. **Ivan Kupala Night**: The deep connection between harvesting herbs and specific calendar dates. Herbs gathered on Kupala night (or St. Simon's day) are believed to have peak potency. The legend of the Fern Flower (Цвіт папороті).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Lexical Confusion**: Using "лікар" (official doctor) interchangeably with "знахар" or "цілитель" (folk healer). *Correction*: Maintain strict distinction; "лікар" implies medical degree, "знахар" implies traditional knowledge.
2. **Preposition Usage**: "Лікуватися від..." (Treat from...) vs "Лікувати щось" (Treat something). *Correction*: "Вона лікує травами" (Instrumental for method).
3. **Imperative Mood**: Incorrect formation in "wishing" formulas. *Correction*: Use of "Хай/Нехай" + 3rd person (Хай згине хвороба) vs Direct Imperative (Іди геть).
