## Common Learner Errors
1. **Lexical Confusion**: Using "лікар" (official doctor) interchangeably with "знахар" or "цілитель" (folk healer). *Correction*: Maintain strict distinction; "лікар" implies medical degree, "знахар" implies traditional knowledge.

3. **Imperative Mood**: Incorrect formation in "wishing" formulas. *Correction*: Use of "Хай/Нехай" + 3rd person (Хай згине хвороба) vs Direct Imperative (Іди геть).
