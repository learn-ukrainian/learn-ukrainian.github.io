## Cultural Hooks
1. **"When the Fern Blooms" (Stankovych):** This folk-opera was banned by Soviet authorities right before its premiere in 1978 and prohibited for decades, symbolizing the repression of Ukrainian identity.
2. **"Melody" by Skoryk:** Originally written for the film "The High Pass", it has become a spiritual anthem of Ukraine, often performed at solemn state events.
3. **Lyatoshynsky's Symphony No. 3:** Subtitled "Peace Shall Defeat War", it was forced to be rewritten by Soviet censors to have a "happier" ending.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **"Movement" in music:** Learners often use «рух» (motion). Correct: «частина» (part of a symphony/concerto).
2. **Prepositions with "Impression":** Learners say «враження про концерт». Correct: «враження від концерту» (impression *from*).
3. **"Contemporary" vs "Modern":** Learners confuse «сучасна» (living/current) with «модерна» (relating to the Modernist era).
