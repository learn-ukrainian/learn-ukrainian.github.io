## Cultural Hooks
1. **The Literary Discussion (1925-1928)**: The famous "Khvylovyi vs. Pylypenko" debates. A prime historical example of high-stakes public polemics in Ukraine, where counterarguments literally shaped the nation's future ("Геть від Москви!").
2. **Ivan Vyshenskyi's Polemics**: The tradition of fiery, rhetorical argumentation dates back to the 16th-17th century religious polemics. Mentioning this grounds the skill in deep Ukrainian literary history.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Naked Disagreement**: Saying "Я не згоден" or "Це неправда" without a concession clause ("Я розумію вашу думку, але..."). *Correction*: Use the "Yes, but..." structure (Так, але / Хоча..., проте...).
2. **Register Mix-up**: Using "брехня" (lie) instead of "хибне твердження" (false statement) in academic/formal debates.
3. **Calque from English "However"**: Starting every sentence with "Однак" mechanically. *Correction*: Vary with "Водночас", "З іншого боку", "Попри це".
