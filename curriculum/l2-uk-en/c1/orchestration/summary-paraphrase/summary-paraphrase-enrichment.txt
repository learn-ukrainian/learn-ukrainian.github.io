## Cultural Hooks
1. **School Tradition**: In Ukrainian education, writing a "переказ" (retelling) is a standardized exam format. Students are trained early on to differentiate between "детальний переказ" (detailed) and "стислий переказ" (concise summary), making this a culturally familiar concept to native speakers but new to learners.
2. **Academic Rigor**: Ukrainian academic culture places high value on "науковий стиль" (scientific style), which demands impersonality and precise paraphrasing. Direct quoting is common, but modern standards increasingly emphasize synthesis and "реферування" (abstracting).

## Common Learner Errors
1. **False Friends**: Confusing "резюме" (often CV/Resume in business context) with "summary" (abstract/synopsis). *Correction*: Use "анотація" or "підсумок" for text summary, "резюме" for CV or specific abstract types.
2. **Patchwriting**: Changing only a few words (synonyms) but keeping the exact sentence structure. *Correction*: Change both vocabulary AND grammatical structure (active ↔ passive, verbal ↔ nominal).
3. **Calquing English Markers**: Using "У інших словах" (calque of "In other words"). *Correction*: Use "Інакше кажучи", "Іншими словами", "Тобто".

## Cross-References
