## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Kyiv vs. Lviv" Coffee Culture Debate**: Often used as a lighthearted example of regional dialect differences (філіжанка vs чашка) in professional settings.
2. **Freedom of Speech in Ukraine**: The vibrant, often chaotic nature of Ukrainian political talk shows (e.g., "Свобода слова") as a model for publicistic discourse, contrasting with controlled media environments.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **CV Calques**: Using "досвід" (experience in general) instead of "стаж" (years of service) or "досвід роботи" in formal documents.
2. **Register Mixing**: Using "Ти" forms or informal particles (ну, давай) in a job interview setting which demands "Ви" and strict etiquette.
3. **Surzhyk Hypercorrection**: Avoiding legitimate Ukrainian words that sound like Russian (e.g., "держати") while trying to purge Surzhyk, instead of learning the actual stylistic nuances.
