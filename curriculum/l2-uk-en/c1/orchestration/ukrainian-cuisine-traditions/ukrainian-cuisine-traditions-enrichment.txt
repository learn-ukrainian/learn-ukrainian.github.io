## Cultural Hooks
1. **UNESCO Status**: Culture of Ukrainian borscht cooking was inscribed on the UNESCO List of Intangible Cultural Heritage in Need of Urgent Safeguarding in 2022.
2. **Kutia Ritual**: The tradition of throwing a spoonful of kutia to the ceiling on Christmas Eve; if it sticks, it predicts a bountiful harvest and good luck for the year.
3. **Bread Sanctity**: Bread is considered sacred ("Хліб — усьому голова"). It is never thrown away; if a piece falls, it must be picked up and kissed or apologized to.
4. **Didukh**: The sheaf of wheat (Didukh) placed in the corner (pokuia) during Christmas, symbolizing the ancestors' spirits.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Instrumental Case with Tools**: Learners often use "з" (with) for tools instead of the bare Instrumental case.
   *   *Error*: Я ріжу хліб з ножем.
   *   *Correct*: Я ріжу хліб ножем.
2. **Cooking Verbs Confusion**: Using "варити" (to boil) for all cooking methods.
   *   *Error*: Я варю торт.
   *   *Correct*: Я печу торт.
3. **Phonetic Rules with Prepositions**: Incorrect use of "з" vs "зі/із".
   *   *Error*: Борщ з сметаною.
   *   *Correct*: Борщ зі сметаною.
