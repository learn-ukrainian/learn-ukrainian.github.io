## Common Learner Errors
1. **Confusion of Regions:** Confusing *Podillia* (Central-West: Vinnytsia, Khmelnytskyi) with *Polissia* (North: forests, swamps).
   *Correction:* Link Podillia to "dolyna" (valleys, canyons) and Polissia to "lis" (forests).
2. **Surzhyk in Architectural Terms:** Using "дом" instead of "хата", "садиба" or "обійстя" in ethnographic contexts.
   *Correction:* Enforce specific terminology: *хата-мазанка*, *ґражда* (Hutsul, but useful contrast), *садиба*.
3. **Improper Agreement with "Товтри":** Treating it as singular.
   *Correction:* *Товтри* is plural only (pluralia tantum).
