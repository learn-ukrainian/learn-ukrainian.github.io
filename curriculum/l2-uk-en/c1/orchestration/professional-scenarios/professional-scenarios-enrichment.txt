## Cultural Hooks
1. **Forms of Address**: In Ukrainian business culture, the use of "Ви" and Patronymics (Ім'я по батькові) is still standard in formal settings and with superiors, though modern IT/creative sectors often move to "Ви + Ім'я" or even "Ти" faster than traditional industries.
2. **Directness vs. Politeness**: Ukrainian professional communication values constructiveness. While "small talk" exists, it is often shorter and more sincere than the formulaic American equivalent. Criticism is often delivered more directly than in Anglosphere cultures.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Calques**: "Аплікуватися на роботу" (from "apply") → Correct: "Подаватися на вакансію" / "Надсилати резюме".
2. **Confusing Adjectives**: `робочий` (working day/table - `робочий день`) vs `робітничий` (working class - `робітничий клас`).
3. **Wrong Prepositions**: "Дзвонити до клієнта" (dialectal/colloquial) vs "Телефонувати клієнту" (standard dative). "Говорити за проект" (incorrect) vs "Говорити про проект" (correct).
