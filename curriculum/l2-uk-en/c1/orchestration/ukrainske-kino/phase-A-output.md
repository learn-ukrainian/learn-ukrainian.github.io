## Cultural Hooks
1. **"Earth" (Земля) recognition**: Oleksandr Dovzhenko's "Earth" was named one of the top 12 films of all time at the Brussels World's Fair in 1958.
2. **The 1965 Protest**: The premiere of Parajanov's "Shadows of Forgotten Ancestors" in Kyiv became the site of the first public political protest against Soviet arrests, led by Vasyl Stus, Vyacheslav Chornovil, and Ivan Dziuba.
3. **Oscar Success**: "20 Days in Mariupol" by Mstyslav Chernov bringing the first Oscar to Ukraine, marking the global impact of Ukrainian war documentary.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Confusion of *грати* vs *гратися***: Learners often use *гратися* (to play with toys) instead of *грати* (to act/play a role). → *Актор майстерно грає свою роль.*
2. **Case usage with *вражати***: Incorrectly using prepositional cases instead of direct accusative. → *Фільм вразив глядачів* (The film impressed the viewers).
3. **Loan translations**: Using *знімати кіно* is correct, but *знімати фільм* is more common; avoiding *робити кіно* (make movie) in favor of *створювати фільм* (create a film).
