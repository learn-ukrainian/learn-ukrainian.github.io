## Cultural Hooks
1. **The "Executed Renaissance" (Розстріляне відродження)**: The tragic 1930s generation (Khvylovy, Pidmohylny) whose destruction artificially interrupted the natural evolution of Ukrainian modernism, creating a "gap" in the canon that is only now being fully healed.
2. **The "Ems Ukaz" (1876) & "Valuev Circular" (1863)**: Historical context explaining why certain genres (like theatre/ethnography) flourished while others (scientific literature, translations) were suppressed, shaping the unique "cordocentric" (heart-centered) nature of 19th-century Ukrainian literature.

## Common Learner Errors
1. **Confusing "руський" vs "російський"**: Learners often mistranslate "Rus'" (Kyivan Rus era) adjectives as "Russian". Correct: *Руська правда* (Rus law), *російська література* (Russian lit).
2. **Calques in Analysis**: Using "автор каже" (too simple/English calque) instead of analytical verbs like "автор стверджує", "наголошує", "підкреслює", "зображує".
3. **Genre confusion**: Calling a short story (*новела*) a "novel" (*роман*). *Новела* is a short story with a twist; *роман* is a novel.
