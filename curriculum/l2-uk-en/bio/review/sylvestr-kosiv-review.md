# Review: Сильвестр Косів: Освітянин і будівничий

**Status:** PASS

## 1. Executive Summary
This module provides a very deep and engaging exploration of Sylvestr Kosiv's life, his defense of the Kyiv Metropolitanate, and his educational strategies. It successfully uses the BIO template and maintains a highly academic register. The text successfully implements the "Would I Stay?" test by offering complex historical analysis rather than just dates and facts.

## 3. Issues Found
The module is very strong, but I found a few minor stylistic issues that could be refined for even better flow:
1. In the historical context section, the sentence «У грудні 1648 року переможний гетьман Богдан Хмельницький урочисто і тріумфально в'їхав до звільненого Києва» is somewhat awkward and could be improved. The words "урочисто і тріумфально" mean almost the same thing in this context. It could be streamlined.
2. The use of primary sources is generally excellent, especially the quote «Перша причина, чому нашому народові потрібна латинська наука, є та, щоб нашої Руси не називали дурною Руссю», but it would be beneficial to add even more direct quotes from his letters if available.
3. The comparative study activity is good, but could include more specific criteria. This is a minor issue.

## 4. Final Recommendation
The module is incredibly detailed and well-written. The minor stylistic redundancies do not detract from the overall excellent quality. It is ready for publication.
