# Research Notes: Natalia Vorozhbyt: Bad Roads

**Track**: lit-drama | **Module**: vorozhbyt-bad-roads | **Researched**: 2026-02-24

## The New Drama
Vorozhbyt captures the fractured, raw, and often brutal reality of the Donbas conflict. Her plays utilize authentic vernacular and Surzhyk to reflect wartime trauma.

## Decolonization Focus
Rejecting idealized or sanitized depictions of war in favor of raw, localized psychological realism.
