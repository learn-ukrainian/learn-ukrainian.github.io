# Research Notes: Pavlo Arie: At the Beginning and End of Time

**Track**: lit-drama | **Module**: arie-chornobyl | **Researched**: 2026-02-24

## Chthonic Realism
Arie's work explores the Exclusion Zone not just as a historical disaster, but as a mythical, liminal space inhabited by marginalized figures (Baba Prisia).

## Decolonization Focus
Reclaiming the Chornobyl narrative from Soviet bureaucratic failure and Western sci-fi, grounding it in Ukrainian folk trauma.
