## Overview

The B2 curriculum is designed to bridge the gap between "competent" (B1) and "fluent" (C1). It focuses on:
1.  **Register & Style:** Knowing *when* to use formal vs. informal language.
2.  **Passive & Complex Syntax:** Mastering the structures used in news, science, and literature.
3.  **Phraseology:** Internalizing idioms, proverbs, and set expressions for natural flow.
4.  **History & Culture:** Understanding the *why* behind the language through a decolonized history course.
5.  **Synonymy:** Moving beyond basic words to precise synonyms (e.g., knowing 5 ways to say "to think").

**Structure:**
Unlike previous levels, B2 modules are **interleaved** to maintain engagement. Instead of 20 modules of grammar followed by 20 modules of history, topics are mixed into four balanced phases.
