# B2 Curriculum Plan: Ukrainian for English Speakers

**Status:** ✅ APPROVED (Implementation: [Issue #71](https://github.com/krisztiankoos/curricula-opus/issues/71))
**Modules:** 201-300 (100 modules)
**Vocabulary Target:** ~3,000 new words
**Cumulative Target:** ~6,500-7,000 words
**Immersion:** 15% English / 85% Ukrainian

---

## Overview

The B2 curriculum bridges "competence" and "fluency". It shifts focus from *learning rules* to *mastering style, nuance, and cultural depth*.

**Core Philosophy:**
1.  **Theory-First:** Explicit, deep grammatical explanations before practice.
2.  **Decolonization:** History and culture presented through a Ukraine-centric lens.
3.  **Stylistic Range:** Official vs. Colloquial vs. Scientific registers.
4.  **Linguistic Depth:** Etymology, cognates, and unique Ukrainian features.

## Implementation Notes

### Migration & Expansion
- **Restoration:** All specific biographies from the original plan (e.g., **Berta Rapoport, Vasyl Vyshyvanyi, Kateryna Bilokur**) have been restored to their dedicated slots.
- **Synonyms:** The full range of synonym topics (Speaking, Informing, Asking, Understanding, Memory) has been preserved in B2.2.
- **Expansion:** New slots in B2.4 allow for coverage of modern heroes (Stus, Chornovil, Zaluzhnyi) and distinct modern history periods (Holodomor, Revolutions) which were previously compressed.

### Structure
- **B2.1:** 201-225 (Formal Language)
- **B2.2:** 226-250 (Nuance & Synonyms)
- **B2.3:** 251-275 (History & Women's Biographies)
- **B2.4:** 276-300 (Men's Biographies & Modern History)
