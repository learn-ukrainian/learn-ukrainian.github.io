## Overview

The B2 curriculum bridges "competent" (B1) and "fluent" (C1). It focuses on:
1. **Register & Style:** Knowing *when* to use formal vs. informal language.
2. **Passive & Complex Syntax:** Mastering structures used in news, science, and literature.
3. **History & Culture:** Understanding Ukrainian identity through decolonized history.
4. **Phraseology:** Internalizing idioms, proverbs, and set expressions.
5. **–í–∏–¥–∞—Ç–Ω—ñ —É–∫—Ä–∞—ó–Ω—Ü—ñ:** Learning about famous Ukrainians who shaped the nation.

**Content Philosophy:**
- All content primarily in Ukrainian (85%+)
- Engaging narrative style, not dry textbook
- "–ß–∏ –∑–Ω–∞–ª–∏ –≤–∏?" boxes, "–ú—ñ—Ñ vs –§–∞–∫—Ç" sections
