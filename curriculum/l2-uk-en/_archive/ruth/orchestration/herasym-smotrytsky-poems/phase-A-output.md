## Деколонізаційний контекст
- **Imperial/Soviet myth:** Often labeled merely as a "defender of Orthodoxy" in a pan-Slavic/Russian context, or his "vernacular" minimized as a dialect. Soviet historiography downplayed his noble status.

He uses the vernacular as a weapon of intellectual independence, proving Ukrainian was capable of high theological debate long before the 19th century.
