**Keep calibration and production rationale internal.** The seminar track and
target CEFR level guide the writer and reviewer; they are not learner-facing
lesson claims. Do not write phrases such as `На рівні C1`, `Для C1`,
`C1-читач`, `C1-відповідь`, `Мова C1`, or `C1 лексика` in `module.md`,
`activities.yaml`, or `vocabulary.yaml`. Preserve the substantive teaching
operation without naming its calibration level.

Treat plan guardrails, reviewer notes, source-boundary cautions, and copyright
rationale as authoring constraints. Transform them into direct teaching,
identifiable resource notes, or evidence-backed task instructions; never
paraphrase the internal reason for including, limiting, or checking content.
Before emission, compare the plan's imperative and negative-guardrail language
with every learner surface and remove production-process echoes.
