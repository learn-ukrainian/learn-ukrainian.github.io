# 🇺🇦 A1 — Початковий

**У розробці — 0/60 готово**

## Think in Ukrainian from Day One

A1 takes you from zero to your first real conversations in Ukrainian. You'll read Cyrillic, introduce yourself, talk about your family, navigate a city, order food, and tell stories about your past and future — all through real situations, dialogues, and cultural immersion.

**What You'll Learn:**
- 🔤 The Cyrillic alphabet, Ukrainian sounds, stress, and intonation
- 💬 Greetings, introductions, family, possessions (У мене є...)
- 🏠 Describing things with gender, adjectives, colors, numbers 1-1000
- ⏰ Time, days, months, weather, daily routines
- 🏙️ Navigating a city — locative, accusative, genitive for location
- 🍽️ Food, shopping, cafés — instrumental "з", accusative objects
- 🗣️ Vocative, imperative, dative, linking ideas, Ukrainian holidays
- ⏳ Past and future tense, emergencies, your life story

**Methodology:** Grammar through real situations — never isolated rules. Inspired by Ukrainian pedagogy (State Standard 2024) and Anna Ohoiko's Ukrainian Lessons methodology.
