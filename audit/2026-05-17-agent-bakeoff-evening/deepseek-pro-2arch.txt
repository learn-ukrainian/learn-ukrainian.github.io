**Design:** Split into two lists. `SCAN_ROOTS` auto-discovers subdirectories via a watcher. `SERVE_ROOTS` remains the manually curated security boundary. The scanner indexes everything under `SCAN_ROOTS`; the server rejects any request whose resolved path falls outside `SERVE_ROOTS` with 404. A reconciler surfaces entries in `SCAN_ROOTS` but missing from `SERVE_ROOTS` as "pending approval."

**Trade-off:** Operational overhead of the approval step — but drift becomes a visible alert instead of a silent breakage.
