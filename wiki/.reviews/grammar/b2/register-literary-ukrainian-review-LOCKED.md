  invented `веснодіяти` were replaced. `являється` guidance is tied to an

  editing guidance.
- **Completeness:** The article still covers artistic style, tropes,
  word-formation, syntax, L2 transfer, decolonization cautions, examples, and
  exercise sequencing.
- **Actionability:** B2 authors can use the page without resolving open source
  questions for the listed examples.
