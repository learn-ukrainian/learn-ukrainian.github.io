## Gaps identified on intake (vs. the locked at-the-cafe + who-am-i + things-have-gender template)

1. **No `Типові помилки L2` section.** Demonstratives are one of the most L2-error-prone slices at A1 because they sit at the intersection of three separate L1-interference traps: (a) English-style copula insertion (`Це є стіл` ← "This IS a table"); (b) invariant-demonstrative Over-generalization leading to gender/case agreement failure (`Я бачу цей книгу`); (c) Russian-background code-switching (`этот/эта/это` directly transferred). Plus the cross-cutting gender-flip risk (learner thinks `біль` is ж.р. because `боль` is f. in Russian, so would write `ця біль`). The wiki had zero learner-facing defenses for any of these.
2. **Vocabulary table wrong format.** The locked peers (at-the-cafe, who-am-i, things-have-gender) use `Українською | Англійською | Рід | Рівень | Джерело` — writer gets English glosses + per-entry provenance. `this-and-that` had a four-column `Частина мови | Рівень | Слово (рід) | Переклад` with no per-entry source column and with the demonstratives themselves (the subject of the wiki) absent from the table.
3. **Chunk guidance missing.** The locked peers pin `велику каву` / `Тут, будь ласка` / `мій стіл / моя ручка / моє вікно` as indivisible chunks, preventing premature grammar teaching. `this-and-that` had no equivalent note — creating risk that the writer would over-teach demonstrative declension (the `той / того / тому / тим` root alternation is A2 material), full adjective agreement, or plural oblique cases at A1 just because the four-gender-agreement table made them visible.
4. **Decolonization #4 off-topic.** The original point #4 used `наглий → "раптовий"` as a false-friend cautionary example. This is a general lexical caveat, not demonstrative-specific — and `наглий` does not appear anywhere else in the wiki. Replaced with a demonstrative-native point (emphatic `оцей/оця/оце` and `отой/ота/ото` are питомо-Ukrainian and must not be silently removed just because Russian lacks a parallel). Added a new #5 on `даний` as kancelyarism vs. `цей` as everyday form — directly relevant to the module.
5. **Typo in Пов'язані статті:** `pedagony/a1/personal-pronouns` (misspelled `pedagony` instead of `pedagogy`). Fixed.
6. **`sources.yaml` had all four entries tagged `type: textbook` without traceability notes** and no dictionary authorities present. Fixed: per-entry `type: textbook-chunk` + notes + new **S5 (VESUM)** and **S6 (СУМ-11)** dictionary authorities with citation notes enumerating what the wiki pulls from each.
7. **Missing `lifecycle` / `last_reviewed` / `reviewed_by` in wiki-meta.**
8. **No Exercise 5** drilling the new L2-errors table one-to-one (mirrors who-am-i Exercise 5 / things-have-gender Приклад 5 pattern required for AC-3 wiki-plan drift check).
