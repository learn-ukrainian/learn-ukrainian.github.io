## Residual non-blockers (documented, not blocking)

- **Overlap with `sounds-letters-and-hello`.** Rows 4, 5, 6, 7 of the new Типові помилки L2 table (iotated after consonant; `Ї`; `Щ`; ДЖ/ДЗ) touch the same phenomena as rows 2–6 of the `sounds-letters-and-hello` L2-errors table. This is INTENTIONAL framing: `sounds-letters` targets sound-production in isolation; `reading-ukrainian` targets decoding-from-print. The two wikis reinforce each other rather than contradict — the wiki's intro paragraph to the table names this explicitly ("Overlap with `pedagogy/a1/sounds-letters-and-hello` is intentional"). A future consolidation (e.g. shared `culture/decolonization/l2-phonetic-errors` wiki) could dedupe but is out of scope for A1 lock.
- **Row 2 `ь` minimal-pair sparsity.** The honest note that "Чистих `N` / `N+ь` мінімальних пар в українській мало" is correct Ukrainian phonology (most `ь`-forms contrast at the lemma level, not as same-stem pairs). The writer will need to rely on the slot-by-slot word list (`день, сіль, тінь, кінь, моль, ось`) rather than paired contrasts — acceptable at A1.
- **Row 8 transfer rules** (`мо-рква` / `мор-ква` both OK; `морк-ва` forbidden) references the specific rule that `й` and `ь` do not separate from the preceding letter and that a single-letter syllable cannot stand alone across a line break. The example `ра-диця` → `рад-ниця` is constructed (`радниця` is VESUM-found as a noun meaning `female adviser`, rare); the rule is correct but a writer might want a more high-frequency example — listed as a non-blocker so a subsequent content-review pass can substitute.
- **No `last_reviewed` inheritance from the sources.** The `.sources.yaml` sidecar for this wiki still has `type: unknown` for every source. This is a repo-wide hygiene issue (tracked in #1349 line), not specific to this wiki. Not blocking — the source keys resolve to the right chunks at build time.
- **Wiki `kid-specific pedagogy inheritance`.** Many claims inherit from Gr 1–2 Ukrainian-L1 textbook pedagogy (Большакова, Захарійчук, Вашуленко), which addresses native Ukrainian children learning to decode their own language. The wiki's L2-adapter framing is carried by the Деколонізаційні застереження section and the new Типові помилки L2 table; at the content-review level the writer must still check that exercises are not infantilizing. Flagged for v6 review, not for wiki lock.
