## Методичний підхід (Methodological Approach)

The primary goal at the A1 level for the "Around the City" topic is to equip learners with the essential vocabulary and grammatical structures needed for basic navigation and orientation. The approach should be communicative, functional, and centered on real-world tasks. Ukrainian pedagogy for young learners often builds from the immediate and concrete to the more abstract (Джерело: `2-klas-ukrmova-kravcova-2019-1_s0099`).

We begin with the core concepts of `місто` (city) and `село` (village), establishing a context that is relatable whether the learner imagines a large metropolis or a smaller town (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-32`). The initial focus is on identifying key places (`школа`, `магазин`, `парк`) and asking where they are.

Instruction revolves around simple questions and answers. The primary question word is `де?` (where?), as seen in dialogues asking for the city center or metro (`А де центр?`, `А де метро?`) (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-287`).

The concept of giving and understanding directions is introduced through imperative verb forms and adverbs. Dialogues are the primary tool for this, as demonstrated in the Ukrainian Lessons Podcast, where a stranger gives directions to the train station using commands like `ідіть прямо` (go straight) and `поверніть праворуч` (turn right) (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-286`). This task-based approach ensures that grammar serves a practical purpose from the outset. Politeness is embedded from the start, with phrases like `Вибачте, будь ласка` and `Дякую` being non-negotiable components of any interaction (Джерело: `7-klas-ukrmova-zabolotnyi-2024_s0240`).

## Послідовність введення (Introduction Sequence)

1.  **Крок 1: Базові локації (Basic Locations).** Start with the highest-frequency nouns that define a populated area. Introduce `місто` (city), `село` (village), `вулиця` (street), `будинок` (house/building), and `центр` (center). Contrast `місто` and `село` to establish scale (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-32`).

2.  **Крок 2: Питання "Де?" (The "Where?" Question).** Introduce the question word `де?` for asking about static location. Model simple questions: `Де центр?`, `Де вокзал?`, `Де метро?`. This is the most fundamental question for navigation (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-287`).

3.  **Крок 3: Основні дієслова та напрямки (Core Verbs and Directions).** Introduce the verb `іти` (to go on foot) in its imperative form `ідіть` (for formal 'you') and the adverb `прямо` (straight). The first command learners should master is `Ідіть прямо` (Go straight) (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-286`). Contrast this with `треба їхати` (one needs to go by transport) to introduce the distinction between walking and using a vehicle (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-287`).

4.  **Крок 4: Вказівка напрямку (Giving Directions).** Introduce the core directional adverbs: `праворуч` (to the right) and `ліворуч` (to the left), along with their common synonyms `направо` and `наліво`. Pair them with the imperative `поверніть` (turn). This creates the next key command pattern: `Поверніть праворуч/ліворуч` (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-286`). Add the concepts of `далеко` (far) and `близько` (close) to help learners gauge distance (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-287`).

5.  **Крок 5: Розширення словника (Vocabulary Expansion).** Introduce more specific, high-utility locations: `вокзал` (station), `музей` (museum), `магазин` (shop), `кав'ярня` (café), `парк` (park), `аптека` (pharmacy), `церква` (church). This allows for more complex and realistic navigation scenarios (Джерело: `6-klas-ukrmova-betsa-2023_s0080`, `ext-ulp_youtube-32`, `6-klas-istoria-ukr-hlibovska-2025_s0142`). Distinguish between `залізничний вокзал` (railway station) for a `поїзд` (train) and `автовокзал` (bus station) for an `автобус` (bus) (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-286`).

6.  **Крок 6: Вживання прийменників (Using Prepositions).** Introduce prepositions of location `в/у` (in) and `на` (on) in fixed, high-frequency phrases. Teach `в центрі`, `на вулиці`, `в парку` as chunks. Introduce the accusative of direction with `в/у`: `іти в парк`, `їхати в центр`. Crucially, teach the `на` + locative construction for transport: `їхати на метро`, `їхати на автобусі` (Джерело: `ext-ulp_youtube-287`).

7.  **Крок 7: Ввічливість (Politeness).** All interactions must be framed with etiquette. Start every request with `Вибачте, будь ласка...` (Excuse me, please...) or `Скажіть, будь ласка...` (Please tell me...).
