The art of disagreement

In Module 97 you learned to express your opinion. But what do you do when someone expresses an opinion you don't agree with?

Today you'll learn three key skills:

  • Agreeing with enthusiasm
  • Partially agreeing
  • Disagreeing without causing offense

üí° Did you know?

Ukrainian has an interesting asymmetry: there are many ways to agree, but even more ways to disagree politely. This reflects the cultural value of harmony in communication. Even when Ukrainians categorically disagree, they often start with "–¢–∞–∫, –∞–ª–µ‚Ä¶" (Yes, but‚Ä¶) ‚Äî acknowledging the other person's position before objecting.
