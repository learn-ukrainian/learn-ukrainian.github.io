üìú History Bite

Ukrainian's seven-case system is inherited from Proto-Slavic. Some Slavic languages lost cases over time (Bulgarian has only traces), while others gained forms (some dialects distinguish additional cases). Ukrainian's seven is the "classic" Slavic set.
