"My phone is faster than yours." "The weather is worse today." "She's taller than her brother." Comparisons are everywhere ‚Äî and in Ukrainian, they carry interesting cultural baggage.

Most adjectives use the synthetic form ‚Äî just add an ending. But you can always use –±—ñ–ª—å—à (more) or –º–µ–Ω—à (less) for emphasis or with complex adjectives.

Two Ways to Say "Than"
