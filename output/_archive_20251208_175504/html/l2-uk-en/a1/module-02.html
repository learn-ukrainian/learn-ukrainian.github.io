üîç Myth Buster

Myth: "Ukrainian –ì is just like Russian –ì"
Fact: Ukrainian –ì is a soft, breathy /…¶/ sound, while Russian –ì is a hard /g/. The hard G sound in Ukrainian uses the special letter “ê - one of Ukraine's unique letters that was suppressed during Soviet rule!
