üìñ Introduction

warm-up

Welcome to Ukrainian!

You're about to learn one of the most beautiful alphabets in the world. But here's a secret: you already know some of it.

Ukrainian uses the Cyrillic alphabet - 33 letters that might look mysterious at first. But don't worry! We're going to crack the code step by step. By the end of this lesson, you'll be reading real Ukrainian words.

üí° Did You Know?

The Cyrillic alphabet was NOT invented by Russians! It was created in the 9th century in Bulgaria by followers of Saints Cyril and Methodius. Ukraine adopted it over 1,000 years ago. Ukrainian Cyrillic has unique letters (–á, –Ñ, “ê) that don't exist in Russian!

The Three Types of Letters

We'll learn Cyrillic letters in three groups:

Group Description Example
