üé≠ Culture Corner: Ukrainian Work Culture

Ukrainian workplaces often have a formal hierarchy but friendly atmosphere. Colleagues often celebrate birthdays together, and the New Year corporate party (–∫–æ—Ä–ø–æ—Ä–∞—Ç–∏–≤) is a big tradition! Tea/coffee breaks are common, and many offices have a kitchen area.

üìú History Bite: Work in Ukraine

During Soviet times, unemployment officially didn't exist - everyone was guaranteed work. Today, Ukraine has a dynamic job market. The IT sector especially has grown tremendously, with cities like Kyiv, Lviv, and Kharkiv becoming tech hubs.
