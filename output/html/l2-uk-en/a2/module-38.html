üìú History Bite: Case-Rich Languages

Ukrainian's 7 cases come from Proto-Slavic, which had even more! Finnish has 15 cases, Hungarian has 18. Ukrainian strikes a balance - enough cases to be precise, not so many as to be overwhelming.
