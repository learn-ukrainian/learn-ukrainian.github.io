Theory

### Welcome to A2+!

You've mastered A2 grammar. Now it's time to multiply your vocabulary through word formation!

The power of prefixes:

  • You know: –π—Ç–∏ (to go), —ó—Ö–∞—Ç–∏ (to drive)

  • One root ‚Üí many words!

In Ukrainian, prefixes don't just modify verbs‚Äîthey create entirely new meanings. Learning prefixes is the fastest way to expand your vocabulary.

üí° Did You Know?

Ukrainian has about 20 common verb prefixes. Each prefix adds a specific shade of meaning. By learning these prefixes, you can understand hundreds of verbs you've never seen before!

### The Prefix System

How it works:

  • Base verb: –π—Ç–∏ (to go on foot)
  • Add prefix: –ø—Ä–∏- + –π—Ç–∏ = –ø—Ä–∏–π—Ç–∏ (to arrive)
  • The prefix changes the meaning AND makes it perfective

Key insight: Most prefixed verbs are perfective. The base verb is usually imperfective.
