üöÇ Culture Corner: The Train Schedule (Ukrzaliznytsia)
Ukrainian trains (Ukrzaliznytsia) are religious about the 24-hour clock.
Your ticket will say 14:00, never 2:00 PM.
If your ticket says 02:00, that is 2 AM!
Many foreigners miss trains because they mix up 02:00 and 14:00.
If you have a train at 00:15, that is 15 minutes after midnight. Do not arrive at noon!
Trains in Ukraine are extremely punctual. If it says 14:00, it leaves at 14:00:00.

Section 7: Punctuality in Ukraine

Are Ukrainians punctual?

  • Business/Trains: Yes. Very.
  • Social: "Ukrainian Time" exists.
  • "Lets meet at 18:00" usually means 18:05 or 18:10.
  • But being 30 minutes late is rude.
  • "Academic Quarter" (15 mins) is generally forgiven socially.
