Welcome to Cases!

üîç Myth Buster: "Cases Are Impossible"

  • "I see him" not "I see he" - that's accusative!

Ukrainian just applies this more consistently. Once you see the patterns, it becomes natural.

üí° Did You Know?

üìú History Bite: Why Feminine Changes

Feminine kept its distinct accusative because the -–∞/-—É distinction was very clear and useful. Languages evolve toward efficiency!
