Section 1: The Invisible Shield (Masculine & Neuter)

Good news. Ukrainian grammar is merciful.
If a noun is Masculine Inanimate (an object, not a person) or Neuter, nothing happens. The laser beam bounces off.
The dictionary form (Nominative) is the same as the target form (Accusative).

Pattern: Subject + Verb + Object (No Change)

    • –ú–∏ –±–µ—Ä–µ–º–æ –∫–≤–∏—Ç–æ–∫. (We take a ticket.)

  2. Neuter

üí° The Logic
Why no change? Because ancient speakers didn't think objects needed special respect. They are just things. "I see a table" is clear enough without a flag.

Deep Dive: The Laser Beam (Transitivity)
