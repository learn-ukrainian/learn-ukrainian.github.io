‚ö° Language Link: The Logic of 5
Why 5? Why does the grammar break at 5?
It is an ancient Proto-Indo-European trait.
1, 2, 3, 4 were "Adjectives" (they describe the noun).
5 and above were "Nouns" (A group of five OF something).
"A five of wolves". "A ten of men".
That is why we use Genitive (OF) for 5+.
