ü•£ Deep Dive: Borscht is not Singular
Every family has a different Borscht recipe.
Some debate: With beans or without? With meat or vegetarian?
But ALL agree: It must be served hot and with Smetana.
UNESCO recognized Ukrainian Borscht as cultural heritage. It is that serious.

üç≤ Culture Corner: The Holy Trinity
Ukrainian cuisine rests on three pillars:

  1. Borsch (Beet soup).
  2. Varenyky (Dumplings).
  3. Salo (Cured pork fat).
    If you are a vegetarian, aim for Varenyky with potato or cabbage.
    Salo is often served as an appetizer with garlic and rye bread. It is "Ukrainian Snickers".
