üí° Deep Dive: The Logic
Why mark the object?
In "Boris hears Ivan", both are masculine.
If grammar didn't change endings, "Boris hear Ivan" could mean "Ivan hears Boris" if word order was loose.
By saying "Boris hears Ivana", we know 100% that Ivan is the target, no matter where he stands in the sentence.
