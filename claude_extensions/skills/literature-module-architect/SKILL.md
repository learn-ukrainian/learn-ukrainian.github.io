## Forbidden Actions

1. **NO STANDARD ACTIVITIES** ‚Äî No "match-up", "quiz", "fill-in". Only essays and deep reading.
2. **NO ENGLISH** ‚Äî 100% Ukrainian immersion (English only in MDX `description` field).
3. **DO NOT SIMPLIFY** ‚Äî LIT learners need complex syntax. Don't dumb it down.
4. **DO NOT IGNORE CONTEXT** ‚Äî Always name oppressors, acknowledge trauma, celebrate resilience.
