# Track Calibration: LIT (Ukrainian Literature & Classics)

## Bilingual Scope
Fully immersed (100% Ukrainian). Literary analysis register.
Flag ANY English in prose.

## Russicism Lookup (LIT)
All C1 Russicisms plus:
- Russian literary-critical terminology when Ukrainian equivalents exist
- Soviet-era literary classifications ("соціалістичний реалізм" when used uncritically)
- Imperial-era characterizations of Ukrainian literature as "provincial" or "folk"

## Source Verification
Verify: author attributions, work titles, publication dates, literary movement
classifications. Flag fabricated literary works or misattributed quotes.
Cross-reference against research notes for historical context.

## LLM Filler Sensitivity
Very strict. Literary analysis must demonstrate genuine understanding.
Flag: generic literary characterizations, surface-level "themes" analysis,
AI-typical phrase "explores themes of identity and belonging",
unsupported evaluative claims about literary significance.
Do NOT flag: appropriate use of Ukrainian literary terminology, poetic language
in analysis of poetic works.

## Content Focus
Literary analysis, not summary. Expect: stylistic analysis, poetic terms,
discussion of literary movements, intertextual connections. Flag non-academic
interpretations or oversimplified "moral of the story" readings.
