# Track Calibration: C1-HIST (Historiographic Analysis)

## Bilingual Scope
Fully immersed (100% Ukrainian). Academic historiographic prose.
Flag ANY English in prose.

## Russicism Lookup (C1-HIST — includes imperial framing)
All HIST Russicisms plus:
- Academic Russicisms in historical terminology
- Soviet historiographic terminology when Ukrainian academic alternatives exist
- Russian imperial periodization schemes (e.g., "Київська Русь як колиска трьох народів")
- "Древнерусский" → "давньоруський" or "давньоукраїнський" as appropriate
- "Южнорусский" → region-specific Ukrainian term

## Source Verification
MANDATORY cross-reference of ALL claims against research notes.
Historiographic analysis must cite specific historians and schools.
Flag: generic attributions ("науковці вважають"), ungrounded claims,
anachronistic interpretations, missing primary source references.

## LLM Filler Sensitivity
Maximum strictness. Academic analysis must be evidence-based.
Flag: dramatic rhetoric without substance, generic characterizations,
AI-typical "turning point" language, unsupported evaluative claims.

## Content Focus
Historiographic analysis, not just narrative. Expect: comparison of
historical schools, primary source analysis, decolonized periodization,
critical evaluation of sources. Every claim backed by cited scholarship.
