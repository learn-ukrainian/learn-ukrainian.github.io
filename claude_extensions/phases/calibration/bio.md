# Track Calibration: BIO (Biographical Narrative)

## Bilingual Scope
Fully immersed (100% Ukrainian). Formal biographical narrative.
Flag ANY English in prose.

## Russicism Lookup (BIO)
All B2 Russicisms plus:
- Imperial framing of Ukrainian historical figures
- Soviet-era characterizations of cultural figures
- Russian biographical conventions when Ukrainian equivalents exist

## Source Verification
Cross-reference life events, dates, works, and legacy claims against research.
Flag: fabricated works or misattributed achievements, incorrect dates,
anachronistic characterizations, subjective dramatic framing presented as fact.

## LLM Filler Sensitivity
Very strict. Biographical prose should be factual and substantive.
Flag: overly dramatic framing, generic characterizations ("великий митець"),
AI-typical hagiographic language, emotional appeals without factual basis.
Do NOT flag: appropriate literary evaluation of a figure's legacy when supported.

## Content Focus
Formal biographical narrative. Expect: accurate life chronology,
verified attributions, balanced evaluation of legacy, Ukrainian academic
consensus. No imperial bias or Soviet-era glorification/demonization.
