## Immersion Rules (100%)

**Full Ukrainian immersion at C1.** All content is in Ukrainian:
- All grammar explanations
- All narratives and dialogues
- All cultural content, analyses, literary critiques
- All activity instructions
- All tips and engagement boxes

**English ONLY in:**
- Vocabulary table "Переклад" column (translations)

**No Language Link boxes at C1.** Students learned all grammar terminology by B1.

## Activity Format Reminders (CRITICAL)

### Error-correction (MUST use all 4 callouts)
```markdown
## error-correction: Title
> Instructions here.

1. Sentence with error.
   > [!error] wrong_word
   > [!answer] correct_word
   > [!options] wrong | correct | distractor1 | distractor2
   > [!explanation] Why it's wrong and the rule.
```

### Unjumble (MUST use [!answer] callout)
```markdown
## unjumble: Title
> Instructions here.

1. слова / в / перемішаному / порядку
   > [!answer] Слова в правильному порядку.
```
