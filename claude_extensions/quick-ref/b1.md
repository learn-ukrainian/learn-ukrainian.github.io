## Immersion Rules

**Grammar Modules (50-55%):**
- UKRAINIAN: Examples, dialogues, cultural boxes, simple rules
- ENGLISH: Complex grammar theory (aspect, motion verbs)

**Vocab Modules (65-70%):**
- UKRAINIAN: Narratives, dialogues, examples, simple explanations
- ENGLISH: Only abstract concept definitions
