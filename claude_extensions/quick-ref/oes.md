## Phase 4: Literary Art (M76-100)

**Register:** Literary Art
**Primary Sources:**
- Слово о полку Ігоревім
- Kyiv-Pechersk Patericon
- Slovo o zakoni i blahodati

### Key Phonology: Fall of Yers (Havlik's Law)
- **Strong Yers → Full Vowels:** сънъ → сон, дьнь → день
- **Weak Yers → ∅:** съна → сна, дьньсь → днесь

### Poetic Features
- Inversion
- Chiasmus
- Parallelism
- Personification (Nature as actor)

### Mythological Terms
- **Боян** - legendary bard
- **Велес** - god of cattle/underworld
- **Див** - omen bird
- **Троян** - mythical figure
