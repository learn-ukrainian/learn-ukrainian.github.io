# Track Calibration: OES (Old East Slavic, X-XIII Century)

## Bilingual Scope
Fully immersed (100% Ukrainian). Highly academic register.
Flag ANY English in prose.

## Russicism Lookup (OES — special handling)
IMPORTANT: Old East Slavic texts naturally contain forms that look like
modern Russian. Do NOT flag Russicisms in quoted historical text (guillemets
«» or blockquotes). Only flag Russicisms in the ANALYSIS prose.
- Soviet linguistic terminology when Ukrainian academic equivalents exist
- Russian imperial framing of Old East Slavic as "Old Russian" (давньоруський ≠ давньоросійський)
- "Древнерусский язык" framing — use "давньосхіднослов'янська мова"

## Source Verification
Cross-reference ALL linguistic reconstructions, manuscript references, and
historical claims against research notes. Flag anachronisms in grammar
descriptions. Paleographic references must be verifiable.

## LLM Filler Sensitivity
Maximum strictness. Academic linguistics must be precise.
Flag: generic characterizations of linguistic evolution, AI-typical
dramatic framing of historical linguistics, unsupported reconstruction claims.

## Content Focus
Historical linguistics, primary source analysis, grammar reconstruction,
paleography. Expect highly specialized terminology. Flag anachronisms
or Soviet linguistic framing.
